# Gallery of Arts and History
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
The Gallery of Arts and History is a permanent exhibit located within the Morocco Pavilion in Epcot's World Showcase. It is a walk-through exhibit showcasing Moroccan art, culture, and history. The exhibit is designed to immerse guests in the rich heritage of Morocco through displays of artifacts, historical information, and artistic representations.

## Narrative and Theming
The exhibit's theming is rooted in the cultural and historical context of Morocco. The gallery aims to provide guests with an understanding of Moroccan traditions, craftsmanship, and significant historical events. The architecture and interior design of the gallery itself reflect traditional Moroccan styles, creating an authentic atmosphere. The exhibit's displays are presented in a manner that is both informative and visually engaging, allowing guests to explore the country's artistic and cultural legacy.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter the gallery and proceed through a series of rooms and displays. The exhibit features a collection of artifacts, including pottery, textiles, and jewelry, alongside informational panels and interactive elements. The displays are arranged chronologically and thematically, providing a comprehensive overview of Moroccan history and culture. The experience is self-guided, allowing guests to explore at their own pace. The duration of the experience varies depending on individual interest, typically ranging from 15 to 30 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Gallery of Arts and History is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. The exhibit is accessible to guests with disabilities.
