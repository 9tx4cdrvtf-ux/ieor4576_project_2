# Silly Symphony Swings
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Silly Symphony Swings is a classic "wave swinger" amusement ride located in the Paradise Gardens Park area of Disney California Adventure. The attraction is themed after Disney's *Silly Symphony* animated shorts, particularly "The Band Concert" (1935). It was manufactured by Zamperla and opened in 2001, coinciding with the park's original opening. The ride provides a gentle, family-friendly experience with a focus on visual theming and a nostalgic atmosphere.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's setting evokes a whimsical bandstand, with the ride vehicles resembling swings suspended from a central tower. The surrounding area features stylized musical notes and imagery inspired by the *Silly Symphony* cartoons. The overall aesthetic is intended to transport guests to a cheerful, animated world, reflecting the spirit of the classic Disney shorts.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are seated in individual swings, which are then lifted and rotated around the central tower. The swings gently arc outwards during the ride cycle, providing a sensation of flight. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds. The attraction incorporates musical accompaniment, featuring selections from the *Silly Symphony* series.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Silly Symphony Swings is designed for a broad audience, including families with young children. Guests must be at least 40 inches (102 cm) tall to ride. Guests with certain medical conditions, including but not limited to, heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, should consult with a medical professional before riding.
