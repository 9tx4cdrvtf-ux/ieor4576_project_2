# Disneyland Railroad Discoveryland Station
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
The Disneyland Railroad Discoveryland Station is a railway station located within the Discoveryland area of Disneyland Park Paris. It serves as one of the four stops on the Disneyland Railroad, a narrow-gauge steam train that encircles the park. The station allows guests to embark and disembark the train, providing transportation between different lands and offering scenic views of the park's attractions. The Disneyland Railroad is a replica of the trains Walt Disney used to enjoy.

## Narrative and Theming
The Discoveryland Station is themed to blend seamlessly with the futuristic aesthetic of Discoveryland. The architecture incorporates elements of retro-futurism, featuring metallic structures, exposed machinery, and stylized geometric designs. The station's design suggests a transportation hub of the future, reflecting the land's focus on science fiction and exploration. The station's presence enhances the immersive environment of Discoveryland, contributing to the overall narrative of a world of innovation and technological advancement.

## Attraction Experience
Guests arriving at the Discoveryland Station can board the Disneyland Railroad. The train travels along a circular route, offering views of Adventureland, Fantasyland, Frontierland, and the surrounding areas. The journey includes passing through various themed landscapes and attractions. The ride duration is approximately 20 minutes for a complete circuit. The station features covered platforms to protect guests from the elements.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Disneyland Railroad is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the train with assistance. Strollers must be folded before boarding. Guests with certain medical conditions should consult with a medical professional before riding.
