# Hyperspace Mountain
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Hyperspace Mountain is an indoor steel roller coaster located in Tomorrowland at Disneyland Hong Kong. It is a re-theming of the original Space Mountain attraction, incorporating a Star Wars theme. The ride was manufactured by Vekoma and opened in 2016. It is a significant attraction within the park, offering a thrilling experience with immersive theming.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction places guests in the midst of a Star Wars battle. Riders are cast as Rebel Alliance pilots, tasked with intercepting an Imperial Star Destroyer. The queue line and ride environment are themed to resemble a Rebel Alliance starship, complete with holographic projections and sound effects that enhance the narrative. The overall setting is a space battle, with the coaster track representing a flight path through the conflict.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual rocket-shaped ride vehicles. The coaster begins with a slow ascent into the darkness, followed by a series of drops, turns, and inversions. The ride is enhanced by special effects, including projections of starfighters, lasers, and explosions, synchronized with the coaster's movements. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes, including the loading and unloading process.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Hyperspace Mountain is designed for a broad audience, but it is recommended for those who enjoy thrill rides. The minimum height requirement is 102 cm (40 inches). Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding. The ride utilizes flashing lights and loud noises, which may be unsuitable for some individuals.
