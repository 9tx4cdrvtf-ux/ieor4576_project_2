# Rustler Roundup Shootin' Gallery
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
The Rustler Roundup Shootin' Gallery is a shooting gallery attraction located in Frontierland at Disneyland Park Paris. It is a classic midway-style game where guests use replica rifles to shoot at targets. The attraction provides a competitive and interactive experience, contributing to the immersive theming of Frontierland.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a frontier town shooting range, consistent with the overall Wild West aesthetic of Frontierland. Guests are invited to test their marksmanship skills in a simulated environment. The setting includes rustic wooden facades, animated targets, and sound effects that enhance the atmosphere of a bustling frontier town. The narrative places guests in the role of a sharpshooter, aiming to hit various targets for points and prizes.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are provided with a replica rifle and stand at a designated shooting station. The targets, which include moving and stationary objects, are illuminated with infrared beams. When a target is hit, a sensor detects the shot, triggering a visual or auditory response, such as a target falling or a sound effect. The attraction is self-paced, allowing guests to shoot at their own speed. The duration of a game is typically a few minutes. Prizes are awarded based on the number of targets hit.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Rustler Roundup Shootin' Gallery is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find the standing position challenging. There are no specific health warnings associated with the attraction.
