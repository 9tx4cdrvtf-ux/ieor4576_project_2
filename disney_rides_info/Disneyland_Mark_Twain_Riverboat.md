# Mark Twain Riverboat
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
The Mark Twain Riverboat is a working, authentic-looking paddle steamer attraction located in Disneyland's Frontierland. It is a scenic boat ride that circles the Rivers of America. Built by the Walt Disney Company and constructed by the WED Enterprises, the Mark Twain Riverboat is a significant opening day attraction and a classic example of Disney's commitment to immersive theming and detailed storytelling.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed to the era of Mark Twain and the Mississippi River. Guests board a replica of a 19th-century riverboat, complete with detailed woodwork, period-accurate furnishings, and a working paddle wheel. The ride offers views of the Rivers of America, which are populated with animatronic wildlife, Native American villages, and other scenes designed to evoke the spirit of the American frontier. The boat's design and atmosphere aim to transport guests back in time.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the Mark Twain Riverboat from a dock in Frontierland. The boat slowly circles the Rivers of America, providing views of the various themed environments along the riverbanks. The journey lasts approximately 12-15 minutes. The boat is propelled by a combination of electric motors and a working paddle wheel. The ride offers a gentle, scenic experience suitable for all ages.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Mark Twain Riverboat is suitable for all ages and has no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the boat, though some areas may be difficult to navigate. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions, but guests prone to motion sickness may wish to consider their sensitivity before riding.
