# Haunted Mansion
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Haunted Mansion is a dark ride attraction located in Fantasyland at Tokyo Disneyland. It is based on the original Haunted Mansion attractions found at Disneyland and Walt Disney World. The ride utilizes a "Doom Buggy" ride system, manufactured by Arrow Development, to transport guests through a series of scenes populated by Audio-Animatronic figures and special effects designed to create a spooky atmosphere. The attraction is a significant draw for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents itself as a stately manor house, seemingly abandoned and haunted. Guests are invited to tour the mansion, encountering 999 happy haunts. The narrative unfolds through a series of interconnected rooms, each depicting various spectral scenes, including a ballroom with dancing ghosts, a séance room, and a graveyard with singing busts. The overall theming emphasizes a blend of humor and suspense, drawing on classic ghost story tropes.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual "Doom Buggies" that move along a track through the mansion. The ride utilizes a combination of Pepper's Ghost illusions, projected images, and Audio-Animatronics to create the illusion of ghostly figures and supernatural occurrences. The ride lasts approximately 8 minutes. The experience includes a pre-show in a stretching room, followed by a slow-moving journey through the mansion's various rooms, culminating in a descent into the graveyard.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Haunted Mansion is generally suitable for all ages, although some scenes may be considered frightening for younger children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with heart conditions, back problems, or those who are pregnant should consider the ride's potential for sudden movements and dark environments before riding.
