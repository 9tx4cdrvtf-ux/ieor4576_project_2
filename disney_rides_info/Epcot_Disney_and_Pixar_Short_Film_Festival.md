# Disney and Pixar Short Film Festival
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
The Disney and Pixar Short Film Festival is a 4D cinema attraction located in the Land Pavilion at Epcot. It presents a rotating selection of animated short films produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios and Pixar Animation Studios. The attraction utilizes 3D projection technology combined with in-theater sensory effects to enhance the viewing experience. It is a family-friendly attraction designed to showcase the artistry and storytelling of Disney and Pixar's short film productions.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction does not feature an overarching narrative or extensive theming beyond the general aesthetic of the Land Pavilion. Guests enter a dedicated theater space designed to accommodate a large audience. The focus is primarily on the presentation of the short films themselves, with the theater environment serving as a neutral backdrop.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are seated in a theater equipped with 3D projection and in-theater effects. The experience typically includes three short films, each approximately five to seven minutes in length. Sensory effects, such as wind, water mist, and tactile sensations, are synchronized with the on-screen action to create an immersive experience. The total duration of the attraction, including pre-shows and film presentations, is approximately 18 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests sensitive to motion or enclosed spaces should consider the nature of the 3D and sensory effects before entering. The attraction is wheelchair accessible.
