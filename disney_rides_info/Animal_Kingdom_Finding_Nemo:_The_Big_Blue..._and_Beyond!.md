# Finding Nemo: The Big Blue... and Beyond!
**Location:** Animal Kingdom

## Overview
"Finding Nemo: The Big Blue... and Beyond!" is a live stage musical production located in the Theater in the Wild at Disney's Animal Kingdom. The show, which replaced the original "Finding Nemo – The Musical," features puppetry, original songs, and theatrical staging to retell the story of the 2003 Disney-Pixar film *Finding Nemo*. The attraction is a significant component of DinoLand U.S.A., providing a family-friendly entertainment option within the land.

## Narrative and Theming
The show follows the narrative of *Finding Nemo*, depicting Marlin's journey across the ocean to find his son, Nemo, who has been captured. The setting is primarily underwater, with the stage design and puppetry creating the illusion of an aquatic environment. The story emphasizes themes of family, friendship, and overcoming adversity. The theater's design immerses guests in the underwater world through lighting, sound effects, and the use of large-scale puppets representing various marine creatures.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are seated in a traditional theater setting. The show utilizes a combination of puppetry, animated projections, and live performers to bring the story to life. The performance includes original songs and choreography. The show's duration is approximately 25 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
"Finding Nemo: The Big Blue... and Beyond!" is designed for a general audience, including children and families. There are no height restrictions. Guests with sensitivity to loud noises or flashing lights should be aware that these elements are present during the performance.
