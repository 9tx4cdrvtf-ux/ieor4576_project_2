# Nemo & Friends SeaRider
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Nemo & Friends SeaRider is a motion simulator attraction located in Port Discovery at Tokyo DisneySea. It utilizes a unique "submarine" vehicle to transport guests on a simulated underwater journey. The attraction, which opened in 2017, is a significant addition to the park's family-friendly offerings, allowing guests to experience the world of Disney•Pixar's *Finding Nemo* and *Finding Dory*.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests board the SeaRider, a miniature submarine developed by the Marine Life Institute, designed to shrink to the size of marine life. The narrative centers around a scientific experiment where guests are shrunk and sent on an underwater expedition to observe the ocean's inhabitants. The attraction's theming immerses guests in the vibrant underwater world, featuring familiar characters and environments from the *Finding Nemo* films.

## Attraction Experience
The ride experience begins with guests entering a pre-show area before boarding the SeaRider. Once aboard, the vehicle simulates underwater movement through a combination of motion simulation and projected visuals. The journey includes encounters with various marine creatures and familiar characters from the films. The ride utilizes a combination of 3D projections, motion platforms, and environmental effects, such as bubbles and water sprays, to enhance the immersive experience. The total ride duration is approximately 5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Nemo & Friends SeaRider is generally suitable for all ages. There is no height requirement. Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those prone to motion sickness are advised to consider their condition before riding. Expectant mothers are also advised to exercise caution.
