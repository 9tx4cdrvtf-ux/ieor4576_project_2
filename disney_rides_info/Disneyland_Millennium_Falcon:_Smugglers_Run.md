# Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run is a motion simulator attraction located in Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge at Disneyland. It allows guests to pilot the iconic Millennium Falcon, experiencing a mission as smugglers. The attraction utilizes a combination of motion simulation, interactive gameplay, and immersive theming to create a unique and engaging experience.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are cast as members of a smuggling crew, recruited by the Rodian, Hondo Ohnaka. The story centers around a mission to acquire valuable cargo for Ohnaka, requiring the crew to pilot the Millennium Falcon on a dangerous journey. The queue area is themed as Ohnaka Transport Solutions' docking bay, complete with detailed props and interactive elements that immerse guests in the Star Wars universe.

## Attraction Experience
The attraction utilizes a six-person cockpit configuration, with each guest assigned a specific role: pilot, gunner, or engineer. Pilots control the Falcon's movements, gunners fire blasters, and engineers manage the ship's systems. The ride experience involves a pre-flight briefing, followed by a simulated hyperspace jump and a mission through space, encountering various obstacles and challenges. The ride duration is approximately five minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run is designed for a broad audience. Guests must be at least 38 inches (97 cm) tall to ride. Guests with certain medical conditions, such as heart problems, back or neck issues, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their health before riding.
