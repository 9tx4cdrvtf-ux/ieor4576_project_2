# Meet Cinderella and a Visiting Princess at Princess Fairytale Hall
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Meet Cinderella and a Visiting Princess at Princess Fairytale Hall is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in Fantasyland at the Magic Kingdom. It provides guests with the opportunity to interact with Disney Princesses, primarily Cinderella, and a rotating selection of other princesses. The attraction is a permanent fixture, designed and built by Walt Disney Imagineering, and is a popular destination for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a regal hall within Cinderella Castle. The queue area is designed to resemble a royal waiting room, decorated with portraits, tapestries, and other elegant furnishings. The setting evokes a sense of grandeur and anticipation, immersing guests in a fairytale environment. The narrative positions guests as visitors to the castle, awaiting an audience with the princesses.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a line that winds through the themed waiting area. Upon reaching the meeting area, guests are greeted by Cinderella and a visiting princess. Guests have the opportunity to take photographs, obtain autographs, and engage in brief conversations with the princesses. The experience typically lasts several minutes per guest group. The princesses are costumed in their signature attire.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues are accommodated via accessible queues. No specific health warnings or restrictions are in place, but guests should be prepared for extended periods of standing while waiting in line.
