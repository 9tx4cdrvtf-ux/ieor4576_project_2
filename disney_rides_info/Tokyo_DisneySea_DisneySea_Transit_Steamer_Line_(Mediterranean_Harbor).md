# DisneySea Transit Steamer Line (Mediterranean Harbor)
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
The DisneySea Transit Steamer Line (Mediterranean Harbor) is a boat-based transportation attraction located within the Mediterranean Harbor area of Tokyo DisneySea. It provides guests with a leisurely scenic tour of the park, connecting Mediterranean Harbor with the American Waterfront and Lost River Delta ports. The attraction is a key component of the park's transportation network, offering a relaxing alternative to walking and a unique perspective on the themed environments.

## Narrative and Theming
The steamers are designed to resemble classic steam-powered vessels, consistent with the early 20th-century aesthetic of Mediterranean Harbor. The queue area is themed to blend seamlessly with the harbor's architecture, creating the illusion of boarding a working passenger ship. The attraction reinforces the park's overall narrative of exploration and discovery, allowing guests to experience the various themed ports from a waterborne vantage point.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of several meticulously detailed steamers. The boats travel along a predetermined route, offering views of the Venetian canals, the towering S.S. Columbia, and the exotic landscapes of the Lost River Delta. The journey lasts approximately 15 minutes, providing ample time to appreciate the park's intricate theming and observe other attractions. The steamers utilize a combination of electric propulsion and visual effects to enhance the immersive experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The DisneySea Transit Steamer Line (Mediterranean Harbor) is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may require assistance to board and disembark the boats. The attraction is generally accessible to guests with disabilities, though specific accommodations may vary.
