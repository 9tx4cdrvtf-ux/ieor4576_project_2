# Bruce's Shark World
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Bruce's Shark World was a dark ride attraction located within the Seas with Nemo & Friends Pavilion at Epcot. It was a re-theming of the original "Living Seas" ride, incorporating characters and elements from the Disney-Pixar film *Finding Nemo*. The attraction provided a gentle, educational experience focused on marine life, particularly sharks, within a fictional narrative.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests were immersed in the underwater world of *Finding Nemo*, encountering familiar characters and environments from the film. The narrative centered around a journey through the ocean, with a focus on the fictional Shark World, a place where sharks, including the friendly Bruce, were portrayed. The attraction aimed to educate guests about marine life while maintaining a lighthearted and family-friendly atmosphere.

## Attraction Experience
Guests boarded clam-shaped Omnimover vehicles that traveled through various scenes. The ride incorporated Audio-Animatronics, projections, and special effects to create the illusion of being underwater. The journey included encounters with Nemo, Marlin, Dory, and other characters, culminating in a visit to Bruce's Shark World. The ride duration was approximately 5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Bruce's Shark World was designed to be accessible to a wide audience, with no height restrictions. The ride was suitable for all ages. Guests with motion sickness or other health concerns were advised to consider the ride's gentle motion before boarding.
