# The Barnstormer
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
The Barnstormer is a junior roller coaster located in the Storybook Circus area of Fantasyland at the Magic Kingdom. Manufactured by Zamperla, the ride is designed to provide a gentle introduction to roller coasters for younger guests. It is themed around Goofy as a barnstorming pilot.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers on Goofy's attempts at aerial acrobatics. Guests are invited to join Goofy's stunt flying school. The queue area features whimsical props and imagery related to aviation, such as vintage airplanes, repair tools, and instructional signage. The ride vehicles are designed to resemble biplanes, further immersing guests in the barnstorming theme.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the biplane-themed ride vehicles and are secured with lap bars. The coaster begins with a slow ascent up a small lift hill. The ride then proceeds through a series of gentle drops, turns, and small hills, providing a brief but exciting experience. The ride duration is approximately one minute. The track layout is relatively simple, designed for a smooth and enjoyable experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Barnstormer is primarily targeted towards children and families. The height requirement is 35 inches (89 cm). Guests with certain health conditions, including back or neck problems, heart conditions, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their condition before riding.
