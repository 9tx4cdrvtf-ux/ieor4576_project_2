# Jungle Cruise: Wildlife Expeditions
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Jungle Cruise: Wildlife Expeditions is a boat-based dark ride attraction located in Adventureland at Tokyo Disneyland. It is a re-theming of the original Jungle Cruise attraction found at several other Disney parks. The ride utilizes a system of electric boats navigating a man-made river, showcasing animatronic animals and themed environments.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests embark on a riverboat expedition led by a live skipper, venturing into the exotic rivers of the world. The narrative centers around a journey through various jungle environments, encountering animatronic wildlife and experiencing the humorous commentary of the skipper. The theming incorporates elements from Asia, Africa, and South America, creating a sense of exploration and discovery.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with guests boarding a themed riverboat. The boat travels along a waterway, passing through various scenes. These scenes include animatronic animals such as hippos, elephants, and crocodiles, as well as native villages and other themed set pieces. The skipper provides a live narration, delivering jokes and pointing out various sights. The ride duration is approximately 10 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for a general audience. There are no specific height restrictions. Guests with a sensitivity to enclosed spaces or those who may be easily frightened by animatronic figures should consider their personal comfort levels before riding.
