# Star Tours – The Adventures Continue
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
Star Tours – The Adventures Continue is a 3D motion simulator attraction located in Disney's Hollywood Studios. It is a reimagining of the original Star Tours attraction, utilizing updated technology and a randomized series of scenes from the Star Wars franchise. The attraction is manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering and features a variety of destinations and characters, offering a unique experience with each ride. It is a significant attraction within the park, drawing large crowds and contributing to the immersive Star Wars theming.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are cast as passengers on a Starspeeder 1000 series space transport, embarking on a seemingly routine trip to a distant planet. However, the journey is quickly disrupted by unexpected events, placing the passengers in the midst of various conflicts and encounters from the Star Wars universe. The queue area is themed as a spaceport, featuring droids, control panels, and other elements designed to immerse guests in the Star Wars setting.

## Attraction Experience
The ride experience involves guests entering a motion simulator cabin. The Starspeeder 1000 is a motion-based vehicle synchronized with a 3D film projected onto a large screen. The ride sequence is randomized, with guests experiencing different combinations of destinations and scenes, including encounters with iconic characters and locations. The ride incorporates sensory effects such as wind, simulated heat, and motion to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for a wide audience. Guests must be at least 40 inches (102 cm) tall to ride. Guests with a history of heart conditions, back or neck problems, motion sickness, or other health concerns should consider the ride's intensity before boarding. Expectant mothers are advised not to ride.
