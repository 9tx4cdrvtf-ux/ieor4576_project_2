# Donald's Boat
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Donald's Boat is a walk-through attraction located in the Toontown area of Tokyo Disneyland. It allows guests to explore a whimsical, grounded boat that serves as Donald Duck's personal residence. The attraction is designed to be family-friendly and offers a playful environment for guests of all ages to interact with.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents Donald Duck's boat as a dilapidated but charming vessel permanently docked in Toontown. The theming emphasizes Donald's personality, with the boat's interior and exterior reflecting his nautical interests and often chaotic lifestyle. Guests are invited to explore the boat's various areas, including the deck, cabin, and storage spaces, all filled with themed props and details.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter the boat via a gangplank and proceed through the various areas at their own pace. The experience is primarily a self-guided exploration, allowing guests to discover the boat's features, such as nautical equipment, personal belongings, and humorous details. There are no ride vehicles or moving elements; the attraction relies on static sets and props to create its immersive environment. The duration of the experience is approximately 5-10 minutes, depending on the guest's exploration.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Donald's Boat is suitable for all ages and has no height restrictions. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions associated with this attraction. Guests are advised to exercise caution while navigating the boat's interior, as some areas may have uneven surfaces or low ceilings.
