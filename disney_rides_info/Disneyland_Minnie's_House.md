# Minnie's House
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Minnie's House is a walk-through attraction located in Mickey's Toontown at Disneyland. It allows guests to explore the whimsical home of Minnie Mouse. The attraction is designed to be interactive, offering opportunities for guests to engage with various props and settings within the house. It is a permanent fixture of Mickey's Toontown, contributing to the land's immersive cartoon aesthetic.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents Minnie Mouse's residence within the fictional town of Toontown. The house is designed with exaggerated proportions and bright colors, reflecting the cartoon style of the land. Guests are invited to visit Minnie's home, implying she is away, allowing them to explore her kitchen, living room, and other areas. The theming emphasizes domesticity and charm, with details such as furniture, appliances, and personal items reflecting Minnie's personality.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter Minnie's House and proceed through a series of themed rooms. The kitchen features interactive elements such as a working oven and telephone. The living room includes a piano and other props. Guests can also explore Minnie's bedroom and backyard. The experience is self-guided, allowing guests to explore at their own pace. The duration of the experience is typically 5-10 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Minnie's House is designed for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with the attraction. The experience is primarily geared towards younger children and families, offering a gentle and engaging environment.
