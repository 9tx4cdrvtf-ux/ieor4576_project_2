# Westernland Shootin' Gallery
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
The Westernland Shootin' Gallery is a shooting gallery attraction located in the Westernland area of Tokyo Disneyland. It is a classic midway-style game where guests use replica rifles to shoot at targets. The attraction provides a brief, interactive experience themed to the American Old West.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a frontier shooting range, consistent with the Westernland area's overall aesthetic. Guests are transported to a dusty, sun-baked town where they can test their marksmanship skills. The setting includes various targets depicting classic Western imagery, such as saloon signs, bottles, and moving figures of animals and outlaws. The environment is designed to evoke the atmosphere of a frontier town, complete with wooden structures and period-appropriate props.

## Attraction Experience
Guests purchase a set of BB pellets for a small fee and are provided with a replica rifle. They then aim at the various targets within the gallery. Upon hitting a target, a corresponding effect is triggered, such as a target falling or a light illuminating. The attraction is self-operated, and the duration of the experience is determined by the guest. There is no ride vehicle; guests stand in designated shooting stalls.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Westernland Shootin' Gallery is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests should be aware that the attraction uses BB pellets, and eye protection is recommended. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
