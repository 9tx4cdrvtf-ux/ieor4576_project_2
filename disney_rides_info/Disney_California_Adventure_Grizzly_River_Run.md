# Grizzly River Run
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Grizzly River Run is a river rapids ride located in the Grizzly Peak area of Disney California Adventure. The attraction, manufactured by Intamin, simulates a whitewater rafting experience through a California wilderness setting. It is notable for its integration with the natural landscape and its use of a large, man-made mountain as a central visual element.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride's narrative centers around a rafting expedition through the fictional Grizzly River. Guests are immersed in a wilderness environment, encountering various themed elements such as waterfalls, geysers, and wildlife. The setting evokes the rugged beauty of the California Sierra Nevada mountains, with the towering Grizzly Peak serving as a backdrop and integral part of the ride's course.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board circular rafts that accommodate approximately twenty riders. The ride begins with a gentle ascent up a conveyor lift hill. The rafts then navigate a winding course through the river, encountering rapids, drops, and a spinning section. The ride incorporates water effects, including a significant drop down a waterfall, resulting in substantial water spray and potential for guests to get wet. The ride duration is approximately five minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Grizzly River Run is suitable for a wide range of guests, but has a minimum height requirement of 42 inches (107 cm). Guests prone to motion sickness or with back problems should consider the ride's potential for rough movement. Expectant mothers are advised against riding.
