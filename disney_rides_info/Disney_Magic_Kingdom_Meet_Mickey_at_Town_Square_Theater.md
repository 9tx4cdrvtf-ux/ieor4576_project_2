# Meet Mickey at Town Square Theater
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Meet Mickey at Town Square Theater is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in the Town Square area of the Magic Kingdom theme park at Walt Disney World. It allows guests to interact with Mickey Mouse in a dedicated, themed environment. The attraction is a permanent fixture, providing a classic Disney experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a classic theater, with a marquee and posters advertising Mickey Mouse's various performances. Guests enter a queue area designed to resemble a backstage area, complete with props and set pieces. The overall atmosphere is intended to evoke the feeling of being behind the scenes of a theatrical production, preparing to meet the star.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue through the themed waiting area before entering Mickey's backstage dressing room. The experience culminates in a one-on-one meet-and-greet with Mickey Mouse. Mickey interacts with guests, posing for photos and signing autographs. The duration of the interaction varies depending on the number of guests in line. The attraction utilizes a FastPass+ system (currently Disney Genie+) to manage wait times.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Meet Mickey at Town Square Theater is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the attraction, though they may need to use the accessible entrance. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
