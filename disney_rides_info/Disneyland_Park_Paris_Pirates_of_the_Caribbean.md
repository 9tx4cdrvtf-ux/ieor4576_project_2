# Pirates of the Caribbean
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Pirates of the Caribbean is a dark ride attraction located in Adventureland at Disneyland Park Paris. The ride, a reimagining of the original Disneyland attraction, transports guests through a pirate-themed adventure. It utilizes a boat ride system and incorporates Audio-Animatronics figures and elaborate set designs to create an immersive experience. The attraction is a significant draw for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride's narrative centers on the Golden Age of Piracy, depicting the lives and conflicts of buccaneers in the Caribbean. Guests are immersed in a world of swashbuckling adventure, encountering scenes of pirate raids, treasure hunts, and imprisonment. The setting evokes a Caribbean port town, complete with detailed buildings, atmospheric lighting, and sound effects, including the iconic "Yo Ho (A Pirate's Life for Me)" theme song.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board boats that navigate through a series of scenes. The ride begins with a descent into a cavern, followed by encounters with pirate battles, a burning town, and a treasure-filled grotto. Audio-Animatronic figures, including Captain Jack Sparrow, are featured throughout the ride. The attraction incorporates water effects, lighting, and sound to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 8 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Pirates of the Caribbean is generally suitable for all ages. There is no height requirement. Guests with heart conditions, back problems, or other health concerns should consider the ride's potential for sudden drops and exposure to darkness. Expectant mothers are advised to avoid the attraction.
