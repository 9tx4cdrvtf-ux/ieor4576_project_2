# Awesome Planet
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Awesome Planet is a 10-minute film presentation located within the Land Pavilion at Epcot. The attraction, produced by Walt Disney Imagineering, showcases the beauty and diversity of planet Earth. It replaced the previous film, *Symbiosis*, and utilizes a large, high-definition screen and immersive sensory effects to enhance the viewing experience. The film focuses on the interconnectedness of Earth's ecosystems and the importance of environmental stewardship.

## Narrative and Theming
The film presents a visual journey across various biomes, from lush rainforests to arid deserts, and explores the impact of human activity on the planet. It emphasizes the delicate balance of nature and the need for conservation. The narrative is delivered through stunning visuals and a musical score, aiming to inspire appreciation for the Earth's natural wonders. The setting is a large, circular theater designed to fully immerse guests in the film's imagery.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter a large theater and take their seats. The film begins with a series of sweeping shots of Earth from space, followed by sequences showcasing diverse landscapes and wildlife. Sensory effects, including wind, mist, and scents, are incorporated to enhance the immersive experience. The film culminates in a message of hope and a call to action regarding environmental responsibility. The total duration of the experience is approximately 10 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Awesome Planet is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction is generally accessible to guests with disabilities. Guests sensitive to flashing lights or loud noises should be aware that these elements are present in the film.
