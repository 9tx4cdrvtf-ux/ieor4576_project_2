# Under the Sea - Journey of The Little Mermaid
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Under the Sea - Journey of The Little Mermaid is a dark ride attraction located in Fantasyland at the Magic Kingdom. It is a slow-moving, omnimover ride system manufactured by the Walt Disney Imagineering. The attraction opened in 2012, replacing the former 20,000 Leagues Under the Sea: Submarine Voyage. It is based on Disney's 1989 animated film *The Little Mermaid* and provides a retelling of the film's key scenes.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the story of Ariel, a mermaid princess who dreams of becoming human. The queue area is themed as an underwater grotto, with rockwork and various sea-themed elements. The ride vehicles, styled as clamshells, transport guests through scenes depicting iconic moments from the film, including Ariel's interactions with Flounder and Sebastian, Ursula's lair, and the climactic battle against the sea witch. The attraction utilizes a combination of Audio-Animatronics figures, projected animation, and special effects to create an underwater environment.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the clamshell vehicles and embark on a journey through the story. The ride progresses through a series of scenes, each featuring Audio-Animatronic figures and projections. The ride includes musical sequences featuring songs from the film, such as "Under the Sea" and "Part of Your World." The ride duration is approximately 5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is generally suitable for all ages. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or those prone to motion sickness should be aware of the ride's effects.
