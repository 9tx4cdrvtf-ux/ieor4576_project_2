# Les Voyages de Pinocchio
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Les Voyages de Pinocchio (The Adventures of Pinocchio) is a dark ride located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park Paris. The attraction is based on the 1940 Disney animated film *Pinocchio*, and it allows guests to experience key scenes from the movie. The ride utilizes a track-based system with vehicles designed to resemble wooden carts.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride immerses guests in the story of Pinocchio, a wooden puppet brought to life by the Blue Fairy. The narrative follows Pinocchio's journey as he strives to become a real boy, encountering various characters and challenges along the way. The attraction's theming recreates iconic locations from the film, including Geppetto's workshop, Pleasure Island, and Monstro the whale's belly. The environments are designed with vibrant colors, detailed props, and animated figures.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board wooden cart-like vehicles that travel along a track through a series of scenes. The ride incorporates audio-animatronics, special lighting effects, and music from the film to enhance the storytelling. The journey includes encounters with characters such as Geppetto, Jiminy Cricket, Stromboli, and the Coachman. The ride duration is approximately 2 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Les Voyages de Pinocchio is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or a sensitivity to enclosed spaces should be aware of the ride's effects. The attraction is accessible to guests using wheelchairs, though they must transfer to a ride vehicle.
