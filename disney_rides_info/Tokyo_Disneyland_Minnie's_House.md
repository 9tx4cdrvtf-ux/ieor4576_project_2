# Minnie's House
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Minnie's House is a walk-through attraction located in Toontown at Tokyo Disneyland. It allows guests to explore the home of Minnie Mouse, offering an interactive experience focused on character interaction and themed environments. The attraction is a standard example of a themed walkthrough, designed to immerse guests in a cartoonish setting.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents Minnie Mouse's residence as a vibrant and welcoming home. The theming is consistent with the Toontown aesthetic, featuring exaggerated shapes, bright colors, and cartoonish details. Guests are invited to step into Minnie's world, encountering various rooms decorated to reflect her personality and interests. The narrative centers around Minnie's daily life, with clues and props suggesting her hobbies, relationships, and activities.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter Minnie's House and proceed through a series of themed rooms, including a living room, kitchen, and bedroom. Interactive elements are present throughout, such as a telephone that "rings" with a message from Mickey Mouse, a kitchen where guests can "bake" a cake, and a vanity where they can admire themselves in a mirror. The experience is self-guided, allowing guests to explore at their own pace. The duration of the experience is approximately 10-15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Minnie's House is designed for guests of all ages, with no height restrictions. The attraction is primarily aimed at families and young children. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions, making it accessible to a wide range of visitors.
