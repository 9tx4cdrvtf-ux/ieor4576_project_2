# Toy Story Mania!
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
Toy Story Mania! is a 4D interactive ride-through attraction located in Toy Story Land at Disney's Hollywood Studios. The attraction is a combination of a traditional dark ride and a carnival midway game, utilizing a trackless ride system and interactive elements. It was developed by Walt Disney Imagineering and is a significant draw for families within the park.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in a vibrant, oversized world inspired by the *Toy Story* film franchise. The queue area and ride environment are designed to make guests feel as though they have shrunk to the size of a toy. The narrative centers around Andy's toy box, where guests are invited to play a series of carnival games hosted by the characters from the *Toy Story* films. The setting is bright, colorful, and filled with familiar characters and props from the movies.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board ride vehicles that travel through a series of virtual shooting galleries. Each gallery features a different carnival game, such as shooting darts at balloons or tossing rings onto aliens. Riders use pull-strings on their vehicles to launch projectiles at the targets displayed on screen. The ride incorporates 3D effects, physical props, and audio-animatronics to enhance the immersive experience. The ride lasts approximately 5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Toy Story Mania! is designed for a family audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with back or neck problems, or those sensitive to strobe effects, should consider the advisability of riding.
