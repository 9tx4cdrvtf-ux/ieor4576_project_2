# Pirates of the Caribbean
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Pirates of the Caribbean is a dark ride attraction located in Adventureland at Tokyo Disneyland. It is a boat ride that immerses guests in a pirate-themed adventure. The attraction is based on the original concept developed for Disneyland in California and is a significant anchor attraction within the park.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction transports guests to the Golden Age of Piracy, specifically the Caribbean. The narrative follows the exploits of pirates as they plunder a town, engage in battles, and ultimately face the consequences of their actions. The theming includes detailed sets, animatronic figures, and atmospheric effects designed to create a convincing pirate world. Guests are presented with scenes of swashbuckling, treasure hunting, and the perils of pirate life.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board boats that navigate through a series of scenes. The ride begins with a tranquil bayou setting before transitioning into a pirate town under siege. The journey includes encounters with animatronic pirates, cannon fire effects, and a descent into a cavern. The ride utilizes Audio-Animatronics technology to bring the pirate characters to life. The duration of the ride is approximately 15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Pirates of the Caribbean is generally suitable for all ages, although younger children may find some scenes intense. There are no height restrictions. Guests with a history of heart conditions, back problems, or those who are pregnant should consider the ride's potential for sudden movements and loud noises before boarding.
