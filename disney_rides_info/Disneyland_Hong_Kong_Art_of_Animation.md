# Art of Animation
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Art of Animation is a walk-through attraction located in the Toy Story Land area of Disneyland Hong Kong. It allows guests to explore the creative process behind Disney animation, focusing on the development of characters and environments. The attraction is a self-guided experience, offering interactive elements and displays showcasing the evolution of animated films.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the world of animation through themed environments inspired by popular Disney-Pixar films. The setting simulates a working animation studio, with concept art, character models, and interactive exhibits. Guests are presented with the opportunity to learn about the techniques used to bring animated characters to life, from initial sketches to final production. The theming emphasizes the collaborative nature of animation, highlighting the roles of artists, storytellers, and technicians.

## Attraction Experience
Guests proceed through a series of themed rooms, each dedicated to a specific film or animation technique. Interactive displays allow guests to experiment with animation principles, such as creating flipbooks or manipulating character models. The attraction features detailed set pieces, including character maquettes and concept art. The experience is self-paced, allowing guests to spend as much time as desired at each exhibit. The duration of the experience is approximately 15-20 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Art of Animation is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction is accessible to guests with disabilities. No specific health warnings or restrictions are in place.
