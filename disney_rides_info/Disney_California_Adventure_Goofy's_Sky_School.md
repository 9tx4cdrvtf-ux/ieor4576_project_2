# Goofy's Sky School
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Goofy's Sky School is a steel, wild mouse roller coaster located in the Paradise Gardens Park area of Disney California Adventure. Manufactured by Mack Rides, the attraction opened in 2001 as Mulholland Madness, and was re-themed and re-opened as Goofy's Sky School in 2012. The ride offers a family-friendly coaster experience with a focus on the character Goofy and his attempts at flight school.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers around Goofy's misadventures as a student pilot. Guests are enrolled in Goofy's Sky School, a flight training program. The queue area features aviation-themed props and instructional signage, creating the illusion of a school environment. The ride vehicles are designed to resemble small, open-cockpit airplanes, further immersing guests in the theme.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with a slow ascent up a lift hill. Upon reaching the summit, the coaster navigates a series of sharp turns, drops, and hairpin curves. The layout is characterized by its compact design and unpredictable movements, typical of a wild mouse coaster. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Goofy's Sky School is designed for a family audience. Guests must be at least 42 inches (107 cm) tall to ride. Guests with a history of heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding.
