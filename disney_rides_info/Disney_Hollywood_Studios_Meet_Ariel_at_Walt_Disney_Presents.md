# Meet Ariel at Walt Disney Presents
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
Meet Ariel at Walt Disney Presents is a character meet-and-greet experience located within the Walt Disney Presents attraction. It allows guests to interact with Ariel, the titular character from Disney's animated film *The Little Mermaid*. The attraction is a standard meet-and-greet, providing photo opportunities and brief interactions with the character.

## Narrative and Theming
The meet-and-greet is situated within a themed environment designed to evoke Ariel's underwater grotto. The setting features elements inspired by the film, including props and backdrops that create an immersive atmosphere. The area is decorated with nautical elements and vibrant colors, reflecting the visual style of *The Little Mermaid*.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area before being directed to meet Ariel. The interaction typically involves a brief conversation, photo opportunities, and autograph signing. The duration of each interaction is generally a few minutes. The experience utilizes standard character meet-and-greet procedures, with Disney PhotoPass photographers available to capture professional photos.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Meet Ariel at Walt Disney Presents is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height requirements or specific health restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the meet-and-greet, but may need to utilize the accessible entrance.
