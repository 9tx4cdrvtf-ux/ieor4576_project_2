# Jasmine's Flying Carpets
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Jasmine's Flying Carpets is a spinner ride located in the Arabian Coast area of Tokyo DisneySea. The attraction is a variation of the "Magic Carpets of Aladdin" ride found in other Disney parks. It provides a gentle, family-friendly experience, offering panoramic views of the Arabian Coast and is a key element in immersing guests in the themed land. The ride is manufactured by Zamperla.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed after the Disney animated film *Aladdin*. Guests board individual carpet vehicles, which are designed to resemble the magic carpets from the film. The surrounding area is designed to evoke the bustling marketplace of Agrabah, with detailed architecture, vibrant colors, and themed props. The queue area is decorated with market stalls and other elements that enhance the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of sixteen carpet vehicles, each accommodating up to four riders. The carpets ascend and descend, allowing riders to control the vertical movement of their individual carpet via a lever. The ride also features a tilting mechanism, allowing for a degree of controlled movement. The ride cycle lasts approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds, providing views of the surrounding Arabian Coast area, including the nearby "Sindbad's Storybook Voyage" attraction.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Jasmine's Flying Carpets is designed for a general audience, including families with young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with certain medical conditions, such as those prone to motion sickness, may wish to consider the ride's gentle nature before boarding.
