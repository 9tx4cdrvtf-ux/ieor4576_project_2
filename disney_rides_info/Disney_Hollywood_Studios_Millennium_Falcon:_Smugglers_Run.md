# Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run
**Location:** Disney Hollywood Studios

## Overview
Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run is a motion simulator attraction located in Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge at Disney's Hollywood Studios. The ride allows guests to pilot, gun, or engineer the iconic *Millennium Falcon* starship on a smuggling mission. The attraction was manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering and is a significant component of the immersive Star Wars-themed land.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are cast as smugglers hired by the space pirate Hondo Ohnaka. The queue winds through Ohnaka Transport Solutions, a company that facilitates smuggling operations. The pre-show introduces the mission and the roles guests will play aboard the *Millennium Falcon*. The attraction's theming immerses guests in the Star Wars universe, recreating the ship's interior with meticulous detail, including the iconic Dejarik (holochess) table.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are assigned one of three roles: pilot, gunner, or engineer. Pilots control the ship's movement, gunners fire blasters, and engineers manage the ship's systems. The ride utilizes a motion simulator to simulate the experience of flying the *Millennium Falcon* through space, encountering obstacles and engaging in combat. The experience lasts approximately five minutes, including the pre-show.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for a wide audience. Guests must be at least 38 inches (97 cm) tall to ride. Guests with certain medical conditions, such as heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their health before riding.
