# Challenge Trails at Camp Discovery
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Challenge Trails at Camp Discovery is an outdoor, interactive adventure course located within the Camp Discovery area of Shanghai Disneyland. This attraction offers guests the opportunity to navigate a series of elevated rope bridges, suspension walkways, and climbing challenges themed to the exploration of undiscovered lands. The course is designed to provide a physical and immersive experience, allowing guests to test their agility and problem-solving skills.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around the spirit of exploration and discovery, placing guests in the role of adventurers exploring the undiscovered lands of Camp Discovery. The environment is designed to evoke a sense of rugged wilderness, with rockwork, themed foliage, and scenic overlooks. Guests are encouraged to embrace the spirit of adventure as they traverse the various challenges, encountering themed elements and interactive features along the way.

## Attraction Experience
Guests begin by selecting one of several trails, each offering a different level of difficulty. The trails consist of a series of interconnected platforms and obstacles, including rope bridges, swinging steps, and climbing walls. Guests are secured with a safety harness and are guided by Cast Members. The experience duration varies depending on the chosen trail and the guest's pace, typically ranging from 15 to 30 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Challenge Trails at Camp Discovery is suitable for guests of varying ages and physical abilities, provided they meet the height requirement. Guests must be at least 105 cm (41 inches) tall to participate. Guests with certain medical conditions, including heart problems, back or neck issues, or those who are pregnant, are advised against participating. Guests should also be in good physical condition and able to follow safety instructions.
