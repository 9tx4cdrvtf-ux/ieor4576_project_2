# Zootopia: Better Zoogether! - NEW!
**Location:** Animal Kingdom

## Overview
"Zootopia: Better Zoogether! - NEW!" is a newly introduced attraction at Animal Kingdom, offering a unique experience within the park's existing themed environments. The attraction is a trackless dark ride, utilizing innovative ride technology to transport guests through a series of scenes based on the animated film *Zootopia*. The ride enhances the park's immersive storytelling capabilities by integrating a popular intellectual property into the existing animal-focused theme.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are invited to become honorary citizens of Zootopia, joining Judy Hopps and Nick Wilde on a new adventure. The narrative centers around a celebration of friendship and unity within the diverse city. The attraction's theming replicates iconic locations from the film, including the bustling city center, the icy Tundratown, and the humid Rainforest District. Detailed set design, animatronics, and projection mapping are employed to create a believable and engaging environment.

## Attraction Experience
The ride utilizes a trackless ride system, allowing vehicles to move freely through the scenes. Guests board vehicles designed to resemble Zootopia transit vehicles. The ride experience involves a series of scenes featuring animatronic characters, special effects, and projection mapping. The duration of the ride is approximately 4 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for a wide range of ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with motion sensitivities or pre-existing health conditions should consider the ride's potential for sudden movements and visual effects before boarding.
