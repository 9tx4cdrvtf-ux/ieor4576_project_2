# A Pirate's Adventure ~ Treasures of the Seven Seas
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
A Pirate's Adventure ~ Treasures of the Seven Seas is an interactive, location-based game experience located within Adventureland at the Magic Kingdom. It utilizes the existing environment and infrastructure of the land, enhancing the guest experience through the use of a collectible treasure map and a series of interactive challenges. The attraction was developed by Walt Disney Imagineering and opened in 2013.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests become "Pirate Recruits" and are tasked with helping Captain Jack Sparrow locate treasures hidden throughout Adventureland. The narrative is interwoven with the existing pirate theme of the land, drawing upon the lore established by the *Pirates of the Caribbean* franchise. The game involves solving clues, completing missions, and interacting with various elements within Adventureland to uncover the treasures.

## Attraction Experience
Guests obtain a treasure map at a designated kiosk and use it to embark on five different adventures. Each adventure involves a series of interactive challenges, triggered by holding the map up to specific locations. These challenges utilize augmented reality technology, special effects, and audio cues to enhance the experience. Completion of each adventure unlocks a "treasure" and contributes to the overall narrative. The duration of each adventure varies, with the entire experience taking approximately 30-45 minutes to complete.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can participate, as the experience primarily involves walking around Adventureland. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
