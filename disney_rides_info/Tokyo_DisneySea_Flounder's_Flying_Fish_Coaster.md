# Flounder's Flying Fish Coaster
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Flounder's Flying Fish Coaster is a junior roller coaster located in the Mermaid Lagoon area of Tokyo DisneySea. Designed by the Walt Disney Imagineering team, the attraction is a family-friendly coaster manufactured by Zamperla. It offers a gentle, yet thrilling, experience suitable for younger guests and those seeking a less intense ride. The coaster is a key component of the Mermaid Lagoon's immersive underwater environment.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around the character Flounder from Disney's *The Little Mermaid*. Guests are invited to join Flounder on a playful adventure through his underwater world. The queue area is designed to resemble a coral reef, with vibrant colors and whimsical sea creature decorations. The coaster cars are designed to look like flying fish, adding to the whimsical atmosphere. The ride's narrative focuses on the joy of flight and exploration within Flounder's domain.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the "flying fish" coaster cars, which ascend a small lift hill. The ride then proceeds through a series of gentle dips, turns, and small hills, providing a brief but enjoyable experience. The ride incorporates minimal special effects, relying primarily on the themed environment and the sensation of movement. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Flounder's Flying Fish Coaster is primarily targeted towards children and families. The minimum height requirement is 90 cm (35 inches). Guests with back problems, heart conditions, or other pre-existing health issues should consider the ride's gentle nature before boarding. Expectant mothers are advised against riding.
