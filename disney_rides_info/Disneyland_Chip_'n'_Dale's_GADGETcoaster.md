# Chip 'n' Dale's GADGETcoaster
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Chip 'n' Dale's GADGETcoaster is a junior roller coaster located in Mickey's Toontown at Disneyland. The attraction, designed by Walt Disney Imagineering, provides a family-friendly thrill experience. It is themed around Gadget Hackwrench, a character from the animated series *Chip 'n Dale: Rescue Rangers*, and her inventions.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride's narrative centers on Gadget's latest invention: a coaster built from various found objects. Guests are invited to experience a thrilling ride through Gadget's workshop and backyard. The queue area features oversized tools, contraptions, and other whimsical elements, immersing riders in the world of the *Rescue Rangers*. The coaster's track is designed to resemble a makeshift contraption, further enhancing the theme.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a train consisting of individual cars. The ride begins with a slow ascent up a small lift hill. The coaster then navigates a series of dips, turns, and small hills, providing gentle thrills suitable for younger riders. The ride incorporates themed elements such as oversized acorns and other props. The entire experience lasts approximately one minute.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Chip 'n' Dale's GADGETcoaster is designed for families with young children. The height requirement is 35 inches (89 cm). Guests with certain medical conditions, including heart problems or back issues, are advised to consult with a medical professional before riding. Expectant mothers are also advised against riding.
