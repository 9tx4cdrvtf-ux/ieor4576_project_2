# Spider-Man W.E.B. Adventure Single Rider
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
Spider-Man W.E.B. Adventure Single Rider is a 3D interactive dark ride located in the Avengers Campus at Disney Adventure World Paris. The attraction, developed by Walt Disney Imagineering, utilizes trackless ride vehicles and interactive projection mapping technology. It allows guests to virtually sling webs alongside Spider-Man to combat rogue Spider-Bots. The Single Rider queue offers an alternative for guests seeking to experience the attraction with a potentially shorter wait time.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are recruited as honorary recruits of the Worldwide Engineering Brigade (W.E.B.) to assist Spider-Man in containing a swarm of out-of-control Spider-Bots. The queue area is themed as a W.E.B. workshop, showcasing various inventions and technology. The narrative unfolds as guests board their vehicles and are immersed in a battle against the Spider-Bots, which are multiplying and causing chaos throughout the facility.

## Attraction Experience
Riders board a vehicle designed to accommodate multiple guests. The trackless system allows for dynamic movement through the show building. Guests are equipped with 3D glasses and use their hands to virtually shoot webs at the Spider-Bots projected onto various screens. The ride incorporates physical effects such as wind and water to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 4 minutes. The Single Rider queue merges guests into available spaces within the ride vehicles.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a broad audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or those prone to motion sickness should consider the ride's effects before experiencing it. Guests with certain medical conditions should consult with a medical professional before riding.
