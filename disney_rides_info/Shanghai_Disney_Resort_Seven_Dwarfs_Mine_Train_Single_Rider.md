# Seven Dwarfs Mine Train Single Rider
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
The Seven Dwarfs Mine Train Single Rider is a family-friendly steel roller coaster located in Fantasyland at Shanghai Disney Resort. Manufactured by Vekoma, the attraction is themed after Disney's 1937 animated film *Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs*. It offers a single rider queue, allowing guests to experience the ride with potentially shorter wait times by filling available seats. The ride is a significant component of Fantasyland, providing a visually appealing and engaging experience for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the world of Snow White, entering a whimsical environment that recreates the dwarfs' diamond mine and their cottage. The queue area features interactive elements and character figures, enhancing the storytelling. The ride vehicles, designed to resemble mine carts, swing gently from side to side, adding to the sensation of traveling through the mine. The attraction incorporates animatronics of the Seven Dwarfs and Snow White, along with scenes depicting the film's key moments.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with a slow ascent up a lift hill, offering views of Fantasyland. The coaster then navigates a series of drops, turns, and gentle hills through the mine and surrounding forest. The ride incorporates audio-animatronics, special lighting effects, and projections to enhance the storytelling. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes. The single rider queue merges with the main queue at the loading station, where guests are assigned to fill available seats.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Seven Dwarfs Mine Train is designed for a broad audience, including families with young children. The height requirement is 97 cm (38 inches). Guests with certain medical conditions, such as heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consult with a medical professional before riding.
