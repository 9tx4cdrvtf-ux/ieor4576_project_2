# Country Bear Musical Jamboree
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
The Country Bear Musical Jamboree is a musical animatronic show located in Frontierland at the Magic Kingdom. It is a theater-style attraction featuring a cast of Audio-Animatronic bears performing a variety of country and western songs. The show was designed by Walt Disney Imagineering and opened with the park on October 1, 1971. It is a classic example of Disney's early animatronic technology and remains a popular attraction.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a rustic, barn-like theater. Guests are seated in a dimly lit auditorium, designed to evoke the atmosphere of a rural music hall. The show's narrative centers around the Country Bears, a group of bears who perform a series of musical numbers. The setting is designed to immerse guests in a lighthearted, frontier-themed environment.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter the theater and are seated. The show begins with the curtain rising to reveal the animatronic bears on stage. The bears perform a series of country and western songs, accompanied by musical instruments. The show features a variety of musical styles within the genre, and the animatronics are synchronized to the music. The show lasts approximately 15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Country Bear Musical Jamboree is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. The show is generally considered a gentle attraction. Guests sensitive to loud noises or darkness should be aware of these elements.
