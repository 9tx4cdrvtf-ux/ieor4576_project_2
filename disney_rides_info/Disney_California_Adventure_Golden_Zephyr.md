# Golden Zephyr
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
The Golden Zephyr is a classic "flying swings" ride located in the Paradise Gardens Park area of Disney California Adventure. Manufactured by Zamperla, the attraction provides guests with a circular, aerial experience, offering views of the surrounding park. It is themed to resemble a collection of antique, streamlined aircraft.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative is rooted in the golden age of aviation. Guests are invited to board one of twelve individual gondolas, each designed to resemble a vintage aircraft. The queue area and surrounding architecture evoke the atmosphere of a seaside amusement park, with Art Deco influences. The ride's setting is intended to transport guests to a bygone era of adventure and exploration.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the gondolas, which are suspended from a central rotating arm. Once all riders are secured, the arm begins to rotate, lifting the gondolas into the air and swinging them outward. The ride provides a gentle, circular motion, offering panoramic views of Paradise Gardens Park, Pixar Pier, and the surrounding areas. The ride cycle lasts approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Golden Zephyr is suitable for guests of all ages, provided they meet the height requirement of 40 inches (102 cm). Guests with a fear of heights or those prone to motion sickness may wish to reconsider riding. Pregnant women and individuals with certain medical conditions should consult with a physician before riding.
