# Meet Beloved Disney Pals at Mickey & Friends
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Meet Beloved Disney Pals at Mickey & Friends is a character meet-and-greet location situated in the World Celebration neighborhood of Epcot. This attraction provides guests with the opportunity to interact with various Disney characters, primarily Mickey Mouse and his friends. It is a permanent fixture, offering a classic Disney experience within the park.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a dedicated space for guests to meet and take photographs with Disney characters. The setting is designed to be bright and inviting, featuring colorful backdrops and thematic elements that reflect the characters' personalities. The environment is intended to create a welcoming atmosphere for guests of all ages to engage with the characters.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area, often with interactive elements to entertain them while waiting. The queue leads to a series of individual meet-and-greet locations where guests can meet and pose for photos with the featured characters. The characters present may vary, but typically include Mickey Mouse, Minnie Mouse, and other popular Disney characters. The experience allows for individual interactions and photo opportunities. The duration of the experience varies depending on wait times and individual interactions.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Meet Beloved Disney Pals at Mickey & Friends is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height requirements or specific health restrictions associated with this attraction. Guests with mobility issues are accommodated through accessible queue lines.
