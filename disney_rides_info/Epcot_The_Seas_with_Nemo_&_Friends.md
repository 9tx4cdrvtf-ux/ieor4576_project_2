# The Seas with Nemo & Friends
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
The Seas with Nemo & Friends is a slow-moving Omnimover dark ride located in the Seas Pavilion at Epcot. The attraction, manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering, opened in 2007 and replaced the previous attraction, "The Living Seas". It is themed after the Disney-Pixar film *Finding Nemo* and serves as a family-friendly experience, showcasing marine life and promoting environmental awareness.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the underwater world of *Finding Nemo*. The narrative follows Nemo and his friends as they embark on a journey through the ocean. The pavilion's architecture and interior design create an immersive environment, simulating the experience of being underwater. The attraction utilizes a combination of animated figures, projected imagery, and physical sets to bring the film's story to life.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board clam-shaped Omnimover vehicles that travel through various scenes from the film. The ride incorporates projected animation onto physical sets, blending the animated characters with the surrounding environment. The journey includes encounters with characters such as Crush, Marlin, Dory, and the menacing Bruce the shark. The ride culminates in a transition to the adjacent SeaBase, a large aquarium where guests can observe real marine life. The ride duration is approximately 5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Seas with Nemo & Friends is designed for a general audience, including families with young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can remain in their wheelchairs or ECVs. The attraction is generally considered safe for all guests, but those prone to motion sickness may experience discomfort.
