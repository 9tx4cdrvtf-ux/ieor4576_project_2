# Meet Disney Stars at Red Carpet Dreams
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
Meet Disney Stars at Red Carpet Dreams is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in Disney's Hollywood Studios. It allows guests to interact with a rotating selection of Disney characters, primarily focusing on those associated with classic Hollywood and contemporary Disney films. The attraction provides a photo opportunity and brief interaction with the characters in a themed environment.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a glamorous Hollywood premiere. Guests enter a roped-off area designed to resemble a red carpet event, complete with velvet ropes, spotlights, and a backdrop suggestive of a movie premiere. The setting aims to immerse guests in the atmosphere of a star-studded event, providing a backdrop for photographs with the featured characters.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a line that winds through the themed queue area. Upon reaching the front of the line, guests are directed to a designated area where they can meet and take photographs with the featured Disney character. The character selection varies, but typically includes characters such as Mickey Mouse, Minnie Mouse, and other popular Disney figures in their Hollywood-themed attire. The interaction typically lasts for a few minutes, allowing for photo opportunities and brief conversations. The entire experience, including queuing, typically lasts between 15-30 minutes, depending on crowd levels.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the attraction, though they may need to use a separate queue. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
