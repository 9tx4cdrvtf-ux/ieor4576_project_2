# Feathered Friends in Flight!
**Location:** Animal Kingdom

## Overview
Feathered Friends in Flight! is a live bird show attraction located in the Asia section of Disney's Animal Kingdom. The show features a variety of avian species performing natural behaviors and showcasing their unique adaptations. It is a non-ride attraction, offering guests an educational and entertaining experience focused on conservation and animal behavior.

## Narrative and Theming
The show is presented as a celebration of birds from around the world. There is no specific overarching narrative or fictional context beyond the presentation of the birds themselves. The setting is an open-air theater, designed to resemble a natural amphitheater, allowing for unobstructed views of the birds in flight and on the ground. The theming emphasizes the natural habitats and behaviors of the featured species.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are seated in the theater and observe a series of bird demonstrations. The show typically features a variety of birds, including macaws, parrots, hawks, and owls. The birds fly, interact with trainers, and demonstrate natural behaviors such as hunting, foraging, and communication. The show incorporates educational elements, with trainers providing information about the birds' species, habitats, and conservation efforts. The show's duration is approximately 25 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Feathered Friends in Flight! is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height requirements or specific health restrictions. However, guests sensitive to loud noises should be aware that the show may involve sudden sounds, such as bird calls and the flapping of wings. Guests with mobility issues should be aware that the seating is on tiered benches.
