# Duffy and Friends Play House
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Duffy and Friends Play House is a walk-through attraction located in the Gardens of Imagination area of Disneyland Hong Kong. It offers guests an interactive experience centered around the popular Duffy the Disney Bear and his friends. The attraction allows guests to meet and take photos with Duffy, ShellieMay, Gelatoni, StellaLou, and CookieAnn in a themed environment.

## Narrative and Theming
The Play House is designed as a charming, whimsical space reflecting the personalities and interests of Duffy and his friends. The environment is decorated with pastel colors, soft textures, and themed props representing each character's hobbies and passions. Guests are invited into Duffy's world, experiencing a space where friendship and creativity are celebrated. The setting emphasizes a sense of warmth and inclusivity, aligning with the characters' core values.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in an outdoor area before entering the Play House. Inside, they proceed through a series of themed rooms, each representing a different aspect of Duffy and Friends' lives. The primary activity is meeting and taking photographs with the characters. Cast members facilitate the interactions, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience. The duration of the experience varies depending on the number of guests and the time spent with each character, typically lasting between 15-30 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is primarily targeted towards families and young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the attraction, though some areas may be more easily navigated with assistance. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
