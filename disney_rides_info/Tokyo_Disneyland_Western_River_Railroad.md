# Western River Railroad
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
The Western River Railroad is a narrow-gauge railroad attraction located in Adventureland and Westernland at Tokyo Disneyland. It is a scenic train ride that offers guests a tour of the Rivers of America and surrounding areas. The attraction is a classic railroad experience, providing a leisurely journey through various themed environments.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers around a journey aboard a steam-powered locomotive. Guests are transported through a variety of themed landscapes, including a Native American village, a dinosaur-filled prehistoric area, and a frontier town. The theming incorporates detailed scenery, audio effects, and animatronic figures to enhance the immersive experience. The train itself is designed to resemble a classic steam train of the American West.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the train at a station located in Westernland. The train travels along a track that circles the Rivers of America. The journey includes passing through themed environments, with animatronic figures and scenic elements. The ride duration is approximately 15 minutes. The train utilizes a traditional railroad track system and is powered by a steam engine.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Western River Railroad is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may require assistance. The attraction is generally considered a gentle ride. Guests with a sensitivity to loud noises or enclosed spaces should take this into consideration.
