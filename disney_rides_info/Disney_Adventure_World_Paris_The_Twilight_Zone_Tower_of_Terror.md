# The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror is a drop tower dark ride located in the Hollywood Tower Hotel area of Disney Adventure World Paris. The attraction is themed after the television series *The Twilight Zone* and utilizes a unique vertical transportation system developed by Walt Disney Imagineering. It is a significant thrill ride within the park, offering a highly immersive and suspenseful experience.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the story of the Hollywood Tower Hotel, a once-glamorous hotel struck by lightning in 1939, resulting in the disappearance of several guests in an elevator. The attraction's queue winds through the hotel's lobby, library, and boiler room, showcasing artifacts and props that contribute to the eerie atmosphere. The narrative culminates in the elevator shaft, where guests experience the hotel's mysterious fate.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with guests boarding a "service elevator" that ascends through the hotel. The elevator then enters a series of show scenes before reaching the drop sequence. The ride system utilizes a combination of vertical lift and drop sequences, providing a sensation of freefall. The ride incorporates visual effects, audio cues, and lighting to enhance the suspense and create a multi-sensory experience. The entire ride duration is approximately 3 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror is designed for guests who meet the minimum height requirement of 102 cm (40 inches). Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding. The attraction may also be unsuitable for guests with a fear of heights or enclosed spaces.
