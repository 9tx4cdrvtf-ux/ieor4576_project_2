# Stitch Encounter
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Stitch Encounter is an interactive, live-show attraction located in Tomorrowland at Tokyo Disneyland. It features the Disney character Stitch from the animated film *Lilo & Stitch*. The attraction utilizes real-time animation technology to allow Stitch to interact with the audience.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are invited to a "Spaceport" where they can communicate with Stitch via a large screen. The premise involves Stitch being contacted from a holding cell, where he is being held for observation. The setting is designed to resemble a futuristic communication center, complete with screens, control panels, and a stylized, space-themed environment. The show's narrative is driven by Stitch's unpredictable behavior and his interactions with the audience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are seated in a theater-style setting and view Stitch on a large screen. A live host, acting as a spaceport operator, facilitates the interaction. Through the use of real-time animation, Stitch responds to the host and the audience, engaging in conversations, making jokes, and performing various actions. The show's content varies, with different scenarios and jokes presented in each performance. The show duration is approximately 15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Stitch Encounter is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction is primarily a visual and auditory experience, with no physical demands. Guests with photosensitivity should be aware of the use of flashing lights and bright visual effects.
