# RC Racer Single Rider
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
RC Racer Single Rider is a steel shuttle coaster located in the Toy Story Playland area of Disney Adventure World Paris. Manufactured by Intamin, the ride simulates a high-speed race between RC, Andy's toy car, and other vehicles. It is a significant attraction within Toy Story Playland, offering a thrilling experience for guests of various ages.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in a larger-than-life version of Andy's backyard, where they become honorary toys. The queue line winds through oversized toy blocks and building materials, setting the scene for a high-octane race. The ride itself is themed as a giant orange Hot Wheels track, with RC the star of the show. The narrative focuses on the excitement of a high-speed race, with guests experiencing the thrill of RC's acceleration and maneuvers.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with guests seated in a vehicle resembling a toy race car. The coaster launches forward and backward along a U-shaped track, reaching speeds of up to 80 km/h (50 mph). The ride features a large, vertical half-pipe element, providing moments of weightlessness. The entire experience lasts approximately 1 minute, including loading and unloading. The single rider queue allows guests to bypass the main queue and board the ride more quickly.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
RC Racer Single Rider is designed for guests who meet the minimum height requirement of 1.20 meters (47 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, including but not limited to heart problems, back problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consult with a medical professional before riding. The ride involves rapid acceleration and deceleration, and may not be suitable for all guests.
