# Autopia, presented by Avis
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Autopia, presented by Avis, is a self-steer, track-guided vehicle attraction located in Discoveryland at Disneyland Park Paris. It allows guests to drive miniature gasoline-powered cars along a winding track. The attraction is a classic Disneyland ride, offering a hands-on driving experience for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's theming is rooted in a futuristic vision of transportation, consistent with Discoveryland's overall aesthetic. Guests are invited to experience the thrill of driving their own personal vehicles on a simulated highway. The track winds through a landscape featuring futuristic architecture, lush greenery, and scenic elements, creating a sense of exploration and adventure.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue and board individual two-seater cars. Once seated, the cars are released onto a guided track. The cars are equipped with a steering wheel, which controls the direction of the vehicle, but the track prevents the cars from leaving the designated path. The ride duration is approximately 3-4 minutes, allowing guests to navigate the course and enjoy the surrounding environment.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Autopia is designed for a broad audience, including children and adults. Guests must be at least 81 cm (32 inches) tall to ride. Children under 1.32 m (52 inches) must be accompanied by a responsible person aged 16 or older. Guests with certain medical conditions, such as back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their health before riding.
