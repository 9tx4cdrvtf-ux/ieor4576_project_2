# Alien Swirling Saucers
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
Alien Swirling Saucers is a spinning amusement ride located in Toy Story Land at Disney's Hollywood Studios. The attraction is a trackless, whip-style ride system manufactured by Zamperla. It opened on June 30, 2018, as part of the land's expansion. The ride offers a family-friendly experience themed around the *Toy Story* franchise.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in a play set designed to resemble a toy playset from the *Toy Story* films. The premise involves being "rescued" by the Green Alien characters from the Pizza Planet restaurant. Riders are seated in rocket-shaped vehicles, which are pulled by a "flying saucer" controlled by the Aliens. The queue area features themed elements such as a claw machine and various toy boxes, further establishing the immersive environment.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of the rocket-shaped vehicles, each accommodating up to two riders. The saucers then begin to spin and glide along a trackless course. The vehicles are pulled by a larger "flying saucer" that moves along a circular path. During the ride, the vehicles also move independently, creating a whip-like motion. The ride experience is enhanced by a soundtrack of space-themed music and sound effects. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Alien Swirling Saucers is designed for a family audience. The minimum height requirement is 32 inches (81 cm). Guests with a history of motion sickness or other health conditions should consider the ride's spinning motion before boarding.
