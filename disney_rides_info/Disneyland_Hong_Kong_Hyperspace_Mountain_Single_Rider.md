# Hyperspace Mountain Single Rider
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Hyperspace Mountain Single Rider is a space-themed indoor roller coaster located in Tomorrowland at Disneyland Hong Kong. It is a re-theming of the original Space Mountain attraction, incorporating elements from the Star Wars franchise. The ride is manufactured by Vekoma and is a significant thrill ride within the park, offering a high-speed experience in a darkened environment. The Single Rider queue allows guests to experience the attraction with potentially shorter wait times.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in a Star Wars narrative, joining a Rebel Alliance mission to scout a Star Destroyer. The queue area features Star Wars-themed elements, including projections and sound effects that establish the setting. Riders are briefed on their mission before boarding their "X-wing" style ride vehicles. The ride takes place in a simulated outer space environment, with projections of starfields, TIE fighters, and other Star Wars elements.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with a slow ascent into the launch area. The coaster then accelerates rapidly through a series of drops, turns, and inversions in the dark. The ride incorporates special effects, including laser blasts and the sounds of space battles. The duration of the ride is approximately 2 minutes and 30 seconds. The Single Rider queue merges guests into available seats, optimizing ride capacity.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Hyperspace Mountain is designed for guests who enjoy thrill rides. The minimum height requirement is 102 cm (40 inches). Guests with a history of heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding. The ride utilizes sudden movements and darkness, which may be disorienting for some guests.
