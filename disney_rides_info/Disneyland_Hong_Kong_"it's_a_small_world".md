# "it's a small world"
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
"it's a small world" is a slow-moving boat ride attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Hong Kong. It is a version of the classic dark ride originally created for the 1964 New York World's Fair, and subsequently adapted for several Disney theme parks worldwide. The attraction is known for its iconic theme song and its depiction of children from around the world singing and dancing in a whimsical, stylized manner.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents a message of global unity and peace. Guests embark on a boat journey through various scenes representing different countries and regions. Each scene showcases Audio-Animatronic figures of children dressed in traditional costumes, singing the attraction's namesake song, "It's a Small World (After All)" in their respective languages. The environments are brightly colored and feature stylized representations of landmarks and cultural elements.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a boat that travels along a waterway. The boat passes through a series of interconnected rooms, each themed to a different part of the world. The ride utilizes a combination of Audio-Animatronics, lighting effects, and music to create an immersive experience. The journey lasts approximately 10 minutes. The boats are propelled by a water-based flume system.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
"it's a small world" is designed to be a family-friendly attraction suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitive epilepsy or other conditions sensitive to flashing lights should be aware of the visual effects within the attraction.
