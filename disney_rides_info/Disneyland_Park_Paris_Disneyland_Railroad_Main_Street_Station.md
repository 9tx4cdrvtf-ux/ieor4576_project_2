# Disneyland Railroad Main Street Station
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
The Disneyland Railroad Main Street Station is a railway station located within Disneyland Park Paris, serving as one of the four stops on the Disneyland Railroad. It is a transportation attraction, providing guests with a scenic tour of the park. The station is themed to the Victorian era, reflecting the architecture of Main Street, U.S.A. It was constructed by the WED Enterprises, the original design and development company for Disneyland.

## Narrative and Theming
The station's theming is consistent with the Main Street, U.S.A. area of the park, evoking the atmosphere of a late 19th-century American town. The station's architecture features ornate detailing, period signage, and a general aesthetic designed to transport guests back in time. The station serves as a gateway to the park, offering guests a glimpse into the past as they prepare to embark on their journey around Disneyland Park Paris.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the Disneyland Railroad at the Main Street Station. The train departs from the station, traveling counter-clockwise around the park. The journey includes views of Fantasyland, Adventureland, Frontierland, and Discoveryland, as well as glimpses of backstage areas. The ride duration is approximately 20 minutes for a complete circuit. The train utilizes a steam-powered locomotive, providing a classic railway experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Disneyland Railroad is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find it challenging to board the train. Guests with certain health conditions should consult with a medical professional before riding.
