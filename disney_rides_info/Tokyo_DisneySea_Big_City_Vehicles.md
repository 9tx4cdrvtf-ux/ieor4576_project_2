# Big City Vehicles
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Big City Vehicles is a self-guided, elevated railway attraction located in the American Waterfront area of Tokyo DisneySea. It provides guests with a leisurely tour of the New York Harbor district, offering views of the surrounding area. The vehicles are designed to resemble vintage automobiles and are a key element in establishing the themed environment of the American Waterfront.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the early 20th-century atmosphere of New York City. The vehicles are presented as classic automobiles, purportedly used by the city's elite. The route passes through meticulously themed environments, including the bustling streets and waterfront of the fictional city, complete with period-appropriate architecture, storefronts, and sound effects. The overall narrative emphasizes the prosperity and technological advancements of the era.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the open-air vehicles at a designated station. The vehicles travel along an elevated track, providing a panoramic view of the American Waterfront. The ride duration is approximately 5 minutes. The route includes a loop around the area, passing by various themed buildings and attractions. The vehicles are electrically powered, ensuring a quiet and smooth ride.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Big City Vehicles is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility impairments can access the attraction, though assistance may be required. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
