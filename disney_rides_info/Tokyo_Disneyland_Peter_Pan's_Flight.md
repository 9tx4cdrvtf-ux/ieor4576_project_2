# Peter Pan's Flight
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Peter Pan's Flight is a suspended dark ride attraction located in Fantasyland at Tokyo Disneyland. Based on Disney's 1953 animated film *Peter Pan*, the ride allows guests to experience a flight over London and Neverland. The attraction is a classic dark ride, utilizing a track-based system with suspended ride vehicles. It is a popular and enduring attraction, consistently drawing long wait times.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests board pirate ship ride vehicles and embark on a journey inspired by the film. The queue area is themed to the Darling children's nursery, setting the scene for the adventure. The ride immerses guests in the story, beginning with a flight over London at night, complete with miniature buildings and twinkling lights. The journey continues to Neverland, where riders encounter Captain Hook, Tinker Bell, and other characters from the film. The environments are designed to evoke the whimsical and adventurous spirit of the story.

## Attraction Experience
The ride vehicles are suspended from an overhead track, giving the illusion of flight. The journey begins with the ship ascending into the London scene. The ride then progresses through various scenes, including a pirate ship battle and a confrontation with Captain Hook. Audio-animatronics and special effects, such as black light and projections, enhance the storytelling. The ride duration is approximately 2 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Peter Pan's Flight is generally suitable for all ages, though young children may find some scenes slightly intense. There are no height restrictions. Guests with a history of heart conditions, back problems, or other health concerns should consult with a medical professional before riding.
