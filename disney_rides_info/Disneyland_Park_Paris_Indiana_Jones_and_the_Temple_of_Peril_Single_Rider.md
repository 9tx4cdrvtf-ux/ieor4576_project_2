# Indiana Jones and the Temple of Peril Single Rider
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Indiana Jones and the Temple of Peril Single Rider is a steel roller coaster located in Adventureland at Disneyland Park Paris. The attraction, manufactured by Intamin, is themed after the Indiana Jones film franchise. It offers a single rider queue, allowing guests traveling alone to bypass a portion of the regular queue and experience the ride more quickly. The attraction is notable for its unique inverted coaster design, where riders' feet dangle freely beneath the track.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride's narrative places guests within an archaeological dig site, where they are exploring the ruins of a lost temple. The story suggests that the temple is cursed, and the mine train, which guests board, is subject to the temple's perils. The queue line is designed to immerse guests in this environment, featuring detailed set pieces, props, and atmospheric lighting that evoke the feeling of an ancient, forgotten civilization.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a mine train-style vehicle that ascends a lift hill. The coaster then traverses a series of inversions, including a loop, and high-speed turns. The ride incorporates various sensory effects, including sudden drops and changes in direction. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds. The single rider queue merges with the main queue at the loading platform.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for guests who meet the minimum height requirement of 1.40 meters (4 ft 7 in). Guests with a history of heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding. Guests should also be in good health and physical condition to withstand the ride's intensity.
