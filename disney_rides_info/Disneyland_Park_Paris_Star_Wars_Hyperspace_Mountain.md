# Star Wars Hyperspace Mountain
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Star Wars Hyperspace Mountain is a steel roller coaster located in Discoveryland at Disneyland Park Paris. It is a re-theming of the original Space Mountain: Mission 2 attraction, incorporating the Star Wars franchise. The ride was manufactured by Vekoma and is known for its indoor setting and inversions. It represents a significant investment in the park's theming and provides a unique immersive experience for guests.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction places guests within a Star Wars narrative, specifically a Rebel Alliance mission. Riders are recruited to join a reconnaissance mission to scout a Star Destroyer. The queue line and ride environment are themed to resemble a Star Wars spaceport, complete with starship models, control panels, and projections. The ride experience is enhanced by a Star Wars soundtrack and projections of starfighters and other spacecraft engaged in battle.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board rocket-shaped ride vehicles and are launched into the darkness. The coaster features inversions, including a corkscrew and a loop. The ride incorporates special effects such as projections of stars, TIE fighters, and the Star Destroyer, creating the illusion of space travel. The ride duration is approximately 2 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Star Wars Hyperspace Mountain is designed for a broad audience, but it has a minimum height requirement of 1.2 meters (47 inches). Guests with a history of heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding. The ride utilizes intense visual effects and rapid movements, which may not be suitable for all guests.
