# Fantasy Gardens
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Fantasy Gardens is a themed area within Fantasyland at Disneyland Hong Kong. It is a walk-through attraction offering a series of whimsical, interactive photo opportunities and character meet-and-greet locations. The area is designed to immerse guests in various Disney fairytale settings, providing a backdrop for interactions with Disney characters.

## Narrative and Theming
Fantasy Gardens presents a collection of themed gardens, each representing a different Disney animated film. These gardens are meticulously landscaped and decorated to reflect the visual styles and narratives of films such as *Alice in Wonderland*, *Beauty and the Beast*, and *Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs*. The theming emphasizes vibrant colors, fantastical architecture, and interactive elements to create an immersive environment.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are free to explore the various themed gardens at their own pace. The primary activity involves taking photographs with the themed backdrops and, when available, interacting with Disney characters who regularly appear for meet-and-greet sessions. The experience is primarily passive, allowing guests to wander through the gardens and enjoy the visual spectacle. There is no set duration, as guests can spend as much or as little time as they wish within the area.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Fantasy Gardens is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. However, guests with mobility issues may find certain areas challenging due to uneven surfaces or narrow pathways.
