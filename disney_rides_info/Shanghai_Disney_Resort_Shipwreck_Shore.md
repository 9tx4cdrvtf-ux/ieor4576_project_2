# Shipwreck Shore
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Shipwreck Shore is a themed interactive play area located within Treasure Cove at Shanghai Disney Resort. It is designed as a pirate-themed adventure playground, offering a variety of climbing structures, water features, and interactive elements for children and families. The attraction is not a ride, but rather a free-roaming experience focused on imaginative play and exploration.

## Narrative and Theming
The area is themed as the remnants of a pirate settlement, ravaged by a storm. Guests explore the wreckage of ships, climb through nets, and discover hidden treasures. The theming incorporates elements of the *Pirates of the Caribbean* franchise, with weathered wood, tattered sails, and pirate-themed props scattered throughout the environment. The narrative encourages guests to imagine themselves as pirates, exploring the aftermath of a shipwreck and searching for lost loot.

## Attraction Experience
Guests can navigate a multi-level play structure featuring rope bridges, slides, and climbing walls. Water cannons and splash zones provide opportunities for interactive water play. The area also includes interactive elements such as treasure chests and hidden passages. The experience is self-guided, allowing guests to explore at their own pace. The duration of a visit is variable, depending on the guest's engagement with the various play elements.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Shipwreck Shore is primarily designed for children and families. There are no height restrictions, but adult supervision is recommended for younger children. Guests with mobility issues may find certain areas challenging to access. There are no specific health warnings, but guests should be aware of the potential for wet surfaces and the physical demands of climbing and playing.
