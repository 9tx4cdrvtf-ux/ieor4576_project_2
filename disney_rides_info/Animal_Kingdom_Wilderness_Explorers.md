# Wilderness Explorers
**Location:** Animal Kingdom

## Overview
Wilderness Explorers is an interactive, self-guided experience located throughout Disney's Animal Kingdom. It is not a traditional ride but rather a series of challenges and activities designed to educate guests about nature, conservation, and animal habitats. The experience allows guests to earn "badges" by completing various tasks and learning about the park's diverse environments.

## Narrative and Theming
The experience is themed around the fictional Wilderness Explorers organization, inspired by the animated film *Up*. Guests are recruited as junior Wilderness Explorers and are tasked with earning badges by completing activities at various stations throughout the park. Each badge represents a different area of expertise, such as animal tracking, bird identification, or conservation efforts. The overall setting immerses guests in the spirit of exploration and discovery, encouraging them to learn about the natural world.

## Attraction Experience
Guests receive a Wilderness Explorers handbook and map upon entry. They then proceed through the park, visiting different stations. At each station, a Wilderness Explorer "Trainee" guides guests through an activity or lesson. Activities vary, including identifying animal calls, learning about animal diets, and participating in conservation-related games. Upon completion of each activity, guests receive a badge. The experience can take several hours to complete, depending on the guest's pace and interest.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Wilderness Explorers is suitable for guests of all ages, although younger children may require assistance from adults. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with the experience. The experience involves walking and standing for extended periods.
