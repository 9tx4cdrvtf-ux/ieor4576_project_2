# Haunted Mansion
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
The Haunted Mansion is a dark ride attraction located in Liberty Square at Magic Kingdom. It is a classic Disney attraction, known for its innovative use of special effects, including the "Pepper's Ghost" illusion. The ride, designed by Walt Disney Imagineering, is a key component of the park's immersive theming and is a popular attraction for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents itself as a stately, yet decaying, mansion inhabited by 999 happy haunts. Guests are invited to tour the mansion, encountering various spectral residents and experiencing a series of supernatural occurrences. The narrative is conveyed through a combination of visual storytelling, audio narration, and the environment's overall atmosphere, which includes eerie lighting, unsettling sound effects, and detailed set design. The mansion's backstory suggests a history of unexplained events and ghostly inhabitants.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board "Doom Buggies," individual ride vehicles that navigate through the mansion. The ride utilizes a combination of physical sets, projected images, and special effects to create illusions. Notable scenes include the stretching room, the ballroom with dancing ghosts, and the graveyard with singing busts. The ride duration is approximately 9 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Haunted Mansion is generally suitable for all ages, though some scenes may be frightening for young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with heart conditions, back problems, or those who are sensitive to strobe lights should consider the ride's potential impact before experiencing it.
