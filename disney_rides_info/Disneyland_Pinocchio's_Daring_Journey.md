# Pinocchio's Daring Journey
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Pinocchio's Daring Journey is a dark ride located in Fantasyland at Disneyland. The attraction, manufactured by Arrow Development, takes guests through key scenes from Disney's 1940 animated film, *Pinocchio*. It is a classic Fantasyland attraction, offering a whimsical and nostalgic experience for park visitors.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride immerses guests in the story of Pinocchio, a wooden puppet brought to life by the Blue Fairy. The attraction's queue and ride vehicles are themed to resemble a quaint Italian village, with elements inspired by the film's setting. Guests witness Pinocchio's adventures, including his encounters with Honest John and Gideon, his time at Pleasure Island, and his eventual rescue by Geppetto. The ride's atmosphere is enhanced by detailed sets, vibrant colors, and iconic character figures.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board wooden ride vehicles that travel along a track through various scenes. The ride utilizes Audio-Animatronics figures, black light effects, and projected imagery to bring the story to life. The journey includes scenes such as Pinocchio's encounter with Stromboli, his time in the Pleasure Island, and his escape from Monstro the whale. The ride lasts approximately 2 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Pinocchio's Daring Journey is suitable for all ages, though younger children may find some scenes frightening. There are no height restrictions. Guests with a sensitivity to dark environments or enclosed spaces should consider their comfort level before riding.
