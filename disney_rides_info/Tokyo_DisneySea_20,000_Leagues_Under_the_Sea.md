# 20,000 Leagues Under the Sea
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
20,000 Leagues Under the Sea is a submarine-themed dark ride located in the Mysterious Island port of Tokyo DisneySea. The attraction, manufactured by Oceaneering International, Inc., simulates a voyage through the ocean depths, based on the Jules Verne novel of the same name. It is a significant attraction within the park, known for its immersive theming and unique ride system.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are cast as explorers aboard the *Nautilus*, a research submarine designed by Captain Nemo. The queue area and ride environment are themed to resemble the interior of the *Nautilus* and the surrounding underwater world. The attraction's narrative focuses on the discovery of various marine life and the exploration of the ocean floor, including encounters with giant squid and other fantastical creatures.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual, eight-person submarines. The ride utilizes a unique trackless system, allowing the submarines to move independently through the show scenes. The journey features various animatronic creatures, projected imagery, and lighting effects to create the illusion of being underwater. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for most guests. There is a height requirement of 90 cm (35 inches). Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised to consider their health before riding.
