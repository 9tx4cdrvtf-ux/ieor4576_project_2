# Animation Academy
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Animation Academy is a walk-through attraction located in the Hollywood Land area of Disney California Adventure. It offers guests the opportunity to learn how to draw Disney characters under the guidance of a Disney animator. The attraction provides a hands-on experience, allowing guests to create their own artwork to take home.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a traditional animation studio, reminiscent of the early days of Disney animation. Guests enter a classroom setting, complete with easels, drawing paper, and examples of Disney character artwork. The environment is designed to evoke a sense of creativity and artistic inspiration, mirroring the atmosphere of a working animation studio.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are seated at individual drawing stations. A Disney animator provides step-by-step instructions on how to draw a featured Disney character. The animator demonstrates each step on a large screen, guiding guests through the process of sketching, outlining, and adding details. The duration of the drawing session is approximately 20-25 minutes. Guests are provided with paper and pencils and are encouraged to keep their artwork. The featured character changes periodically.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Animation Academy is suitable for guests of all ages and skill levels. There are no height requirements or specific health restrictions. The attraction is accessible to guests with disabilities.
