# Mickey's House and Meet Mickey Mouse
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Mickey's House and Meet Mickey Mouse is a walk-through attraction located in Mickey's Toontown at Disneyland. It allows guests to explore Mickey Mouse's house and then meet Mickey Mouse for a photo opportunity and autograph. The attraction is a permanent fixture of the land, designed by Walt Disney Imagineering, and provides a character meet-and-greet experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as Mickey Mouse's residence in the whimsical town of Toontown. Guests enter through a cartoon-style facade and proceed through various rooms of the house, including the living room, kitchen, and backyard. Each area is decorated with props and gags that reflect Mickey's personality and lifestyle, as depicted in Disney animated shorts. The environment is designed to immerse guests in the world of Mickey Mouse.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue through a themed outdoor area before entering Mickey's house. Inside, they walk through the house at their own pace, observing the details of Mickey's home. The experience culminates in a meet-and-greet with Mickey Mouse himself in his backyard. Guests can pose for photos and obtain autographs. The entire experience typically lasts between 10 to 20 minutes, depending on wait times.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can navigate the attraction, though some areas may be crowded. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
