# Canada Far and Wide in Circle-Vision 360
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Canada Far and Wide in Circle-Vision 360 is a 12-minute, 360-degree film attraction located in the Canada Pavilion at Epcot's World Showcase. It replaced the original "O Canada!" film in 2020, utilizing the same theater and Circle-Vision 360 technology. The attraction showcases various Canadian landscapes and cultural elements.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents a cinematic journey across Canada, highlighting its diverse geography, including the Rocky Mountains, Niagara Falls, and the Canadian Arctic. The film aims to provide a comprehensive overview of the country's natural beauty and cultural heritage, emphasizing its vastness and the variety of experiences it offers. There is no specific narrative beyond the visual tour.

## Attraction Experience
Guests stand within a circular theater and view the film projected on nine screens that completely surround them. The film utilizes Circle-Vision 360 technology, providing a panoramic view of the featured locations. The experience incorporates various sensory effects, including wind and the sensation of movement, to enhance the immersive quality. The attraction's duration is approximately 12 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with a sensitivity to motion or enclosed spaces should consider their tolerance before experiencing the attraction.
