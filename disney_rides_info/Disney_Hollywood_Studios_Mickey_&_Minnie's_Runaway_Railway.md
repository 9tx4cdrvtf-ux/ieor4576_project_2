# Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway is a trackless dark ride located in the Hollywood Studios theme park at Walt Disney World. It replaced The Great Movie Ride in 2020. The attraction utilizes a trackless ride system and incorporates 2.5D technology to immerse guests in a cartoon world. It is significant as the first ride-through attraction themed to Mickey Mouse and Minnie Mouse.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride's narrative centers around a premiere of a new Mickey Mouse cartoon short, "Perfect Picnic." Guests enter the Chinese Theatre, where they are invited to watch the cartoon. The story then transitions, and guests are transported into the cartoon world, encountering Mickey, Minnie, and other characters. The attraction's theming is based on the art style of the modern Mickey Mouse shorts, with vibrant colors and a whimsical aesthetic.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a ride vehicle that moves through various scenes, experiencing a series of events from the cartoon. The trackless ride system allows for fluid movements and unexpected turns. The ride incorporates projections, physical sets, and special effects, including simulated weather and motion, to create a dynamic and immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway is designed for a general audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or those prone to motion sickness should be aware of the ride's effects.
