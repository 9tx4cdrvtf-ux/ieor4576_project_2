# Les Mystères du Nautilus
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Les Mystères du Nautilus is a walk-through attraction located in the Discoveryland area of Disneyland Park Paris. It is themed after the submarine *Nautilus* from Jules Verne's novel *Twenty Thousand Leagues Under the Sea*. The attraction provides guests with an immersive experience, allowing them to explore the interior of Captain Nemo's iconic submarine.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the world of Captain Nemo and the *Nautilus*. The theming recreates the submarine's interior, featuring detailed sets representing various compartments such as the Grand Salon, the Captain's Cabin, and the Engine Room. The narrative focuses on the submarine's exploration of the ocean depths, hinting at encounters with marine life and the dangers of the deep sea.

## Attraction Experience
Guests embark on a self-guided tour through the *Nautilus*. The experience includes viewing various rooms and exhibits, each meticulously themed with props and special effects. The Grand Salon features a large organ and a collection of artifacts. The Engine Room showcases the submarine's inner workings. The attraction culminates in a simulated attack sequence, complete with lighting effects, sound effects, and the illusion of water flooding the interior. The entire experience lasts approximately 10-15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Les Mystères du Nautilus is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. However, the attraction features dark environments, loud noises, and special effects that may be unsettling for some individuals. Guests with claustrophobia or sensitivity to special effects should consider their comfort level before entering.
