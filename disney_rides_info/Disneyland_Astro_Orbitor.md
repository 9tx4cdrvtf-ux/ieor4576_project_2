# Astro Orbitor
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
The Astro Orbitor is a spinner ride located in Tomorrowland at Disneyland. It is a variation of the "Star Jets" ride system, manufactured by Zamperla. The attraction allows guests to board individual rockets that rotate around a central tower, offering aerial views of Tomorrowland. It is a popular attraction for its visual appeal and its location at the entrance to Tomorrowland.

## Narrative and Theming
The Astro Orbitor is themed to a retro-futuristic vision of space travel. Guests are presented with a queue area designed to resemble a spaceport. The ride vehicles are stylized rockets, and the central tower is adorned with planetary models and celestial imagery. The overall aesthetic is intended to evoke a sense of exploration and adventure in the cosmos, consistent with Tomorrowland's theme of futuristic innovation.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of twelve rocket-shaped vehicles, each accommodating up to two riders. The rockets ascend approximately 60 feet (18 meters) into the air as they rotate around the central tower. Riders can control the height of their rocket using a joystick located inside the vehicle. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Astro Orbitor is generally suitable for all ages, though young children may require adult supervision. There is no height requirement. Guests with a fear of heights or those prone to motion sickness should consider their sensitivity before riding. Guests with certain medical conditions should consult with a medical professional before riding.
