# Soarin' Over California
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Soarin' Over California is a simulated aerial flight attraction located in the Grizzly Peak area of Disney California Adventure. It is a motion-based simulator ride, manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering, and is a significant attraction within the park, known for its immersive experience and popularity. The ride opened in 2001 and was later updated to Soarin' Around the World in 2016, before reverting to its original form in 2023.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents itself as a hang gliding simulation over various locations in California. Guests are presented with the pre-show in a hangar setting, themed as a California aviation museum. The narrative focuses on the experience of flight and the beauty of the Californian landscape.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are seated in a suspended ride system, with rows of seats arranged in tiers. The seats are lifted towards a massive IMAX projection screen. The ride simulates the sensation of flight through motion, wind effects, and scents that correspond to the visuals. The experience lasts approximately five minutes, during which riders "soar" over iconic Californian landmarks such as the Golden Gate Bridge, Yosemite National Park, and the Pacific coastline.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Soarin' Over California is generally suitable for all ages, though children must meet the minimum height requirement of 40 inches (102 cm) to ride. Guests with a fear of heights or motion sickness may experience discomfort. Pregnant women and guests with certain health conditions are advised to consult with a physician before riding.
