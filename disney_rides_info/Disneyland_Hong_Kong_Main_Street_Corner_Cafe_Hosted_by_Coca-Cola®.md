# Main Street Corner Cafe Hosted by Coca-Cola®
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Main Street Corner Cafe Hosted by Coca-Cola® is a table-service restaurant located on Main Street, U.S.A., in Disneyland Hong Kong. It is themed to resemble a turn-of-the-century cafe and is sponsored by The Coca-Cola Company. The restaurant offers a variety of American-style cuisine and beverages, providing guests with a themed dining experience within the park.

## Narrative and Theming
The cafe's theming evokes the atmosphere of a classic American town square eatery. The interior design incorporates elements of the early 20th century, including ornate woodwork, vintage advertisements, and period-appropriate furnishings. The setting is designed to transport guests back in time, creating a nostalgic ambiance that complements the overall Main Street, U.S.A. aesthetic. The Coca-Cola branding is subtly integrated throughout the decor, reflecting the sponsor's presence.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are seated at tables and provided with menus. The restaurant offers a selection of American comfort food, including burgers, sandwiches, salads, and desserts. Coca-Cola products are prominently featured on the beverage menu. Service is provided by cast members who interact with guests, enhancing the immersive experience. The duration of a dining experience varies depending on the guest's order and the pace of service.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Main Street Corner Cafe Hosted by Coca-Cola® is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with specific dietary requirements can consult the menu for options or inquire with cast members. No specific health warnings or restrictions are listed, but guests with allergies should inform the staff.
