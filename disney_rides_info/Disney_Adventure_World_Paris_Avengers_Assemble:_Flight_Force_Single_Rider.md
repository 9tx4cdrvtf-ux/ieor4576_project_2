# Avengers Assemble: Flight Force Single Rider
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
Avengers Assemble: Flight Force Single Rider is a steel roller coaster located in the Avengers Campus area of Disney Adventure World Paris. It is a launched coaster manufactured by Vekoma. The attraction is a significant thrill ride within the park, offering high speeds and intense maneuvers themed around the Marvel Cinematic Universe. The Single Rider queue allows guests to experience the ride with potentially shorter wait times.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are recruited as potential recruits to assist the Avengers in a mission to intercept a threat from space. The queue line immerses guests in the S.H.I.E.L.D. Research Facility, featuring interactive elements and displays related to the Avengers' technology and missions. The ride vehicles are designed to resemble Stark Industries' advanced aircraft, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with a launch sequence propelling the train to high speeds. The coaster features multiple inversions, including a loop and a corkscrew, along with high-speed turns and drops. The ride incorporates special effects, such as onboard audio and lighting, to enhance the sensation of flight and combat. The duration of the ride is approximately one minute.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for thrill-seekers. The minimum height requirement is 1.20 meters (47 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, including but not limited to heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised against riding. Guests should also be aware of the ride's intense nature, including high speeds, inversions, and sudden movements.
