# Disneyland Railroad
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
The Disneyland Railroad is a 3-foot (914 mm) narrow-gauge heritage railroad attraction located in Disneyland Park. It is a complete circle tour of the park, offering guests a leisurely journey through several themed environments. The railroad is a significant opening day attraction, designed by Walt Disney himself, and is a key element in the park's overall design and guest experience. The locomotives are replicas of historic steam engines, manufactured by WED Enterprises (now Walt Disney Imagineering).

## Narrative and Theming
The Disneyland Railroad provides guests with a scenic tour of Disneyland, offering glimpses into various themed lands. The journey is presented as a trip aboard a vintage steam train, evoking the romance of rail travel. The route passes through Frontierland, Fantasyland, and Tomorrowland, with the option to disembark at Main Street, U.S.A., New Orleans Square, Tomorrowland, and Mickey's Toontown stations. The trains also traverse the Grand Canyon Diorama and the Primeval World diorama, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of five authentic-looking steam trains, each named after a significant figure in railroad history. The trains travel a 1.2-mile (1.9 km) track, completing a circuit of the park in approximately 18 minutes. The ride features scenic views, audio narration, and the sights and sounds of a working steam train. The locomotives are powered by diesel engines, with steam effects added for authenticity.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Disneyland Railroad is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may require assistance boarding and disembarking the trains. Guests with sensitivity to loud noises or enclosed spaces should consider their comfort levels before riding.
