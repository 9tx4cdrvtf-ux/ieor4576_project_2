# Alice's Tea Party
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Alice's Tea Party is a spinning tea cup ride located in Fantasyland at Tokyo Disneyland. It is a classic amusement park ride manufactured by Zamperla, based on the Mad Tea Party attraction found in other Disney parks. The attraction allows guests to spin individual tea cups on a rotating platform, offering a whimsical and interactive experience. It is a popular family-friendly attraction, contributing to the immersive atmosphere of Fantasyland.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed after the iconic tea party scene from Disney's animated film *Alice in Wonderland*. Guests are immersed in a whimsical environment featuring oversized teacups, colorful decorations, and a central teapot structure. The surrounding area is designed to evoke the chaotic and nonsensical atmosphere of the Mad Hatter's tea party, with vibrant colors and playful details.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual teacups, each capable of seating several riders. The platform begins to rotate, and riders can control the spinning of their individual teacups via a wheel located in the center. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds. The attraction utilizes simple mechanical technology to provide the spinning motion, with no special effects beyond the visual theming.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Alice's Tea Party is designed for a general audience, including families with young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with a sensitivity to motion sickness or those who are uncomfortable with spinning are advised to consider their condition before riding.
