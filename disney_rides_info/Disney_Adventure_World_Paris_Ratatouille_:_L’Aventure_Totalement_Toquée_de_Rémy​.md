# Ratatouille : L’Aventure Totalement Toquée de Rémy​
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
Ratatouille : L’Aventure Totalement Toquée de Rémy​ (Ratatouille: The Adventure) is a trackless dark ride located in the Walt Disney Studios Park at Disneyland Paris. The attraction, which opened in 2014, is themed after the Disney-Pixar film *Ratatouille*. It was manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering and is a significant addition to the park, offering a unique immersive experience.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are "shrunk" to the size of a rat and embark on a culinary adventure through the bustling kitchen and dining room of Gusteau's restaurant in Paris. The storyline follows Remy, the film's protagonist, as he attempts to create a delicious meal while avoiding the restaurant's staff, particularly Chef Skinner. The attraction's theming incorporates oversized props, detailed sets, and projections to create the illusion of being small and navigating the environment from a rat's perspective.

## Attraction Experience
The ride utilizes trackless ride vehicles that move independently, allowing for dynamic movement and interaction with the environment. Guests wear 3D glasses to enhance the visual effects. The ride incorporates a combination of physical sets, projected animation, and sensory effects such as heat, water, and smells to immerse riders in the story. The ride duration is approximately 5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for a wide audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or motion sickness may experience discomfort. Specific health warnings are available at the attraction's entrance.
