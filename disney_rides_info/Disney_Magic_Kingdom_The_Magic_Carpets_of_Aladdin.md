# The Magic Carpets of Aladdin
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
The Magic Carpets of Aladdin is a spinner ride located in Adventureland at Disney's Magic Kingdom. It is a family-friendly attraction themed after the 1992 Disney animated film *Aladdin*. The ride is manufactured by Zamperla and opened in May 2001. It provides guests with a gentle, aerial experience, offering views of Adventureland and interactive elements.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the fictional setting of Agrabah. The queue area is themed as a bustling marketplace, featuring details inspired by the film. Guests board individual carpet vehicles, each accommodating four riders. The central hub of the ride features a large genie statue, and the surrounding area is decorated with themed props and architectural elements reminiscent of the movie.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of sixteen carpet vehicles. Each carpet can be controlled by the riders; the front seat controls the carpet's height, while the back seat controls the carpet's tilt. The ride cycle lasts approximately 90 seconds. During the ride, guests may encounter interactive elements, such as spitting camels that spray water at riders.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Magic Carpets of Aladdin is designed for a general audience. There is a height requirement of 36 inches (91 cm) to ride. Guests with a fear of heights or those prone to motion sickness should consider their comfort level before riding. Expectant mothers and guests with certain medical conditions should consult with a cast member before boarding.
