# Pixar Pal-A-Round – Non-Swinging
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
The Pixar Pal-A-Round – Non-Swinging is a Ferris wheel attraction located in Pixar Pier at Disney California Adventure. It is a visually prominent structure, offering panoramic views of the park and surrounding areas. The attraction is manufactured by Zamperla and features 24 gondolas, 12 of which are stationary and do not swing. This version provides a more stable and predictable ride experience compared to the swinging gondolas.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed to Pixar films, with each gondola painted with a different Pixar character or film logo. The overall design incorporates bright colors and playful shapes, consistent with the Pixar Pier aesthetic. The structure itself is designed to resemble a classic Ferris wheel, but with a modern, stylized appearance. The ride's setting is within the broader narrative of Pixar Pier, a land celebrating the stories and characters of Pixar Animation Studios.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the gondolas, which are enclosed and climate-controlled. The wheel rotates slowly, providing riders with views of the park. The ride cycle lasts approximately 8-10 minutes. The gondolas are stationary, offering a smooth and consistent ride experience. The attraction utilizes standard Ferris wheel technology, with a central hub and spokes supporting the gondolas.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Pixar Pal-A-Round – Non-Swinging is suitable for guests of all ages. There is no height requirement. Guests with a fear of heights or those seeking a less intense experience may prefer this version over the swinging gondolas. Guests with certain medical conditions, such as heart problems or back issues, should consult with a physician before riding.
