# Kidcot Fun Stops
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Kidcot Fun Stops is a series of interactive stations located throughout the World Showcase at Epcot. It is a complimentary, self-guided activity designed for children and families, offering educational and cultural experiences. The attraction is not manufactured by a specific company but is a collaborative effort between Disney Imagineering and the various cultural representatives staffing the World Showcase pavilions. It provides a unique way for guests to engage with the diverse cultures represented in the park.

## Narrative and Theming
Kidcot Fun Stops do not have a singular narrative or overarching theme. Instead, each stop within a World Showcase pavilion is themed to the specific country it represents. The stations are designed to introduce children to the culture, traditions, and art of each nation. The fictional context is provided through the cultural representatives who staff each station, offering insights and activities related to their home country.

## Attraction Experience
Guests receive a complimentary "Ziploc" style card at the first Kidcot Fun Stop. At each subsequent stop, children can interact with the cultural representatives, who provide information, answer questions, and often offer a small craft activity or a stamp on their card. Activities vary by location but may include coloring, creating paper crafts, or learning basic phrases in the local language. The duration of each stop is variable, depending on the guest's engagement.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Kidcot Fun Stops are primarily targeted at children aged 3-12, but are open to guests of all ages. There are no height requirements or health restrictions associated with participating in the activity. The experience is designed to be accessible to all guests, regardless of physical ability.
