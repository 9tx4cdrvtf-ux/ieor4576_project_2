# RC Racer
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
RC Racer is a steel shuttle coaster located in Toy Story Land at Disneyland Hong Kong. Manufactured by Intamin, the ride simulates the experience of being on a giant toy car racing along a U-shaped track. It is a significant attraction within Toy Story Land, offering a thrilling experience for guests of various ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride is themed around the "RC Racer" toy car from the *Toy Story* film series. Guests are immersed in a giant toy box environment, scaled to the size of Andy's backyard. The queue area features oversized toy blocks and other familiar elements from the films, enhancing the sense of being shrunk down to toy size. The ride itself is presented as a high-speed race, with RC Racer speeding back and forth along the track.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a vehicle designed to resemble RC Racer. The ride begins with a launch up one side of the U-shaped track, reaching a height of approximately 24.4 meters (80 feet). The vehicle then accelerates rapidly, reaching speeds of up to 80 kilometers per hour (50 miles per hour) before traversing the track and repeating the process in the opposite direction. The ride duration is approximately 60 seconds, including loading and unloading.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
RC Racer is generally suitable for guests who meet the minimum height requirement of 120 cm (47 inches). Guests with a history of heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding. The ride involves significant acceleration and deceleration forces.
