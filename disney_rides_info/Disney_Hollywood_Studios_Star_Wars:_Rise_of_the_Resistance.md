# Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance is a trackless dark ride located within Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge at Disney's Hollywood Studios. The attraction, notable for its immersive storytelling and use of multiple ride systems, provides a highly detailed and cinematic experience. It is considered one of the most technologically advanced attractions built by Walt Disney Imagineering.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the Star Wars universe, joining the Resistance in a secret mission against the First Order. The narrative begins with guests boarding a transport ship, which is then captured by a Star Destroyer. The experience continues as guests navigate through the ship, encountering First Order Stormtroopers, Kylo Ren, and other characters from the Star Wars saga. The theming incorporates detailed sets, projections, and audio-animatronics to create a realistic and engaging environment.

## Attraction Experience
The ride experience utilizes multiple ride systems, including a motion-based simulator, trackless vehicles, and a drop sequence. Guests move through a series of pre-shows and ride segments, encountering various scenes and special effects. The entire experience lasts approximately 18 minutes, including pre-show elements. The attraction features extensive use of projections, physical sets, and audio-animatronics to enhance the immersive storytelling.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a broad audience, but it includes intense scenes and effects. Guests must be at least 40 inches (102 cm) tall to ride. Due to the ride's motion, enclosed spaces, and potential for sudden movements, individuals with certain health conditions, including heart problems, back or neck issues, and those who are pregnant, are advised against riding.
