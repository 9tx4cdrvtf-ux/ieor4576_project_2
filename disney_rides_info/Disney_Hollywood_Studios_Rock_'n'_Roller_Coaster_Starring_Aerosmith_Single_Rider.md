# Rock 'n' Roller Coaster Starring Aerosmith Single Rider
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
Rock 'n' Roller Coaster Starring Aerosmith is an enclosed, high-speed steel roller coaster located in the Sunset Boulevard area of Disney's Hollywood Studios. Manufactured by Vekoma, the attraction is notable for its launch system, which propels vehicles from 0 to 57 mph (92 km/h) in approximately 2.8 seconds. It is one of the fastest attractions at Walt Disney World. The Single Rider queue allows guests to experience the attraction with potentially shorter wait times.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers around the fictional recording studio, G-Force Records. Guests are invited to attend an Aerosmith recording session. Upon learning that the band is heading to a concert across town, guests are offered a ride in a super-stretch limousine to join them. The queue area features music memorabilia and references to Aerosmith, establishing the rock-and-roll theme.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a limousine-themed train. The ride begins with a high-speed launch into a darkened tunnel. The coaster features inversions, including a loop and two corkscrews. Onboard audio plays Aerosmith music synchronized to the ride's movements. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 22 seconds. The Single Rider queue merges guests into available ride vehicles to maximize capacity.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for thrill-seekers. Guests must be at least 48 inches (122 cm) tall to ride. Due to the ride's intensity, including high speeds, inversions, and sudden movements, individuals with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding.
