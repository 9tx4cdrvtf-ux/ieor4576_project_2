# Journey of Water, Inspired by Moana
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Journey of Water, Inspired by Moana is a walk-through attraction located in World Nature at Epcot, Walt Disney World. It is a self-guided experience that opened in October 2023. The attraction allows guests to interact with water in various forms, inspired by the animated film *Moana*. It is a key component of Epcot's reimagining, emphasizing the park's focus on nature and exploration.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in a lush environment where they learn about the water cycle. The theming is based on the film *Moana*, featuring characters and elements from the movie. Guests are invited to interact with water in different ways, such as creating waves, moving water through pathways, and witnessing water's journey through the environment. The setting evokes a tropical island landscape, with rockwork, vegetation, and ambient soundscapes.

## Attraction Experience
Guests traverse a winding path through the attraction, encountering various interactive water features. These include spraying fountains, flowing streams, and areas where guests can manipulate water flow. The experience utilizes a combination of physical effects and technological elements to create the interactions. The duration of the experience is self-paced, typically lasting between 10-20 minutes, depending on guest interaction.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Journey of Water, Inspired by Moana is designed for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find some areas challenging due to uneven terrain. Guests prone to motion sickness are unlikely to be affected. There are no specific health warnings, but guests should be aware of the potential for getting wet.
