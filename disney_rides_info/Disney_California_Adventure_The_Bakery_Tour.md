# The Bakery Tour
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
The Bakery Tour was a self-guided experience located within the Boudin Bakery in the Pacific Wharf area of Disney California Adventure. It provided guests with a behind-the-scenes look at the process of sourdough bread baking, a signature offering of the park. The attraction was not a ride but a walk-through exhibit.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction was themed to resemble a working bakery, showcasing the various stages of sourdough bread production. Guests were immersed in the sights, sounds, and smells of a real bakery environment. The narrative focused on the history of Boudin Bakery and the unique sourdough starter used in its recipes. Informational displays and video presentations explained the fermentation process, shaping techniques, and baking methods.

## Attraction Experience
Guests entered the bakery tour and proceeded through a series of viewing windows. These windows offered unobstructed views of bakers at work, preparing and baking sourdough bread in various shapes, including the iconic Mickey Mouse loaf. The tour included video presentations detailing the history of the bakery and the sourdough process. The experience culminated in a complimentary sample of fresh sourdough bread. The entire experience lasted approximately 10-15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Bakery Tour was suitable for all ages. There were no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with the attraction. The experience was accessible to guests with disabilities.
