# Cinderella's Fairy Tale Hall
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Cinderella's Fairy Tale Hall is a walk-through attraction located within Fantasyland at Tokyo Disneyland. It is themed after the Disney animated film *Cinderella* (1950). The attraction allows guests to experience key scenes from the film through the display of dioramas, character figures, and other visual elements. It is a permanent fixture of the park, offering a classic Disney experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the story of Cinderella, following her journey from a mistreated servant to a princess. The hall is designed to resemble Cinderella Castle's interior, featuring ornate decorations, portraits, and displays that depict pivotal moments from the film. These include Cinderella's encounters with her stepmother and stepsisters, the Fairy Godmother's transformation, the ball, and the glass slipper's search for its owner.

## Attraction Experience
Guests proceed through a series of rooms, each showcasing a different scene from the film. Dioramas and figures, including Cinderella, Prince Charming, and the Fairy Godmother, are presented in detailed settings. The attraction utilizes lighting, sound effects, and music from the film to enhance the storytelling. The experience culminates in a grand ballroom scene, offering a photo opportunity with Cinderella and Prince Charming. The entire walk-through experience typically lasts approximately 10-15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Cinderella's Fairy Tale Hall is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction is a walk-through experience and does not involve any physical exertion. Guests with mobility issues may navigate the attraction, but may require assistance. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
