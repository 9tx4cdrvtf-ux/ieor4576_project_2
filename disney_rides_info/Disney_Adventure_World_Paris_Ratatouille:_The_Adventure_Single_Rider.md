# Ratatouille: The Adventure Single Rider
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
Ratatouille: The Adventure Single Rider is a trackless dark ride located in the World of Pixar area of Disney Adventure World Paris. The attraction, manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering, allows guests to experience a 4D adventure based on the Disney-Pixar film *Ratatouille*. It is a popular attraction known for its innovative ride system and immersive theming. The Single Rider queue allows guests traveling alone to bypass a portion of the regular queue.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are shrunk to the size of a rat and embark on a culinary adventure through Gusteau's restaurant in Paris. The story follows Remy, the film's protagonist, as he attempts to create a delicious meal while avoiding the restaurant's staff. The attraction features detailed sets, including the restaurant's kitchen, dining room, and the streets of Paris, all designed to create a believable and immersive environment.

## Attraction Experience
The ride utilizes trackless ride vehicles that move independently through the scenes. Guests wear 3D glasses to enhance the experience. The ride incorporates various sensory effects, including heat, water, and smells, to further immerse riders in the story. The ride duration is approximately 5 minutes. The Single Rider queue merges with the main queue at a specific point, reducing wait times for solo riders.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is generally suitable for all ages, though younger children may find some scenes intense. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or motion sickness should consider the ride's effects before boarding. Guests with certain medical conditions should consult the park's guidelines or a medical professional before riding.
