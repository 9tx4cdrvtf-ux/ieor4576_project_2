# "it's a small world"
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
"it's a small world" is a slow-moving boat ride located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park Paris. It is a dark ride attraction, originally conceived for the 1964 New York World's Fair. The ride features Audio-Animatronic figures of children from around the world singing the attraction's eponymous theme song. The ride is a significant and iconic attraction, representing themes of global unity and childhood innocence.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents a journey through various regions of the world, each represented by a distinct scene populated by Audio-Animatronic dolls. These dolls, dressed in traditional costumes, sing and dance to the "It's a Small World (After All)" song. The scenes are brightly colored and stylized, creating a cheerful and optimistic atmosphere. The ride's narrative focuses on the shared humanity of children across different cultures.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a boat that travels along a waterway. The boat passes through a series of rooms, each depicting a different continent or region. The scenes incorporate various special effects, including lighting, sound, and animated figures. The ride lasts approximately 10 minutes. The boats are propelled by a submerged track system.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
"it's a small world" is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitive epilepsy should be aware of the flashing lights used in some scenes. The ride is a slow-moving boat ride and is accessible to guests with mobility impairments, though they may need to transfer from a wheelchair.
