# Expedition Everest - Legend of the Forbidden Mountain
**Location:** Animal Kingdom

## Overview
Expedition Everest - Legend of the Forbidden Mountain is a steel roller coaster located in the Asia section of Disney's Animal Kingdom. Manufactured by Vekoma, the attraction is notable for its unique blend of coaster elements, immersive theming, and backward motion sequence. It is a significant investment in the park's storytelling, representing a major thrill ride experience within the themed environment.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers on a journey to the base of Mount Everest, where guests are portrayed as explorers venturing into the Forbidden Mountain. The story involves a cursed Yeti, a mythical creature said to inhabit the mountain. The queue line features artifacts and research materials, establishing the expedition's premise. The ride's environment incorporates detailed theming, including a Nepalese village, a tea house, and the mountain itself, creating an immersive atmosphere.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a train designed to resemble a narrow-gauge railway. The ride begins with a slow ascent, followed by a forward drop. The train then enters a darkened show building, encountering the Yeti. The coaster then unexpectedly stops, and the train is propelled backward through the mountain. The ride concludes with a final forward descent and return to the station. The ride incorporates audio-animatronics, lighting effects, and a variety of sensory experiences to enhance the narrative. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Expedition Everest is designed for a broad audience, but has a height requirement of 44 inches (112 cm). Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding. The attraction's intensity includes high speeds, steep drops, and backward motion, which may not be suitable for all riders.
