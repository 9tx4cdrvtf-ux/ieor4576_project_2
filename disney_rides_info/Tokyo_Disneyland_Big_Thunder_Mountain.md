# Big Thunder Mountain
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Big Thunder Mountain is a mine train roller coaster located in Tokyo Disneyland's Westernland. It is a clone of the original attraction at Disneyland and Magic Kingdom, designed by Walt Disney Imagineering. The ride is a significant thrill attraction within the park, offering a family-friendly coaster experience with elaborate theming.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around a runaway mine train in a fictional Old West town, set within a mountain range. Guests are transported to the town of Rainbow Ridge, which was once a thriving mining community. The narrative suggests that the mountain is haunted, and the mine train is cursed, leading to unpredictable and thrilling journeys through the mountain's tunnels and caverns. The theming incorporates elements of the American Southwest, including desert landscapes, rock formations, and abandoned mining equipment.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a mine train that ascends a lift hill before embarking on a series of drops, turns, and twists through the mountain. The ride features special effects, including simulated explosions, lighting effects, and audio-animatronic figures of bats, animals, and miners. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes and 30 seconds. The coaster utilizes a traditional steel track layout.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Big Thunder Mountain is generally suitable for families and thrill-seekers. The minimum height requirement is 102 cm (40 inches). Guests with a history of heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding.
