# The Enchanted Tiki Room: Stitch Presents “Aloha E Komo Mai!”
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
The Enchanted Tiki Room: Stitch Presents “Aloha E Komo Mai!” is a musical animatronic show located in Adventureland at Tokyo Disneyland. It is a reimagining of the original Enchanted Tiki Room attraction, featuring characters from Disney's *Lilo & Stitch* franchise. The attraction, which utilizes advanced audio-animatronics, provides a tropical-themed entertainment experience for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The show takes place within a Polynesian-themed longhouse. Guests are welcomed by Stitch, who acts as the host, alongside a cast of singing birds, flowers, and tiki statues. The narrative revolves around a celebration of Hawaiian culture, with Stitch leading the audience through a series of musical numbers and comedic interactions. The setting is designed to evoke a tropical paradise, complete with lush foliage and vibrant colors.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter a large, circular theater and are seated facing a central stage. The show begins with Stitch's introduction, followed by performances from the animatronic figures. The show incorporates various special effects, including lighting, sound, and projection mapping, to enhance the immersive experience. The performance lasts approximately 15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. The show is presented in Japanese, with some English phrases. Guests sensitive to loud noises or flashing lights should be aware of these elements within the show.
