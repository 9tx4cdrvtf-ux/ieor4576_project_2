# Kilimanjaro Safaris
**Location:** Animal Kingdom

## Overview
Kilimanjaro Safaris is a guided safari attraction located in the Africa section of Disney's Animal Kingdom. It is a slow-moving, open-sided vehicle ride that simulates a wildlife safari through a savanna environment. The attraction allows guests to observe a variety of African animals in a naturalistic habitat. It is a significant attraction within Animal Kingdom, emphasizing the park's conservation and animal welfare themes.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative places guests as participants in a wildlife safari tour operated by the fictional Harambe Wildlife Reserve. The setting is designed to resemble the East African savanna, complete with diverse landscapes, including grasslands, watering holes, and rocky outcrops. The theming incorporates elements of a working safari, including a research station and a vehicle that appears to be a modified safari truck. The narrative emphasizes the importance of animal conservation and the preservation of natural habitats.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a large, open-sided safari vehicle. The vehicle travels along a designated route, providing views of various African animals, including giraffes, elephants, lions, zebras, and rhinoceroses. The ride incorporates several environmental effects, such as simulated weather and terrain changes. The duration of the ride is approximately 20 minutes. The vehicles are equipped with a guide who provides factual information about the animals and their habitats.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Kilimanjaro Safaris is suitable for a wide audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with back, neck, or heart problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider the ride's motion and potential for bumps.
