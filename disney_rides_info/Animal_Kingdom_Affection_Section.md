# Affection Section
**Location:** Animal Kingdom

## Overview
The Affection Section is a walk-through animal encounter located within Rafiki's Planet Watch at Disney's Animal Kingdom. It allows guests to interact with and observe a variety of domesticated animals, primarily focusing on species that are typically receptive to human interaction. The attraction emphasizes animal care, conservation, and education.

## Narrative and Theming
The Affection Section is presented as a petting zoo and animal interaction area, seamlessly integrated into the broader Rafiki's Planet Watch environment. The theming focuses on the importance of animal welfare and the bond between humans and animals. Signage and Cast Members provide information about the animals' care, diets, and natural behaviors. The area is designed to resemble a well-maintained farm setting, with appropriate enclosures and landscaping.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are invited to enter the Affection Section and interact with various animals under the supervision of Cast Members. Animals typically include goats, sheep, pigs, and other domesticated species. Guests are permitted to pet and brush the animals, providing a tactile experience. Hand-washing stations are available at the exit. The duration of the experience is self-paced, typically lasting between 15 and 30 minutes, depending on guest interaction.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Affection Section is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with allergies to animals or hay should exercise caution. Children must be supervised by an adult. Guests are advised to wash their hands thoroughly after interacting with the animals.
