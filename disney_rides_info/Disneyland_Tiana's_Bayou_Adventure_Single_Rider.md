# Tiana's Bayou Adventure Single Rider
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Tiana's Bayou Adventure Single Rider is a water flume ride located in New Orleans Square at Disneyland Park. It is a reimagining of the former Splash Mountain attraction. The ride features a new narrative based on Disney's 2009 animated film, *The Princess and the Frog*, and incorporates Audio-Animatronics figures, practical effects, and projected media. The ride was manufactured by Intamin.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the story of Tiana's continued journey after the events of the film. The ride's narrative follows Tiana as she prepares for a Mardi Gras celebration, with guests assisting in finding the missing ingredients for the party. The attraction's theming evokes the Louisiana bayou, with detailed environments, including swamps, forests, and the sounds of the bayou. The ride incorporates original music and songs from the film, as well as new compositions.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with guests boarding a log flume vehicle. The journey progresses through various scenes populated by Audio-Animatronics figures of characters from *The Princess and the Frog*. The ride includes several small drops and culminates in a significant plunge down a five-story drop. The ride utilizes a combination of physical sets, projections, and lighting effects to create an immersive experience. The total ride duration is approximately 10 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Tiana's Bayou Adventure is designed for a general audience. Guests must be at least 40 inches (102 cm) tall to ride. Due to the nature of the ride, including drops and potential exposure to water, guests with certain health conditions, including heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their health before riding.
