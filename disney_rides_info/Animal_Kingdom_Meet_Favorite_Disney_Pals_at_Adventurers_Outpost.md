# Meet Favorite Disney Pals at Adventurers Outpost
**Location:** Animal Kingdom

## Overview
Meet Favorite Disney Pals at Adventurers Outpost is a character meet-and-greet location in Animal Kingdom. It allows guests to interact with Mickey Mouse and Minnie Mouse, dressed in their safari attire. The attraction provides a traditional photo opportunity and is a popular destination for families.

## Narrative and Theming
The Adventurers Outpost is presented as a base camp for explorers and adventurers. The building's architecture and interior design reflect a global aesthetic, incorporating elements from various cultures and geographical regions. The setting suggests a place where explorers gather, share stories, and prepare for expeditions. The presence of Mickey and Minnie, dressed in safari outfits, reinforces the theme of exploration and discovery, aligning with Animal Kingdom's overall focus on nature and adventure.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue through a themed pathway leading to the main meeting area. Upon entering, guests encounter Mickey and Minnie Mouse, posed for photographs. Cast members assist with photo opportunities using guests' cameras or Disney PhotoPass services. The interaction typically lasts for a few minutes per group. The entire experience, including queue time, can vary depending on park attendance.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for all ages. There are no height requirements or specific health restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the attraction, and assistance is available.
