# Hong Kong Disneyland Railroad – Fantasyland Station
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Fantasyland Station is one of the four stations of the Hong Kong Disneyland Railroad, a narrow-gauge steam train attraction. It provides transportation around the park's perimeter, offering guests views of various themed lands. The railroad is a replica of the classic Disneyland Railroad concept, utilizing steam-powered locomotives built by the WED Enterprises. Fantasyland Station serves as a key transportation hub, connecting guests to other areas of the park.

## Narrative and Theming
The station is themed to blend seamlessly with the whimsical atmosphere of Fantasyland. Its architecture evokes a Victorian-era train depot, featuring ornate detailing, colorful signage, and a covered platform. The station's design incorporates elements of classic fairy tales and fantasy, creating an immersive environment that complements the surrounding attractions. The station's design is intended to transport guests to a bygone era of rail travel, enhancing the overall storytelling experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the train at Fantasyland Station and embark on a scenic journey around the park. The train travels along a track that encircles the park, passing through Adventureland, Tomorrowland, and Main Street, U.S.A. The ride duration is approximately 20 minutes, offering views of the park's landscaping and themed environments. The steam-powered locomotives provide a classic train experience, complete with the sounds of the engine and the whistle.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Hong Kong Disneyland Railroad is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the train with assistance. Strollers must be folded before boarding. Guests with certain health conditions should consult with a medical professional before riding.
