# The Leonardo Challenge
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
The Leonardo Challenge is a walk-through attraction located in the Mediterranean Harbor area of Tokyo DisneySea. It is a self-guided experience themed around the workshop of Leonardo da Vinci, offering guests an interactive exploration of his inventions and artistic creations. The attraction does not utilize a ride system and focuses on immersive theming and interactive elements.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests enter a recreation of Leonardo da Vinci's workshop, set within a Renaissance-era Italian palazzo. The narrative posits that guests are invited to examine the inventor's various projects, including flying machines, mechanical devices, and artistic studies. The environment is filled with detailed props, sketches, and interactive displays designed to simulate the atmosphere of a working workshop. The theming emphasizes the intersection of art, science, and engineering, reflecting da Vinci's multifaceted genius.

## Attraction Experience
The experience is self-paced, allowing guests to explore the workshop at their leisure. Interactive elements include levers to pull, buttons to press, and models to examine. Guests can view replicas of da Vinci's inventions, such as a flying machine and a tank, and learn about their design and function. The attraction incorporates lighting and sound effects to enhance the immersive environment, creating a sense of discovery and exploration. The duration of the experience is approximately 15-20 minutes, depending on the guest's pace.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Leonardo Challenge is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction is primarily a visual and tactile experience, with no intense physical elements. Guests with mobility issues can navigate the attraction, though some areas may be more challenging. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
