# Tarzan's Treehouse
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Tarzan's Treehouse is a walk-through attraction located in Adventureland at Disneyland Hong Kong. It is a re-theming of the Swiss Family Treehouse, originally found in Disneyland Park (California). The attraction allows guests to explore a multi-level treehouse, themed after the Disney animated film *Tarzan*.

## Narrative and Theming
The treehouse is presented as the home of Tarzan, Jane, and their family. The environment depicts the resourceful construction of the treehouse from salvaged materials, reflecting the family's ingenuity and adaptation to the jungle environment. Various scenes throughout the treehouse illustrate key moments from the film, including Tarzan's discovery of his family, his interactions with Jane, and his life in the jungle. The theming incorporates elements of both the jungle and the family's human origins.

## Attraction Experience
Guests access the treehouse via a series of rope bridges and stairs. The attraction is a self-guided experience, allowing visitors to explore at their own pace. Inside, guests encounter various interactive elements, including dioramas, sound effects, and animated figures. The treehouse offers panoramic views of Adventureland. The entire experience typically lasts between 10-15 minutes, depending on the guest's exploration speed.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Tarzan's Treehouse is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. However, guests with mobility impairments may find the stairs and rope bridges challenging. Guests with a fear of heights should consider the nature of the attraction before entering.
