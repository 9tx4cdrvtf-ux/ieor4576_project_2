# Rex’s Racer
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Rex’s Racer is a high-speed, U-shaped steel shuttle coaster located in Toy Story Land at Shanghai Disney Resort. Manufactured by Vekoma, the ride features two vehicles that launch from opposite ends of a track, accelerating towards each other before reaching a peak height and returning. It is a significant thrill ride within the family-friendly Toy Story Land, offering a unique coaster experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around the character Rex from the *Toy Story* film franchise. Guests are immersed in a world scaled to the size of toys, with the ride vehicles resembling toy cars. The queue area is designed to appear as though guests are in Andy's backyard, with oversized toys and building blocks creating the environment. The narrative suggests that Rex is playing with a toy car set, providing the context for the ride's high-speed movements.

## Attraction Experience
Riders board one of two toy car-themed vehicles. The ride begins with a launch, accelerating the vehicle up a steep incline. The vehicle then travels along the U-shaped track, reaching a peak height before descending and accelerating towards the opposite end. The vehicles then repeat this motion, reaching a maximum speed of approximately 85 kilometers per hour (53 mph). The ride duration is approximately 60 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Rex’s Racer is designed for guests who meet the minimum height requirement of 95 cm (37 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, including heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised against riding. The ride involves high speeds and sudden changes in direction, which may not be suitable for all guests.
