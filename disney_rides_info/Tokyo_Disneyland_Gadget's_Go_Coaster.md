# Gadget's Go Coaster
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Gadget's Go Coaster is a junior roller coaster located in Toontown at Tokyo Disneyland. It is a family-friendly attraction designed by Walt Disney Imagineering and manufactured by Zamperla. The coaster offers a gentle, winding ride experience, making it suitable for younger guests and those seeking a less intense thrill. It is a key element of Toontown's immersive environment, contributing to the land's overall playful atmosphere.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around Gadget Hackwrench, a character from the Disney animated series *Chip 'n Dale: Rescue Rangers*. Guests are invited to experience a miniature roller coaster built by Gadget using found objects and repurposed materials. The queue line and surrounding area are designed to resemble Gadget's workshop, filled with oversized tools, contraptions, and whimsical details that reflect her inventive nature.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a small, open-air coaster train designed to resemble a wooden contraption. The ride begins with a slow ascent up a small lift hill, followed by a series of gentle drops, turns, and curves. The track layout is relatively short and compact, providing a brief but enjoyable experience. The ride incorporates minimal special effects, focusing primarily on the visual theming and the sensation of movement. The total ride duration is approximately one minute.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Gadget's Go Coaster is designed for families and children. The height requirement is 90 cm (35 inches). Guests with back problems, heart conditions, or other health concerns should consider the ride's suitability before boarding. Pregnant women are advised against riding.
