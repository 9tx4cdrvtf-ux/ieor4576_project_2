# “it’s a small world with Groot”
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
“it’s a small world with Groot” is a reimagining of the classic “it’s a small world” boat ride attraction located in Tokyo Disneyland's Fantasyland. The attraction, a collaboration between Walt Disney Imagineering and Marvel Studios, incorporates characters from the *Guardians of the Galaxy* franchise, specifically Groot, into the existing ride system. It maintains the original ride's core concept of celebrating global unity through animatronic dolls and a repetitive, iconic theme song.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction maintains the original premise of a boat journey through various regions of the world, represented by animatronic children and animals. The addition of Groot is subtle, with the character integrated into several scenes. Guests can spot Groot in various forms, such as a small potted plant or a larger animatronic figure, interacting with the existing scenes. The overall theme remains focused on peace, harmony, and the interconnectedness of humanity, with the *Guardians of the Galaxy* elements enhancing the experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a boat that travels along a waterway through a series of brightly colored scenes. The ride features hundreds of animatronic figures representing children from different countries, all singing the attraction's signature song, "It's a Small World (After All)." The journey lasts approximately 10 minutes. The ride utilizes a simple flume system, with gentle inclines and declines. The attraction incorporates various sensory effects, including lighting, music, and animatronics.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
“it’s a small world with Groot” is designed for a general audience, including families with young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or sensitivity to repetitive music should be aware of the attraction's characteristics. The ride is generally accessible to guests with disabilities, but specific accessibility information is available at the park.
