# Tiana's Bayou Adventure
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Tiana's Bayou Adventure is a log flume ride located in New Orleans Square at Disneyland. It is a reimagining of the former Splash Mountain attraction, incorporating the characters and storyline from Disney's 2009 animated film, *The Princess and the Frog*. The ride was developed by Walt Disney Imagineering and features new animatronics, music, and scenes. It offers a family-friendly experience with a focus on storytelling and musical entertainment.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative follows Princess Tiana as she prepares to host a Mardi Gras celebration for her friends and family. Guests are invited to join Tiana on a journey through the Louisiana bayou, encountering various animal characters and experiencing the vibrant culture of New Orleans. The ride's theming emphasizes the beauty of the bayou, with lush vegetation, detailed set pieces, and a soundtrack featuring original music from the film.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board hollowed-out logs that float through a series of scenes. The ride includes several gentle drops, culminating in a final, significant plunge. Throughout the experience, riders encounter Audio-Animatronic figures of Tiana, Louis the alligator, and other characters. The ride incorporates water effects, lighting, and projections to enhance the storytelling. The total ride duration is approximately 10 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Tiana's Bayou Adventure is designed for a general audience. Guests must be at least 40 inches (102 cm) tall to ride. Guests with certain health conditions, including back or neck problems, heart conditions, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider the ride's intensity before boarding.
