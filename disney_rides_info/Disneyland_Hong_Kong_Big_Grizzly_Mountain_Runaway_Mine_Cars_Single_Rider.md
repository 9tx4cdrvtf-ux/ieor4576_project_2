# Big Grizzly Mountain Runaway Mine Cars Single Rider
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Big Grizzly Mountain Runaway Mine Cars is a steel roller coaster located in the Grizzly Gulch area of Disneyland Hong Kong. The ride is a custom-designed coaster manufactured by Vekoma. It is notable for its unique backwards launch and multiple launches throughout the ride experience, as well as its integration with the surrounding themed environment. The attraction offers a Single Rider queue to reduce wait times.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride is themed around a gold mining operation in the fictional town of Grizzly Gulch. Guests are purportedly boarding a runaway mine train that has been disrupted by a family of grizzlies. The narrative is conveyed through the queue line theming, which includes mining equipment, and the ride's integration with the surrounding mountain landscape. The story culminates in a series of unexpected twists and turns, including a backwards launch through a mountain tunnel.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board mine car-themed vehicles that accommodate multiple riders. The ride begins with a slow ascent, followed by a series of drops, turns, and a forward launch. The coaster then unexpectedly launches backwards into a dark tunnel. The ride continues with additional forward launches and maneuvers, utilizing the terrain to create a dynamic experience. The ride incorporates audio-animatronics, special effects, and lighting to enhance the immersive experience. The total ride duration is approximately 3 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a broad audience, but riders must meet a minimum height requirement of 112 cm (44 in). Guests with a history of heart conditions, back or neck problems, or other health concerns should consult with a medical professional before riding. Expectant mothers are advised not to ride.
