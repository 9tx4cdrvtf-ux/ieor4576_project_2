# Main Street Cinema
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Main Street Cinema is a walk-through attraction located on Main Street, U.S.A., at Disneyland. It showcases a rotating selection of classic animated short films produced by Walt Disney Studios. The attraction provides a nostalgic experience, allowing guests to view vintage cartoons in a setting designed to evoke the atmosphere of an early 20th-century movie theater.

## Narrative and Theming
The Main Street Cinema is themed as a turn-of-the-century movie theater. The building's exterior features a marquee displaying the current film selection. Inside, the waiting area and viewing space are designed to resemble a traditional cinema, complete with vintage posters and decorative elements reminiscent of the era when the featured cartoons were originally released. The setting aims to transport guests back in time, immersing them in the experience of watching animated shorts as they were originally intended.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter the cinema and proceed to a viewing area where they sit on benches to watch the selected animated shorts. The films are projected onto a large screen. The program typically consists of several classic Disney cartoons, with the selection changing periodically. The experience is passive, allowing guests to relax and enjoy the animated content. The duration of the experience varies depending on the length of the featured shorts, typically lasting between 15 to 20 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Main Street Cinema is suitable for all ages. There are no height requirements or specific health restrictions. The attraction is a passive experience and poses no physical challenges.
