# Slinky Dog Spin
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Slinky Dog Spin is a spinning amusement ride located in the Toy Story Land area of Shanghai Disney Resort. The attraction, manufactured by Zamperla, is designed to resemble Slinky Dog from the *Toy Story* film franchise. It offers a family-friendly experience, providing gentle spinning motion and themed visual elements.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in a playful environment, shrunk to the size of toys and invited to play in Andy's backyard. The ride's narrative centers around Slinky Dog, who is playfully chasing his own tail. The surrounding area features oversized toys and familiar elements from the *Toy Story* films, enhancing the immersive experience. The queue line is themed to resemble a toy box, further establishing the setting.

## Attraction Experience
Riders are seated in circular rows on Slinky Dog's body. The ride begins with Slinky Dog spinning in a circular motion, gradually increasing in speed. The ride vehicle also bobs up and down, adding to the sensation. The ride cycle lasts approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds. The attraction utilizes colorful lighting and themed music to enhance the visual and auditory experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Slinky Dog Spin is designed for a broad audience, particularly families with young children. The minimum height requirement is 80 cm (31.5 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, particularly those susceptible to motion sickness or with back problems, are advised to consider their condition before riding. Expectant mothers are also advised against riding.
