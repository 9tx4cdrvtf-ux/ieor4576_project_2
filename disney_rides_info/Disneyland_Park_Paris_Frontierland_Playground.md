# Frontierland Playground
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Frontierland Playground is a themed children's play area located within the Frontierland area of Disneyland Park Paris. It offers a variety of climbing structures, slides, and interactive elements designed to immerse guests in the atmosphere of the American Wild West. The playground provides a space for younger visitors to expend energy and engage in imaginative play, complementing the more passive attractions within the land.

## Narrative and Theming
The playground is themed to resemble a frontier fort, complete with wooden structures, rope bridges, and lookout towers. The design incorporates elements reminiscent of a Wild West settlement, including rustic buildings and themed props. The setting aims to transport children to a bygone era, encouraging them to role-play as pioneers, cowboys, or explorers. The immersive environment allows children to interact with the environment and create their own adventures within the themed setting.

## Attraction Experience
The Frontierland Playground offers a self-guided experience. Children can navigate the various play structures at their own pace. The playground features multiple levels, slides of varying sizes, climbing nets, and interactive elements such as tunnels and bridges. There is no set duration for the experience, as children are free to explore the area for as long as they wish.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Frontierland Playground is primarily designed for children aged 3 to 12 years old. There are no specific height requirements, but parental supervision is recommended. Guests with mobility issues may find certain areas of the playground difficult to access. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions listed, but parents should consider their child's physical capabilities and any pre-existing health conditions before allowing them to play.
