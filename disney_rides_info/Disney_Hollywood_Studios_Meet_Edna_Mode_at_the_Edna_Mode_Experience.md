# Meet Edna Mode at the Edna Mode Experience
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
"Meet Edna Mode at the Edna Mode Experience" is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in Pixar Plaza at Disney's Hollywood Studios. The experience allows guests to interact with the character Edna Mode from Disney-Pixar's *The Incredibles* franchise. It is a stationary experience, offering photo opportunities and brief interactions with the character.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed to resemble Edna Mode's high-fashion studio and design space. The setting is modern and minimalist, reflecting Edna's distinct aesthetic. Guests are invited to meet the renowned designer and possibly be considered for a new super suit. The environment incorporates elements from the *Incredibles* films, including design sketches, mannequins, and other props associated with Edna's work.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area before entering the studio. Upon entering, guests encounter Edna Mode, who interacts with them through dialogue and poses for photographs. The interaction typically lasts for a few minutes, allowing guests to capture photos and briefly converse with the character. The experience is entirely dependent on the character's presence and does not involve any moving elements or special effects beyond the theming of the environment.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for all ages. There are no height requirements or specific health restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the experience.
