# Pirates of the Caribbean Battle for the Sunken Treasure
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Pirates of the Caribbean: Battle for the Sunken Treasure is a dark ride attraction located in Treasure Cove at Shanghai Disney Resort. It is a boat ride utilizing a trackless ride system, a significant technological advancement over previous iterations of the Pirates of the Caribbean attraction. The ride was developed by Walt Disney Imagineering and is notable for its immersive storytelling and advanced animatronics.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride's narrative centers around a pirate adventure on the high seas, with guests becoming part of a crew searching for sunken treasure. The story unfolds through a combination of elaborate sets, projections, and animatronic figures, including Captain Jack Sparrow. The attraction features a blend of Chinese and Western pirate lore, creating a unique cultural experience. The theming immerses guests in a pirate world, complete with shipwrecks, underwater scenes, and naval battles.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board boats that navigate a trackless system, allowing for dynamic movement and unexpected turns. The ride incorporates large-scale projections, animatronic figures, and water effects to create a multi-sensory experience. The boats travel through various scenes depicting pirate battles, underwater exploration, and encounters with mythical creatures. The ride duration is approximately 6 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is generally suitable for all ages, though young children may find some scenes intense. There is no height requirement. Guests with pre-existing health conditions, particularly those sensitive to motion sickness or with heart conditions, should consider the ride's intensity before boarding.
