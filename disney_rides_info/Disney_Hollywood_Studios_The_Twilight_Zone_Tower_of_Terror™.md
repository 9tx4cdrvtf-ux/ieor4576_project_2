# The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror™
**Location:** Disney Hollywood Studios

## Overview
The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror™ is a drop tower dark ride located at Disney's Hollywood Studios. Manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering, the attraction is themed after the television series *The Twilight Zone*. It is a significant anchor attraction within the park, known for its immersive theming and thrilling ride experience.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the story of the Hollywood Tower Hotel, a luxurious hotel struck by lightning in 1939, causing the disappearance of five guests in an elevator. The attraction's queue and pre-show build suspense, presenting the hotel as abandoned and haunted. The narrative establishes the premise of a journey into the fifth dimension, where guests will experience the events of that fateful night.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a "service elevator" that ascends through the hotel. The ride incorporates various special effects, including projections, audio-animatronics, and lighting, to enhance the storytelling. The elevator then enters a "Twilight Zone" sequence, where the elevator car transitions to a freefall drop sequence. The ride utilizes a unique vertical drop system, providing multiple drops of varying heights. The entire experience lasts approximately three minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror™ is designed for a broad audience, but it is recommended for guests who enjoy thrill rides. Guests must be at least 40 inches (102 cm) tall to ride. Due to the ride's intense nature, including sudden drops and darkness, it is not recommended for guests with certain medical conditions, including heart problems, back or neck issues, or those who are pregnant.
