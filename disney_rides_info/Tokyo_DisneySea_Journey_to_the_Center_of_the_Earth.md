# Journey to the Center of the Earth
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Journey to the Center of the Earth is a dark ride attraction located in the Mysterious Island port of Tokyo DisneySea. It is a high-speed, thrill-ride experience utilizing a unique combination of ride vehicles and special effects. The attraction is a significant draw for the park, known for its immersive theming and dramatic finale.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around the Jules Verne novel, *Journey to the Center of the Earth*. Guests, as explorers, board a subterranean vehicle designed by Captain Nemo. The queue area and ride environment are meticulously themed to resemble a volcanic cavern, complete with geothermal activity, bioluminescent flora, and various scientific instruments. The narrative involves a descent into the Earth's core, encountering strange creatures and geological formations.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a vehicle resembling a mining cart. The ride begins with a slow descent into the caverns, showcasing various scenes. The vehicle then accelerates rapidly, navigating through a series of themed environments, including a mushroom forest and a cavern filled with lava. The climax involves an encounter with a giant lava monster before a high-speed ascent back to the surface. The ride utilizes animatronics, projections, and special effects, including heat and water, to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 6 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for a wide audience, though it features a high-speed element and some dark scenes. Guests must be at least 112 cm (44 in) tall to ride. Individuals with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are otherwise sensitive to motion sickness are advised against riding.
