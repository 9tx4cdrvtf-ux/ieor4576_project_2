# Splash Mountain
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Splash Mountain is a log flume ride located in Critter Country at Tokyo Disneyland. It is based on the 1946 Disney film *Song of the South*. The attraction was manufactured by Arrow Dynamics and opened on October 1, 1992. It is a significant water-based thrill ride, offering a combination of dark ride scenes and a large drop, making it a popular attraction within the park.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride's narrative follows Br'er Rabbit as he leaves his home in search of adventure, encountering Br'er Fox and Br'er Bear along the way. The attraction features numerous Audio-Animatronic figures depicting scenes from the film, set within a Southern American bayou environment. The theming incorporates detailed sets, vibrant colors, and musical sequences based on the film's soundtrack, creating an immersive experience for guests.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board hollowed-out logs that navigate through a series of indoor scenes and outdoor flume sections. The ride includes several small drops and culminates in a 52.5-foot (16 m) drop down a steep flume, resulting in a significant splash. The ride duration is approximately 10 minutes. The attraction utilizes water effects, lighting, and sound to enhance the storytelling and create a dynamic experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Splash Mountain is generally suitable for guests of all ages, though it is a thrill ride and may be frightening for some. Guests must be at least 90 cm (35 in) tall to ride. Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding.
