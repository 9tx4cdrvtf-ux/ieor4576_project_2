# Frozen Ever After – Presented by Blue Cross
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Frozen Ever After is a boat ride attraction located in the World of Frozen at Disneyland Hong Kong. It is a dark ride that utilizes Audio-Animatronics and projection mapping to immerse guests in the world of Disney's *Frozen* film franchise. The ride is a significant component of the park's expansion, offering a highly themed experience based on the popular animated film.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction transports guests to the kingdom of Arendelle during its annual Winter in Summer celebration. Guests are invited to join the festivities hosted by Queen Elsa and Princess Anna. The ride's setting is a blend of the film's iconic locations, including the ice palace and the village of Arendelle, all decorated for the celebration. The narrative focuses on the joy and camaraderie of the characters, with a focus on the themes of family and love.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board boats that navigate through a series of scenes depicting key moments from the *Frozen* films. The ride incorporates Audio-Animatronic figures of characters such as Elsa, Anna, Olaf, Kristoff, and Sven. Projection mapping is used to create dynamic environments and enhance the storytelling. The ride includes a backward descent down a small drop. The entire experience lasts approximately 4 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Frozen Ever After is designed for a general audience. There is a height requirement of 102 cm (40 inches). Guests with a history of back or neck problems, heart conditions, or those who are pregnant should consider the ride's physical demands before boarding.
