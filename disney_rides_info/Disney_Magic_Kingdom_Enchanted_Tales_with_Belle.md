# Enchanted Tales with Belle
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Enchanted Tales with Belle is an interactive storytelling experience located in Fantasyland at the Magic Kingdom. The attraction allows guests to participate in a retelling of the classic Disney animated film, *Beauty and the Beast*. It is a walk-through experience that utilizes interactive elements and live performers to immerse guests in the story.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as Maurice's cottage, Belle's father. Guests enter the cottage and are guided through various rooms, including Maurice's workshop and a library. The narrative centers around Belle and her friends preparing a surprise for the Beast. The setting is designed to replicate the look and feel of the animated film, with detailed props and set pieces.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are ushered into a room where they are greeted by Belle. Select guests are chosen to participate in the retelling of the story, taking on roles such as Lumiere or Mrs. Potts. The experience culminates in a brief performance, including the use of special effects such as lighting and projections, and concludes with a photo opportunity with Belle. The entire experience lasts approximately 15-20 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Enchanted Tales with Belle is designed for a general audience, particularly families with young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may encounter difficulties due to the walk-through nature of the attraction. The attraction is generally considered safe for all guests, but those sensitive to enclosed spaces or loud noises should be aware of the potential sensory elements.
