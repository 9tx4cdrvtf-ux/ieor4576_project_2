# Monsters, Inc. Mike & Sulley to the Rescue!
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Monsters, Inc. Mike & Sulley to the Rescue! is a dark ride attraction located in the Pixar Pier area of Disney California Adventure. The ride is a slow-moving, track-based attraction that immerses guests in the world of the Pixar film *Monsters, Inc.* It is a significant attraction within the park, providing a family-friendly experience and contributing to the themed environment of Pixar Pier.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative follows the storyline of *Monsters, Inc.*, where guests are transported into Monstropolis. The ride's setting recreates key locations from the film, including the Scare Floor, the CDA (Child Detection Agency) headquarters, and various city streets. Theming elements include detailed set pieces, animatronic figures of characters such as Mike Wazowski, James P. "Sulley" Sullivan, and Boo, and sound effects that enhance the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board ride vehicles designed to resemble taxi cabs. The vehicles travel through a series of scenes, encountering various moments from the film. The ride utilizes a combination of animatronics, projected images, and special effects, including lighting and sound, to bring the story to life. The ride duration is approximately 2.5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a general audience, including families with young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or those who may be sensitive to loud noises should be aware of the sensory effects.
