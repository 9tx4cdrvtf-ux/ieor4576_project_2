# Dumbo the Flying Elephant
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Dumbo the Flying Elephant is a classic spinner ride located in the Storybook Circus area of Fantasyland. It is a re-imagining of the original attraction that debuted at Disneyland in 1955. The ride allows guests to experience the sensation of flight aboard a themed vehicle resembling the titular character from the 1941 Disney animated film, *Dumbo*. The attraction is a popular family-friendly experience, known for its gentle motion and iconic status within the park.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around the circus setting of the *Dumbo* film. Guests queue within a circus tent environment, featuring interactive elements and air conditioning. The ride vehicles are designed to resemble Dumbo, complete with flapping ears. The surrounding area is decorated with circus posters and other thematic elements, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of sixteen Dumbo vehicles, each accommodating up to two riders. The ride cycle lasts approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds. During the ride, the Dumbos rotate around a central hub while also moving up and down, controlled by a lever within each vehicle. This allows riders to control the height of their Dumbo during the flight. The ride offers views of Fantasyland and the surrounding areas.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Dumbo the Flying Elephant is designed for a general audience, particularly families with young children. There is no height requirement. Guests with a fear of heights or motion sickness may wish to consider their sensitivity before riding. Guests with certain medical conditions should consult with a medical professional before riding.
