# Fortress Explorations
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Fortress Explorations is a walk-through attraction located within the Fortress, a large, themed structure situated on the shores of Mediterranean Harbor. It offers guests a self-guided exploration of Leonardo da Vinci's research facility, featuring interactive exhibits and panoramic views of the harbor. The attraction is a key component of the park's theming, providing a detailed backstory and immersive environment.

## Narrative and Theming
The Fortress is presented as a research facility built by the Society of Explorers and Adventurers (S.E.A.), a fictional organization dedicated to scientific discovery and exploration. Guests are invited to explore the various chambers and workshops of the fortress, discovering the inventions and experiments of da Vinci and other S.E.A. members. The theming incorporates Renaissance-era aesthetics with steampunk-inspired technology, creating a blend of historical and fantastical elements.

## Attraction Experience
Guests navigate the Fortress at their own pace, exploring various areas. These include a large-scale model of the solar system, a working water mill, a cannon firing range, and a control room overlooking the harbor. Interactive elements allow guests to experiment with da Vinci's inventions, such as a water-powered clock and a rotating telescope. The attraction offers multiple levels and viewpoints, culminating in a panoramic view of Mediterranean Harbor. The duration of the experience is dependent on the guest's pace, typically ranging from 15 to 30 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Fortress Explorations is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find some areas challenging due to stairs and uneven surfaces. The attraction does not contain any specific health warnings or restrictions.
