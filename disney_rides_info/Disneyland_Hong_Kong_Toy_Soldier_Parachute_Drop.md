# Toy Soldier Parachute Drop
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Toy Soldier Parachute Drop is a family-friendly drop tower ride located in the Toy Story Land area of Disneyland Hong Kong. The attraction simulates the experience of parachuting from a great height, themed around the popular *Toy Story* film franchise. It was manufactured by S&S – Sansei Technologies, a prominent ride manufacturer.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are "recruited" as honorary toy soldiers and are instructed to participate in a training exercise. The ride area is themed as a military training ground, complete with oversized toy soldiers, camouflage netting, and other military-themed props. The queue line winds through a detailed environment, immersing guests in the world of Andy's backyard. The ride vehicles are designed to resemble parachutes, further enhancing the thematic experience.

## Attraction Experience
Riders are seated in open-air gondolas, each holding multiple guests. The gondolas are then hoisted up a tower, providing views of Toy Story Land. Upon reaching the summit, the gondolas are released, providing a series of controlled drops and bounces. The ride cycle includes multiple ascents and descents, simulating the sensation of parachuting. The entire experience lasts approximately one minute.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Toy Soldier Parachute Drop is designed for families and children. Guests must meet a minimum height requirement of 81 cm (32 inches) to ride. Guests with pre-existing health conditions, including but not limited to heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consult with a medical professional before riding.
