# Sailing Ship Columbia
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
The Sailing Ship Columbia is a full-scale replica of a late 18th-century merchant vessel, offering guests a scenic cruise around the Rivers of America in Disneyland. It is a major attraction within the New Orleans Square and Frontierland areas, providing a unique maritime experience. Built by the Disney Imagineers, the Columbia is a significant example of themed attraction design.

## Narrative and Theming
The Columbia is presented as a working sailing ship, offering guests a glimpse into the era of maritime exploration and trade. The ship's theming is historically inspired, with details reflecting the design and operation of a real sailing vessel. Guests are immersed in the atmosphere of a working ship, complete with period-accurate details and costumed crew members. The ship's journey is presented as a voyage, with the Rivers of America serving as the ship's navigable waters.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the Columbia from a dock located in Frontierland. The ship then embarks on a 12- to 15-minute journey around the Rivers of America. The voyage includes views of the park's natural and themed environments, including scenes from the Indian Village and the Haunted Mansion. The ship is propelled by a combination of hidden electric motors and the illusion of wind-filled sails. The experience includes narration and sound effects to enhance the immersive atmosphere.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Sailing Ship Columbia is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find boarding and disembarking the ship challenging. Guests prone to motion sickness should consider the potential for discomfort.
