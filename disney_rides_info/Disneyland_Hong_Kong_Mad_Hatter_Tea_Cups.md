# Mad Hatter Tea Cups
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Mad Hatter Tea Cups is a spinning teacup ride located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Hong Kong. The attraction is a classic amusement park ride, manufactured by Zamperla, and is a staple of Disneyland parks worldwide. It provides a whimsical and visually engaging experience for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed after the Mad Hatter's tea party from Disney's animated film *Alice in Wonderland*. Guests are immersed in a vibrant and colorful environment, surrounded by oversized teacups and whimsical decorations. The setting evokes a sense of playful chaos and invites guests to participate in the Mad Hatter's eccentric world. The surrounding area features elements from the film, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual teacups, each capable of seating several riders. The ride begins with the teacups rotating on a circular platform. Riders can control the spinning of their individual teacup via a wheel located in the center. The ride cycle lasts approximately 90 seconds, providing a moderate spinning sensation. The attraction utilizes simple mechanical technology to achieve its effects, relying on the visual theming and the spinning motion to create the experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Mad Hatter Tea Cups is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. However, guests prone to motion sickness or with certain health conditions should exercise caution before riding. Pregnant women and individuals with back or neck problems are advised to consider the ride's spinning motion before boarding.
