# Peter Pan's Flight
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Peter Pan's Flight is a dark ride attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park Paris. It is a classic "flying" ride system, manufactured by Arrow Development, that allows guests to experience scenes from Disney's 1953 animated film, *Peter Pan*. The attraction is a popular and enduring fixture of the park, known for its immersive theming and whimsical atmosphere.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the world of Neverland. Guests board "flying" pirate ships and embark on a journey through the Darling children's nursery, soaring over London at night, and encountering Captain Hook, Tinker Bell, and other characters from the film. The ride's theming incorporates detailed set pieces, painted backdrops, and special effects to create a sense of flight and adventure.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue through a themed indoor queue area before boarding the ride vehicles. Each vehicle, resembling a pirate ship, is suspended from an overhead track. The ride begins in the Darling children's nursery, then transports guests through various scenes, including a flight over London, a confrontation with Captain Hook, and a final scene in Neverland. The ride utilizes a combination of practical effects, such as animatronics and set pieces, and projected imagery to enhance the storytelling. The ride duration is approximately 2 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Peter Pan's Flight is generally suitable for all ages. There is no height requirement. Guests with back problems, heart conditions, or those who are pregnant should consider the ride's motion before boarding.
