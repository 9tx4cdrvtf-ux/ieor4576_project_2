# DisneySea Transit Steamer Line (Lost River Delta)
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
The DisneySea Transit Steamer Line (Lost River Delta) is a boat-based transportation attraction within Tokyo DisneySea. It provides guests with a scenic journey across the waterways of the Lost River Delta, connecting the area with other ports within the park. The attraction is a themed boat ride, offering views of the surrounding environments and contributing to the overall immersive experience of the park.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed to resemble a classic riverboat, designed to fit the aesthetic of the Lost River Delta, a land inspired by archaeological expeditions and ancient ruins. The boats are designed to appear aged and weathered, reflecting the adventurous spirit of the area. The narrative suggests the boats are used for transporting explorers and their discoveries through the treacherous waterways. The surrounding environments feature jungle foliage, ancient temples, and the remnants of a lost civilization, enhancing the thematic immersion.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the steamer at a designated dock within the Lost River Delta. The boat travels along a predetermined route, passing through various themed areas. The journey offers views of the surrounding landscapes, including the Indiana Jones Adventure: Temple of the Crystal Skull and the Raging Spirits roller coaster. The ride duration is approximately 8 minutes, providing a relaxing and scenic tour of the area. The boats are powered by electric motors, ensuring a quiet and environmentally friendly operation.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the boats, but may require assistance. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions listed for this attraction.
