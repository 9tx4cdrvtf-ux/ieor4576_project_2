# Incredicoaster
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
The Incredicoaster is a steel launched roller coaster located in Pixar Pier at Disney California Adventure. It is a significant attraction within the park, offering a high-speed thrill ride experience. The coaster was manufactured by Intamin and opened in 2001 as California Screamin', before being re-themed and re-opened in 2018.

## Narrative and Theming
The Incredicoaster's narrative is based on the Disney-Pixar film *The Incredibles*. Guests are immersed in a storyline where the Parr family (Mr. Incredible, Elastigirl, Violet, Dash, and Jack-Jack) are attempting to catch Jack-Jack, who has escaped and is using his various superpowers. The queue and ride experience incorporate elements from the film, including character appearances and themed environments.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with a launch sequence, accelerating the coaster to high speeds. The track features inversions, including a loop, and several high-speed turns. Throughout the ride, guests encounter show scenes featuring the Incredibles characters. The ride incorporates audio and visual effects, including projections and lighting, to enhance the storytelling. The ride duration is approximately 2 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Incredicoaster is designed for guests who enjoy thrill rides. The minimum height requirement is 48 inches (122 cm). Guests with certain medical conditions, including heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised against riding.
