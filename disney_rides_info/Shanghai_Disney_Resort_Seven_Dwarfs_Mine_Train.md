# Seven Dwarfs Mine Train
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
The Seven Dwarfs Mine Train is a family-friendly steel roller coaster located in Fantasyland at Shanghai Disney Resort. Manufactured by Vekoma, the ride is themed after Disney's 1937 animated film, *Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs*. It offers a gentle coaster experience combined with dark ride elements, providing a blend of thrills and storytelling. The attraction is a significant component of Fantasyland, contributing to the immersive environment of the area.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are invited to journey into the diamond mine of the Seven Dwarfs. The queue line incorporates interactive elements and storytelling, setting the stage for the narrative. The ride vehicles, designed to resemble mine cars, traverse through scenes depicting the Dwarfs' daily lives, including their work in the mine and their cottage. The attraction features Audio-Animatronics figures of the Dwarfs and Snow White, enhancing the storytelling experience.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with a slow ascent up a lift hill, followed by a series of drops and turns through the mine. The coaster then enters the mine itself, where riders encounter scenes with the Dwarfs and glittering gemstones. The ride transitions to the cottage, where Snow White is seen with the Dwarfs. The experience concludes with a final descent and a view of the Evil Queen. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Seven Dwarfs Mine Train is designed for a broad audience, particularly families. The minimum height requirement is 97 cm (38 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, including heart problems, back or neck issues, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their health before riding.
