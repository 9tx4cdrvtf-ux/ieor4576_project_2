# Woody’s Roundup
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Woody’s Roundup is a family-friendly dark ride located in the Toy Story Land area of Shanghai Disney Resort. The attraction is a track-based ride system, themed around the fictional television show "Woody's Roundup" from the *Toy Story* franchise. It offers a gentle, immersive experience suitable for a wide range of guests.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are transported to a playful, oversized recreation of a classic Wild West town, as seen in the "Woody's Roundup" television show. The queue area features various props and set pieces, including a vintage television set and character cutouts. The ride vehicles, designed to resemble toy train cars, take guests on a journey through scenes depicting Woody, Jessie, Bullseye, and other characters from the show. The environment is filled with vibrant colors, stylized landscapes, and animated figures.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with guests boarding the train cars. The vehicles move along a predetermined track, passing through a series of themed scenes. Each scene incorporates Audio-Animatronics figures, projected imagery, and special effects to enhance the storytelling. The ride duration is approximately 2 minutes and 30 seconds. The experience is designed to be smooth and gentle, with no sudden drops or intense movements.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Woody’s Roundup is designed for a general audience, particularly families with young children. There is no height requirement for this attraction. Guests with pre-existing health conditions should consult with a medical professional before riding. The ride is accessible to guests with disabilities; however, specific accommodations may be required.
