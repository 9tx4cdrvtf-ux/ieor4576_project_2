# Spider-Man W.E.B. Adventure
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
Spider-Man W.E.B. Adventure is a 3D interactive dark ride located in the Avengers Campus area of Disney Adventure World Paris. The attraction, developed by Walt Disney Imagineering, allows guests to virtually sling webs alongside Spider-Man to capture Spider-Bots that have gone haywire. It is a trackless ride system, utilizing innovative technology to provide a unique and immersive experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is set within the Worldwide Engineering Brigade (W.E.B.) workshop, a facility where Peter Parker and other young inventors are developing new technologies. Guests are recruited to help Spider-Man contain a swarm of rogue Spider-Bots that have escaped and are wreaking havoc. The queue line immerses guests in the W.E.B. workshop environment, showcasing various inventions and providing context for the mission.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board vehicles equipped with interactive web-slinging technology. Throughout the ride, riders use their hands to virtually shoot webs at the Spider-Bots, accumulating points based on their accuracy and speed. The ride features 3D projections, physical sets, and special effects, including lighting and sound, to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 4 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a general audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or motion sickness may experience discomfort. Guests with certain medical conditions should consult with a medical professional before riding.
