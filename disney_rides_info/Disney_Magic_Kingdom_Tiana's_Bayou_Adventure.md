# Tiana's Bayou Adventure
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Tiana's Bayou Adventure is a log flume ride located in Frontierland at the Magic Kingdom. It is a reimagining of the former Splash Mountain attraction, utilizing the same ride system. The attraction is themed after the 2009 Disney animated film *The Princess and the Frog* and features Audio-Animatronics figures, practical effects, and projected media.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride's narrative follows Princess Tiana and Louis the alligator as they prepare for a Mardi Gras celebration. Guests are invited to join Tiana on an adventure through the bayou, encountering various characters and experiencing the sights and sounds of New Orleans. The setting incorporates elements of Louisiana culture, including music, food, and wildlife, creating an immersive environment.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board hollowed-out logs that float through a winding waterway. The ride features several scenes with Audio-Animatronics figures, including Tiana, Louis, and other characters from the film. The journey culminates in a large drop down a waterfall, providing a splash effect. The ride incorporates music from the film, as well as new compositions. The total ride duration is approximately 10 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Tiana's Bayou Adventure is designed for a general audience. Guests must be at least 40 inches (102 cm) tall to ride. Guests with certain health conditions, including heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider the ride's intensity before boarding.
