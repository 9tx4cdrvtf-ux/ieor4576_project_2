# Frozen Ever After
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Frozen Ever After is a boat ride attraction located in the Norway Pavilion at Epcot's World Showcase. It replaced the Maelstrom ride in 2016. The attraction is manufactured by Intamin and features Audio-Animatronics, projection mapping, and other special effects to immerse guests in the world of Disney's *Frozen*. It is a significant attraction for families and contributes to the overall popularity of the Norway Pavilion.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride's narrative centers around the "Summer Snow Day" celebration in Arendelle, hosted by Queen Elsa and Princess Anna. Guests are invited to visit Arendelle during this festive occasion. The attraction's theming incorporates elements from both *Frozen* and *Frozen II*, including the kingdom of Arendelle, the North Mountain, and the characters' homes. The setting is designed to evoke the atmosphere of the films, with detailed set pieces and immersive environments.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board boats that travel through various scenes. The ride features Audio-Animatronic figures of Anna, Elsa, Kristoff, Olaf, and other characters. The boats navigate through indoor and outdoor sections, including a backward descent down a small drop. Projection mapping is used to create visual effects, such as Elsa's ice magic. The ride lasts approximately 5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Frozen Ever After is designed for a family audience. The height requirement is 40 inches (102 cm). Guests with a sensitivity to flashing lights or those with certain health conditions should consider the ride's intensity before boarding.
