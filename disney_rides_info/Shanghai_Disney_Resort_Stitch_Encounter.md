# Stitch Encounter
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Stitch Encounter is a live interactive show attraction located in Tomorrowland at Shanghai Disney Resort. It features a real-time, animated conversation with the character Stitch from the *Lilo & Stitch* franchise. The attraction utilizes advanced audio and visual technology to create the illusion of Stitch interacting directly with the audience.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests enter a futuristic observation station, themed as a spaceport. The premise involves a live video feed of Stitch, who is being held in a containment cell. The show's narrative centers around Stitch's mischievous antics and his attempts to interact with the audience. The setting is designed to immerse guests in a space-age environment, complete with control panels and futuristic lighting.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are seated in a theater-style setting and watch a large screen. Through the use of sophisticated animation and puppetry techniques, Stitch appears on the screen and engages in a live conversation with the audience. A cast member, unseen by the audience, provides Stitch's voice and controls his movements, reacting to audience participation. The show incorporates humor, visual gags, and personalized interactions, with each performance being unique. The show duration is approximately 15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Stitch Encounter is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction is generally accessible to guests with disabilities, though specific accommodations may vary. The show utilizes loud noises and flashing lights, which may be a concern for individuals sensitive to these stimuli.
