# DisneySea Electric Railway (Port Discovery)
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
The DisneySea Electric Railway (Port Discovery) is a transportation attraction located within the Port Discovery area of Tokyo DisneySea. It is a dual-track elevated railway system that provides guests with scenic views of the park. The attraction serves as a convenient method of travel between Port Discovery and American Waterfront.

## Narrative and Theming
The Electric Railway is themed as a modern transportation system, seamlessly integrated into the futuristic and aquatic-themed Port Discovery. The railway cars are designed to resemble sleek, streamlined electric trains, reflecting the area's emphasis on technological advancement and exploration. The route offers glimpses of the various themed lands, including the underwater world of *20,000 Leagues Under the Sea* and the bustling harbor of American Waterfront.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the open-air railway cars at either the Port Discovery or American Waterfront stations. The train travels along an elevated track, offering panoramic views of the park. The journey lasts approximately 5 minutes, providing a comfortable and efficient way to traverse between the two themed lands. The ride is smooth and quiet, utilizing electric propulsion.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The DisneySea Electric Railway (Port Discovery) is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the attraction, but may require assistance. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
