# Zootopia Hot Pursuit Single Rider
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Zootopia Hot Pursuit Single Rider is a trackless dark ride located within the Zootopia-themed land at Shanghai Disney Resort. The attraction, developed by Walt Disney Imagineering, utilizes a trackless ride system to transport guests through a series of scenes based on the animated film *Zootopia*. It offers a single rider queue to expedite wait times.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests become recruits of the Zootopia Police Department (ZPD), joining Judy Hopps and Nick Wilde in a high-speed chase to apprehend a criminal. The ride immerses guests in the vibrant city of Zootopia, featuring environments such as Sahara Square, Tundratown, and the Rainforest District. The attraction incorporates detailed set design, animatronics, and projection mapping to create a believable and engaging environment.

## Attraction Experience
The ride vehicles, designed to resemble police cruisers, accommodate several riders. The trackless system allows for fluid movement through the scenes. The ride features animatronic characters, including Judy Hopps and Nick Wilde, interacting with the vehicles. Projection mapping and special effects, such as water sprays and wind gusts, enhance the sensory experience. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Zootopia Hot Pursuit Single Rider is designed for a general audience. Guests must meet a minimum height requirement of 100 cm (39 inches) to ride. Guests with certain medical conditions, including heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their health before riding.
