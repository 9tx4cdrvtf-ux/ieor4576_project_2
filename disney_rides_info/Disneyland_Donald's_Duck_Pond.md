# Donald's Duck Pond
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Donald's Duck Pond was a gentle, interactive water play area located in Mickey's Toontown at Disneyland Park. It provided a space for young children to engage in water-based activities. The attraction was designed to be a safe and accessible environment for toddlers and young children, allowing them to interact with themed water features.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction was themed around Donald Duck's backyard, featuring a whimsical environment with oversized flowers, cartoonish landscaping, and playful water elements. The setting was designed to immerse guests in the world of Donald Duck, with visual cues and sound effects contributing to the overall atmosphere. The area was intended to be a place of fun and relaxation, reflecting the lighthearted nature of the character.

## Attraction Experience
Guests could interact with various water features, including fountains, sprayers, and small water jets. The area was designed to allow children to splash and play in a controlled environment. The attraction did not involve any ride vehicles or complex mechanisms. The duration of a visit was self-determined, based on the guest's preference.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Donald's Duck Pond was primarily targeted towards toddlers and young children. There were no height restrictions. Guests were advised to wear appropriate swimwear or clothing that could get wet. Parents were expected to supervise their children at all times. There were no specific health warnings or restrictions, but guests with sensitivities to water or chlorine were advised to take precautions.
