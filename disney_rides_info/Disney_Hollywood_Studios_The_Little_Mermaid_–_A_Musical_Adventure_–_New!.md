# The Little Mermaid – A Musical Adventure – New!
**Location:** Disney Hollywood Studios

## Overview
"The Little Mermaid – A Musical Adventure – New!" is a dark ride attraction located in the Animation Courtyard area of Disney's Hollywood Studios. It is a reimagining of the classic dark ride concept, immersing guests in the story of Disney's animated film, *The Little Mermaid*. The attraction utilizes a combination of Audio-Animatronics, projected animation, and practical sets to tell the story.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are transported under the sea to experience Ariel's journey. The attraction follows the film's narrative, beginning with Ariel's fascination with the human world and culminating in her triumph over Ursula. The environments are designed to evoke the underwater kingdom of Atlantica and the surface world, with vibrant colors and detailed set pieces.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board clamshell ride vehicles that travel along a track. The ride progresses through various scenes, featuring Audio-Animatronic figures of Ariel, Prince Eric, Ursula, and other characters. The scenes incorporate projected animation, lighting effects, and musical sequences from the film. The ride duration is approximately 6 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a general audience. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or other health concerns should consult with a cast member before riding.
