# Big Grizzly Mountain Runaway Mine Cars
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Big Grizzly Mountain Runaway Mine Cars is a steel roller coaster located in the Grizzly Gulch area of Disneyland Hong Kong. The attraction is a unique hybrid of a traditional roller coaster and a mine train ride, featuring a backwards launch and multiple launches throughout the course. It was designed and manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering and Vekoma. The ride is a significant attraction within the park, offering a thrilling experience with a distinctive narrative and immersive theming.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride's narrative centers around a gold mining town, Grizzly Gulch, during the California Gold Rush era. Guests are transported on a runaway mine train through the mountain, encountering various animatronic bears and other thematic elements. The story involves a series of unexpected events, including the mine cars being launched forward and backward due to the mischievous actions of the grizzly bears. The setting is designed to evoke the atmosphere of a rugged, untamed wilderness.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board mine cars that traverse a winding track through a mountainous landscape. The ride incorporates a unique backwards launch, followed by multiple forward launches, creating a dynamic and unpredictable experience. The ride features a variety of special effects, including simulated rock slides, sudden drops, and animatronic figures. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of various ages, provided they meet the height requirement of 112 cm (44 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, including heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consult with a medical professional before riding.
