# Soarin' Around the World
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Soarin' Around the World is a motion simulator ride located in The Land Pavilion at Epcot. The attraction utilizes a unique "flying theater" system, manufactured by Dynamic Attractions, to simulate the sensation of flight over various global landmarks. It is a popular attraction, known for its immersive experience and panoramic views.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are presented with the premise of a hang-gliding flight simulator experience. The queue area is themed as an airport, with pre-flight safety videos and a simulated control tower. The narrative centers around a journey across the globe, showcasing natural wonders and iconic locations. The ride's theming emphasizes the beauty of the planet and the thrill of flight.

## Attraction Experience
Riders are seated in rows of suspended seats that rise in front of a massive, curved screen. The seats tilt forward, enhancing the sensation of flight. The film, projected onto the screen, depicts various locations, including the Eiffel Tower, the Great Wall of China, and the Taj Mahal. Sensory effects, such as wind, scents, and subtle movements, are synchronized with the film to create a multi-sensory experience. The ride lasts approximately five minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Soarin' Around the World is generally suitable for all ages, though children must meet the minimum height requirement of 40 inches (102 cm). Guests with a fear of heights or motion sickness may experience discomfort. Pregnant women and individuals with certain health conditions are advised to consult with a medical professional before riding.
