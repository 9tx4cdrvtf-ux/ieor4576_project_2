# Great Moments with Mr. Lincoln
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Great Moments with Mr. Lincoln is a theatrical Audio-Animatronics presentation located in Disneyland's Main Street, U.S.A. The attraction features a lifelike Audio-Animatronic figure of Abraham Lincoln delivering excerpts from his speeches. Originally created by Walt Disney and his Imagineers, the show has undergone several iterations since its debut at the 1964 New York World's Fair. It is a significant historical and technological landmark within the park.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents a patriotic and educational experience centered on the life and legacy of Abraham Lincoln. Guests enter a theater designed to evoke a sense of historical grandeur. The pre-show area typically features historical artifacts and displays related to Lincoln's life and the Civil War. The main show focuses on Lincoln's words and ideals, emphasizing themes of unity, freedom, and democracy.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are seated in a theater and watch a stage presentation. The Audio-Animatronic Lincoln rises from his chair and delivers a speech, accompanied by a musical score and projected imagery. The figure's movements and facial expressions are synchronized with the pre-recorded audio. The show typically lasts approximately 15 minutes, including the pre-show and the main presentation.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. The show is presented in a seated theater environment and does not involve any physical exertion. Guests with sensitivity to loud noises or flashing lights should be aware that these elements are present during the performance.
