# Luigi's Rollickin' Roadsters
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Luigi's Rollickin' Roadsters is a trackless, whip-style ride located in Cars Land at Disney California Adventure. The attraction, manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering, features a fleet of Italian roadsters that perform choreographed dances on a large, open-air platform. It replaced the previous attraction, Luigi's Flying Tires, in 2016.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around Luigi's Casa Della Tires, the tire shop owned by Luigi from the *Cars* film franchise. Guests are invited to participate in a "cousin's day" celebration, where Luigi and his family showcase their roadsters' unique dance moves. The setting is a festive Italian courtyard, complete with colorful buildings, string lights, and Italian music.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of the eight roadsters, each with a unique personality and design. The vehicles move in a trackless manner, performing a series of synchronized dances across the ride platform. The roadsters spin, slide, and perform various maneuvers, changing direction unpredictably. The ride experience lasts approximately 1.5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Luigi's Rollickin' Roadsters is designed for a general audience. There are no height restrictions. Guests with motion sickness or other health concerns should consider the ride's spinning and unpredictable movements before boarding.
