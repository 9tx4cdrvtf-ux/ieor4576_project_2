# Meet Disney Princesses at Royal Hall
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Meet Disney Princesses at Royal Hall is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park. It allows guests to interact with various Disney Princesses, pose for photographs, and obtain autographs. The attraction is a permanent fixture, providing a classic Disney experience focused on character interaction.

## Narrative and Theming
The Royal Hall is themed as a regal ballroom, designed to evoke a sense of elegance and enchantment. The interior features ornate decorations, including tapestries, chandeliers, and portraits of the Disney Princesses. The setting is intended to transport guests into a fairytale environment, creating a suitable backdrop for interactions with the princesses. The queue area often incorporates themed elements related to the specific princesses scheduled to appear.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area, often with interactive elements to entertain them while waiting. Upon reaching the front of the line, guests are escorted into the main hall where one or more Disney Princesses are present. Guests have the opportunity to speak with the princesses, take photographs, and receive autographs. The experience typically lasts several minutes per guest or group, depending on the number of guests waiting. The specific princesses available vary throughout the day and are subject to change.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may require assistance. Strollers are permitted in the queue area but may need to be parked before entering the main hall. No specific health warnings or restrictions are in place, but guests should be aware of potential wait times, especially during peak seasons.
