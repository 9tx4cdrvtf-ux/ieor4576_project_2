# Les Tapis Volants - Flying Carpets Over Agrabah®
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
Les Tapis Volants - Flying Carpets Over Agrabah® is a family-friendly, spinner-type amusement ride located in the Adventureland area of Disney Adventure World Paris. The attraction allows guests to experience the sensation of flight aboard individual carpet vehicles. It is a variation of the "Magic Carpets of Aladdin" ride found in other Disney parks, manufactured by Zamperla. The ride is themed after the Disney animated film *Aladdin* and offers views of the surrounding Agrabah-themed environment.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is situated within a recreation of the fictional city of Agrabah, inspired by the film *Aladdin*. Guests queue through a marketplace setting, encountering themed details such as market stalls and architectural elements reminiscent of the film's setting. The ride vehicles are designed to resemble flying carpets, and the surrounding environment includes props and decorations that enhance the immersive experience, contributing to the overall narrative of adventure and fantasy.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual carpet-shaped vehicles, each accommodating a small number of riders. The carpets are mounted on a central rotating mechanism. During the ride, guests can control the vertical movement of their carpet, allowing them to raise or lower it. The ride cycle lasts approximately 90 seconds, providing views of the surrounding area, including the nearby *Indiana Jones et le Temple du Péril* roller coaster.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Les Tapis Volants - Flying Carpets Over Agrabah® is designed for a broad audience, particularly families with young children. There is a minimum height requirement, typically around 90 cm (35 inches). Guests with certain medical conditions, such as heart problems or back issues, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their health before riding.
