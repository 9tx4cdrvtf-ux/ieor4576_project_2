# Camp Discovery
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Camp Discovery is an adventure-themed attraction located in Adventure Isle at Shanghai Disney Resort. It offers guests a series of elevated trails and climbing challenges, allowing them to explore the fictional landscape of Adventure Isle. The attraction is a unique blend of a ropes course and a themed walk-through experience, providing physical activity and immersive storytelling.

## Narrative and Theming
The narrative centers around the fictional "League of Adventurers," who have established a research camp within Adventure Isle. Guests are cast as explorers, tasked with navigating the challenging terrain to discover the secrets of the island. The area is themed to resemble a rugged, natural environment, complete with rock formations, waterfalls, and lush vegetation. The setting evokes a sense of exploration and discovery, with thematic elements integrated throughout the course.

## Attraction Experience
Guests choose from three distinct trails: the Challenge Trails, the Legend of the Forbidden Mountain, and the Canyon of the Ancients. Each trail presents a different level of difficulty, incorporating rope bridges, suspension walkways, and climbing walls. The trails utilize a continuous belay system for safety. The experience duration varies depending on the chosen trail, ranging from approximately 15 to 20 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Camp Discovery is designed for guests of varying ages and physical abilities, though a certain level of fitness is required. Height restrictions apply, with a minimum height requirement of 107 cm (42 inches) to participate. Guests with certain medical conditions, including heart problems, back or neck issues, or those who are pregnant, are advised against riding. Guests must also be able to fit safely within the provided safety harnesses.
