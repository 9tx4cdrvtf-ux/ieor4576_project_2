# Meet Mickey Mouse
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Meet Mickey Mouse is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in the Fantasyland area of Disneyland Park Paris. It allows guests to interact with the iconic Disney character, Mickey Mouse, in a themed setting. The attraction provides a classic photo opportunity and is a staple experience for visitors of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as Mickey Mouse's backstage dressing room and theatre. Guests enter a whimsical space filled with props, posters, and memorabilia related to Mickey's career as a performer. The environment suggests a behind-the-scenes look at the preparations for a show, creating an immersive experience that enhances the anticipation of meeting Mickey.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue through a themed waiting area before entering the main meet-and-greet space. Mickey Mouse is positioned in a designated area, allowing for individual or group photographs. The interaction typically lasts for a few minutes, providing time for guests to take pictures and briefly interact with the character. The duration of the experience, including the queue, varies depending on park attendance.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Meet Mickey Mouse is designed for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. However, guests with mobility issues may find the queue and meet-and-greet area challenging due to potential crowds.
