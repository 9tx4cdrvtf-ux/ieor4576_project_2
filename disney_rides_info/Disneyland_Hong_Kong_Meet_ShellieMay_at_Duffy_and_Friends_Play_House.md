# Meet ShellieMay at Duffy and Friends Play House
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Meet ShellieMay at Duffy and Friends Play House is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in the World of Disney area of Disneyland Hong Kong. It allows guests to interact with ShellieMay, a plush teddy bear character and friend of Duffy the Disney Bear. The attraction provides a dedicated space for photo opportunities and brief interactions with the character.

## Narrative and Theming
The Play House is themed as a cozy and inviting space, designed to resemble a comfortable living area. The setting is consistent with the Duffy and Friends storyline, which centers around Duffy's adventures and friendships. The environment is decorated with soft colors, plush furnishings, and nautical elements, reflecting Duffy's origins as a teddy bear created by Minnie Mouse for Mickey Mouse.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area before entering the Play House. Upon entering, guests are greeted by ShellieMay. Guests are given the opportunity to take photos with ShellieMay and briefly interact with the character. The interaction typically lasts for a few minutes, allowing for a personalized experience. The attraction utilizes standard meet-and-greet procedures, with Disney Cast Members managing the flow of guests and ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is primarily targeted towards families and children. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. Guests of all ages are welcome to meet ShellieMay.
