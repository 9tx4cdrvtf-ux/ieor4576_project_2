# Zootopia: Hot Pursuit
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Zootopia: Hot Pursuit is a trackless dark ride located in the Zootopia-themed land at Shanghai Disney Resort. The attraction, developed by Walt Disney Imagineering, utilizes a unique ride system to immerse guests in a high-speed chase through the city of Zootopia. It is a significant component of the park's expansion, bringing the popular animated film to life in a highly detailed and interactive experience.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests become rookie police recruits in the Zootopia Police Department (ZPD). They are tasked with assisting Judy Hopps and Nick Wilde in pursuing a criminal through various districts of Zootopia. The ride's environment meticulously recreates the diverse environments of the film, including Sahara Square, Tundratown, and the Rainforest District, with detailed set design and animatronics. The narrative unfolds through projected media, audio cues, and physical effects, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
The ride vehicles, designed to resemble ZPD cruisers, move along a trackless path, allowing for dynamic movements and interactions with the environment. The ride incorporates a combination of physical sets, large-scale projections, and animatronic figures. Guests experience simulated speeds and direction changes as they navigate the chase. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes, featuring multiple scenes and climactic moments.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Zootopia: Hot Pursuit is designed for a broad audience. Guests must meet a minimum height requirement of 100 cm (39 inches) to ride. Guests with pre-existing health conditions, including heart problems, back or neck issues, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their condition before riding. The ride involves sudden movements and simulated high speeds.
