# The Whirlpool
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
The Whirlpool is a spinning teacup ride located in the Port Discovery area of Tokyo DisneySea. It is a variation of the classic Mad Tea Party attraction, manufactured by Zamperla. The ride offers a whimsical, spinning experience themed around marine life and the ocean's currents. It is a family-friendly attraction designed to provide a gentle, yet engaging, experience for guests of various ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers on a playful interpretation of ocean currents and marine life. Guests board individual teacups designed to resemble various sea creatures, such as jellyfish and seahorses. The surrounding environment is themed to evoke a vibrant underwater world, with colorful murals, stylized coral formations, and ambient sound effects that mimic the sounds of the ocean. The overall aesthetic is intended to create a sense of immersion in a fantastical aquatic environment.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of several spinning teacups. Each teacup is mounted on a rotating platform. Riders can control the speed of their individual teacup's spinning action via a central wheel. The ride cycle lasts approximately 90 seconds, during which the platform rotates while the teacups also spin. The attraction utilizes basic mechanical engineering and lighting effects to enhance the visual experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Whirlpool is designed for a general audience, including children and adults. There is no height requirement. Guests with a sensitivity to motion sickness or other health conditions that may be aggravated by spinning are advised to consider their condition before riding. Expectant mothers are also advised to exercise caution.
