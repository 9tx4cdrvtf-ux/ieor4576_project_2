# Star Tours - The Adventures Continue
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Star Tours – The Adventures Continue is a 3D motion simulator ride located in Tomorrowland at Disneyland. It is a reimagining of the original Star Tours attraction, incorporating updated technology and a randomized storyline featuring characters and locations from the Star Wars franchise. The ride was developed by Walt Disney Imagineering and utilizes a motion simulator system to create the sensation of space travel. It is a significant attraction within Tomorrowland, offering a popular immersive experience for guests.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are presented as passengers on a Starspeeder 1000 series space transport, en route to various destinations across the Star Wars galaxy. The queue area is themed as a spaceport, with droids and other Star Wars characters interacting with guests. The narrative is established through pre-show videos and announcements, setting the stage for a chaotic and unpredictable journey. The ride's storyline is randomized, ensuring a different experience with each ride, featuring iconic characters such as C-3PO and R2-D2.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a Starspeeder 1000 vehicle, which seats approximately 40 passengers. The ride utilizes a motion simulator combined with a 3D video projection system. The experience lasts approximately 4 minutes, during which the Starspeeder encounters various perils, including hyperspace jumps, asteroid fields, and encounters with Imperial forces. Sensory effects include motion, wind, and simulated impacts, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is generally suitable for all ages, though it is recommended for guests who are at least 40 inches (102 cm) tall. Guests with a history of motion sickness, heart conditions, back or neck problems, or other health concerns should consider the ride's intensity before boarding. Expectant mothers are advised against riding.
