# The Little Mermaid - Ariel's Undersea Adventure
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
The Little Mermaid - Ariel's Undersea Adventure is a dark ride attraction located in the Paradise Gardens Park area of Disney California Adventure. It is a slow-moving "clam shell" ride system manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering, based on the 1989 Disney animated film *The Little Mermaid*. The attraction opened in 2011 and replaced the former Golden Zephyr attraction. It provides a family-friendly experience, immersing guests in scenes from the film.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative follows the story of Ariel, a mermaid princess who dreams of becoming human. Guests are transported through key scenes from the film, including Ariel's grotto, the underwater kingdom of Atlantica, and the climactic battle with Ursula. The ride environment utilizes vibrant colors, detailed set pieces, and animatronic figures to create an immersive underwater atmosphere. The queue area features themed elements, including a statue of Ariel and various nautical props.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board two-person clamshell vehicles that travel along a track through various show scenes. The ride incorporates Audio-Animatronics, projections, and special effects, such as bubbles and lighting, to enhance the storytelling. The ride duration is approximately 6 minutes. The journey begins with Ariel's longing for the human world and progresses through musical sequences featuring songs from the film.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a general audience, including families with young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitive epilepsy or other conditions sensitive to flashing lights should be aware of the special effects used.
