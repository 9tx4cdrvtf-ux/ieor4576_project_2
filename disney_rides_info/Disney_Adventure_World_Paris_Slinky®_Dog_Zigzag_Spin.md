# Slinky® Dog Zigzag Spin
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
Slinky® Dog Zigzag Spin is a spinning amusement ride located in Toy Story Playland at Disney Adventure World Paris. The attraction is a Zamperla Disk'O Coaster, offering a family-friendly experience. It is designed to simulate the sensation of being on Slinky Dog's back as he chases his tail. The ride is a significant component of Toy Story Playland, contributing to the immersive theming of the area.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the world of *Toy Story*, shrunk to the size of a toy. The ride is themed around Slinky Dog, the beloved character from the *Toy Story* franchise. Riders are seated on Slinky Dog's back as he attempts to catch his tail. The surrounding environment is designed to resemble a child's backyard, with oversized toys and playful elements.

## Attraction Experience
The ride consists of a circular platform that spins and slides along a curved track. Guests are seated in outward-facing seats. The platform accelerates, reaching a moderate speed, while simultaneously rotating. The ride lasts approximately 2 minutes. The experience provides a combination of spinning and sliding motions, offering a mild thrill.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Slinky® Dog Zigzag Spin is designed for families and children. The minimum height requirement is 81 cm (32 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, particularly those susceptible to motion sickness, or with back or neck problems, are advised to consider their condition before riding. Expectant mothers are also advised against riding.
