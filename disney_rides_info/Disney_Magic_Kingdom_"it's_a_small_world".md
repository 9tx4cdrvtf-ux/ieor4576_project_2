# "it's a small world"
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
"it's a small world" is a slow-moving boat ride attraction located in the Fantasyland section of Disney Magic Kingdom. Designed by WED Enterprises (now Walt Disney Imagineering), the attraction is a classic "dark ride" featuring hundreds of Audio-Animatronic dolls representing children from various nations around the world. It is renowned for its iconic theme song and its message of global unity and peace.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents a stylized, idealized vision of global harmony. Guests embark on a boat journey through a series of interconnected rooms, each representing a different region of the world. The environments are filled with brightly colored sets and Audio-Animatronic dolls dressed in traditional costumes, singing the attraction's theme song, "It's a Small World (After All)." The ride's design emphasizes a sense of childlike wonder and optimism.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a boat that gently glides along a waterway. The boat travels through a series of themed rooms, each showcasing a different continent or region. The Audio-Animatronic dolls perform choreographed movements and sing the attraction's theme song in multiple languages. The ride incorporates various sensory effects, including lighting, sound, and visual projections. The entire experience lasts approximately 10-12 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
"it's a small world" is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity may be affected by the lighting effects. The ride is a slow-moving boat ride and is accessible to guests with mobility impairments, though they may need to transfer from a wheelchair.
