# Snow White's Enchanted Wish
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Snow White's Enchanted Wish is a dark ride attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland. It is based on Disney's 1937 animated film, *Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs*. The ride takes guests through key scenes from the film, utilizing a track-based ride system and various animated figures and special effects. It has undergone several renovations throughout its history, with the most recent completed in 2021, enhancing its visual quality and narrative depth.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the story of Snow White, a princess pursued by her jealous stepmother, the Evil Queen. The ride follows Snow White's journey, from her escape into the forest to her encounter with the Seven Dwarfs, her interaction with the Evil Queen disguised as an old hag, and finally, the Prince's kiss that breaks the poisoned apple's spell. The ride's theming includes detailed sets, animated figures, and atmospheric lighting to create a sense of immersion within the film's narrative.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a mine cart-shaped ride vehicle that travels along a predetermined track. The ride progresses through a series of scenes, each depicting a significant moment from the film. These scenes feature animated figures of Snow White, the Seven Dwarfs, the Evil Queen, and the Prince. Special effects include lighting, sound, and projections to enhance the storytelling. The ride duration is approximately 2 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Snow White's Enchanted Wish is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. However, the ride may contain some frightening elements, including depictions of the Evil Queen and the poisoned apple. Guests with a sensitivity to dark environments or sudden movements should consider their personal comfort level before riding.
