# Casey Jr. – le Petit Train du Cirque
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Casey Jr. – le Petit Train du Cirque is a gentle, outdoor train ride attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park Paris. It is a scaled-down replica of the train featured in the 1941 Disney animated film *Dumbo*. The attraction provides a scenic tour of Fantasyland, offering views of several other attractions and themed areas. The train was manufactured by Intamin.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the world of *Dumbo*, specifically the circus setting. The train itself is designed to resemble Casey Jr., the train that carries the circus performers and equipment. The surrounding environment includes miniature versions of iconic scenes and structures from the film, such as the circus tents and various animal figures. The overall theming evokes a whimsical and nostalgic atmosphere.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board open-air carriages that are pulled by the Casey Jr. locomotive. The train travels along an elevated track, providing a leisurely tour of Fantasyland. The ride passes through various themed areas, including a tunnel and offers views of other attractions like *Le Pays des Contes de Fées* (Storybook Land Canal Boats). The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Casey Jr. – le Petit Train du Cirque is designed for a general audience, including young children and families. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find it difficult to board the train. The attraction is generally considered safe for all guests, but those with a sensitivity to slow-moving rides should take caution.
