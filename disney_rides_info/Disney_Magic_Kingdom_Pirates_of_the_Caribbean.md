# Pirates of the Caribbean
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Pirates of the Caribbean is a dark ride attraction located in Adventureland at Disney Magic Kingdom. It is a boat ride that transports guests through a series of scenes depicting a pirate raid on a Caribbean town. The attraction is notable for its innovative use of Audio-Animatronics and immersive storytelling, and is a classic Disney attraction.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride's narrative centers on the Golden Age of Piracy. Guests are immersed in a world of swashbuckling pirates, treasure, and conflict. The setting is a fictional Caribbean port town under siege by a band of marauding pirates. Scenes depict the pirates' plunder, including the burning of the town, the pursuit of women, and the search for treasure. The attraction features a blend of humor and adventure, with a focus on the pirates' chaotic and often comical behavior.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board boats that navigate through a series of indoor scenes. The ride begins with a descent into a cavern, followed by scenes depicting a pirate ship battle and the raid on the town. Audio-Animatronic figures bring the pirates and townspeople to life. The ride incorporates special effects such as water, fire, and lighting to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 8.5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Pirates of the Caribbean is generally suitable for all ages. There is no height requirement. Guests with a history of heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant should consider the ride's potential for sudden drops and dark environments before riding.
