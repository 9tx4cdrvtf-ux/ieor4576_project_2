# Peter Pan's Flight
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Peter Pan's Flight is a dark ride attraction located in Fantasyland at the Magic Kingdom. It is a classic "flying" ride system, manufactured by Arrow Development, that has been a staple of Disney theme parks since its debut. The attraction allows guests to experience scenes from Disney's 1953 animated film, *Peter Pan*, by simulating flight over London and Neverland. It is one of the most popular attractions in the park, often experiencing long wait times.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the world of Peter Pan. The queue area features elements of the Darling children's nursery, setting the scene for the adventure. Guests board "flying" pirate ships, which transport them through various scenes from the film. These scenes depict iconic moments such as Peter Pan and Wendy's flight over London, the encounter with Captain Hook and his crew, and the Lost Boys' hideout in Neverland. The ride utilizes black light effects and detailed set pieces to create an immersive environment.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board suspended ride vehicles designed to resemble pirate ships. The ride vehicles travel along a track, moving through various scenes. The "flying" effect is achieved by suspending the vehicles from an overhead track, allowing them to glide through the scenes. The ride incorporates special effects, including projections, lighting, and audio-animatronics, to enhance the storytelling. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Peter Pan's Flight is generally suitable for all ages. There is no height requirement. Guests with a fear of heights or enclosed spaces may experience discomfort. The attraction is a gentle ride and does not contain any intense physical elements.
