# Prince Charming Regal Carrousel
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Prince Charming Regal Carrousel is a classic carousel attraction located in Fantasyland at the Magic Kingdom. It is a traditional, stationary carousel featuring 90 intricately hand-painted horses. The attraction is a significant element of the park's Fantasyland, offering a timeless and gentle ride experience for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed after the classic fairy tale of Cinderella and Prince Charming. The carousel's setting evokes a royal atmosphere, with a grand, ornate canopy and elegant detailing. The horses are adorned with regal saddles and bridles, and the entire structure is designed to resemble a royal stable. The music played during the ride further enhances the fairytale ambiance, featuring classic Disney melodies.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a covered area before boarding one of the carousel's horses. The ride cycle lasts approximately two minutes. The carousel rotates in a circular motion, with the horses moving up and down in a traditional carousel fashion. The ride is accompanied by a selection of Disney-themed music. The attraction utilizes traditional mechanical systems for its operation.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Prince Charming Regal Carrousel is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, should consider the gentle nature of the ride before boarding.
