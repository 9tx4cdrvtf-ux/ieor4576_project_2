# Fairy Tinker Bell's Busy Buggies
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Fairy Tinker Bell's Busy Buggies is a trackless dark ride located within the Lost River Delta area of Tokyo DisneySea. The attraction is a family-friendly experience designed to immerse guests in the world of Tinker Bell and her fairy friends. It is a unique attraction within the park, offering a gentle ride suitable for a wide range of ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers around Tinker Bell and her fellow fairies as they prepare for a special event. Guests are invited to assist the fairies in collecting various items needed for the celebration. The queue area is themed as a whimsical workshop, filled with oversized tools, glowing flora, and other details that evoke the miniature world of the fairies. The ride vehicles are designed to resemble small, whimsical buggies, further enhancing the sense of scale and immersion.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the trackless buggies, which navigate through a series of scenes depicting the fairies' preparations. The ride utilizes a combination of physical sets, animatronics, and projected imagery to create the environments. The buggies move smoothly and silently, allowing guests to focus on the visual storytelling. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Fairy Tinker Bell's Busy Buggies is designed for a general audience, including young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or other health concerns should consult with a Cast Member before riding.
