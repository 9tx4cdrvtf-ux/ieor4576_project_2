# Fantasia Carousel
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Fantasia Carousel is a classic carousel attraction located in the Gardens of Imagination at Shanghai Disney Resort. It features 62 exquisitely crafted horses and two chariots, themed after the iconic Disney animated film *Fantasia*. The carousel is a central feature of the Gardens of Imagination, offering a traditional amusement park experience within a themed environment.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the whimsical world of *Fantasia*. The carousel's design incorporates imagery from the film, including characters and scenes. The horses are adorned with ornate details and vibrant colors, reflecting the film's visual style. The central canopy is decorated with celestial motifs and scenes from the film, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of the horses or chariots. The carousel rotates in a circular motion, providing a gentle and enjoyable ride. The horses move up and down in a traditional carousel fashion. The ride cycle lasts approximately two minutes. Music from *Fantasia* plays throughout the ride, further enhancing the themed experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Fantasia Carousel is suitable for guests of all ages. There is no height requirement to ride. Guests with certain medical conditions or those prone to motion sickness should exercise caution. Guests are advised to remain seated and hold onto the safety bars during the ride.
