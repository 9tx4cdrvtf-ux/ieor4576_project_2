# Cars Quatre Roues Rallye
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
Cars Quatre Roues Rallye is a spinning ride attraction located in the Cars-themed area of Disney Adventure World Paris, specifically in the Cars Route 66 area. The attraction is manufactured by Zamperla and is based on the popular Disney-Pixar film *Cars*. It provides a family-friendly experience, allowing guests to experience the sensation of a car rally within the fictional town of Radiator Springs.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the world of *Cars*, placing them in the midst of a rally race. The queue line is themed to resemble a garage and pit stop area, complete with props and details inspired by the film. The ride vehicles are designed to resemble the cars from the movie, further enhancing the immersive experience. The surrounding environment features themed buildings, landscaping, and music from the *Cars* soundtrack, creating a cohesive and engaging atmosphere.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of eight individual car-shaped vehicles. The ride begins with the vehicles rotating around a central hub. As the ride progresses, the vehicles also move in a circular motion, creating a spinning effect. The ride lasts approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds. The attraction utilizes a simple but effective mechanical system to provide the spinning motion, offering a gentle and enjoyable experience for riders of all ages.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Cars Quatre Roues Rallye is designed for a general audience, particularly families with young children. There is no height requirement. Guests with a sensitivity to motion sickness should consider the spinning nature of the ride before boarding. Pregnant women and individuals with certain medical conditions should consult with a medical professional before riding.
