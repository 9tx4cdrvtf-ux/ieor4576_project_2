# Wandering Oaken’s Sliding Sleighs
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Wandering Oaken’s Sliding Sleighs is a steel roller coaster located in the World of Frozen at Disneyland Hong Kong. The family-friendly coaster, manufactured by Vekoma, offers a moderate thrill experience suitable for a wide range of guests. It is themed after the popular Disney animated film *Frozen* and features a unique launch system.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the world of Arendelle, specifically the trading post of Wandering Oaken's Trading Post and Sauna. The ride's narrative centers around Oaken's desire to deliver sleighs to the village. Guests board sleighs designed to resemble those used in the film, embarking on a journey through a snowy landscape. The queue area is themed to resemble the interior and exterior of Oaken's Trading Post, complete with details from the film.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual sleighs that are pulled up a small lift hill before being launched. The coaster then navigates a winding track, incorporating several turns and small drops, simulating a sleigh ride down a snowy mountain. The ride utilizes a combination of physical sets, animatronics, and projected imagery to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately one minute.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Wandering Oaken’s Sliding Sleighs is designed for a family audience. The minimum height requirement is 95 cm (37 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, particularly those affecting the heart, back, or neck, are advised to consult with a medical professional before riding. Expectant mothers are also advised against riding.
