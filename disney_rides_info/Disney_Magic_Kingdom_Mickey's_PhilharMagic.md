# Mickey's PhilharMagic
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Mickey's PhilharMagic is a 4D film attraction located in Fantasyland at the Magic Kingdom. It is a musical show featuring a 150-seat theater and utilizes a combination of 3D film projection, in-theater effects, and Audio-Animatronics. The attraction, which opened in 2003, was developed by Walt Disney Imagineering and is known for its use of classic Disney animated characters and memorable musical sequences.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests enter the concert hall of the PhilharMagic orchestra, where Donald Duck is preparing for a performance. The show's premise involves Donald's accidental journey through various Disney animated films after he puts on Mickey Mouse's sorcerer's hat. The theater's theming is a grand concert hall, complete with ornate decorations and a large stage. The narrative allows for a compilation of musical numbers and character appearances from various Disney films.

## Attraction Experience
The attraction begins with a pre-show featuring Donald Duck. The main show is a 12-minute 3D film experience. Guests wear 3D glasses and are immersed in scenes from classic Disney films, including *Beauty and the Beast*, *The Little Mermaid*, and *Aladdin*. The experience incorporates sensory effects such as scents, wind, and water sprays to enhance the immersive nature of the film. Audio-Animatronic figures of Disney characters also appear.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Mickey's PhilharMagic is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests sensitive to loud noises, flashing lights, or sudden movements should be aware of these elements within the attraction.
