# Indiana Jones™ and the Temple of Peril
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Indiana Jones™ and the Temple of Peril is a steel roller coaster located in Adventureland at Disneyland Park Paris. The attraction, manufactured by Intamin, opened in 1993. It is themed after the Indiana Jones film franchise and offers a high-speed, inverted roller coaster experience. The ride is notable for its immersive theming and integration within the Adventureland landscape.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in a jungle environment, encountering the ruins of an ancient temple. The narrative centers around a lost archaeological expedition and a cursed temple. The queue winds through the temple ruins, featuring detailed props and atmospheric lighting. The story suggests that the temple is haunted and that the ride vehicle is a runaway mine cart, adding to the sense of peril.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with a slow ascent up a lift hill. Upon reaching the summit, the coaster plunges into a series of inversions, including a loop. The ride features a high-speed layout with sharp turns and drops, providing a thrilling experience. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds. Sensory effects include speed, G-forces, and the visual immersion of the themed environment.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for thrill-seekers. Guests must meet a minimum height requirement of 1.40 meters (4'7") to ride. Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding.
