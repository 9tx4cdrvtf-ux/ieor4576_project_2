# Na'vi River Journey
**Location:** Animal Kingdom

## Overview
Na'vi River Journey is a boat ride attraction located within Pandora – The World of Avatar at Disney's Animal Kingdom. It is a dark ride utilizing a trackless ride system, designed to immerse guests in the bioluminescent rainforest of Pandora. The attraction was developed by Walt Disney Imagineering and opened in 2017, alongside the land's other major attraction, Avatar Flight of Passage.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride transports guests down a sacred river within the Valley of Mo'ara on Pandora. The narrative focuses on the beauty and tranquility of the environment, showcasing the unique flora and fauna of the alien world. Guests are presented with a serene journey through a bioluminescent rainforest, encountering various Pandoran creatures and experiencing the Na'vi culture. The theming emphasizes the interconnectedness of all living things on Pandora.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a boat that gently floats along the river. The ride utilizes a trackless ride system, allowing the boats to move smoothly and unpredictably through the environment. The journey features detailed animatronics, including a Shaman of Songs, and immersive projections that create the illusion of a vibrant, living ecosystem. The ride lasts approximately five minutes and incorporates various sensory effects, including lighting, sound, and mist.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Na'vi River Journey is designed for a general audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with certain health conditions, such as those sensitive to strobe lights or with a history of motion sickness, should consider the ride's effects before boarding.
