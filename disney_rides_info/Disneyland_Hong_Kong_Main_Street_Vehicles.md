# Main Street Vehicles
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Main Street Vehicles is a transportation attraction located on Main Street, U.S.A. in Disneyland Hong Kong. It offers guests a leisurely ride along the street in classic, turn-of-the-century vehicles. The vehicles, which include a horseless carriage, a jitney, a fire engine, and a double-decker bus, provide a unique perspective of the themed land and a convenient way to travel its length.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the atmosphere of a turn-of-the-century American town. The vehicles are designed to resemble those that would have been common in the early 20th century. The setting is enhanced by the architecture, shops, and cast members dressed in period attire along Main Street, U.S.A., creating a cohesive and immersive experience. The vehicles contribute to the overall theming, transporting guests back in time.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the vehicles at designated loading zones located at either end of Main Street, U.S.A. The ride travels the length of Main Street, offering views of the shops, restaurants, and other attractions. The duration of the ride is approximately 5-7 minutes, depending on traffic and loading times. The vehicles are powered by electric motors, providing a quiet and environmentally friendly mode of transport.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Main Street Vehicles is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find it challenging to board and disembark the vehicles, depending on the specific vehicle type. Guests should be aware of the potential for brief stops and starts during the ride.
