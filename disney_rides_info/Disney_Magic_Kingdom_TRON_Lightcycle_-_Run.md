# TRON Lightcycle / Run
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
TRON Lightcycle / Run is a launched steel roller coaster located in Tomorrowland at Disney's Magic Kingdom. Manufactured by Vekoma Rides Manufacturing, the attraction is based on the *TRON* film franchise. It is a significant addition to the park's thrill ride offerings, providing a high-speed, immersive experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative places guests within the digital world of *TRON*. Riders are "Users" entering the Grid for a Lightcycle race. The queue area and ride environment are themed to resemble the digital landscape, with neon lighting, sleek architecture, and soundscapes designed to evoke the film's aesthetic. The story involves a race between two teams, with guests joining the blue team.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual Lightcycle vehicles, which are seated in a prone position. The ride begins with a launch sequence, accelerating riders to a high speed. The coaster then traverses a track that includes both indoor and outdoor sections. The indoor portion features projections and special effects, enhancing the sense of speed and immersion. The ride lasts approximately one minute.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a broad audience, but has a minimum height requirement of 48 inches (122 cm). Guests with certain medical conditions, including heart problems, back or neck issues, and those who are pregnant, are advised against riding. The ride also features intense speeds and may not be suitable for all guests.
