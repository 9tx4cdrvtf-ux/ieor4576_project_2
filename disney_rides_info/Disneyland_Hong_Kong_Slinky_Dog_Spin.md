# Slinky Dog Spin
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Slinky Dog Spin is a spinning amusement ride located in Toy Story Land at Disneyland Hong Kong. The attraction is a Zamperla Disk'O Coaster, offering a family-friendly experience. It is themed after the character Slinky Dog from the *Toy Story* franchise and provides a visually engaging and interactive experience for guests.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the world of *Toy Story*, shrunk down to the size of toys. The ride is presented as a game set up by Andy, the child from the films. Riders are seated on a circular platform attached to Slinky Dog's body. The surrounding environment is designed to resemble Andy's backyard, with oversized toys and props creating a playful atmosphere.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with the circular platform rotating and simultaneously moving along a wavy track. This motion provides a sensation of spinning and gentle ups and downs. The platform's speed gradually increases, offering a moderate thrill. The ride cycle lasts approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds. The attraction utilizes bright colors, themed music, and visual elements to enhance the immersive experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Slinky Dog Spin is designed for a broad audience, particularly families with young children. The minimum height requirement is 81 cm (32 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, particularly those susceptible to motion sickness or with back problems, are advised to consider their condition before riding. Expectant mothers are also advised against riding.
