# Walt Disney's Enchanted Tiki Room
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Walt Disney's Enchanted Tiki Room is an audio-animatronic show attraction located in Adventureland at Disneyland. It is notable as the first attraction to utilize advanced audio-animatronics, a technology developed by Walt Disney Imagineering. The attraction opened on June 23, 1963, and is a significant example of early immersive entertainment.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a Polynesian-style tiki room. Guests are welcomed by four "host" macaws, Pierre, José, Michael, and Fritz, who introduce the show. The narrative centers around a musical performance by over 225 animatronic birds, flowers, and tiki statues. The setting evokes a tropical paradise, complete with simulated rain and thunderstorms, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter a large, dimly lit room and are seated on benches. The show begins with the host macaws introducing the performance. The animatronic figures come to life, singing and performing a variety of songs, including "The Tiki Tiki Tiki Room." The show incorporates various special effects, including lighting, sound, and simulated weather. The total duration of the show is approximately 15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. The show is presented in a darkened environment with loud music and simulated weather effects, which may be potentially frightening for very young children or those sensitive to such stimuli. Guests with photosensitive epilepsy should exercise caution.
