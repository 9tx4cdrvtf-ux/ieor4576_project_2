# Walt Disney's Enchanted Tiki Room
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Walt Disney's Enchanted Tiki Room is an audio-animatronic show attraction located in Adventureland at the Magic Kingdom. It is a theater-in-the-round experience, notable for being the first attraction to utilize advanced audio-animatronics. The attraction showcases a variety of singing and dancing birds, flowers, and tiki statues, all brought to life through sophisticated robotic technology.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a Polynesian-inspired tropical paradise. Guests enter a dimly lit room designed to resemble a tiki temple. The narrative centers around a performance hosted by four "hosts" – a quartet of macaws named Pierre, Jose, Fritz, and Michael. These hosts introduce the audience to the various animatronic characters, who then perform a series of musical numbers celebrating the beauty and wonder of the tropics. The setting is designed to evoke a sense of escapism and exoticism.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are seated in a circular theater and observe the show from a central stage. The show features over 225 audio-animatronic figures, including singing birds, flowers, and tiki drummers. The performance incorporates synchronized movements, lighting effects, and surround sound to create an immersive experience. The show lasts approximately 15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. The show is presented in a darkened theater, and some guests may find the loud music and sudden movements startling. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
