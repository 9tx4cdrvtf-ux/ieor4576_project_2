# Walt Disney World Railroad - Fantasyland
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
The Walt Disney World Railroad (WWRR) in Fantasyland is a narrow-gauge railroad attraction operating within the Magic Kingdom theme park. It is a scenic train ride that provides guests with transportation between various park areas and offers views of themed environments. The railroad utilizes authentic steam-powered locomotives, manufactured by WED Enterprises (now Walt Disney Imagineering), and is a classic opening-day attraction.

## Narrative and Theming
The WWRR does not have a specific narrative beyond its function as a transportation system. However, the train travels through themed environments, including a diorama of the Grand Canyon and a prehistoric landscape. The Fantasyland station, located near "it's a small world," is designed to blend with the whimsical architecture of the surrounding area. The overall theming focuses on a nostalgic, turn-of-the-century railroad experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the train at one of several stations: Main Street, U.S.A., Frontierland, or Fantasyland. The train travels a 1.5-mile (2.4 km) loop around the park, offering views of various attractions and themed lands. The ride includes a brief stop at the Grand Canyon diorama and a journey through the Primeval World, featuring Audio-Animatronic dinosaurs. The entire journey takes approximately 20 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Walt Disney World Railroad is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may need assistance boarding and disembarking the train. Strollers must be folded before boarding. Guests with certain health conditions should consult with a medical professional before riding.
