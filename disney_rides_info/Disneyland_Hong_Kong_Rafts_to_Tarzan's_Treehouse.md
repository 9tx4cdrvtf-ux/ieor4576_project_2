# Rafts to Tarzan's Treehouse
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Rafts to Tarzan's Treehouse is a water-based transportation attraction located in Adventureland at Disneyland Hong Kong. It provides guests with access to Tarzan's Treehouse, a walk-through attraction. The attraction consists of a series of manually-operated rafts that ferry guests across a lagoon. It is a simple but essential component of Adventureland, offering a unique perspective of the surrounding environment and enhancing the immersive theming of the area.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is thematically integrated into the Adventureland setting, specifically connecting to the story of Tarzan. Guests are transported across a lagoon, designed to resemble a jungle river, to reach the iconic Tarzan's Treehouse. The environment is designed to evoke the atmosphere of a dense jungle, with lush vegetation and ambient sound effects contributing to the immersive experience. The rafts themselves are constructed to appear rustic and hand-built, further reinforcing the jungle theme.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue at a designated loading area and board one of several rafts. Each raft is manually pulled across the lagoon by a cast member using a rope system. The journey across the water is brief, lasting approximately one to two minutes. During the crossing, guests can observe the surrounding jungle environment and the various details incorporated into the theming. Upon reaching the opposite shore, guests disembark and proceed to Tarzan's Treehouse.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Rafts to Tarzan's Treehouse is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may require assistance to board and disembark the rafts. The attraction is generally accessible to guests with disabilities, though specific accommodations may vary. Guests should be aware that they may get slightly wet during the ride.
