# Meet StellaLou at Duffy and Friends Play House
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Meet StellaLou at Duffy and Friends Play House is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in the Fantasy Gardens area of Disneyland Hong Kong. It allows guests to interact with StellaLou, a purple rabbit character from the Duffy and Friends franchise. The attraction provides photo opportunities and brief interactions with the character in a themed environment.

## Narrative and Theming
The Play House is designed to resemble a cozy, artistic studio space. The theming reflects StellaLou's passion for dance and performance. The environment is decorated with ballet-themed props, such as ribbons, tutus, and stage backdrops. The overall atmosphere is intended to be inviting and cheerful, reflecting the character's personality.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area before entering the Play House. Upon entering, guests are guided to a specific location where StellaLou is present. Guests are given the opportunity to take photographs with StellaLou and engage in brief interactions, such as exchanging greetings or receiving autographs. The duration of each interaction is typically a few minutes. The attraction utilizes standard character meet-and-greet procedures, with cast members assisting in the process.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. Guests with mobility issues can access the attraction, but may require assistance.
