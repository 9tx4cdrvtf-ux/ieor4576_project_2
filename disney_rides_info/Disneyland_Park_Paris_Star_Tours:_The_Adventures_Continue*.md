# Star Tours: The Adventures Continue*
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Star Tours: The Adventures Continue* is a motion simulator ride located in Discoveryland at Disneyland Park Paris. It is a 3D film-based attraction developed by Walt Disney Imagineering, utilizing a motion simulator system to simulate space travel within the Star Wars universe. The attraction is a reimagining of the original Star Tours, offering multiple randomized ride sequences. It is a significant attraction within Discoveryland, providing a key immersive experience based on the popular Star Wars franchise.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are presented as passengers on a Starspeeder 1000 series space transport, embarking on a journey from the spaceport. The narrative places riders within the Star Wars galaxy, with the premise of a routine flight that quickly escalates into a series of perilous adventures. The queue area is themed as a spaceport terminal, featuring droids and other Star Wars elements to enhance the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
The ride experience involves guests entering a motion simulator cabin that seats approximately 40 passengers. The simulator synchronizes with a 3D film projected onto a screen, creating the illusion of flight through space. The ride features randomized sequences, offering a variety of destinations and encounters with characters from the Star Wars saga. The ride duration is approximately 4 minutes. Sensory effects include motion, simulated turbulence, and environmental effects such as wind and simulated explosions.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for a wide audience, though children must meet the minimum height requirement of 1.02 meters (40 inches). Guests with a history of heart conditions, back problems, or motion sickness are advised to consider the ride's intensity before boarding. Pregnant women are also advised against riding.
