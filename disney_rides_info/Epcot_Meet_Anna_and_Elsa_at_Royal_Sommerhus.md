# Meet Anna and Elsa at Royal Sommerhus
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Meet Anna and Elsa at Royal Sommerhus is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in the Norway Pavilion at Epcot's World Showcase. It allows guests to interact with the characters Anna and Elsa from Disney's animated film *Frozen*. The attraction is a walk-through experience, providing photo opportunities and brief interactions with the characters within a themed environment.

## Narrative and Theming
The Royal Sommerhus is presented as the summer home of Anna and Elsa, located in the village of Arendelle. The building's architecture is inspired by traditional Norwegian design, featuring wooden construction, intricate carvings, and a cozy interior. The setting evokes the atmosphere of the film, with details such as a fireplace, portraits, and other props that reference the characters and their story.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a line that winds through a themed queue area. Upon reaching the meet-and-greet location, guests are greeted by Anna and Elsa. Guests are given the opportunity to take photos and briefly converse with the characters. The interaction typically lasts for a few minutes per guest group. The attraction is designed to accommodate a high volume of guests, with a continuous flow of visitors.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. Guests with mobility issues can access the attraction, though wait times may vary.
