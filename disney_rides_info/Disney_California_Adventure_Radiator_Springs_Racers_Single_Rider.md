# Radiator Springs Racers Single Rider
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Radiator Springs Racers Single Rider is a high-speed, dark ride and slot car-style attraction located within Cars Land at Disney California Adventure. Manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering, the ride is a significant draw for the park, known for its immersive theming and popularity. It is a clone of the original attraction, Radiator Springs Racers, but offers a separate queue for guests who are willing to ride alone to reduce wait times.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed after the Pixar film *Cars*. Guests are immersed in the fictional town of Radiator Springs, experiencing the world of Lightning McQueen and his friends. The queue winds through the landscape, featuring iconic locations and characters from the movie. The ride's narrative involves a scenic tour of Radiator Springs, followed by a race against another vehicle.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board six-passenger vehicles designed to resemble automobiles. The ride begins with a leisurely tour through the desert landscape and into a show building. Inside, the vehicles encounter various scenes featuring Audio-Animatronics of *Cars* characters. The vehicles then transition to a high-speed race on a track, with two vehicles racing side-by-side. The ride incorporates special effects, including lighting, sound, and projections. The entire experience lasts approximately four minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Radiator Springs Racers Single Rider is suitable for guests of all ages, provided they meet the height requirement of 40 inches (102 cm). Guests with certain medical conditions, such as heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consult with a physician before riding. The ride involves high speeds and sudden movements.
