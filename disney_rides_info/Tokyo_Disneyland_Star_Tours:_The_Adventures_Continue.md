# Star Tours: The Adventures Continue
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Star Tours: The Adventures Continue is a 3D motion simulator attraction located in Tomorrowland at Tokyo Disneyland. It is a reimagining of the original Star Tours attraction, utilizing updated technology and a randomized storyline system. The ride is manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering and serves as a significant immersive experience within the park's Tomorrowland, drawing heavily on the Star Wars franchise.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are presented as passengers on a Starspeeder 1000 series space transport, embarking on a seemingly routine flight from the spaceport. The narrative is set within the Star Wars universe, with the premise being a journey to various planets and locations. The queue line features references to the Star Wars galaxy, including droids and other familiar characters, further immersing guests in the themed environment.

## Attraction Experience
The attraction utilizes a motion simulator synchronized with a 3D film projected onto a large screen. The ride experience is randomized, offering multiple possible storylines and destinations, including familiar locations such as Hoth, Tatooine, and the Death Star. The ride incorporates sensory effects such as wind, simulated heat, and motion to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 4-5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is generally suitable for a wide audience. Guests must be at least 102 cm (40 inches) tall to ride. Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding.
