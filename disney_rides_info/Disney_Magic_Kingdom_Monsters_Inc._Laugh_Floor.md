# Monsters Inc. Laugh Floor
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Monsters Inc. Laugh Floor is a live-interactive comedy show attraction located in the Tomorrowland area of Magic Kingdom. It is a theater-style show featuring characters from the Disney-Pixar film *Monsters, Inc.*. The attraction utilizes real-time animation and audience participation to generate laughter, which powers the city of Monstropolis.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests enter a theater designed to resemble the Laugh Floor from the film. The premise involves monsters collecting human laughter to power their city, replacing the previous energy source of screams. The show is hosted by Mike Wazowski, who interacts with the audience and introduces various monster comedians. The setting is consistent with the film's aesthetic, featuring industrial elements and vibrant colors.

## Attraction Experience
Upon entering the theater, guests are seated and the show begins. Mike Wazowski appears on a large screen and interacts with the audience, telling jokes and introducing other monster comedians. The show incorporates real-time animation, allowing the characters to respond to audience members and their jokes. The show's duration is approximately 12 minutes. Guests can also text jokes to be used during the show.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Monsters Inc. Laugh Floor is suitable for all ages. There are no height requirements. The attraction is accessible to guests using wheelchairs. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
