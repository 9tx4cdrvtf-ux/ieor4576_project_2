# World of Color Happiness!
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
World of Color Happiness! is a nighttime spectacular presented at Paradise Bay in Disney California Adventure. It is a water show featuring fountains, projections, lasers, and pyrotechnics. The show is produced by Walt Disney Imagineering and is a seasonal iteration of the larger World of Color show, focusing on themes of joy and celebration.

## Narrative and Theming
The show celebrates the concept of happiness through a montage of Disney and Pixar animated film clips. These clips are projected onto water screens, accompanied by music and lighting effects. The narrative is a compilation of various stories, highlighting moments of friendship, love, and triumph from a range of Disney properties. The atmospheric setting is enhanced by the use of colored lights, fog, and the backdrop of the park's iconic landmarks.

## Attraction Experience
Guests view the show from designated viewing areas around Paradise Bay. The show utilizes a combination of over 1,200 programmable fountains that shoot water up to 200 feet in the air. Projections are displayed on mist screens created by the fountains, and lasers and pyrotechnics are synchronized with the music. The show's duration is approximately 22 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
World of Color Happiness! is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with sensitivity to strobe lights or loud noises should be aware of these elements within the show. Wheelchair and ECV accessibility is available in designated viewing areas.
