# Meet Princess Tiana and a Visiting Princess at Princess Fairytale Hall
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Meet Princess Tiana and a Visiting Princess at Princess Fairytale Hall is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in Fantasyland at the Magic Kingdom. It allows guests to interact with Disney Princesses, specifically Princess Tiana from *The Princess and the Frog*, alongside a rotating selection of other Disney Princesses. The attraction provides a traditional meet-and-greet experience within a themed environment.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is housed within a regal, castle-like structure designed to resemble a royal hall. The interior is decorated with ornate details, including tapestries, portraits, and elegant furnishings, creating a setting appropriate for welcoming royalty. The theming aims to immerse guests in a fairytale atmosphere, suggesting a space where princesses reside and receive visitors.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a line that winds through the themed queue area. Upon reaching the designated meeting area, guests are able to meet and take photos with Princess Tiana and a visiting princess. The visiting princess varies, but typically includes characters such as Cinderella, Aurora, or Rapunzel. The interaction allows for brief conversations and photo opportunities. The entire experience, including wait time, can vary depending on park attendance.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. However, guests with mobility issues may find the queue challenging depending on crowd levels.
