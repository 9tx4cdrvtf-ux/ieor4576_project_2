# Slinky Dog Dash
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
Slinky Dog Dash is a family-friendly launched roller coaster located in Toy Story Land at Disney's Hollywood Studios. Manufactured by Vekoma, the coaster features a unique layout and theming based on the *Toy Story* film franchise. It is a significant attraction within Toy Story Land, offering a thrilling yet accessible experience for a wide range of guests.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in Andy's backyard, where they are shrunk to the size of a toy. The premise involves Slinky Dog, constructed by Andy, being used as a coaster by the other toys. The queue area and ride environment are meticulously themed to resemble a child's imagination, with oversized toys, colorful structures, and playful details. The coaster track itself is designed to look like it was built from various toy parts.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a coaster vehicle designed to resemble Slinky Dog. The ride begins with a slow launch, followed by a series of hills, drops, and turns. A mid-course launch provides additional momentum. The ride incorporates several airtime hills, offering brief moments of weightlessness. The duration of the ride is approximately 1 minute and 40 seconds. The ride utilizes a combination of traditional coaster elements and themed environments to enhance the experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Slinky Dog Dash is designed for a broad audience, including families with children. The height requirement is 38 inches (97 cm). Guests with certain medical conditions, such as heart problems, back or neck issues, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consult with a physician before riding.
