# Mickey's PhilharMagic
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Mickey's PhilharMagic is a 4D film attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Hong Kong. It is a show featuring a 3D film combined with in-theater sensory effects. The attraction is a variation of the original show that debuted at Magic Kingdom in Walt Disney World. It is a significant family-friendly attraction, offering a multi-sensory experience based on classic Disney animated films.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests enter a grand concert hall, where they are invited to attend a performance conducted by Donald Duck. The pre-show area features musical instruments and posters related to Disney animated films. The narrative centers around Donald Duck's accidental journey through various Disney animated musical sequences after he attempts to steal Mickey Mouse's Sorcerer's hat.

## Attraction Experience
The show utilizes a 3D film projected onto a large screen, with additional effects such as scents, wind, and water sprays synchronized to the on-screen action. Guests wear 3D glasses to enhance the visual experience. The film features musical numbers and characters from classic Disney films, including *Beauty and the Beast*, *The Little Mermaid*, *Aladdin*, and *The Lion King*. The show lasts approximately 12 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Mickey's PhilharMagic is designed for a general audience, including families with young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with sensitivity to loud noises, flashing lights, or strong scents should be aware of the sensory effects. The attraction is wheelchair accessible.
