# Toy Soldiers Parachute Drop
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
Toy Soldiers Parachute Drop is a drop tower-style amusement ride located in the Toy Story Playland area of Disney Adventure World Paris. The attraction simulates a parachute jump, offering guests a brief but thrilling experience. Manufactured by S&S – Sansei Technologies, the ride is themed after the green plastic army men featured in the *Toy Story* film series. It is designed to provide a family-friendly thrill, suitable for a wide range of ages.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are "recruited" as honorary soldiers and are briefed by a giant, animated Sarge, the leader of the toy soldiers. The queue line winds through a military-themed environment, including oversized toy boxes and camouflage netting. The ride vehicles are designed to resemble parachutes, and the surrounding area is decorated to create the illusion of being in a vast toy-filled landscape. The overall setting immerses guests in the world of *Toy Story*, enhancing the ride's narrative.

## Attraction Experience
Riders are seated in a circular gondola, which is then hoisted up a central tower. The gondola ascends slowly, providing views of the surrounding Toy Story Playland. Upon reaching the top, the gondola is released, and riders experience a series of controlled drops and bounces, simulating a parachute descent. The ride lasts approximately one minute, including the ascent and descent phases. The ride utilizes a compressed air launch system for its drop sequence.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Toy Soldiers Parachute Drop is designed for a broad audience, with a minimum height requirement of 81 cm (32 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, particularly those affecting the heart, back, or neck, are advised to consult with a medical professional before riding. Pregnant women and guests susceptible to motion sickness are also advised to consider the ride's intensity.
