# Toy Story Midway Mania!
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Toy Story Midway Mania! is a 4D interactive ride-through attraction located in Pixar Pier at Disney California Adventure. The ride is a track-based shooting gallery experience, manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering. It is a significant attraction, offering a high-capacity, family-friendly experience that is popular with guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in a carnival-themed environment hosted by the characters from the *Toy Story* film franchise. Guests are shrunk down to the size of a toy and enter Andy's bedroom, where they are invited to play a series of carnival games. The queue area is themed as a toy box, with oversized game pieces and colorful decorations. The ride vehicles are designed to resemble carnival ride cars.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board ride vehicles that travel through a series of virtual game booths. Each booth features a different game, such as throwing rings at Green Army Men, shooting darts at balloons, or tossing eggs at targets. Riders use pull-strings on their vehicles to launch projectiles at the virtual targets displayed on screens. The ride incorporates 3D effects, physical props, and audio-animatronics to enhance the immersive experience. The ride lasts approximately 4 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Toy Story Midway Mania! is designed for a general audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with motion sickness or other health concerns should consider the ride's potential effects before boarding.
