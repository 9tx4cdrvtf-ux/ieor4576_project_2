# Walt Disney World Railroad - Main Street, U.S.A.
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
The Walt Disney World Railroad (WWRR) is a 3-foot (914 mm) narrow-gauge heritage railroad attraction located within the Magic Kingdom theme park at Walt Disney World. It is a scenic train ride that circles the park, offering guests views of various themed lands. The railroad utilizes authentic steam-powered locomotives, built by the WED Enterprises, the original design and development company of Walt Disney. The WWRR is a key element of the park's transportation system and a significant part of the Magic Kingdom experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The WWRR provides a nostalgic journey reminiscent of the late 19th and early 20th centuries. The locomotives are named after significant figures in American history and are designed to evoke the spirit of the era. The route passes through several themed areas, including Main Street, U.S.A., Frontierland, Fantasyland, and Tomorrowland, offering glimpses of the park's attractions and landscapes. The experience is designed to immerse guests in the atmosphere of each land as they travel.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the train at one of four stations: Main Street, U.S.A., Frontierland, Fantasyland, and Tomorrowland. The train travels a 1.5-mile (2.4 km) loop, with a ride duration of approximately 20 minutes. The locomotives are powered by steam, and the journey provides views of the park's scenery, including dioramas and wildlife exhibits. The ride offers a relaxing and scenic overview of the Magic Kingdom.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Walt Disney World Railroad is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the train with assistance. Strollers must be folded before boarding. Guests with certain health conditions should consult with a medical professional before riding.
