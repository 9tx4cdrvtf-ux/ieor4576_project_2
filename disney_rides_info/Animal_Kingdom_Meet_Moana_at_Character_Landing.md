# Meet Moana at Character Landing
**Location:** Animal Kingdom

## Overview
Meet Moana at Character Landing is a character meet-and-greet experience located within the Discovery Island area of Disney's Animal Kingdom. This attraction allows guests to interact with the Disney character Moana from the animated film of the same name. It is a permanent fixture, providing a photo opportunity and brief conversation with the character.

## Narrative and Theming
The meet-and-greet area is themed to resemble a tropical island setting, evocative of Moana's home in Oceania. The backdrop features lush foliage, vibrant flowers, and elements inspired by Polynesian culture. The environment aims to immerse guests in the world of the film, creating a sense of place and adventure. The setting is designed to be visually appealing and conducive to photo opportunities.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area, often under shade, before approaching Moana. The interaction typically involves a brief conversation, photo opportunities, and the chance to obtain autographs. The duration of each interaction is typically a few minutes. The attraction utilizes a standard meet-and-greet format, with cast members assisting in managing the line and ensuring a smooth flow of guests.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Meet Moana at Character Landing is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. Guests with mobility issues or other disabilities are accommodated through the standard Disney accessibility procedures.
