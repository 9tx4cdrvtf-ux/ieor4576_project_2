# Mad Tea Party
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Mad Tea Party is a spinning tea cup ride located in Fantasyland at Disneyland. The attraction is a classic amusement ride manufactured by Arrow Development, and is based on the "Mad Tea Party" scene from Walt Disney's 1951 animated film, *Alice in Wonderland*. It is a quintessential Disneyland attraction, known for its whimsical theming and simple, yet engaging, ride experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the chaotic tea party hosted by the Mad Hatter and the March Hare. The ride area is designed to resemble a whimsical garden setting, with oversized teacups placed on a large, rotating platform. The surrounding environment features colorful decorations, including oversized teacups, teapots, and other fanciful elements inspired by the film. The overall atmosphere is intended to evoke a sense of playful disorientation and childlike wonder.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual, brightly colored teacups that are mounted on a rotating platform. The platform itself revolves, while each teacup is also capable of being spun manually by the riders via a wheel located in the center of the cup. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds. The attraction utilizes no special effects beyond the spinning motion and the surrounding themed environment.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Mad Tea Party is generally suitable for all ages, although the spinning motion may cause motion sickness in some individuals. There are no height restrictions. Guests with a history of motion sickness, vertigo, or other health conditions that could be aggravated by spinning are advised to consider their condition before riding.
