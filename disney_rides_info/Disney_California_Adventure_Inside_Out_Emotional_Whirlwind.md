# Inside Out Emotional Whirlwind
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Inside Out Emotional Whirlwind is a spinner ride located in Pixar Pier at Disney California Adventure. The attraction is themed to the Pixar film *Inside Out* (2015) and features characters and imagery from the movie. It is a family-friendly ride designed to provide a gentle, visually engaging experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the world of Riley's emotions. Riders are invited to join Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear, and Disgust as they work together to keep Riley's emotions balanced. The ride vehicles are themed as Memory Movers, each representing a different emotion. The surrounding environment features vibrant colors and stylized representations of Riley's mind, including the Headquarters and various islands of personality.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of eight Memory Mover vehicles, each capable of holding several riders. The ride begins with the Memory Movers rotating around a central structure. As the ride progresses, the vehicles move in a circular motion, ascending and descending slightly. The ride path is relatively simple, with the primary focus on the visual experience. The attraction incorporates projections and lighting effects to enhance the theming. The ride duration is approximately 2 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Inside Out Emotional Whirlwind is designed for a general audience, particularly families with young children. There is no height requirement. Guests with a sensitivity to spinning motions should consider the ride's effects before boarding. Expectant mothers and guests with certain health conditions should consult with a cast member before riding.
