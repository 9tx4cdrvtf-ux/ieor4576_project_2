# Explorer Canoes
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Explorer Canoes is a water-based attraction at Shanghai Disneyland, offering guests a guided paddling experience across the waters of the park's Wishing Star Lake. The attraction allows guests to manually propel a canoe, providing a unique and interactive perspective of the surrounding scenery. It is a non-powered attraction, relying solely on the efforts of the riders.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the spirit of exploration and adventure. The canoes are designed to resemble traditional wooden vessels, evoking a sense of discovery and the thrill of navigating uncharted waters. The surrounding environment, including the Wishing Star Lake and its adjacent landscapes, is themed to complement the exploration narrative, featuring scenic views and themed elements.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the canoes, which are manned by a Disney Cast Member who provides guidance and ensures safety. Each canoe accommodates a specific number of riders who are provided with paddles. Guests then work together to propel the canoe along a designated route across Wishing Star Lake. The journey offers views of various park landmarks and themed areas. The ride duration is approximately 20 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Explorer Canoes is suitable for guests of all ages and physical abilities, provided they are able to paddle. There are no specific height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may require assistance. Pregnant women and guests with certain medical conditions should consult with a Cast Member before boarding.
