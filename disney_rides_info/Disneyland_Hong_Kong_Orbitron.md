# Orbitron
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Orbitron is a spinning, aerial carousel attraction located in the Tomorrowland area of Disneyland Hong Kong. It is a variation of the classic "rocket" ride, offering guests a simulated flight experience. The attraction allows riders to control the elevation of their individual vehicles, providing a degree of interactivity.

## Narrative and Theming
The Orbitron is themed as a futuristic space station, with a central, towering structure surrounded by individual rocket-shaped vehicles. The setting evokes a sense of exploration and discovery, consistent with the Tomorrowland theme. The attraction's design incorporates elements of retro-futurism, with metallic finishes and geometric shapes. Guests are presented with the idea of embarking on a journey through the cosmos.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual rocket vehicles that rotate around a central axis. Each rocket is equipped with a control lever that allows riders to adjust the height of their vehicle, offering a degree of control over the ride experience. The ride cycle lasts approximately 90 seconds. The attraction provides views of Tomorrowland and the surrounding park.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Orbitron is generally suitable for all ages, although young children may require assistance from an adult. There is a height requirement of 81 cm (32 inches). Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised to consider their health before riding.
