# Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run Single Rider
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run Single Rider is a motion simulator attraction located within Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge at Disneyland. The ride allows guests to experience a simulated flight aboard the iconic Millennium Falcon starship. The attraction was developed by Walt Disney Imagineering and utilizes a motion simulator system to provide an immersive experience. It is a key attraction within Galaxy's Edge, offering guests an interactive role in a Star Wars adventure.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the Star Wars universe, specifically within the Black Spire Outpost on the planet Batuu. The premise involves guests taking on the role of a smuggler crew, hired by the character Hondo Ohnaka to pilot the Millennium Falcon on various missions. The queue area is themed as Ohnaka Transport Solutions, a company that facilitates smuggling operations. The environment is designed to replicate the look and feel of the Millennium Falcon's interior, including the iconic cockpit.

## Attraction Experience
The ride experience begins with guests being assigned a role: pilot, gunner, or engineer. The Single Rider queue allows guests to bypass the standard queue and be placed in available positions. Once inside the cockpit, guests interact with the controls corresponding to their assigned role. The motion simulator synchronizes with the on-screen action, creating the sensation of flight, including turbulence and G-forces. The ride duration is approximately five minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for a wide audience. Guests must be at least 38 inches (97 cm) tall to ride. Guests with certain medical conditions, such as heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their health before riding. The ride utilizes motion simulation and may cause motion sickness in susceptible individuals.
