# Remy's Ratatouille Adventure Single Rider
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Remy's Ratatouille Adventure Single Rider is a trackless dark ride located in the France Pavilion expansion at Epcot's World Showcase. Based on the Disney-Pixar film *Ratatouille*, the attraction utilizes trackless ride vehicles and 4D effects to immerse guests in a culinary adventure. The ride was manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering and is a significant addition to the park, expanding the France Pavilion and offering a family-friendly experience.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests shrink to the size of a rat and embark on a culinary journey through Gusteau's restaurant in Paris. The storyline follows Remy, the protagonist of the film, as he attempts to create a delicious meal. The queue area is themed to resemble the rooftops of Paris and the restaurant's kitchen, further immersing guests in the film's setting.

## Attraction Experience
The ride vehicles, designed to resemble rats, move along a trackless path, navigating through various scenes. Guests experience 4D effects, including heat, water, and scents, enhancing the immersive environment. The ride utilizes large projection screens and physical sets to create the illusion of movement and scale. The ride duration is approximately five minutes. The Single Rider queue allows guests to bypass a portion of the main queue by filling in empty seats on the ride vehicles.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Remy's Ratatouille Adventure is designed for a general audience, including families. Guests must meet a minimum height requirement of 40 inches (102 cm). Guests with motion sickness or other health concerns should consider the ride's potential effects before experiencing the attraction.
