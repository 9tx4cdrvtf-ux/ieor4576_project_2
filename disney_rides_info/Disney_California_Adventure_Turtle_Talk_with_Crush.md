# Turtle Talk with Crush
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Turtle Talk with Crush is an interactive show attraction located in the Animation Building at Disney California Adventure. The attraction utilizes a combination of computer animation and live performance to allow guests to converse with Crush, the sea turtle character from the Disney-Pixar film *Finding Nemo*. It is a unique form of entertainment, blending theatrical performance with technological innovation.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests enter a simulated underwater theater, designed to resemble the ocean environment. The setting is themed to evoke the world of *Finding Nemo*, with projections of coral reefs and other marine life. The premise is that guests are able to communicate directly with Crush, who appears on a large screen.

## Attraction Experience
The show begins with a cast member, acting as a "sea turtle wrangler," welcoming guests. Crush then appears on the screen, interacting with the audience in real-time. Using sophisticated animation and voice acting, Crush responds to questions and comments from the audience, improvising and creating unique interactions for each show. The show lasts approximately 15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Turtle Talk with Crush is designed for a general audience, with no height restrictions. The attraction is suitable for all ages. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions, although guests sensitive to loud noises or enclosed spaces should take this into consideration.
