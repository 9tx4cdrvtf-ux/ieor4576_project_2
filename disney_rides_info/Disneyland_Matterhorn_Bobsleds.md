# Matterhorn Bobsleds
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
The Matterhorn Bobsleds is a steel roller coaster located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park. Opened on June 14, 1959, it is one of the park's original attractions and is notable for being the first tubular steel-tracked roller coaster. The ride simulates a bobsled run down the Swiss Alps' Matterhorn mountain.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed after the Matterhorn, a famous mountain in the Swiss Alps. Guests are transported to a snowy alpine environment, encountering icy caverns, waterfalls, and the Abominable Snowman, a fictional creature that inhabits the mountain. The ride's theming incorporates rockwork, snow effects, and lighting to create an immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board bobsled-style vehicles that navigate a winding track through the mountain. The ride features two separate tracks, each offering a slightly different experience. The bobsleds ascend and descend through the mountain, encountering sharp turns, drops, and encounters with the Abominable Snowman. The ride duration is approximately 2 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Matterhorn Bobsleds is suitable for guests of various ages, though it may be too intense for very young children. Guests must be at least 32 inches (81 cm) tall to ride. Due to the ride's intensity, guests with back or neck problems, heart conditions, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding.
