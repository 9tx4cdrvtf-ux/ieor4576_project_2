# Selfie Spot with Princesses
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Selfie Spot with Princesses is a meet-and-greet attraction located within Shanghai Disney Resort. It provides guests with the opportunity to interact with and take photographs with various Disney Princess characters. The attraction is a permanent fixture within the park, offering a classic Disney experience focused on character interaction.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed to resemble a regal courtyard or a princess's private garden, depending on the specific location within the park. The setting is designed to evoke a sense of fairytale magic and elegance, with ornate backdrops, themed props, and ambient music. The environment aims to create an immersive experience that complements the presence of the Disney Princesses.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area, often decorated with princess-themed elements. Upon reaching the front of the line, guests are escorted to a photo opportunity with one or more Disney Princesses. The interaction typically involves a brief conversation, posing for photographs taken by either a Disney PhotoPass photographer or guests using their own devices. The duration of each interaction varies depending on the number of guests and the specific princess.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is primarily targeted towards families and children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues are accommodated through accessible queuing and photo locations. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions, but guests are advised to be mindful of other guests and to follow the instructions provided by cast members.
