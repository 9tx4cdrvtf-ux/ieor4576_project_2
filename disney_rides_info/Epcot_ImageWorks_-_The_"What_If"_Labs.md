# ImageWorks - The "What If" Labs
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
ImageWorks - The "What If" Labs is an interactive exhibit located within the Imagination! pavilion at Epcot. Originally opened in 1982, it was designed to encourage creativity and exploration through hands-on activities. The attraction allows guests to experiment with various aspects of imagination, perception, and technology. It is a walk-through experience, featuring several interactive stations.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around the concept of a laboratory where guests can explore the power of imagination. The environment is designed to resemble a research facility, with various stations dedicated to different aspects of creative expression and sensory perception. The overall atmosphere is meant to be playful and encourage experimentation.

## Attraction Experience
Guests move through a series of interactive exhibits. These include the "Dreamfinder's School of Drama," where guests can create their own animated characters, and "Stepping Tones," a musical floor that responds to movement. The most notable feature is the "ImageWorks Playground," a large area with interactive games and activities. The experience duration varies depending on the guest's engagement with the exhibits, typically lasting between 30 to 60 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
ImageWorks is designed for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction is accessible to guests with disabilities. No specific health warnings or restrictions are in place.
