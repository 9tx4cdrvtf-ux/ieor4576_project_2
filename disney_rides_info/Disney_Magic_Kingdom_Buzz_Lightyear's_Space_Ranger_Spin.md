# Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin is a shooting dark ride attraction located in the Tomorrowland section of Disney's Magic Kingdom. The ride, manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering, allows guests to become Space Rangers and participate in a laser-shooting game to defeat the Evil Emperor Zurg. It is a popular interactive attraction, offering a competitive element and appealing to a wide range of ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around the *Toy Story* franchise, specifically the character Buzz Lightyear. Guests are recruited as Space Rangers and are briefed on their mission to stop Zurg from stealing batteries from the toys. The queue area features various space-themed elements, including a briefing room and a Star Command control center. The ride vehicles are styled as XP-38 Star Cruisers, each equipped with two laser cannons.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the XP-38 Star Cruisers, which travel along a track through various scenes depicting Zurg's invasion. Riders use their laser cannons to shoot at targets, earning points based on the target's difficulty. The vehicles are capable of 360-degree rotation, allowing riders to aim at targets in any direction. The ride incorporates audio-animatronics, special effects, and vibrant lighting. The ride duration is approximately 4 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin is suitable for guests of all ages. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or those prone to motion sickness should be aware of the ride's effects. The attraction is wheelchair accessible.
