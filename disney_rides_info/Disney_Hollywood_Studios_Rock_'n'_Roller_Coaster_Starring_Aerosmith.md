# Rock 'n' Roller Coaster Starring Aerosmith
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
Rock 'n' Roller Coaster Starring Aerosmith is an enclosed, high-speed roller coaster located in the Sunset Boulevard section of Disney's Hollywood Studios. Manufactured by Vekoma, it is notable for its launch system, which accelerates riders from 0 to 57 mph (92 km/h) in approximately 2.8 seconds. The attraction combines a thrilling coaster experience with a rock music theme, featuring the band Aerosmith.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers around a fictional recording studio, G-Force Records, where guests are invited to attend an Aerosmith recording session. Upon learning that the band needs to get to a concert across town, guests are offered a ride in a super-stretch limousine to make it in time. The queue area is themed as the recording studio and its associated offices, complete with music memorabilia.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a limousine-themed train that launches into a darkened show building. The ride features inversions, including a corkscrew and a series of loops, accompanied by a custom Aerosmith soundtrack played through onboard speakers. The ride utilizes linear induction motor (LIM) technology for its launch. The entire experience lasts approximately 1 minute and 22 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for thrill-seekers. Guests must be at least 48 inches (122 cm) tall to ride. Due to the ride's intensity, it is not recommended for guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant.
