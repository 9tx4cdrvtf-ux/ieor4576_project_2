# DisneySea Electric Railway (American Waterfront)
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
The DisneySea Electric Railway (American Waterfront) is a transportation attraction located within Tokyo DisneySea's American Waterfront and Port Discovery areas. It is a themed elevated railway system, providing guests with scenic views and convenient access between the two ports. The attraction is a custom-built electric railway, designed to blend seamlessly with the park's early 20th-century aesthetic.

## Narrative and Theming
The Electric Railway is presented as a mode of transport from the early 1900s, reflecting the era of industrial progress and technological advancement. The trains are designed to resemble vintage electric streetcars, complete with period-appropriate details. The American Waterfront station is themed as a bustling city terminal, while the Port Discovery station evokes a futuristic, yet retro, vision of the future. The railway serves as a vital link, connecting the two themed lands and offering glimpses of the park's overall narrative of exploration and discovery.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the open-air or enclosed cars at either the American Waterfront or Port Discovery stations. The journey takes approximately 3 minutes, traversing a route that offers panoramic views of the surrounding areas, including the New York Harbor and the futuristic landscapes of Port Discovery. The ride is smooth and quiet, utilizing electric motors for propulsion. The trains pass through themed tunnels and over bridges, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The DisneySea Electric Railway is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the attraction, though assistance may be required. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
