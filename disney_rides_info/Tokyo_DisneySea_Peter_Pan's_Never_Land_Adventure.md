# Peter Pan's Never Land Adventure
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Peter Pan's Never Land Adventure is a trackless dark ride located in the Neverland section of Tokyo DisneySea's Fantasy Springs expansion. The attraction, developed by Walt Disney Imagineering, immerses guests in the world of Peter Pan. It is a significant addition to the park, offering a technologically advanced and highly themed experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative follows the story of Peter Pan, Wendy, John, and Michael's adventures in Never Land. Guests become part of the story, joining the Darling children as they journey to Never Land. The queue line and ride environment are meticulously themed to resemble locations from the film, including the Darling children's nursery, Skull Rock, and Captain Hook's pirate ship. The immersive setting utilizes elaborate sets, projections, and special effects to create a convincing illusion of flight and adventure.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board ride vehicles that travel through multiple scenes, experiencing various encounters with Peter Pan, Tinker Bell, Captain Hook, and other characters. The trackless ride system allows for fluid movement and dynamic interactions with the environments. The attraction incorporates 3D projections, animatronics, and sensory effects such as wind and water to enhance the storytelling. The ride duration is approximately 6 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a general audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or those prone to motion sickness should consider the ride's effects before experiencing it. Specific health warnings are posted at the attraction entrance.
