# Meet ‘Olu Mel at Duffy and Friends Play House
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Meet ‘Olu Mel at Duffy and Friends Play House is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in the World Bazaar area of Disneyland Hong Kong. It allows guests to interact with ‘Olu Mel, a turtle character from the Duffy and Friends franchise. The attraction provides photo opportunities and brief interactions with the character in a themed environment.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a tropical beach house, reflecting ‘Olu Mel's origins as a ukulele-playing turtle from the Hawaiian island of O'ahu. The Play House features vibrant colors, beach-themed props, and a relaxed atmosphere. The setting is designed to immerse guests in a playful and inviting environment, consistent with the Duffy and Friends storyline of friendship and adventure.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area before entering the Play House. Upon entering, guests are guided to a meeting area where ‘Olu Mel is present. Guests are given the opportunity to take photos with ‘Olu Mel and briefly interact with the character. The interaction typically lasts for a few minutes per group. The attraction utilizes standard character meet-and-greet procedures, including a PhotoPass service for professional photographs.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. Guests are advised to be mindful of other guests and to follow the instructions provided by the Cast Members.
