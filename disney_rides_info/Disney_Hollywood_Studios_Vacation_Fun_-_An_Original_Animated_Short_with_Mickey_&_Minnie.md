# Vacation Fun - An Original Animated Short with Mickey & Minnie
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
"Vacation Fun - An Original Animated Short with Mickey & Minnie" is a 2D animated short film attraction located within the Mickey Shorts Theater at Disney's Hollywood Studios. The attraction showcases Mickey Mouse and Minnie Mouse in a series of animated vignettes centered around the theme of vacation and travel. The short film is presented in a dedicated theater environment, providing a comfortable viewing experience for guests.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents a compilation of animated shorts featuring Mickey and Minnie, along with their friends, experiencing various vacation-related scenarios. The vignettes depict the characters engaging in activities such as beach trips, camping adventures, and international travel. The overall theme emphasizes the joy of friendship, exploration, and creating lasting memories. The theater's interior is themed to complement the animated shorts, featuring colorful and playful designs.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter the Mickey Shorts Theater and are seated in a traditional theater setting. The film is projected onto a large screen, utilizing 2D animation techniques. The short film runs for approximately 6 minutes. The experience is primarily visual, with no physical ride elements or interactive components. The theater is equipped with standard seating and is fully accessible.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
"Vacation Fun - An Original Animated Short with Mickey & Minnie" is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height requirements or specific health restrictions associated with this attraction. The experience is designed to be family-friendly and accessible to a wide audience.
