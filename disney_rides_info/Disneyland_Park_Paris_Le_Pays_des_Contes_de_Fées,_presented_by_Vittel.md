# Le Pays des Contes de Fées, presented by Vittel
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Le Pays des Contes de Fées (Fairy Tale Land), presented by Vittel, is a gentle boat ride attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park Paris. It offers a scenic tour through miniature scenes depicting classic Disney fairy tales. The attraction is a scaled-down version of similar rides found in other Disney parks, providing a whimsical and family-friendly experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in a world of beloved Disney stories. Themed around various fairy tales, the ride showcases miniature, brightly colored scenes representing iconic moments from films such as *Pinocchio*, *The Little Mermaid*, *Beauty and the Beast*, *Cinderella*, and *Sleeping Beauty*. The setting is designed to evoke a sense of wonder and enchantment, with detailed models, vibrant colors, and gentle music.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board small, open-air boats that navigate a winding waterway. The boats glide past a series of miniature sets, each representing a different fairy tale. The ride features animated figures, detailed scenery, and subtle lighting effects to enhance the storytelling. The journey lasts approximately 5 minutes, providing a leisurely and visually engaging experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Le Pays des Contes de Fées is designed for a general audience, particularly families with young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues should be aware that boarding and disembarking the boats may require assistance. The ride is generally considered suitable for all ages, but individuals sensitive to dark environments or enclosed spaces should take this into consideration.
