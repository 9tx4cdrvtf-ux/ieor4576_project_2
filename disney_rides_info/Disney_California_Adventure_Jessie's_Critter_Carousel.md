# Jessie's Critter Carousel
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Jessie's Critter Carousel is a carousel attraction located in Pixar Pier at Disney California Adventure. It features a variety of whimsical, hand-painted animal figures inspired by the *Toy Story* franchise. The carousel was manufactured by Zamperla and opened in 2018, replacing the former King Triton's Carousel. It offers a family-friendly experience and is a key attraction within the Pixar Pier area.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around the character Jessie from the *Toy Story* films. Guests are invited to ride on a collection of playful, oversized critters that Jessie has gathered. The carousel's design incorporates elements of a rustic, hand-crafted aesthetic, with vibrant colors and playful details. The surrounding area features themed decorations and music, enhancing the immersive experience within the Pixar Pier environment.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of the 56 ride vehicles, which include various animal figures such as a turtle, snake, and a skunk. The carousel rotates in a circular motion, ascending and descending gently. The ride cycle lasts approximately two minutes. The attraction utilizes traditional carousel technology, with a central mechanism powering the rotation and vertical movement of the ride vehicles.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Jessie's Critter Carousel is designed for a general audience, particularly families with young children. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or other health concerns should consult with a cast member before riding.
