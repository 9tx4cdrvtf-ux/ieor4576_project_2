# Marvel Universe
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Marvel Universe is an interactive area within Tomorrowland at Shanghai Disney Resort, dedicated to the Marvel Cinematic Universe. It offers a variety of experiences, including meet-and-greets, interactive exhibits, and a themed stunt show. The area is designed to immerse guests in the world of Marvel superheroes and villains.

## Narrative and Theming
The area is themed as a Stark Industries facility, showcasing advanced technology and providing a hub for superhero activity. Guests are presented with the opportunity to become recruits and interact with various Marvel characters. The environment features futuristic architecture, detailed props, and thematic soundscapes to enhance the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
The primary attraction is the "Marvel Universe Live!" stunt show, featuring live performances by stunt actors portraying Marvel characters. The show incorporates pyrotechnics, special effects, and aerial stunts. Guests can also participate in interactive experiences, such as meeting Spider-Man and other Marvel heroes. The area also features photo opportunities and themed merchandise locations. The duration of the stunt show varies, typically lasting around 20 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Marvel Universe is designed for a broad audience, particularly those interested in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. There are no specific height restrictions for entering the area. However, the stunt show may contain loud noises, flashing lights, and pyrotechnics, which may not be suitable for all guests, particularly those with sensitivities to such effects. Guests with pre-existing health conditions should consider their personal limitations before participating.
