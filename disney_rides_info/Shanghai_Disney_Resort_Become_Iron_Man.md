# Become Iron Man
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Become Iron Man is a motion-based simulator attraction located in the Marvel Universe area of Shanghai Disney Resort. It allows guests to experience a simulated flight in an Iron Man Mark L armor, offering a 3D cinematic experience. The attraction was developed by Walt Disney Imagineering and is a unique offering within Disney's theme park portfolio, blending immersive storytelling with cutting-edge technology.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are recruited as potential Iron Man armor testers at the Stark Expo, a showcase of Tony Stark's technological advancements. The narrative centers around a mission to defend Shanghai from a Chitauri invasion. The queue area features interactive displays and exhibits detailing Iron Man's technology and the threat posed by the alien invaders. The environment is designed to resemble a Stark Industries facility, complete with holographic projections and detailed set pieces.

## Attraction Experience
The experience begins with guests entering a pre-show area where they receive mission briefings. Subsequently, guests board a motion simulator vehicle, which seats them in a row facing a large, curved screen. The simulator synchronizes with the 3D film, creating the illusion of flight through Shanghai. Sensory effects, including wind, water mist, and seat vibrations, enhance the immersive experience. The attraction lasts approximately 4 minutes, including pre-show elements.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Become Iron Man is designed for a broad audience, although it is recommended for guests aged 4 and older. Guests must meet a minimum height requirement of 102 cm (40 inches). Due to the motion simulation and potential for visual effects, guests with pre-existing health conditions, including but not limited to heart problems, back or neck issues, and those prone to motion sickness, are advised to consider their condition before riding. Expectant mothers are also advised against riding.
