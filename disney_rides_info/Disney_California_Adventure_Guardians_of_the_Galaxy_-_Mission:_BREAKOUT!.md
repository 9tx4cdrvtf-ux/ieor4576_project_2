# Guardians of the Galaxy - Mission: BREAKOUT!
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Guardians of the Galaxy – Mission: BREAKOUT! is a drop tower dark ride located in the Hollywood Land area of Disney California Adventure. Replacing the Twilight Zone Tower of Terror, the attraction utilizes the same ride system, manufactured by Intamin, but features a new theme based on the *Guardians of the Galaxy* film franchise. It is a significant thrill ride and a major draw for guests visiting the park.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers on the Collector, Taneleer Tivan, who has imprisoned the Guardians of the Galaxy within his fortress. Guests are recruited by Rocket, via a pre-show video, to assist in the Guardians' escape. The queue and ride environment are themed to the Collector's extensive collection of artifacts and creatures, creating a visually rich and immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a gantry lift, which ascends through the fortress. The ride features randomized drop sequences synchronized with music and visual effects, including projections and lighting. The lift travels up and down the tower multiple times, providing varying ride experiences. The ride duration is approximately three minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a broad audience, but due to its intense nature, it is recommended for guests who enjoy thrill rides. The height requirement is 40 inches (102 cm). Guests with certain medical conditions, including heart problems, back or neck issues, and those who are pregnant, are advised against riding.
