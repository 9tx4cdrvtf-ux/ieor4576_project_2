# The Hall of Presidents
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
The Hall of Presidents is a theatrical Audio-Animatronics show located in Liberty Square at the Magic Kingdom. It features lifelike robotic figures of all U.S. Presidents, offering a historical presentation of the American presidency. The attraction is a significant component of the park's educational and patriotic offerings, showcasing Disney's advanced animatronics technology.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is housed within a grand theater designed to resemble Independence Hall in Philadelphia. The pre-show introduces the concept of the American presidency and the importance of the office. The main show presents a chronological overview of the presidency, culminating in a speech by the current U.S. President. The setting is designed to evoke a sense of solemnity and respect for the nation's leaders.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter the theater and are seated to watch a pre-show film. Following the film, the main show begins. The curtains open to reveal the stage, where Audio-Animatronics figures of all U.S. Presidents are present. Each figure is programmed with realistic movements and vocalizations. The show incorporates lighting, sound effects, and a patriotic musical score. The entire experience lasts approximately 25 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Hall of Presidents is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction is a seated show and is accessible to guests using wheelchairs. Guests sensitive to loud noises or sudden movements should be aware that the show includes these elements.
