# Turtle Talk With Crush
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Turtle Talk With Crush is an interactive show attraction located in The Seas with Nemo & Friends Pavilion at Epcot. The attraction utilizes advanced digital puppetry and real-time animation to facilitate a conversation between guests and Crush, the sea turtle character from the Disney-Pixar film *Finding Nemo*. It is a unique blend of live performance and technology, offering an engaging experience for audiences of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests enter a simulated underwater theater, designed to resemble the ocean environment. The setting is themed to the world of *Finding Nemo*, with coral reefs, animated sea creatures, and ambient sound effects. The premise involves Crush, appearing on a large screen, interacting directly with the audience. He answers questions, tells jokes, and engages in improvised conversations, creating a sense of direct communication.

## Attraction Experience
The show begins with a brief introduction, followed by Crush's appearance. Using a combination of puppetry and computer animation, Crush's movements and responses are controlled by a performer backstage. The performer improvises based on audience interactions, making each show unique. The show lasts approximately 15 minutes. The technology allows Crush to "see" and respond to individual audience members, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Turtle Talk With Crush is designed for a general audience, with no height restrictions. The attraction is suitable for all ages, including young children. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions. The show is presented in English.
