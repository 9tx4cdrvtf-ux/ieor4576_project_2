# Blanche-Neige et les Sept Nains®
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Blanche-Neige et les Sept Nains® (Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs) is a dark ride located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park Paris. Based on the 1937 Disney animated film *Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs*, the attraction takes guests through key scenes from the story. It is a classic dark ride, utilizing a track-based system to transport guests through various themed environments.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the fairytale world of Snow White. The narrative follows the story of Snow White, a princess pursued by her jealous stepmother, the Evil Queen. Guests witness scenes such as Snow White's encounter with the Seven Dwarfs, her escape from the Queen's huntsman, and the Queen's transformation into the Old Hag. The environments are designed to evoke the atmosphere of the film, featuring detailed sets, animatronic figures, and special effects.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board mine carts that travel along a predetermined track. The ride progresses through a series of scenes, each depicting a significant moment from the film. The experience incorporates various effects, including lighting, sound, and projections, to enhance the storytelling. The ride duration is approximately 2 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for a general audience. There are no height restrictions. Guests with a history of heart conditions, back problems, or who are pregnant should consider the ride's intensity before boarding.
