# Ant-Man and The Wasp: Nano Battle!
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Ant-Man and The Wasp: Nano Battle! is a 3D interactive dark ride attraction located in the Tomorrowland area of Disneyland Hong Kong. It is a unique attraction, being the first Marvel-themed ride-through attraction at a Disney park featuring Ant-Man and The Wasp. The ride system was developed by Walt Disney Imagineering.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative places guests as recruits of S.H.I.E.L.D., tasked with assisting Ant-Man and The Wasp in a mission to defend Hong Kong from Hydra. The villainous Arnim Zola has unleashed an army of Swarmbots, and guests are equipped with EMP Blasters to shrink and eliminate them. The queue line features interactive elements and displays related to the Quantum Realm and Pym Technologies. The ride environment immerses guests in a high-tech setting, blending physical sets with digital projections.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a vehicle that travels through various scenes. Riders use their EMP Blasters to target Swarmbots and other threats on screens. The ride incorporates 3D projections, physical sets, and special effects such as heat and wind to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 4 minutes. The attraction features a high score system, allowing guests to compete for the highest number of targets hit.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a general audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or those prone to motion sickness should consider the ride's effects before boarding.
