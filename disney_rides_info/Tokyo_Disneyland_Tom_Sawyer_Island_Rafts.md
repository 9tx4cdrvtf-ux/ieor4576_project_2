# Tom Sawyer Island Rafts
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Tom Sawyer Island Rafts is a transportation attraction located within the Adventureland area of Tokyo Disneyland. It provides guests with access to Tom Sawyer Island, a themed land based on the stories of Mark Twain. The attraction consists of two raft vessels that ferry guests across the Rivers of America.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the world of Mark Twain's novels, specifically *The Adventures of Tom Sawyer*. The rafts are designed to resemble rustic, wooden vessels, evoking the era of river travel in the American South. The queue area and loading dock are themed to blend seamlessly with the Adventureland environment, featuring wooden structures, crates, and other props that enhance the illusion of a frontier setting.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of the two rafts at a designated loading dock. The rafts are manually operated by cast members, who steer the vessels across the Rivers of America. The journey is a short, approximately two-minute ride, providing views of the surrounding scenery, including the island itself and the various attractions located there. The rafts transport guests to and from Tom Sawyer Island, allowing them to explore the island's various interactive elements and attractions.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility impairments can access the rafts, though assistance may be required. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions associated with riding the Tom Sawyer Island Rafts.
