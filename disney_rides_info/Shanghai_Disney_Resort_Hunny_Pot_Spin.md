# Hunny Pot Spin
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Hunny Pot Spin is a spinning teacup ride located in the Gardens of Imagination at Shanghai Disney Resort. The attraction is themed after the classic Disney character Winnie the Pooh and his love for honey. It is a family-friendly ride designed to provide a gentle and whimsical experience for guests of all ages. The ride system is a standard teacup ride, manufactured by Zamperla.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the Hundred Acre Wood, the fictional setting of Winnie the Pooh. Guests board oversized honey pots, each capable of seating multiple riders. The surrounding environment features vibrant colors, whimsical character figures, and themed landscaping, all designed to evoke the atmosphere of the beloved animated series. The ride's narrative centers around Pooh's pursuit of honey, with visual and audio elements reinforcing this theme throughout the experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter the queue and board their designated honey pot. Once seated, the ride begins with the honey pots rotating on a circular platform. Riders can control the spinning of their individual honey pot via a central wheel. The ride features gentle music and sound effects, including Pooh's voice and honey-related sound cues. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Hunny Pot Spin is designed for a general audience, including children and adults. There are no height restrictions. Guests with a history of motion sickness or other health concerns should consider the spinning nature of the ride before boarding. Pregnant women and individuals with back or neck problems are advised to exercise caution.
