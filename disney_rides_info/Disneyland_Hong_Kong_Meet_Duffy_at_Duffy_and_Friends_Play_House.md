# Meet Duffy at Duffy and Friends Play House
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Meet Duffy at Duffy and Friends Play House is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in the Fantasy Gardens area of Disneyland Hong Kong. The attraction allows guests to interact with Duffy the Disney Bear and his friends, including ShellieMay, Gelatoni, StellaLou, and CookieAnn. It is a walk-through experience designed to provide photo opportunities and character interactions.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a cozy playhouse, reflecting the friendly and inviting nature of Duffy and his friends. The environment is designed to resemble a comfortable living space, with soft furnishings, colorful decorations, and themed props. The setting reinforces the characters' personalities and their shared friendship, creating a welcoming atmosphere for guests of all ages.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area before entering the playhouse. Upon entering, guests are guided through the space, where they can meet and take photos with Duffy and his friends. The characters rotate throughout the day. The experience typically lasts for a few minutes, allowing for individual interactions and photo opportunities. The attraction utilizes traditional character meet-and-greet techniques, with cast members assisting in the process.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may require assistance. No specific health warnings or restrictions are in place.
