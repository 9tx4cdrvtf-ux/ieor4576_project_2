# Indiana Jones™ Epic Stunt Spectacular!
**Location:** Disney Hollywood Studios

## Overview
The Indiana Jones™ Epic Stunt Spectacular! is a live-action stunt show located in Disney's Hollywood Studios. It is a theatrical performance featuring elaborate stunts, pyrotechnics, and special effects, based on the *Indiana Jones* film franchise. The show is a significant attraction within the park, providing entertainment and demonstrating the art of movie stunts.

## Narrative and Theming
The show is themed around the filming of a movie scene, placing guests as onlookers to the action. The narrative follows Indiana Jones as he navigates various perilous situations, including a recreation of scenes from the *Raiders of the Lost Ark* film. The setting includes a recreation of an ancient temple, a bustling marketplace, and other environments inspired by the films.

## Attraction Experience
The show begins with a pre-show featuring audience participation. The main performance involves stunt performers executing various feats, including fist fights, whip cracks, falls, and explosions. The show incorporates practical effects, such as fire, collapsing structures, and rolling vehicles. The entire performance lasts approximately 30 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for a general audience. There are no height restrictions. Guests should be aware of loud noises, pyrotechnics, and sudden movements. Guests with certain health conditions, such as heart problems or sensitivity to loud noises, should consider the show's effects before attending.
