# “Once Upon a Time” Adventure
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
“Once Upon a Time” Adventure is a dark ride attraction located within the Gardens of Imagination area of Shanghai Disneyland. The ride is themed around classic Disney Princess stories, offering guests a journey through iconic scenes from various animated films. The attraction utilizes a trackless ride system and was manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering. It is a significant family-friendly attraction within the park, providing a gentle and immersive experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers on a journey through the stories of several Disney Princesses. Guests are invited to relive key moments from films such as *Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs*, *Cinderella*, and *Tangled*. The ride environment is designed to evoke the atmosphere of each film, featuring detailed sets, animatronic figures, and projected imagery. The overall theming emphasizes the themes of love, courage, and the triumph of good over evil, central to the Disney Princess narrative.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board ride vehicles that move along a trackless path, allowing for dynamic movement through the scenes. The ride incorporates various special effects, including lighting, sound, and projections, to enhance the storytelling. Animatronic figures of the princesses and other characters are featured throughout the ride. The duration of the ride is approximately 5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
“Once Upon a Time” Adventure is designed for a general audience, particularly families with young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or other health concerns should consult the park's advisory information before riding.
