# The Happy Ride with Baymax
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
The Happy Ride with Baymax is a trackless, spinning ride located in Tomorrowland at Tokyo Disneyland. The attraction, manufactured by a subsidiary of Walt Disney Imagineering, opened in 2020. It is themed around the animated film *Big Hero 6* and features Baymax, the inflatable healthcare companion robot, as the central character. The ride offers a lighthearted and energetic experience suitable for a wide range of guests.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are invited to join Baymax for his first-ever "Happy Ride" experience. The setting is a futuristic, neon-lit laboratory where Baymax and his friends are testing various health and happiness-related technologies. The queue area features interactive elements and displays showcasing Baymax's development and the science behind his caregiving abilities. The overall atmosphere is bright, cheerful, and emphasizes the themes of friendship and well-being.

## Attraction Experience
The ride vehicles, designed to resemble medical pods, move along a trackless path through the laboratory. Each vehicle spins independently while navigating the course, choreographed to music and lighting effects. Baymax appears throughout the ride, interacting with guests and encouraging them to embrace happiness. The ride incorporates various sensory effects, including flashing lights and upbeat music. The duration of the ride is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Happy Ride with Baymax is designed for a general audience. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or those prone to motion sickness should consider the ride's spinning motion before boarding.
