# Princess Pavilion
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
The Princess Pavilion is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park Paris. It allows guests to meet and interact with various Disney Princesses. The attraction is a permanent fixture, offering a dedicated space for character interactions and photo opportunities.

## Narrative and Theming
The Princess Pavilion is themed as a charming, ornate building, designed to evoke a sense of royalty and elegance. The exterior features architectural details reminiscent of various Disney Princess castles, creating a fairytale atmosphere. Inside, the queue area is decorated with portraits, tapestries, and other regal elements, enhancing the immersive experience. The setting aims to transport guests into the world of Disney Princesses, providing a backdrop for memorable interactions.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue within the Pavilion, progressing through the themed queue area. Upon reaching the meeting area, guests are given the opportunity to meet and take photographs with a randomly selected Disney Princess. The Princesses present vary throughout the day and are subject to change. The interaction typically lasts for several minutes, allowing for conversation and photo opportunities. The entire experience, including queuing, can vary in duration depending on crowd levels.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Princess Pavilion is primarily targeted towards children and families. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues are accommodated via a separate queue. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions, but guests should be aware that the experience involves close proximity to other guests and potential waiting times.
