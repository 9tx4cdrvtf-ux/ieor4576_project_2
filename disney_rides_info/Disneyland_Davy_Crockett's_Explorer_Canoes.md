# Davy Crockett's Explorer Canoes
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Davy Crockett's Explorer Canoes is a water-based attraction at Disneyland, offering guests a hands-on experience of paddling a replica frontier-era canoe around the Rivers of America. The attraction is unique in that it requires guests to actively participate in propelling the vessel. It is a classic Disneyland experience, emphasizing physical activity and immersion in the park's Frontierland setting.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative places guests in the role of early American explorers, navigating the waterways of the American frontier. The canoes are designed to resemble those used by Davy Crockett and other pioneers. The Rivers of America, which the canoes traverse, are themed to represent various aspects of the American wilderness, including Native American villages and wildlife scenes, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of several canoes, each accommodating approximately twenty passengers. Cast members, dressed in period attire, provide basic paddling instructions. The canoes are propelled solely by the guests, using paddles to navigate the river. The journey takes approximately 15-20 minutes, offering views of the surrounding Frontierland scenery, including the Haunted Mansion and Big Thunder Mountain Railroad.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Davy Crockett's Explorer Canoes is suitable for guests of all ages and physical abilities, provided they are capable of paddling. There are no height restrictions. Guests must be able to board and disembark the canoe with minimal assistance. Guests with mobility issues or those unable to paddle may find the attraction challenging.
