# Seven Dwarfs Mine Train
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
The Seven Dwarfs Mine Train is a family-friendly steel roller coaster located in the Fantasyland section of Disney's Magic Kingdom. Manufactured by Vekoma, the attraction opened in 2014 and replaced the former 20,000 Leagues Under the Sea attraction. It is themed after Disney's 1937 animated film *Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs* and is a significant addition to the park's immersive storytelling.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the world of the Seven Dwarfs, entering a queue that winds through a rustic, animated environment. The ride's narrative follows the Dwarfs' journey to and from their diamond mine. The attraction features Audio-Animatronics of the Dwarfs, Snow White, and the Evil Queen. The mine itself is depicted with glowing gems and animated scenes, enhancing the storytelling experience.

## Attraction Experience
The ride vehicles are designed to resemble mine cars and feature a unique "tilting" motion, enhancing the sensation of following the terrain. The coaster includes several drops and turns, offering views of Fantasyland. The ride incorporates projections and special effects, including scenes of the Dwarfs singing "Heigh-Ho" and a final encounter with the Evil Queen. The ride duration is approximately 2 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Seven Dwarfs Mine Train is designed for a broad audience, particularly families. The minimum height requirement is 38 inches (97 cm). Guests with a history of heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant should consider the ride's intensity before boarding.
