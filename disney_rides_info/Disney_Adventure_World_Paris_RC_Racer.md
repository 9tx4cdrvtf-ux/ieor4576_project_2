# RC Racer
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
RC Racer is a steel shuttle roller coaster located in the Toy Story Playland area of Disney Adventure World Paris. Manufactured by Intamin, the ride simulates a high-speed race between a toy car and its remote control. It is a key attraction within Toy Story Playland, offering a thrilling experience for guests of various ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride is themed around Andy's toy car, RC, from the *Toy Story* film franchise. Guests are immersed in a giant toy box environment, scaled to the size of Andy's toys. The coaster track is designed to resemble a Hot Wheels track, with the ride vehicle being a large-scale RC car. The queue line and surrounding area are filled with oversized toys and props, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a coaster vehicle designed to resemble RC. The ride begins with a launch up a vertical U-shaped track. The vehicle then travels back and forth along the track, reaching speeds of up to 80 km/h (50 mph). The ride provides a sensation of weightlessness during the transitions. The entire experience lasts approximately 80 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
RC Racer is designed for guests who meet the minimum height requirement of 1.20 meters (47 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, particularly those affecting the heart, back, or neck, are advised to consult with a medical professional before riding. Pregnant women and guests susceptible to motion sickness are also discouraged from experiencing the attraction.
