# Alice in Wonderland
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Alice in Wonderland is a dark ride attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland. Based on the 1951 animated film of the same name, the ride transports guests through key scenes from the story. It is a classic Fantasyland attraction, known for its whimsical theming and imaginative use of practical effects.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the world of Lewis Carroll's *Alice's Adventures in Wonderland*. Guests board oversized "caterpillar" vehicles and journey through scenes depicting Alice's encounters with iconic characters such as the White Rabbit, the Mad Hatter, the Cheshire Cat, and the Queen of Hearts. The ride's theming utilizes vibrant colors, oversized props, and stylized character figures to create a surreal and fantastical atmosphere.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with guests entering a queue area themed to the whimsical world of Wonderland. The caterpillar vehicles then travel through a series of scenes. These scenes incorporate a combination of physical sets, blacklight effects, and audio-animatronics to bring the story to life. The ride culminates in a confrontation with the Queen of Hearts, followed by a return to the real world. The ride duration is approximately 3.5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Alice in Wonderland is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or a sensitivity to enclosed spaces should be aware of the ride's effects. The ride vehicles are open, but the ride path includes some tight turns and sudden movements.
