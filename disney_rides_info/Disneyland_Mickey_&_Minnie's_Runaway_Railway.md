# Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway is a trackless dark ride located in Mickey's Toontown at Disneyland. It is based on the recent Mickey Mouse cartoon shorts and utilizes a trackless ride system developed by Walt Disney Imagineering. The attraction is significant as it replaced the former "Roger Rabbit's Car Toon Spin" and represents a modern addition to the park's family-friendly offerings.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests enter the El CapiTOON Theater, where they are invited to watch the premiere of a new Mickey Mouse cartoon short. The queue area features posters and props referencing various Mickey Mouse cartoons. The premise involves Mickey and Minnie Mouse embarking on a picnic, which is disrupted when they encounter Goofy, who is driving a train. The cartoon short's narrative then unfolds, leading guests through a series of chaotic and whimsical scenarios.

## Attraction Experience
The ride vehicles, designed to resemble train cars, move along a trackless path, allowing for dynamic movements and transitions between scenes. The attraction incorporates a combination of physical sets, projections, and special effects, including immersive environments and 3D imagery without the need for 3D glasses. The ride experience lasts approximately four minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway is designed for a general audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with motion sensitivities or pre-existing health conditions should consider the ride's potential effects before boarding.
