# Haunted Mansion
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
The Haunted Mansion is a dark ride attraction located in New Orleans Square at Disneyland. It is a classic Omnimover ride system attraction, designed and built by Walt Disney Imagineering. The Haunted Mansion is a significant and popular attraction, known for its innovative use of special effects, storytelling, and its blend of humor and spookiness.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents itself as a stately, yet decaying, Southern Gothic mansion inhabited by 999 happy haunts. Guests are invited to tour the mansion, encountering various spectral residents and supernatural phenomena. The narrative unfolds through a series of interconnected scenes, each depicting a different ghostly encounter, from a ballroom filled with dancing spirits to a graveyard populated by singing busts. The theming emphasizes a sense of mystery and the supernatural, with a focus on visual gags and illusions.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board "Doom Buggies," individual Omnimover vehicles that guide them through the mansion. The ride utilizes a combination of practical effects, such as Pepper's Ghost illusions, animatronics, and projected imagery to create the ghostly apparitions. The ride lasts approximately 10 minutes, traversing various rooms and scenes, culminating in a ride through the graveyard and a final encounter with hitchhiking ghosts.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Haunted Mansion is generally suitable for all ages, though some scenes may be considered mildly frightening for younger children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with heart conditions, back problems, or those who are sensitive to strobe lights should exercise caution.
