# River Rogue Keelboats
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
The River Rogue Keelboats is a slow-moving boat ride attraction located in the Adventureland area of Disneyland Park Paris. It offers guests a scenic tour of the Rivers of the Far West, providing views of themed environments and animatronic figures. The attraction is a classic boat ride experience, similar to those found in other Disney parks, and contributes to the immersive theming of Adventureland.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the atmosphere of the American frontier. The keelboats, designed to resemble early river transport vessels, navigate through a landscape populated by animatronic figures and themed environments. These environments depict scenes of frontier life, including Native American villages, wildlife encounters, and glimpses of the untamed wilderness. The overall narrative focuses on exploration and the spirit of adventure.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of several keelboats, each accommodating a limited number of passengers. The boats are propelled along a predetermined course through the Rivers of the Far West. The ride duration is approximately 5 minutes. During the journey, guests observe various scenes, including animatronic animals and figures, and pass through themed environments. The ride is a gentle experience suitable for all ages.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The River Rogue Keelboats is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find boarding and disembarking the boats challenging. The attraction is generally considered safe, but guests with pre-existing health conditions should consult with a medical professional if they have concerns.
