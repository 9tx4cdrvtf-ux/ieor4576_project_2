# Expedition Everest - Legend of the Forbidden Mountain Single Rider
**Location:** Animal Kingdom

## Overview
Expedition Everest - Legend of the Forbidden Mountain is a steel roller coaster located in the Asia section of Disney's Animal Kingdom. Manufactured by Vekoma, the attraction combines a high-speed roller coaster experience with a dark ride component, incorporating both forward and backward motion. It is notable for its immersive theming and its integration into the park's narrative of exploration and discovery. The Single Rider queue allows guests to experience the attraction with potentially shorter wait times.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers around a journey to the base of Mount Everest, where guests are purportedly traveling to find the Yeti. The queue and surrounding areas are meticulously themed to resemble a Tibetan village, complete with artifacts, expedition equipment, and the visual presence of the mountain itself. The story involves a broken track and the Yeti, a mythical creature said to inhabit the mountain, which is encountered during the ride.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a train designed to resemble a narrow-gauge railway car. The ride begins with a slow ascent, followed by a high-speed outdoor section. The train then enters the mountain, encountering a broken track and a backward section. The ride incorporates various sensory effects, including darkness, wind, and audio-animatronic figures, culminating in a close encounter with the Yeti. The ride duration is approximately three minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a broad audience, but has a height requirement of 44 inches (112 cm). Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding. The Single Rider queue is available for guests who are willing to ride alone to potentially reduce wait times.
