# TRON Lightcycle Power Run – Presented by Chevrolet
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
TRON Lightcycle Power Run is a launched steel roller coaster located in Tomorrowland at Shanghai Disney Resort. Manufactured by Vekoma, the ride is themed after the *TRON* film franchise and is a significant attraction within the park, known for its unique ride vehicles and immersive environment. It is one of the fastest attractions at Shanghai Disney Resort.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the digital world of *TRON*, entering the Grid as "Users" participating in a Lightcycle race. The queue area features a futuristic aesthetic, with neon lights and digital projections. The ride's narrative places guests within a high-speed competition, racing against other programs within the Grid. The attraction's design incorporates elements from the *TRON* films, including Lightcycle vehicles and a futuristic cityscape.

## Attraction Experience
Riders board individual Lightcycle vehicles, which are arranged in a launch position. The ride begins with a high-speed launch, propelling riders through a darkened show building filled with projections, special effects, and lighting. The coaster then exits the building for a brief outdoor section before returning inside for the final portion of the ride. The ride incorporates a variety of sensory effects, including music, lighting, and sound effects, to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of various ages, provided they meet the height requirement of 120 cm (47 inches). Guests with certain health conditions, including heart problems, back or neck issues, or those who are pregnant, are advised against riding. Guests are also advised to secure loose articles before boarding.
