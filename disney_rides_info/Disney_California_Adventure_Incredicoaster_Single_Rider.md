# Incredicoaster Single Rider
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
The Incredicoaster Single Rider is a steel launched roller coaster located in Pixar Pier at Disney California Adventure. Manufactured by Intamin, the coaster is themed after the Disney-Pixar film *The Incredibles*. It is a significant attraction within the park, offering high-speed thrills and immersive theming. The Single Rider queue allows guests traveling alone or those willing to be separated from their party to experience the ride with a potentially shorter wait time.

## Narrative and Theming
The Incredicoaster's narrative centers around the Parr family's attempt to catch Jack-Jack, who has escaped and is using his various superpowers. The queue and ride experience incorporate elements from the film, including character appearances, sound effects, and visual gags. The coaster's track layout is designed to simulate Jack-Jack's unpredictable movements, with the ride vehicles representing the family's pursuit.

## Attraction Experience
Guests in the Single Rider queue are directed to fill available seats on the coaster trains. The ride begins with a high-speed launch, followed by a series of inversions, drops, and turns. The coaster incorporates enclosed tunnels with special effects, including audio-animatronics and lighting, to enhance the storytelling. The ride duration is approximately two minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Incredicoaster is designed for guests of all ages, but riders must meet a minimum height requirement of 48 inches (122 cm). Guests with certain medical conditions, including heart problems, back or neck issues, or those who are pregnant, are advised against riding. The attraction also features fast speeds and intense forces that may not be suitable for all riders.
