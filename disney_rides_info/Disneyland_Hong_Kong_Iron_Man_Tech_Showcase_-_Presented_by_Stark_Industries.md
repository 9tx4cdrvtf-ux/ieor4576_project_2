# Iron Man Tech Showcase - Presented by Stark Industries
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
The Iron Man Tech Showcase - Presented by Stark Industries is a motion-simulator based attraction located in the Tomorrowland area of Disneyland Hong Kong. It opened in January 2017, and is themed around the Marvel Cinematic Universe, specifically the Iron Man character. The attraction allows guests to experience a simulated flight over Hong Kong alongside Iron Man.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are invited to Stark Expo, a Stark Industries exhibition showcasing Iron Man's technology. The narrative centers on Iron Man's efforts to protect Hong Kong from Hydra forces. The queue area features exhibits displaying Iron Man suits and Stark Industries innovations. The pre-show introduces the mission and provides context for the simulated flight experience. The overall setting immerses guests in the world of Iron Man, blending futuristic technology with the urban landscape of Hong Kong.

## Attraction Experience
The attraction utilizes a motion simulator to create the sensation of flight. Guests board a vehicle designed to resemble a Stark Industries vehicle. The ride experience involves a pre-show followed by the main simulator sequence. During the simulated flight, guests encounter various threats and engage in aerial combat alongside Iron Man. The experience incorporates 3D visuals, motion effects, and environmental effects such as wind and water to enhance the immersive experience. The total duration of the attraction is approximately 15 minutes, including the pre-show.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a general audience. There is a height requirement of 102 cm (40 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, including but not limited to heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their condition before riding.
