# Star Wars Hyperspace Mountain Single Rider
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Star Wars Hyperspace Mountain Single Rider is a steel roller coaster attraction located within Discoveryland at Disneyland Park Paris. It is a re-theming of the original Space Mountain: Mission 2, incorporating the Star Wars franchise. The ride is manufactured by Vekoma and offers a single rider queue to expedite wait times. It is a significant attraction, drawing large crowds and contributing to the park's immersive Star Wars experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction places guests within the Star Wars universe, specifically during a Rebel Alliance mission. Riders are recruited as pilots, tasked with navigating through space to intercept an Imperial Star Destroyer. The ride's theming includes iconic Star Wars elements such as TIE fighters, X-wings, and the visual representation of hyperspace travel. The queue line and ride environment are designed to immerse guests in the Star Wars atmosphere.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual ride vehicles resembling spacecraft. The coaster features a launch sequence, inversions, and a series of high-speed maneuvers. The ride incorporates projections, special effects, and a custom score to enhance the immersive experience. The duration of the ride is approximately 2 minutes and 30 seconds. The single rider queue allows guests to bypass the main queue by filling empty seats on the ride vehicles.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests who meet the minimum height requirement of 1.2 meters (47 inches). Guests with a history of heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding. The ride involves high speeds, inversions, and sudden movements, which may not be suitable for all guests.
