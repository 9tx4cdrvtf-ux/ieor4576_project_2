# The Royal Reception Hall
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
The Royal Reception Hall is a walk-through attraction located within Fantasyland at Disneyland Hong Kong. It serves as a meet-and-greet location where guests can interact with Disney Princesses. The attraction is thematically integrated into the park's overall fairytale setting, providing a dedicated space for character interactions and photo opportunities.

## Narrative and Theming
The Royal Reception Hall is presented as a grand ballroom within a majestic castle, the home of the Disney Princesses. The architecture and decor are designed to evoke a sense of elegance and royalty, featuring ornate chandeliers, tapestries, and portraits. The setting is intended to immerse guests in a world of fantasy and allow them to feel as though they are visiting a royal court.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a themed area before entering the main reception hall. Inside, they are given the opportunity to meet and take photographs with various Disney Princesses. The specific princesses present may vary throughout the day. The experience is primarily focused on personal interaction and creating memorable moments. The duration of the experience is dependent on the number of guests and the time spent with each princess.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Royal Reception Hall is designed to appeal to guests of all ages, particularly children and families. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. However, guests with mobility issues may find the queue and interaction areas challenging.
