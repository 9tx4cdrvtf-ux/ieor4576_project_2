## Narrative and Theming
Guests are cast as adventurers exploring the ruins of an ancient temple, guided by the archaeologist Indiana Jones. The temple is said to be guarded by the Crystal Skull, a powerful artifact that unleashes curses upon those who disturb it. The queue line winds through the temple grounds, featuring detailed set dressing, ancient hieroglyphs, and foreboding warnings. The narrative builds suspense, hinting at the dangers within the temple and the potential consequences of encountering the Crystal Skull.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board EMVs, which are capable of significant lateral and vertical movement. The ride begins with a jeep journey through the temple, encountering various animatronic figures, special effects, and dramatic scenes. The EMVs navigate through a series of chambers, facing perils such as collapsing ceilings, dart traps, and a giant snake. The ride incorporates audio-animatronics, projections, and lighting to create a highly immersive and thrilling experience, lasting approximately 3 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a broad audience, but contains intense scenes and motion. Guests must be at least 110 cm (43 in) tall to ride. Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding.
