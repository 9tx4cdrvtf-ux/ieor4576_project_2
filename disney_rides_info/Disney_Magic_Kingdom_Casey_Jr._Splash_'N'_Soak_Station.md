# Casey Jr. Splash 'N' Soak Station
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Casey Jr. Splash 'N' Soak Station is a water play area located in Fantasyland at Disney Magic Kingdom. The attraction is themed after the train Casey Jr. from the 1941 Disney animated film *Dumbo*. It provides a refreshing, interactive experience for guests, particularly children, offering a respite from the Florida heat. The attraction is not a ride but a designated splash zone.

## Narrative and Theming
The area is designed to resemble a circus setting, consistent with the film's narrative. Guests encounter Casey Jr., the train, along with various circus-themed elements such as animal figures and water features. The theming immerses guests in the world of *Dumbo*, creating a playful and engaging environment. The area's design incorporates vibrant colors and whimsical details, enhancing the overall experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests can interact with various water features, including spraying jets, geysers, and tipping buckets. Casey Jr. itself serves as a central element, with water spraying from its smokestack and other areas. The attraction allows guests to cool off and play in the water. The experience is self-guided, with no set duration, allowing guests to enjoy the area at their leisure.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Casey Jr. Splash 'N' Soak Station is primarily designed for children. There are no height restrictions. Guests are advised to wear appropriate swimwear or clothing that can get wet. Parents should supervise their children at all times. Guests with sensitivity to water or those who prefer to avoid getting wet may choose to bypass this attraction.
