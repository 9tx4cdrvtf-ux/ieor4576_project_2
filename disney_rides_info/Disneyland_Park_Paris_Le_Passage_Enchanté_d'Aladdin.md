# Le Passage Enchanté d'Aladdin
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Le Passage Enchanté d'Aladdin is a walk-through attraction located in Adventureland at Disneyland Park Paris. It allows guests to experience scenes from Disney's animated film *Aladdin* (1992). The attraction is a themed corridor, offering a series of dioramas and visual displays that depict key moments from the film.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the bustling marketplace of Agrabah and the magical world of Aladdin. The theming focuses on recreating iconic scenes, such as Aladdin's first encounter with the Genie in the Cave of Wonders, the magic carpet ride, and the final confrontation with Jafar. The environment is designed to evoke the atmosphere of the film, utilizing detailed set pieces, lighting effects, and sound design to enhance the storytelling.

## Attraction Experience
Guests proceed through a winding corridor, encountering a series of intricately designed scenes. Each scene features detailed figures, props, and lighting effects to bring the film's moments to life. The attraction utilizes static displays and audio-animatronics to create an immersive experience. The duration of the walk-through is approximately 5-7 minutes, depending on the guest's pace.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Le Passage Enchanté d'Aladdin is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction is a gentle experience and does not involve any physical exertion or sudden movements. Guests with mobility issues can navigate the attraction, but may require assistance. There are no specific health warnings associated with this attraction.
