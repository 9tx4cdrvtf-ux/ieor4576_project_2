# Silly Symphony Swings Single Rider
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Silly Symphony Swings Single Rider is a swing ride attraction located in the Paradise Gardens Park area of Disney California Adventure. The ride is a variation of a classic "wave swinger" type, manufactured by Zamperla. It is themed after the "Silly Symphony" series of animated shorts produced by Walt Disney Studios in the 1930s. The Single Rider queue allows guests to experience the attraction with a shorter wait time by filling in available seats.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's design evokes the whimsical atmosphere of the "Silly Symphony" cartoons. The central structure is adorned with colorful panels and imagery depicting musical instruments and animated characters. The ride area is further enhanced by the surrounding Paradise Gardens Park, which features other attractions and theming elements inspired by classic amusement park settings.

## Attraction Experience
Guests seated in the swings are lifted and rotated in a circular motion. The swings are suspended from a central tower. As the ride progresses, the tower tilts, increasing the speed and height of the swings. The ride cycle lasts approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds. The Single Rider queue merges with the main queue at the loading platform, where cast members assign guests to available seats.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of various ages and heights, provided they meet the minimum height requirement of 40 inches (102 cm). Guests with certain medical conditions, including but not limited to heart problems, back or neck issues, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consult with a medical professional before riding.
