# Mickey’s PhilharMagic
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Mickey’s PhilharMagic is a 4D film attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park Paris. It is a musical show featuring a compilation of classic Disney animated sequences. The attraction utilizes a combination of 3D film projection, in-theater effects, and Audio-Animatronics to create an immersive experience. The show is produced by Walt Disney Imagineering and is a popular family-friendly attraction.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests enter the concert hall of the "PhilharMagic" orchestra, where Donald Duck is preparing for a performance. The premise involves Donald's accidental journey through various Disney animated musical numbers after he puts on Mickey Mouse's Sorcerer's Hat. The show's narrative is a series of musical vignettes, each featuring characters and songs from different Disney animated films. The theater's theming is that of a grand concert hall, with ornate decorations and a large screen acting as the stage.

## Attraction Experience
The attraction begins with guests seated in a large theater equipped with 3D glasses. The show incorporates 3D film projection, along with sensory effects such as wind, water spray, and scents, timed to coincide with the on-screen action. Audio-Animatronic figures, including Donald Duck, are also present. The show lasts approximately 12 minutes, featuring musical sequences from films such as *Beauty and the Beast*, *The Little Mermaid*, and *The Lion King*.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Mickey’s PhilharMagic is designed for a general audience, including children and adults. There are no height restrictions. Guests with sensitivity to strobe lights or strong scents should be aware of these elements within the attraction. Wheelchair access is available.
