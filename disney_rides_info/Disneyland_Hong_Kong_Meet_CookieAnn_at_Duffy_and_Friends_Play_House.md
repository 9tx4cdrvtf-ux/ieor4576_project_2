# Meet CookieAnn at Duffy and Friends Play House
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Meet CookieAnn at Duffy and Friends Play House is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in the Duffy and Friends-themed area of Disneyland Hong Kong. It provides guests with the opportunity to interact with CookieAnn, a character from the Duffy and Friends franchise. The attraction is a simple meet-and-greet, offering photo opportunities and brief interactions with the character.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around a cozy playhouse setting, reflecting the overall Duffy and Friends narrative of friendship and shared experiences. The environment is designed to evoke a sense of warmth and comfort, with pastel colors and playful decorations. The setting is consistent with the broader Duffy and Friends storyline, where the characters enjoy spending time together.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area before entering the playhouse. Upon entering, guests are guided to a designated area where CookieAnn is present. Guests are given the opportunity to take photographs with CookieAnn and briefly interact with the character. The interaction typically lasts for a few minutes per guest or group. The entire experience, including queuing, typically lasts between 10-20 minutes, depending on crowd levels.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. Guests are advised to be mindful of other guests and to follow the instructions provided by the cast members.
