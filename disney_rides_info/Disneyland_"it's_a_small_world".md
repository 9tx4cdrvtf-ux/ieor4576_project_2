# "it's a small world"
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
"it's a small world" is a boat ride attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland. Designed by WED Enterprises (now Walt Disney Imagineering), it is a classic "dark ride" featuring hundreds of Audio-Animatronic dolls representing children from various nations around the world. The attraction is renowned for its iconic theme song and its message of global unity.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers on a journey around the world, celebrating the diversity of cultures through song and dance. The ride's exterior is a whimsical, brightly colored facade. Inside, guests travel through a series of themed rooms representing different continents and regions, each populated by dolls dressed in traditional costumes and singing the attraction's namesake song, "It's a Small World (After All)." The overall atmosphere is intended to be optimistic and celebratory.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a boat that gently floats through a waterway. The boat passes through various scenes, each showcasing a different part of the world. The Audio-Animatronic dolls perform synchronized movements and sing the repetitive theme song. The ride incorporates various special effects, including lighting, projections, and sound, to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
"it's a small world" is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with sensitivity to repetitive music or flashing lights should be aware of these elements. The ride is a slow-moving boat ride and is accessible to guests with mobility impairments, though they may need to transfer from a wheelchair.
