# Cars ROAD TRIP
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
Cars ROAD TRIP is a dark ride attraction located in the Cars Route 66 area of Disney Adventure World Paris. It is a slow-moving, track-based ride that immerses guests in the world of Disney Pixar's *Cars* franchise. The attraction focuses on a journey along Route 66, featuring Audio-Animatronics figures and elaborate set designs.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers around a scenic road trip through the American Southwest, mirroring the setting of the *Cars* films. Guests are transported in a vintage car, encountering various scenes and characters from the movies. The ride incorporates elements of classic Route 66 roadside attractions, such as a dinosaur park and a flooded gas station, all presented within the stylized aesthetic of the *Cars* universe. Theming includes detailed rockwork, desert landscapes, and iconic landmarks.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a vehicle designed to resemble a classic car. The ride begins with a gentle ascent, leading into a series of themed scenes. The journey includes encounters with Audio-Animatronics figures of characters like Mater and Lightning McQueen. The ride utilizes projections, lighting effects, and sound design to enhance the immersive experience. The total ride duration is approximately 4 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Cars ROAD TRIP is designed for a general audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with back or neck problems, or those sensitive to sudden movements, should consider the ride's suitability. The attraction is accessible to guests in wheelchairs.
