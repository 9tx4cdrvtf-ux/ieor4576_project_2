# Selfie Spot with Mickey
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Selfie Spot with Mickey is a character meet-and-greet experience located within Shanghai Disney Resort. It allows guests to interact with and take photographs with the iconic Disney character, Mickey Mouse. The attraction is a permanent fixture within the park, providing a classic Disney experience for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The Selfie Spot with Mickey is themed to resemble a classic, brightly lit photo studio. The setting is designed to be inviting and cheerful, featuring backdrops and props that change seasonally or in accordance with special events. The environment aims to create a sense of anticipation and excitement for the opportunity to meet Mickey Mouse.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area, often decorated with themed elements. Upon reaching the front of the line, guests are escorted to a designated photo area where Mickey Mouse is present. Guests are given the opportunity to pose for photographs with Mickey, utilizing their own cameras or mobile devices. Disney PhotoPass photographers are also available to capture professional-quality images. The interaction typically lasts for a few minutes, allowing for multiple photo opportunities.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Selfie Spot with Mickey is designed for guests of all ages. There are no height requirements or specific health restrictions associated with this attraction. Guests with mobility issues are accommodated through accessible queuing and photo areas.
