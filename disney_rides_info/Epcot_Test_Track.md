# Test Track
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Test Track is a simulated high-speed thrill ride located in the World Discovery section of Epcot at Walt Disney World. It is a dark ride and simulated test track experience, themed around the design and testing of automobiles. The attraction is a collaboration between Walt Disney Imagineering and General Motors. It is notable for its high capacity and its use of a unique ride system allowing for both indoor and outdoor segments.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in a fictional General Motors testing facility. The queue area features interactive exhibits showcasing vehicle design and safety testing. The narrative centers on the process of designing and evaluating a new vehicle, with guests participating in a simulated test of their own design. The attraction's theming emphasizes the technological advancements in automotive engineering.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a six-passenger "SIM Car" and proceed through a series of simulated tests. These tests evaluate the vehicle's capability in categories such as capability, efficiency, responsiveness, and power. The ride culminates in a high-speed outdoor loop around the building, reaching speeds of up to 65 miles per hour (105 km/h). The entire experience lasts approximately five minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Test Track is designed for a broad audience, but has a minimum height requirement of 40 inches (102 cm). Guests with certain health conditions, including heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised against riding. The ride involves sudden starts and stops, as well as exposure to high speeds.
