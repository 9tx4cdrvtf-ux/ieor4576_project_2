# House of the Whispering Willows
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
House of the Whispering Willows is a dark ride attraction located within the World Showcase section of Epcot. It is a slow-moving, omnimover-style ride that utilizes a combination of practical effects, audio-animatronics, and projected imagery to immerse guests in a fictional narrative. The attraction is notable for its intricate theming and its focus on storytelling through visual and auditory experiences.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around a Victorian-era manor house, rumored to be haunted by the spirits of its former inhabitants. Guests are invited to explore the house and uncover the secrets of the Willow family, who once resided there. The narrative unfolds through a series of interconnected scenes, each depicting a different room within the manor and revealing fragments of the family's history and the events leading to their mysterious disappearance. The atmosphere is designed to evoke a sense of unease and suspense, utilizing dim lighting, eerie soundscapes, and the illusion of spectral apparitions.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a continuous-loading ride vehicle that slowly progresses through the manor. The ride features a series of scenes, each showcasing a different room within the house, such as the library, dining room, and ballroom. Audio-animatronic figures and projected illusions depict the Willow family and their interactions. Sensory effects, including temperature changes and subtle air movements, are employed to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately five minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. However, the ride may be frightening for young children or those sensitive to dark environments and suspenseful themes. Guests with heart conditions, back problems, or other health concerns should consult with a medical professional before riding.
