# Test Track Presented by Chevrolet Single Rider
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Test Track Presented by Chevrolet Single Rider is a high-speed, simulated automobile testing attraction located in the World Discovery neighborhood of Epcot at Walt Disney World. The ride is a dark ride and thrill ride hybrid, designed by Walt Disney Imagineering and manufactured by a consortium of companies including Siemens. It allows guests to design their own virtual concept vehicles and then experience them through a series of simulated tests. The attraction is notable for its high capacity and its integration of interactive elements.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in a General Motors automotive testing facility. The queue area features interactive displays showcasing vehicle design and engineering principles. The narrative centers around the testing of vehicles for various performance metrics, including capability, efficiency, responsiveness, and safety. Guests are presented with the opportunity to design their own virtual vehicles before embarking on the ride.

## Attraction Experience
After designing their vehicle, guests board a six-passenger SIM Car. The ride begins with a series of simulated tests, including handling, efficiency, and power, conducted in a climate-controlled environment. The vehicles then move outdoors for a high-speed test on a track that simulates highway conditions. The ride concludes with a final assessment of the vehicle's performance based on the design choices made by the guest. The entire experience lasts approximately five minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Test Track is designed for a broad audience. Guests must be at least 40 inches (102 cm) tall to ride. Guests with certain health conditions, including heart problems, back or neck problems, and those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their health before riding.
