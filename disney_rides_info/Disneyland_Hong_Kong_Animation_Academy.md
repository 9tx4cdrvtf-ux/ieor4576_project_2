# Animation Academy
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Animation Academy is a walk-through attraction located in the Toy Story Land area of Disneyland Hong Kong. It offers guests the opportunity to learn how to draw Disney characters under the guidance of a Disney animator. The attraction provides a hands-on experience, allowing guests to create their own artwork to take home.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a classroom within the Toy Story universe. Guests enter a brightly lit studio space, designed to resemble a traditional animation studio. The walls are adorned with character sketches and animation cels, creating an immersive environment that celebrates the art of animation. The fictional context is that guests are attending a special class taught by a Disney animator.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are seated at individual drawing stations equipped with paper, pencils, and erasers. A Disney animator, either in person or via a pre-recorded video, guides the audience through the process of drawing a specific Disney character. The animator demonstrates each step, from basic shapes to the final details. The duration of the drawing session is approximately 20 minutes. Guests are encouraged to keep their drawings as souvenirs.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Animation Academy is suitable for guests of all ages and does not have any height restrictions. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions associated with this attraction.
