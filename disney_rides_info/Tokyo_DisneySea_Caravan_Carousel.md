# Caravan Carousel
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Caravan Carousel is a classic carousel attraction located within the Arabian Coast port of Tokyo DisneySea. It features a Middle Eastern theme, offering guests a traditional carousel experience with a unique Disney twist. The carousel is a two-story structure, providing riders with a choice of various Arabian-themed creatures to ride.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed to the fictional world of Aladdin, specifically the city of Agrabah. The carousel's design incorporates elements of Arabian architecture, including ornate carvings, vibrant colors, and intricate mosaics. The queue area is designed to resemble a bustling marketplace, immersing guests in the atmosphere of Agrabah before they board. The carousel itself is presented as a magical ride, conjured by Genie, offering a glimpse into the wonders of the Arabian Nights.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the carousel and select from a variety of ride vehicles, including camels, griffins, and other fantastical creatures. The carousel rotates in a circular motion, accompanied by Arabian-inspired music and ambient sound effects. The ride duration is approximately two minutes. The upper level of the carousel offers a slightly elevated view of the surrounding area.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Caravan Carousel is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with certain medical conditions, such as those prone to motion sickness, may wish to consider the ride's potential effects before boarding.
