# Meet LinaBell at Duffy and Friends Play House
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Meet LinaBell at Duffy and Friends Play House is a character meet-and-greet experience located within the Duffy and Friends Play House area of Disneyland Hong Kong. This attraction allows guests to interact with LinaBell, a fox character and friend of Duffy the Disney Bear. The experience is a standard meet-and-greet, offering photo opportunities and brief interactions with the character.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around the Duffy and Friends universe. Guests are invited to visit LinaBell in her designated area within the Play House. The setting is designed to reflect LinaBell's personality and interests, often incorporating elements of nature and exploration, reflecting her character's love for solving mysteries and discovering new things. The environment is bright and colorful, consistent with the overall aesthetic of the Duffy and Friends area.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated line, which may vary in length depending on park attendance. Upon reaching the front of the line, guests are given the opportunity to meet LinaBell. The interaction typically involves posing for photographs with the character and a brief conversation. The entire experience, including queuing and the meet-and-greet, can last from a few minutes to upwards of 30 minutes, depending on wait times.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this meet-and-greet experience. Guests with mobility issues can access the area, but may need assistance depending on the crowd levels.
