# Astro Orbiter
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
The Astro Orbiter is a spinner ride attraction located in the Tomorrowland area of Disney's Magic Kingdom. It is a variation of the classic "rocket" ride, offering guests a simulated flight experience. The attraction allows riders to control the height of their individual rocket vehicles as they circle a central, themed structure.

## Narrative and Theming
The Astro Orbiter is themed as a futuristic spaceport, with a central tower adorned with planetary models and stylized rockets. Guests are immersed in a retro-futuristic aesthetic, reminiscent of classic science fiction. The queue area features depictions of planets and celestial bodies, enhancing the sense of embarking on a space journey. The attraction's narrative centers on the idea of taking a simulated flight through the cosmos.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual rocket-shaped vehicles, each accommodating two riders. The rockets ascend and descend via a control lever operated by the riders. The ride vehicles circle a central tower at a moderate speed, providing views of Tomorrowland. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Astro Orbiter is generally suitable for all ages, though young children may require assistance from an adult. There is a height requirement of 32 inches (81 cm). Guests with a fear of heights or those prone to motion sickness should consider the ride's intensity before boarding. Guests with certain health conditions should consult with a medical professional before riding.
