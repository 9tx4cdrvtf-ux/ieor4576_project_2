# Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance Single Rider
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance is a multi-part, trackless dark ride located within Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge at Disney's Hollywood Studios. It is a highly immersive and technologically advanced attraction, incorporating multiple ride systems and show scenes. The ride is a significant draw for the park, known for its complex storytelling and innovative use of ride technology. A Single Rider queue is available to expedite wait times for guests traveling alone or willing to be separated from their party.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the Star Wars universe, joining the Resistance in a secret mission against the First Order. The experience begins in a Resistance base, where riders are briefed on their mission. The narrative continues as guests are captured by the First Order and transported aboard a Star Destroyer, encountering characters such as Kylo Ren and stormtroopers. The theming is meticulously detailed, utilizing elaborate sets, animatronics, and projections to create a believable Star Wars environment.

## Attraction Experience
The ride experience involves multiple ride vehicles and show scenes. Guests first experience a pre-show, followed by boarding a transport vehicle. The journey includes a simulated flight, a trackless ride through the Star Destroyer, and a final escape sequence. The attraction utilizes a combination of animatronics, special effects, and projection mapping to create a dynamic and immersive experience. The entire experience lasts approximately 18 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for a wide audience, though it may be intense for younger children. There is no height requirement. Guests with certain health conditions, including heart problems, back or neck problems, or those sensitive to motion sickness, should consider the ride's intensity before experiencing it. Guests should also be aware of enclosed spaces and sudden movements.
