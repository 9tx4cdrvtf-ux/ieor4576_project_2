# Finding Nemo Submarine Voyage
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Finding Nemo Submarine Voyage is a submarine-themed dark ride attraction located in the Tomorrowland area of Disneyland. It opened in 2007, replacing the original Submarine Voyage attraction, which operated from 1959 to 1998. The ride utilizes the existing submarine vehicles and lagoon, incorporating characters and settings from the Disney-Pixar film *Finding Nemo*.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests embark on a simulated underwater journey aboard a submarine, exploring the ocean depths. The narrative follows the story of *Finding Nemo*, with encounters featuring Nemo, Marlin, Dory, and other characters from the film. The lagoon is transformed with animated coral reefs, shipwrecks, and other underwater environments, enhanced by projections and special effects to create the illusion of depth and marine life.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with guests boarding one of the eight diesel-powered submarines. The submarines submerge into the lagoon, and the journey commences. Guests view the underwater scenes through portholes. The ride incorporates audio-animatronics, projected animation, and lighting effects to depict various scenes from the film, including encounters with sea turtles, sharks, and the sunken remains of a ship. The ride duration is approximately 15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Finding Nemo Submarine Voyage is generally suitable for all ages. There is no height requirement. Guests with claustrophobia or sensitivity to enclosed spaces should be aware that the submarine cabins are relatively small and enclosed. Guests with mobility issues may find boarding and disembarking the submarines challenging.
