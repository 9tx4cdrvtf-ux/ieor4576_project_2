# Buzz Lightyear Astro Blasters
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Buzz Lightyear Astro Blasters is a shooting dark ride attraction located in Tomorrowland at Disneyland. The ride is a clone of the original Buzz Lightyear Astro Blasters attraction at Tokyo Disneyland, and is themed after the *Toy Story* franchise. Guests use laser pistols to shoot at targets throughout the ride, earning points based on target accuracy.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are recruited as Space Rangers by Buzz Lightyear to help defeat the Evil Emperor Zurg, who is attempting to steal batteries from toys to power a weapon. The ride environment is designed to resemble a brightly colored, intergalactic space station. The queue area features various interactive elements and character appearances, further immersing guests in the *Toy Story* universe.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual two-person vehicles that travel along a track through various scenes. Each vehicle is equipped with a laser pistol and a joystick to control its direction. Targets are located throughout the ride, and guests score points by shooting them. The ride incorporates various special effects, including animatronics, sound effects, and blacklights. The ride duration is approximately 4 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Buzz Lightyear Astro Blasters is suitable for a wide audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or those prone to motion sickness should consider the ride's effects before boarding.
