# Garden of Wonders
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
The Garden of Wonders is a themed area within the Mystic Point land at Disneyland Hong Kong. It is a walk-through attraction featuring a collection of 24 optical illusions and interactive exhibits. The area was designed by Walt Disney Imagineering and opened in 2013, enhancing the immersive experience of Mystic Point.

## Narrative and Theming
The Garden of Wonders is presented as a collection of ancient artifacts and mystical objects gathered by Lord Henry Mystic, the proprietor of Mystic Manor. The garden is said to be a place where the laws of nature are playfully defied, showcasing illusions that challenge perception and reality. The theming incorporates elements of exploration, discovery, and the supernatural, consistent with the overall Mystic Point narrative.

## Attraction Experience
Guests explore the garden at their own pace, encountering various exhibits that utilize forced perspective, mirrors, and other optical techniques. These include illusions that make objects appear to float, change size, or defy gravity. There are no ride vehicles or specific show sequences; the experience is self-guided and allows for individual exploration and interaction with the exhibits. The duration of a visit varies depending on individual interest, typically ranging from 15 to 30 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Garden of Wonders is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or those prone to motion sickness should be aware that some exhibits may trigger these conditions. The attraction is accessible to guests with disabilities.
