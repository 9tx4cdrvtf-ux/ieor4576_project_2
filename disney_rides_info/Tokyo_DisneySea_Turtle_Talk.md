# Turtle Talk
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Turtle Talk is an interactive show attraction located in the American Waterfront area of Tokyo DisneySea. It features Crush, the sea turtle from the Disney-Pixar film *Finding Nemo*, engaging in live conversations with the audience. The attraction utilizes a combination of animation and live performance technology, offering a unique and engaging experience for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests enter a simulated underwater theater, designed to resemble the ocean's depths. The setting is themed to evoke the feeling of being submerged in the sea, with ambient lighting and sound effects creating an immersive environment. The premise involves Crush appearing on a large screen and interacting directly with the audience, answering questions and sharing jokes.

## Attraction Experience
Upon entering the theater, guests are seated facing a large screen. Crush appears and begins a live, unscripted conversation with the audience. A puppeteer, hidden from view, controls Crush's movements and voice, responding to questions and comments from the audience in real-time. The show lasts approximately 15 minutes. The technology allows for a unique experience each time, as Crush's responses are tailored to the specific questions and interactions from the audience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Turtle Talk is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction is generally accessible to guests with disabilities. However, due to the nature of the show, which involves audience participation and a darkened environment, guests sensitive to these factors should consider their personal comfort levels before entering.
