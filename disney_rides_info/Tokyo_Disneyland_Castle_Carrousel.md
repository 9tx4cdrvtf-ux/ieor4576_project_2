# Castle Carrousel
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Castle Carrousel is a classic carousel attraction located in Fantasyland at Tokyo Disneyland. It is a traditional, two-story carousel featuring intricately designed horses and other figures. The ride is a staple of Disney parks worldwide, offering a gentle and visually appealing experience for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed to resemble a grand, ornate carousel within a medieval castle setting. The central structure is adorned with detailed murals and decorative elements, evoking a sense of fairytale elegance. The carousel's figures, primarily horses, are painted in vibrant colors and feature elaborate saddles and harnesses. The overall atmosphere aims to transport guests to a world of fantasy and enchantment, consistent with the Fantasyland theme.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the carousel from a designated loading area. The ride cycle lasts approximately two minutes, during which the carousel rotates in a circular motion. The horses move up and down in a gentle, rhythmic pattern. The attraction is accompanied by classic carousel music, enhancing the overall experience. The ride utilizes traditional mechanical systems for its operation.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Castle Carrousel is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may require assistance to board the ride. Pregnant women and individuals with certain medical conditions should consult with a cast member before riding.
