# Storybook Land Canal Boats
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Storybook Land Canal Boats is a slow-moving boat ride attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland. It allows guests to cruise through miniature, whimsical recreations of iconic settings from Disney animated films. The attraction was designed by WED Enterprises (now Walt Disney Imagineering) and opened in 1955, making it one of the park's original attractions.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests embark on a narrated journey through a series of meticulously crafted miniature scenes. These scenes depict settings from classic Disney animated films such as *Pinocchio*, *Alice in Wonderland*, *Cinderella*, *Peter Pan*, and *The Little Mermaid*. The environments are populated with detailed figures, buildings, and landscaping, all scaled to a miniature size. The narrative is provided by a recorded audio track, enhancing the storytelling experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a small, open-air boat that travels along a winding canal. The boats are guided by an underwater track system. The ride lasts approximately 10-12 minutes, allowing ample time to view the various scenes. The attraction utilizes forced perspective and intricate lighting to create the illusion of depth and scale within the miniature environments.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Storybook Land Canal Boats is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find it difficult to board and disembark the boats. The attraction is a gentle ride and does not feature any intense movements or frightening elements.
