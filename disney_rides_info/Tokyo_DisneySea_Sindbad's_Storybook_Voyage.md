# Sindbad's Storybook Voyage
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Sindbad's Storybook Voyage is a dark boat ride attraction located in the Arabian Coast area of Tokyo DisneySea. It is a slow-moving, indoor boat ride manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering. The attraction is notable for its extensive use of Audio-Animatronics and its original score, making it a significant and popular attraction within the park.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction tells the story of Sindbad the Sailor and his tiger cub companion, Chandu, as they embark on a series of voyages across the seas. The narrative follows Sindbad's growth and development as he faces various challenges and learns the importance of friendship and courage. The ride's theming is inspired by the tales of *One Thousand and One Nights*, featuring vibrant and detailed scenes depicting different environments, including seascapes, jungles, and treasure-filled caves.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board boats that travel through a series of interconnected scenes. The journey features over 100 Audio-Animatronic figures, including Sindbad and Chandu, interacting within the environments. The ride incorporates various sensory effects, such as lighting, sound, and projections, to enhance the storytelling. The attraction's original musical score, "Compass of Your Heart," plays throughout the ride. The total ride duration is approximately 10 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Sindbad's Storybook Voyage is designed to be accessible to a wide audience. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or other health concerns should be aware of the lighting effects and enclosed spaces. The ride is generally considered suitable for all ages.
