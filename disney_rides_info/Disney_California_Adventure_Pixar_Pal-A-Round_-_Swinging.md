# Pixar Pal-A-Round - Swinging
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
The Pixar Pal-A-Round - Swinging is a Ferris wheel attraction located in Pixar Pier at Disney California Adventure. It is a modified version of a traditional Ferris wheel, featuring gondolas that swing freely as the wheel rotates. The attraction offers panoramic views of the park and surrounding areas. It was originally known as the Sun Wheel before being rethemed to its current Pixar-inspired aesthetic. The ride is manufactured by Zamperla.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's theming is based on various Pixar films, with each gondola featuring a different Pixar character or icon. The overall design incorporates bright colors and playful shapes, consistent with the Pixar Pier area's aesthetic. The ride's structure is painted in vibrant colors, contributing to the festive atmosphere of the pier.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board enclosed gondolas that are suspended from the wheel. Unlike the non-swinging gondolas, these gondolas are designed to swing outwards and inwards as the wheel turns, providing a more dynamic experience. The ride cycle lasts approximately 8-10 minutes, offering multiple rotations and opportunities to view the park from various heights. The gondolas are designed to accommodate a specific number of passengers, and the ride operates continuously throughout the day.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Pixar Pal-A-Round - Swinging is generally suitable for guests of all ages, although the swinging motion may be more intense for some. Guests must be able to transfer into the gondolas. There is a height requirement, typically around 40 inches (102 cm) to ride. Guests with a fear of heights, motion sickness, or certain medical conditions should consider the ride's potential impact before boarding.
