# Siren's Revenge
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Siren's Revenge is a boat ride attraction located in Treasure Cove at Shanghai Disney Resort. It is a unique dark ride experience, themed around pirates and sirens, and is notable for its integration of advanced audio-animatronics and projection mapping technology. The attraction provides a thrilling journey through a pirate-infested cove, culminating in an encounter with mythical sirens.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the world of Treasure Cove, a pirate-themed land. The narrative centers on a pirate crew's perilous journey, seeking treasure and encountering the legendary sirens. The queue area is designed to resemble a pirate ship's hold and a coastal cave, setting the stage for the adventure. The ride's environment incorporates detailed set pieces, lighting effects, and atmospheric projections to create a believable pirate world.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board boats that navigate through a series of scenes. The ride utilizes a combination of physical sets, animatronic figures, and projection mapping to tell the story. The journey includes encounters with pirate battles, treasure hunts, and the climactic appearance of the sirens. The ride features sudden drops and turns, enhancing the thrill factor. The entire experience lasts approximately 6 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Siren's Revenge is designed for a broad audience, including families and thrill-seekers. Guests must meet a minimum height requirement of 97 cm (38 inches) to ride. Guests with certain medical conditions, including heart problems, back or neck issues, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their health before riding.
