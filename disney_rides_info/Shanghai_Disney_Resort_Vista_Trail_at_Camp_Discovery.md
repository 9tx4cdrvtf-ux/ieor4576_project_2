# Vista Trail at Camp Discovery
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Vista Trail is a self-guided hiking trail attraction located within the Camp Discovery area of Shanghai Disneyland. It offers guests a physically engaging experience, allowing them to traverse a series of elevated rope bridges, rock formations, and other obstacles. The attraction is designed to simulate an exploration of a natural environment, providing panoramic views of the surrounding park landscape.

## Narrative and Theming
The Vista Trail is themed to the fictional Camp Discovery, a research and exploration facility. Guests are cast as adventurers, tasked with navigating the rugged terrain and discovering the wonders of the natural world. The environment incorporates elements of a lost world, with themed rockwork, waterfalls, and lush vegetation, creating an immersive atmosphere of discovery and adventure. The trail's design emphasizes the thrill of exploration and the beauty of the natural world.

## Attraction Experience
Guests begin the Vista Trail by entering the designated queue area. The trail itself consists of a series of interconnected pathways, including rope bridges, suspension bridges, and climbing sections. The route is designed to be moderately challenging, requiring guests to utilize their physical abilities to navigate the course. The duration of the experience varies depending on the guest's pace, typically lasting between 15 to 20 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Vista Trail is suitable for guests of varying ages and physical abilities, provided they meet the height requirement. Guests must be at least 107 cm (42 inches) tall to participate. Guests with pre-existing health conditions, including but not limited to heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their physical limitations before riding.
