# Phantom Manor
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Phantom Manor is a dark ride attraction located in Frontierland at Disneyland Park Paris. It is a variation of the Haunted Mansion attractions found at other Disney parks, but with a unique storyline and thematic elements. The ride utilizes a combination of practical effects, Audio-Animatronics, and projection mapping to create a spooky and immersive experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction tells the story of Melanie Ravenswood, a young woman haunted by the phantom of a groom who was murdered on her wedding day. The manor itself is situated in Thunder Mesa, a fictional town in the American West, and is presented as a decaying mansion, reflecting the tragic events that have transpired within its walls. The narrative explores themes of love, loss, and the supernatural.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board "Doom Buggies" and are guided through various rooms of the manor, encountering spectral figures, eerie illusions, and unsettling scenes. The ride incorporates the "Pepper's Ghost" illusion to create ghostly apparitions. The experience includes a ballroom scene with dancing ghosts, a séance room, and a graveyard with singing busts. The ride duration is approximately 5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Phantom Manor is generally suitable for most guests. There are no height restrictions. However, the attraction may be frightening for young children or those sensitive to dark environments and sudden loud noises. Guests with heart conditions, back problems, or other health concerns should consider the ride's intensity before boarding.
