# Tomorrowland Transit Authority PeopleMover
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
The Tomorrowland Transit Authority PeopleMover (TTA PeopleMover) is a continuous-loop, elevated mass transit system attraction located in Tomorrowland at the Magic Kingdom. Manufactured by WED Enterprises (now Walt Disney Imagineering), the PeopleMover provides guests with a leisurely tour of Tomorrowland, offering views of other attractions and a glimpse into the area's design and functionality. It is a slow-moving, family-friendly ride designed to showcase the future of transportation.

## Narrative and Theming
The PeopleMover is presented as a demonstration of a futuristic transportation system. Guests board open-air vehicles that travel along a track elevated above the land. The ride's narrative focuses on the concept of automated transit, with the vehicles smoothly gliding through the area. The theming incorporates elements of futuristic architecture and design, showcasing the imagined possibilities of urban planning and transportation.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the PeopleMover from a moving platform. The ride travels along a 10-minute circuit, passing through various Tomorrowland attractions, including Space Mountain and Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin. The vehicles are propelled by a series of linear induction motors embedded in the track. The ride offers views of the land below, as well as glimpses inside other attractions. The ride is a continuous loop, with vehicles constantly circulating.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The PeopleMover is suitable for all ages and has no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can remain in their wheelchairs or ECVs. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions, making it accessible to a wide range of guests.
