# Monsters, Inc. Ride & Go Seek!
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Monsters, Inc. Ride & Go Seek! is a dark ride attraction located in the Tomorrowland area of Tokyo Disneyland. The ride is based on the Disney-Pixar film *Monsters, Inc.* and utilizes a trackless ride system. It is a significant attraction within the park, offering a unique interactive experience for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the world of Monstropolis, the city inhabited by monsters. The narrative centers around a game of hide-and-seek played by the monsters, with guests participating as "humans" being sought out by the characters. The queue area features themed elements from the film, including the Scare Floor and various props. The ride environment recreates key locations from the movie, such as the factory floor and the doors storage facility.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board vehicles designed to resemble "scream-powered" vehicles. The trackless ride system allows the vehicles to move freely throughout the show building. Riders use flashlights to illuminate the monsters hidden throughout the scenes, triggering various effects and interactions. The ride incorporates animatronics, projections, and sound effects to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 4 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for a wide audience, including families with young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or those sensitive to sudden movements should be aware of the ride's effects. Specific health warnings are not publicly listed, but guests with any health concerns should consult with a cast member before riding.
