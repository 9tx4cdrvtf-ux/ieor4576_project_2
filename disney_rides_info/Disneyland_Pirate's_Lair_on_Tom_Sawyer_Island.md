# Pirate's Lair on Tom Sawyer Island
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Pirate's Lair on Tom Sawyer Island is a walk-through attraction located on Tom Sawyer Island in Disneyland. It offers guests an immersive experience themed around pirates, replacing the original "Tom Sawyer Island" theme with a pirate narrative. The attraction allows guests to explore a themed environment with interactive elements and scenic views.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers on a pirate encampment, transforming the island into a setting of swashbuckling adventure. Guests encounter various pirate-themed environments, including a shipwreck, a pirate's cave, and a treasure trove. The theming incorporates elements of pirate lore, such as hidden treasures, skeletal remains, and depictions of pirate life, creating an atmosphere of danger and excitement.

## Attraction Experience
Guests access the island via rafts that depart from the mainland. Once on the island, visitors are free to explore the various themed areas at their own pace. The experience includes traversing wooden walkways, exploring caves, and interacting with props and set pieces. The attraction features audio effects, lighting, and detailed scenery to enhance the immersive experience. The duration of the experience is dependent on the guest's exploration time.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Pirate's Lair on Tom Sawyer Island is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests should be aware that the attraction involves walking on uneven surfaces and navigating dimly lit areas. Guests with mobility issues may find certain areas challenging.
