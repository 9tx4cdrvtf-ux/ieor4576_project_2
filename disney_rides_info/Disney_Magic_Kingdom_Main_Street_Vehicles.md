# Main Street Vehicles
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Main Street Vehicles is a transportation attraction located on Main Street, U.S.A., in the Magic Kingdom at Walt Disney World. It offers guests a leisurely ride along Main Street, providing a unique perspective of the area. The vehicles are replicas of early 20th-century automobiles and transportation methods, manufactured by various companies. They serve as a practical means of transport for guests and contribute to the immersive theming of the land.

## Narrative and Theming
The Main Street Vehicles are designed to evoke the atmosphere of a turn-of-the-century American town. The vehicles themselves are themed to fit this era, including a horseless carriage, a jitney, a fire engine, and a double-decker bus. The vehicles are intended to transport guests back in time, allowing them to experience the charm and elegance of a bygone era as they travel down Main Street, U.S.A.

## Attraction Experience
Guests can board the vehicles at designated loading zones at the end of Main Street, U.S.A., near the Town Square Theater, or at the opposite end near Cinderella Castle. The vehicles travel at a slow pace, allowing passengers to observe the shops, architecture, and performers along the street. The ride duration is approximately 5-10 minutes, depending on the vehicle and traffic conditions. The vehicles utilize a combination of electric motors and internal combustion engines, depending on the specific model.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Main Street Vehicles are suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find it difficult to board some vehicles; however, assistance is available. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
