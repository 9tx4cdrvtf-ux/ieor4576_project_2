# Sleeping Beauty Castle Walkthrough
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
The Sleeping Beauty Castle Walkthrough is a walk-through attraction located within Sleeping Beauty Castle at Disneyland Park in Anaheim, California. It is a self-guided experience featuring dioramas that depict scenes from Walt Disney's 1959 animated film, *Sleeping Beauty*. The attraction utilizes a combination of forced perspective, special effects, and audio narration to immerse guests in the story. It is a significant element of Fantasyland, offering a unique storytelling experience within a park icon.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction tells the story of Princess Aurora, also known as Sleeping Beauty, and her encounter with the evil Maleficent. Guests journey through various scenes, including the christening, Aurora's life in the forest, her encounter with Prince Phillip, and the climactic battle between the Prince and Maleficent. The castle's architecture and interior design are themed to evoke a medieval European aesthetic, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter the castle and ascend a staircase to begin the walkthrough. Dioramas are presented in a series of rooms, each depicting a key scene from the film. The scenes incorporate animated figures, lighting effects, and audio narration to bring the story to life. The walkthrough culminates in a final scene depicting the battle with Maleficent, featuring a projection of the dragon. The entire experience lasts approximately 5-7 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Sleeping Beauty Castle Walkthrough is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction involves walking up stairs and through dimly lit areas. Guests with mobility issues may find the stairs challenging. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
