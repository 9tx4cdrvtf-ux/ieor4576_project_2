# The Magic Lamp Theater
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
The Magic Lamp Theater is a live-action stage show attraction located in the Arabian Coast area of Tokyo DisneySea. It features a 3D film combined with live performers and special effects. The attraction is a unique blend of theatrical performance and cinematic technology, offering guests an immersive experience based on the Disney animated film *Aladdin*.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests enter a grand theater inspired by the architecture of Agrabah. The pre-show area features ornate decorations and sets the scene for a performance by a magical Genie. The theater itself is designed to resemble a palace interior, with rich colors, intricate carvings, and atmospheric lighting. The narrative centers around a comedic retelling of the *Aladdin* story, with the Genie as the central character.

## Attraction Experience
The show combines a 3D film projected onto a large screen with live actors on stage. The Genie, portrayed by a live performer, interacts with the 3D animation, creating the illusion of a shared space. Special effects, including pyrotechnics, lighting, and physical props, enhance the immersive experience. The show lasts approximately 23 minutes. Guests are seated in a tiered auditorium, providing clear views of the stage and screen.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Magic Lamp Theater is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction utilizes special effects, including loud noises and sudden movements, which may not be suitable for guests with certain sensitivities. Guests with photosensitive epilepsy or other health concerns should consult with a cast member before entering.
