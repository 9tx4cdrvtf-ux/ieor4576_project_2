# Dumbo the Flying Elephant
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Dumbo the Flying Elephant is a classic spinner ride located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park Paris. Based on the 1941 Disney animated film *Dumbo*, the attraction allows guests to experience the sensation of flight aboard a Dumbo-themed elephant. The ride is a standard model manufactured by Zamperla, a prominent amusement ride manufacturer. It is a popular family-friendly attraction, offering a gentle and visually appealing experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is situated within a circus-themed environment, evoking the setting of the film. Guests queue in an area designed to resemble a circus tent. The ride vehicles are stylized as Dumbo the elephant, each equipped with a lever that allows riders to control the vertical movement of their elephant, allowing them to ascend and descend during the ride cycle. The surrounding area features colorful decorations and circus-themed props, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of sixteen Dumbo ride vehicles, each accommodating two riders. Once seated, the ride begins with the elephants rotating around a central hub. Riders can use the lever to control the height of their elephant, providing a degree of individual control over the ride experience. The ride cycle lasts approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds. The attraction utilizes simple mechanical technology for its operation.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Dumbo the Flying Elephant is designed for a broad audience, particularly families with young children. There is no height requirement. Guests must be able to sit upright and maintain proper posture during the ride. Guests with certain medical conditions, such as heart problems or back issues, should consult with a medical professional before riding.
