# Toy Soldier Parachute Drop Single Rider
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Toy Soldier Parachute Drop Single Rider is a drop tower-style amusement ride located in the Toy Story Land area of Disneyland Hong Kong. The attraction simulates the experience of parachuting from a great height, themed to the popular *Toy Story* film franchise. It is manufactured by Zamperla, a prominent ride manufacturer. The ride offers a single rider queue to expedite wait times.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the world of *Toy Story*, specifically the backyard setting. The ride is themed as a training exercise for Andy's toy soldiers. Riders are recruited as "green army men" and are instructed to prepare for a parachute jump. The queue line features oversized toy props and military-themed decorations, enhancing the immersive environment. The ride vehicles are designed to resemble parachutes, further reinforcing the theme.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are seated in open-air gondolas, each holding multiple riders. The gondolas are lifted to a height of approximately 25 meters (82 feet). After a brief pause, the gondolas are released, providing a series of controlled drops and bounces, simulating a parachute descent. The ride incorporates visual effects, including the illusion of height and the sensation of freefall. The entire ride experience lasts approximately one minute.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is generally suitable for children and adults. The minimum height requirement is 81 cm (32 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, particularly those with heart problems, back or neck problems, or who are pregnant, are advised to consult with a medical professional before riding. Guests should also be aware of the ride's motion sickness potential.
