# Welcome to Starport: A Star Wars Encounter
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Welcome to Starport: A Star Wars Encounter is a meet-and-greet attraction located in Discoveryland at Disneyland Park Paris. It offers guests the opportunity to interact with characters from the *Star Wars* franchise. The attraction provides a themed environment for photo opportunities and character interactions, enhancing the immersive experience within the *Star Wars*-themed area of the park.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a spaceport, a bustling hub within the *Star Wars* universe. Guests are welcomed into a hangar-like setting, designed to evoke the atmosphere of a starship docking bay. The environment incorporates detailed props, lighting, and sound effects to create a sense of being transported to a location within the *Star Wars* galaxy. The narrative positions guests as visitors to this spaceport, encountering various characters as they pass through.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue within the themed environment before entering a designated area for character interactions. The experience typically involves meeting and taking photographs with one or more *Star Wars* characters, such as Darth Vader, Chewbacca, or a Stormtrooper. The specific characters available may vary. The duration of the experience is dependent on the length of the queue and the time spent with each character.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the attraction, though they may need to queue for a longer period. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions, but guests should be aware that the environment may contain loud noises and flashing lights.
