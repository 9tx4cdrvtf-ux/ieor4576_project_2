# Casey Jr. Circus Train
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
The Casey Jr. Circus Train is a railroad-themed amusement ride located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park. It is a narrow-gauge train ride that offers guests a scenic tour of the Storybook Land Canal Boats attraction. The ride is themed after the train from the 1941 Disney animated film *Dumbo*.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the world of *Dumbo*. The train is designed to resemble Casey Jr., the circus train that transports the circus performers and animals. The surrounding landscape, viewed from the train, features miniature recreations of iconic scenes and buildings from classic Disney animated films, including *Pinocchio*, *Cinderella*, and *Peter Pan*. The theming emphasizes a whimsical and nostalgic atmosphere.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board open-air cars or the caboose of the Casey Jr. Circus Train. The train travels along a winding track, passing through tunnels and over bridges. The ride provides views of the Storybook Land Canal Boats' miniature environments. The journey lasts approximately 3-4 minutes. The train utilizes a diesel-electric locomotive to propel the cars.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Casey Jr. Circus Train is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find it challenging to board the train due to the step up required. Guests with back or neck problems should consider the ride's potential for jostling.
