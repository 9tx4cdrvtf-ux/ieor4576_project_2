# WEB SLINGERS: A Spider-Man Adventure
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
WEB SLINGERS: A Spider-Man Adventure is a 3D interactive dark ride located in the Avengers Campus area of Disney California Adventure. The attraction utilizes a trackless ride system and was developed by Walt Disney Imagineering. It allows guests to virtually sling webs alongside Spider-Man to capture Spider-Bots that have gone haywire. The ride is significant as a key attraction within the Avengers Campus, immersing guests in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

## Narrative and Theming
The narrative centers around the Worldwide Engineering Brigade (WEB), a workshop showcasing inventions from various young geniuses. Guests are recruited to help Spider-Man contain a swarm of malfunctioning Spider-Bots that are replicating uncontrollably. The queue area features various WEB lab environments, including displays of inventions and character appearances. The overall theming is a high-tech, industrial setting, consistent with the Avengers Campus aesthetic.

## Attraction Experience
Guests wear 3D glasses and board vehicles that move through multiple show scenes. Using hand gestures, riders simulate web-slinging to capture the rogue Spider-Bots. The ride incorporates a combination of physical sets, projections, and special effects, including lighting and sound, to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately four minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a broad audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with motion sickness or other health conditions should consider the ride's potential effects before experiencing it.
