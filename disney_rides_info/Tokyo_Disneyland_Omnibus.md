# Omnibus
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
The Omnibus is a slow-moving, open-air, double-decker bus attraction located in World Bazaar at Tokyo Disneyland. It provides guests with a leisurely tour of the park's main entrance area, offering views of the shops and architecture. The attraction is a non-thrill ride designed for all ages and is a classic example of a transportation-based attraction found in many Disney parks.

## Narrative and Theming
The Omnibus is themed as a vintage, early 20th-century vehicle, fitting seamlessly into the turn-of-the-century aesthetic of World Bazaar. The attraction's narrative is simply that of a public transportation system, allowing guests to experience the atmosphere of a bygone era while traversing the park's central hub. The vehicle's design incorporates ornate detailing and period-appropriate signage, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the Omnibus from a designated loading area within World Bazaar. The ride begins with a slow, steady journey along a predetermined route, offering panoramic views of the surrounding shops and buildings. The route typically circles the central plaza, allowing passengers to observe the various themed facades and the activity of other park guests. The ride duration is approximately 5-7 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Omnibus is suitable for guests of all ages and does not have any height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can be accommodated, though assistance may be required. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions associated with the ride, making it accessible to a wide range of visitors.
