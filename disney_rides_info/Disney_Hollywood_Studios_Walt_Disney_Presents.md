# Walt Disney Presents
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
Walt Disney Presents is a walk-through attraction located in the Animation Courtyard area of Disney's Hollywood Studios. It serves as a showcase of Walt Disney's legacy, featuring exhibits, concept art, models, and a short film about his life and the history of The Walt Disney Company. The attraction provides guests with a glimpse into the creative process behind Disney's films and theme parks.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a gallery and exhibition space, celebrating the life and achievements of Walt Disney. The environment is designed to evoke a sense of nostalgia and appreciation for Disney's contributions to entertainment. Guests are invited to explore various displays that highlight different aspects of Disney's work, from early animation to the development of Disneyland and beyond.

## Attraction Experience
Guests begin their experience by entering a large theater to view a film titled "Walt Disney: One Man's Dream." The film, approximately 15 minutes in length, chronicles Walt Disney's life and career, including his early struggles, successes, and vision for the future. Following the film, guests proceed through a series of galleries. These galleries feature original concept art, models of theme park attractions, props, and other artifacts from Disney films and parks. The duration of the entire experience is approximately 30 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Walt Disney Presents is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction is primarily a walk-through experience and does not involve any physical exertion. Guests with mobility issues can navigate the attraction, though some areas may be crowded. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
