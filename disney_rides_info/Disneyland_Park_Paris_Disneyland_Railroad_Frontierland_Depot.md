# Disneyland Railroad Frontierland Depot
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
The Disneyland Railroad Frontierland Depot is a station stop on the Disneyland Railroad, a narrow-gauge railway system encircling Disneyland Park Paris. It serves as one of the four stations providing access to the park's various lands. The Disneyland Railroad, built by the WED Enterprises, offers guests a scenic tour of the park, providing views of attractions and landscapes.

## Narrative and Theming
The Frontierland Depot is themed to resemble a classic American Old West train station. The architecture reflects the rustic style of the American frontier, with wooden structures, exposed beams, and period-appropriate signage. The depot's design aims to immerse guests in the atmosphere of Frontierland, preparing them for the adventures and experiences awaiting them in that themed land.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the Disneyland Railroad trains at the Frontierland Depot. The train departs, traveling clockwise around the park. The journey includes views of Adventureland, Fantasyland, Discoveryland, and the Rivers of the Far West. The ride duration is approximately 20 minutes for a full circuit, with the Frontierland Depot being one of the stops where guests can disembark or board.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Disneyland Railroad is generally suitable for all ages. There are no specific height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may require assistance. Strollers must be folded before boarding. Guests with certain health conditions should consult with a medical professional before riding.
