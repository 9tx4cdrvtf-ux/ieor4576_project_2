# Tomorrowland Speedway
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Tomorrowland Speedway is a self-steer, gasoline-powered car ride located in the Tomorrowland section of Disney's Magic Kingdom. It allows guests to drive miniature race cars along a guided track. The attraction is a classic Disney experience, offering a hands-on driving experience for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed to a futuristic race track, consistent with the Tomorrowland aesthetic. Guests are presented with a vision of the future of transportation, though the technology is rooted in the past. The track layout features various turns and inclines, simulating a real-world racing environment within a controlled setting. The surrounding environment includes futuristic architecture and landscaping, reinforcing the Tomorrowland theme.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue and board individual race cars. Each car is equipped with a gas pedal and steering wheel, though the cars are guided by a center rail, preventing them from leaving the designated track. The ride lasts approximately five minutes, during which guests navigate the course. The track is approximately 0.4 miles (0.64 km) in length.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Tomorrowland Speedway is suitable for guests of all ages, though children must be at least 32 inches (81 cm) tall to ride. Guests with back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their condition before riding.
