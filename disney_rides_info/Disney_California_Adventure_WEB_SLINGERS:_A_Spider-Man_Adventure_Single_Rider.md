# WEB SLINGERS: A Spider-Man Adventure Single Rider
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
WEB SLINGERS: A Spider-Man Adventure is a 3D interactive dark ride located in the Avengers Campus area of Disney California Adventure. The attraction utilizes a trackless ride system and was developed by Walt Disney Imagineering. It allows guests to virtually sling webs alongside Spider-Man to capture Spider-Bots that have escaped and are replicating uncontrollably. The Single Rider queue offers a reduced wait time for guests willing to be separated from their party.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are recruited as new recruits at WEB (Worldwide Engineering Brigade), a workshop showcasing innovative technology developed by Peter Parker and other young inventors. The storyline involves a demonstration of Spider-Man's web-slinging technology gone awry, resulting in the accidental release of Spider-Bots. Guests are tasked with helping Spider-Man contain the rogue robots throughout the WEB facility. The attraction's environment is themed to resemble a high-tech workshop with various references to the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board vehicles equipped with interactive web-slinging technology. The ride system is trackless, allowing for free-flowing movement through the scenes. Riders use hand gestures to virtually sling webs at the Spider-Bots, earning points for each capture. The experience incorporates 3D visuals, physical sets, and special effects, including projections and lighting, to enhance the immersive environment. The ride duration is approximately four minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
WEB SLINGERS: A Spider-Man Adventure is designed for a broad audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with motion sickness or other health concerns should consider the ride's intensity before boarding. The Single Rider queue is available for guests who are willing to ride separately from their party to potentially reduce wait times.
