# Main Street Vehicles
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Main Street Vehicles is a collection of antique-style vehicles that transport guests along Main Street, U.S.A. at Disneyland. These vehicles, including a horseless carriage, a fire engine, a jitney, and a double-decker bus, offer a leisurely and scenic mode of transportation, providing a unique perspective of the themed land. The vehicles are operated by Disneyland cast members and are a long-standing tradition of the park.

## Narrative and Theming
The vehicles are designed to evoke the early 20th century, mirroring the idealized American small town depicted on Main Street, U.S.A. The theming is consistent with the land's overall narrative, which presents a nostalgic vision of a turn-of-the-century town. The vehicles contribute to the immersive environment by enhancing the sense of stepping back in time.

## Attraction Experience
Guests can board the vehicles at designated loading zones at the entrance and exit of Main Street, U.S.A. The vehicles travel at a slow pace, allowing passengers to observe the shops, architecture, and performers along the street. The ride duration is approximately 5-7 minutes, depending on traffic and the vehicle type. The vehicles utilize a combination of electric and gasoline engines, depending on the model.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Main Street Vehicles are suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find it difficult to board some vehicles. Guests should be aware of the vehicle's open-air design and potential exposure to the elements.
