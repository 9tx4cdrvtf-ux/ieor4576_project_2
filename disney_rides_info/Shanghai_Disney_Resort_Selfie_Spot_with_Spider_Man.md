# Selfie Spot with Spider-Man
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
The Selfie Spot with Spider-Man is a character meet-and-greet experience located within the Marvel Universe area of Shanghai Disney Resort. It allows guests to interact with a costumed Spider-Man character and capture photographs. This attraction provides a popular photo opportunity, enhancing the immersive experience of the Marvel-themed land.

## Narrative and Theming
The Selfie Spot is situated within a themed environment designed to resemble a New York City cityscape, consistent with the Marvel Cinematic Universe. The setting includes elements such as brick walls, fire escapes, and other urban details. Spider-Man's presence is explained within the context of the Marvel Universe, where he is portrayed as a friendly neighborhood hero. The area aims to create a sense of realism and immersion, allowing guests to feel as though they are encountering Spider-Man in his natural environment.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area before being directed to the photo opportunity. The experience involves a brief interaction with the Spider-Man character, where guests can pose for photographs. Disney PhotoPass photographers are typically present to capture professional images, which guests can purchase. Guests are also permitted to use their own cameras and mobile devices to take photos. The entire experience, including queuing and the photo session, typically lasts for a few minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Selfie Spot with Spider-Man is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. However, guests with mobility issues may need assistance navigating the queue.
