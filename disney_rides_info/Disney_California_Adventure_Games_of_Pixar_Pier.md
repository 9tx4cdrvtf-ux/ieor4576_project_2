# Games of Pixar Pier
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Games of Pixar Pier is a collection of midway-style games located on Pixar Pier in Disney California Adventure. These games offer guests the opportunity to test their skills and win prizes themed to various Pixar films. The games are a permanent fixture of Pixar Pier, providing interactive entertainment for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
Each game booth is themed to a specific Pixar film, immersing guests in the worlds of these animated features. The surrounding area features colorful decorations, character cutouts, and thematic music, creating a festive atmosphere reminiscent of a classic boardwalk. The games themselves are presented as friendly competitions, encouraging guests to engage with the Pixar stories in a playful manner.

## Attraction Experience
Guests purchase game tickets and then select from a variety of games, each with its own rules and objectives. Examples include games based on *Toy Story*, *A Bug's Life*, and *Monsters, Inc.*. Players aim to successfully complete the game's challenge, such as tossing rings, throwing balls, or shooting targets. Successful players win prizes, ranging from small trinkets to larger, more desirable items. The duration of each game varies depending on the specific challenge.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Games of Pixar Pier are designed for guests of all ages and abilities. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can participate, though some games may be easier to play than others. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions, but guests should be aware of the physical nature of some games, such as throwing or aiming.
