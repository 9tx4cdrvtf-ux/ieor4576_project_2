# Soaring: Fantastic Flight
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Soaring: Fantastic Flight is a motion-simulator ride located in the Mediterranean Harbor area of Tokyo DisneySea. It is a clone of the original Soarin' Over California at Disney California Adventure and Soaring at Shanghai Disneyland. The attraction, manufactured by Dynamic Attractions, simulates the experience of flight over various global landmarks. It is a highly popular attraction due to its immersive nature and large capacity.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are presented with the fictional Museum of Fantastic Flight, a museum dedicated to the history of flight and the dreams of soaring. The queue area features exhibits showcasing various flying machines and the history of aviation. The pre-show introduces the "Dream Flyer," a revolutionary aerial vehicle designed to allow guests to experience the sensation of flight. The narrative emphasizes the power of imagination and the ability to transcend boundaries.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are seated in a suspended ride system, with their legs dangling freely. The ride system then lifts guests into a massive, curved screen. The film, projected onto the screen, depicts various global locations, including the Eiffel Tower, the Great Wall of China, and the Matterhorn. Sensory effects, such as wind, scents, and subtle movements, enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately five minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is generally suitable for all ages, though young children may find the height and motion slightly intimidating. Guests must be at least 102 cm (40 inches) tall to ride. Individuals with a fear of heights, motion sickness, or certain medical conditions should consider the ride's intensity before participating. Pregnant women and those with heart conditions are advised to consult with a physician before riding.
