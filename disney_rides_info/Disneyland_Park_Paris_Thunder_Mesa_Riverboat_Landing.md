# Thunder Mesa Riverboat Landing
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Thunder Mesa Riverboat Landing is a water-based attraction located in Frontierland at Disneyland Park Paris. It features two authentic-looking paddle-wheel riverboats, the *Mark Twain* and the *Molly Brown*, offering guests a scenic tour of the Rivers of the Far West. The attraction is a classic example of a slow-moving boat ride, providing a relaxing experience and contributing significantly to the immersive theming of Frontierland.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is set within the fictional town of Thunder Mesa, a boomtown inspired by the American Old West. The riverboats navigate the Rivers of the Far West, passing through various themed environments, including the wilderness, a Native American village, and the remnants of a gold mine. The setting evokes a sense of adventure and exploration, immersing guests in the atmosphere of the American frontier.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board either the *Mark Twain* or the *Molly Brown* riverboat. The boats slowly cruise around the Rivers of the Far West, offering views of the surrounding scenery and themed elements. The journey includes passing by animatronic figures and themed set pieces. The ride duration is approximately 15 minutes. The boats are powered by electric motors, and the paddle wheels are primarily for show.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find boarding and disembarking challenging. The ride is generally considered gentle and does not feature any intense sensory effects.
