# Alice's Curious Labyrinth
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Alice's Curious Labyrinth is a walk-through attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park Paris. It is a hedge maze themed after the 1951 Disney animated film *Alice in Wonderland*. The attraction allows guests to navigate a series of winding pathways, encountering various visual gags and themed elements inspired by the film. It was designed and built by Walt Disney Imagineering and opened with the park in 1992.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the whimsical world of *Alice in Wonderland*. The maze is designed to resemble the Queen of Hearts' garden. Guests encounter various characters and scenes from the film, including the Cheshire Cat, the Mad Hatter's tea party, and the Queen of Hearts' croquet game. The theming incorporates oversized props, forced perspective illusions, and character figures to enhance the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter the maze and navigate a network of hedges. The pathways are designed to be confusing, with dead ends and multiple routes. Along the way, guests encounter various interactive elements and visual gags. At the center of the maze is the Queen of Hearts' castle, which guests can climb to gain a view of the entire maze. The attraction typically takes between 10-20 minutes to complete, depending on the guest's navigation skills.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Alice's Curious Labyrinth is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find navigating the maze challenging due to the narrow pathways and uneven surfaces. There are no specific health warnings associated with the attraction.
