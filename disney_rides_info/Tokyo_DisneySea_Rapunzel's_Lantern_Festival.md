# Rapunzel's Lantern Festival
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Rapunzel's Lantern Festival is a nighttime spectacular attraction located in the Mediterranean Harbor area of Tokyo DisneySea. It is a seasonal event, typically held during the spring, and features a water-based show incorporating projections, pyrotechnics, and illuminated floats. The attraction is produced by the Walt Disney Company and utilizes advanced projection mapping technology to create immersive visual effects on water screens and surrounding structures.

## Narrative and Theming
The show is themed around the climactic lantern scene from Disney's animated film *Tangled*. Guests are immersed in a romantic atmosphere, with the harbor transformed to resemble the kingdom of Corona. The narrative focuses on Rapunzel and Flynn Rider's journey, culminating in the release of thousands of floating lanterns into the night sky. The show aims to evoke feelings of hope, love, and the fulfillment of dreams, mirroring the film's central themes.

## Attraction Experience
The show begins with the harbor lights dimming, and the water screens rising. Projections depict scenes from *Tangled*, including Rapunzel's tower and the lantern launch. Illuminated floats, representing various elements from the film, move through the harbor. The show incorporates water fountains, pyrotechnics, and lasers to enhance the visual spectacle. The finale features the release of virtual lanterns, projected onto the water screens, accompanied by the film's iconic song, "I See the Light." The show's duration is approximately 20 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Rapunzel's Lantern Festival is designed for a general audience and is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with sensitivity to loud noises, flashing lights, or pyrotechnics should be aware of these elements. The show is presented outdoors and may be subject to cancellation due to inclement weather.
