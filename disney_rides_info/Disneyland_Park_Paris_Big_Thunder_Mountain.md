# Big Thunder Mountain
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Big Thunder Mountain is a steel roller coaster located in Frontierland at Disneyland Park Paris. It is a mine train-themed coaster manufactured by Vekoma. The attraction is a significant element of the park's Frontierland, offering a thrilling ride experience within a detailed and immersive environment.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around a runaway mine train through the legendary Big Thunder Mountain. Guests are transported to the fictional town of Rainbow Ridge, a gold-mining settlement, where they board a train that has been cursed. The narrative suggests the mountain is haunted, and the train is destined to careen through the mountain's caverns and across its rugged terrain. The theming incorporates elements of the American Old West, with detailed rockwork, abandoned mining equipment, and a flooded lake adding to the immersive atmosphere.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a train consisting of several mine cars. The ride begins with a slow ascent up a lift hill, followed by a series of drops, turns, and tunnels. The coaster utilizes a combination of indoor and outdoor sections, with the train navigating through the mountain's interior and around its exterior. The ride incorporates special effects, including simulated explosions and lighting, to enhance the experience. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Big Thunder Mountain is suitable for guests of various ages, though it is a thrill ride. The minimum height requirement is 1.02 meters (40 inches). Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding.
