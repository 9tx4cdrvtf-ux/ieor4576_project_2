# Peter Pan’s Flight
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Peter Pan’s Flight is a suspended dark ride attraction located in Fantasyland at Shanghai Disney Resort. Based on Disney's 1953 animated film *Peter Pan*, the ride allows guests to experience a flight over London and Neverland. The attraction is a classic "flying" ride system, manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering, and is a popular family-friendly experience.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests board pirate ship ride vehicles and embark on a journey inspired by the film. The queue area is themed to the Darling children's nursery, complete with detailed set pieces and interactive elements. The ride's narrative follows Peter Pan, Wendy, John, and Michael as they fly to Neverland, encountering Captain Hook, Tinker Bell, and other characters from the story. The environments are designed to immerse riders in the film's settings, including a nighttime view of London and the vibrant landscapes of Neverland.

## Attraction Experience
The ride vehicles, suspended from an overhead track, simulate the sensation of flight. The journey begins with a flight over London, featuring miniature buildings and twinkling lights. The ride then transitions to Neverland, where guests encounter various scenes, including a battle with Captain Hook and a visit to Skull Rock. The ride incorporates special effects, such as projections, lighting, and audio-animatronics, to enhance the storytelling. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Peter Pan’s Flight is designed for a general audience, particularly families with young children. There is no height requirement. Guests with a sensitivity to enclosed spaces or those prone to motion sickness should consider the ride's effects before boarding.
