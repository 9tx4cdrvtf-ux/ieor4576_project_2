# Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind is a launched steel roller coaster located in the World Discovery neighborhood of Epcot at Walt Disney World. Manufactured by Vekoma, it is one of the longest enclosed roller coasters in the world. The attraction combines a thrilling ride experience with a narrative based on Marvel's *Guardians of the Galaxy* franchise, representing a significant investment in Epcot's reimagining.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is set within the Wonders of Xandar pavilion, a Nova Corps facility showcasing Xandarian culture and technology. Guests are invited to experience the "Galaxarium," a planetarium-like exhibit exploring the similarities between Xandar and Earth. The narrative involves the Guardians of the Galaxy intervening to save the galaxy from a Celestial threat, with guests becoming entangled in the conflict. The queue incorporates pre-shows featuring characters from the films, establishing the story's context.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with a backward launch, followed by a series of twists, turns, and inversions within a darkened show building. The coaster vehicles rotate 360 degrees, allowing riders to view the action. The ride features a soundtrack of classic rock songs, chosen to enhance the experience. The ride duration is approximately three minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a broad audience, but has a minimum height requirement of 42 inches (107 cm). Guests with a history of heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding. The ride utilizes strobe lights and other intense visual effects.
