# Crush's Coaster
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
Crush's Coaster is a steel spinning roller coaster located in the Toon Studio area of Disney Adventure World Paris. The attraction, themed after the animated film *Finding Nemo*, is a unique indoor/outdoor experience. It is notable for its enclosed launch and spinning ride vehicles, providing a distinctive thrill within the park.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the world of *Finding Nemo*, boarding turtle shells to join Crush on a journey through the East Australian Current. The queue area features elements from the film, including the jellyfish forest and the sunken ship, setting the stage for the underwater adventure. The ride's narrative focuses on the experience of riding the current, encountering various characters and environments from the movie.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with a launch inside a darkened show building, simulating the descent into the ocean. The spinning coaster cars then ascend a lift hill before transitioning to an outdoor section. The ride features twists, turns, and drops, enhanced by special effects such as lighting and projections. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Crush's Coaster is designed for a broad audience, but has a minimum height requirement of 1.07 meters (42 inches). Guests with a history of back or neck problems, heart conditions, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding. The ride involves rapid movements and spinning, which may cause motion sickness.
