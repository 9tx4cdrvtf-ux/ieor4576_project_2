# Toy Story Mania!
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Toy Story Mania! is a 4D interactive shooting dark ride located in the Toyville area of Tokyo DisneySea. The attraction, manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering, is based on the popular *Toy Story* film franchise. It is a significant attraction within the park, offering a family-friendly experience with a high capacity and immersive theming.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in a carnival-themed environment within Andy's room, as seen in the *Toy Story* films. The queue area features oversized toys and game boxes, creating the illusion of being shrunken to the size of a toy. The ride's narrative involves guests playing a series of carnival games hosted by the *Toy Story* characters.

## Attraction Experience
Riders board ride vehicles that travel through a series of virtual game booths. Each booth presents a different game, such as shooting darts at targets or throwing rings. Guests use pull-strings on their ride vehicles to launch projectiles at the targets displayed on screens. The attraction utilizes 3D glasses and various sensory effects, including air blasts and water sprays, to enhance the immersive experience. The ride lasts approximately 5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Toy Story Mania! is designed for a broad audience, including families with children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or other health concerns should consult the park's guidelines before riding.
