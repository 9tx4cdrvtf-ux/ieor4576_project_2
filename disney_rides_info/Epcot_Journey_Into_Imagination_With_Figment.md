# Journey Into Imagination With Figment
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Journey Into Imagination With Figment is a dark ride attraction located in the Imagination! pavilion at Epcot in Walt Disney World. The ride, a reimagining of the original 1983 attraction, offers a whimsical exploration of the senses and creativity. It is a slow-moving, track-based ride system designed by Walt Disney Imagineering. The attraction is a significant component of the Imagination! pavilion, contributing to its educational and entertainment goals.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction centers around Figment, a small purple dragon, and Dr. Nigel Channing, a scientist. Guests are invited to explore the Imagination Institute, a research facility dedicated to the study of imagination. The narrative follows Figment and Dr. Channing as they demonstrate the five senses and how they contribute to the creative process. The ride incorporates various environments, including a sound lab, a visual arts studio, and a sensory lab, all designed to stimulate the imagination.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board vehicles that travel through various scenes. The ride utilizes Audio-Animatronics, projections, and special effects to create immersive environments. The journey includes encounters with Figment and Dr. Channing, as well as demonstrations of sight, sound, smell, taste, and touch. The ride duration is approximately 13 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a general audience and is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or other sensory sensitivities should be aware of the use of strobe lights and other special effects. Guests with mobility issues should be aware that they must be able to transfer from a wheelchair to the ride vehicle.
