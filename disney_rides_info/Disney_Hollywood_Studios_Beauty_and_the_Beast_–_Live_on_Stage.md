# Beauty and the Beast – Live on Stage
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
"Beauty and the Beast – Live on Stage" is a live musical stage show presented at the Theater of the Stars amphitheater in Disney's Hollywood Studios. The show is a theatrical adaptation of Disney's 1991 animated film, "Beauty and the Beast," and is a significant entertainment offering within the park, providing a family-friendly experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The show closely follows the narrative of the animated film, recounting the story of Belle, a young woman who becomes a prisoner in the castle of a cursed Beast. The setting is a French provincial village and the Beast's enchanted castle, recreated on stage with elaborate set pieces and costumes. The story explores themes of love, acceptance, and inner beauty.

## Attraction Experience
The performance features live actors portraying the film's characters, including Belle, the Beast, Gaston, Lumiere, Cogsworth, and Mrs. Potts. The show incorporates musical numbers from the film, featuring choreographed dance sequences and vocal performances. The show utilizes stage lighting, sound effects, and costuming to enhance the storytelling. The performance lasts approximately 30 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
"Beauty and the Beast – Live on Stage" is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. The show is presented outdoors and may be affected by weather conditions. Guests with mobility issues should be aware that seating is in an amphitheater setting.
