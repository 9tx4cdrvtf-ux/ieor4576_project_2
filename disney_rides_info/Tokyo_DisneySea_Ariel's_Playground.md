# Ariel's Playground
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Ariel's Playground is an interactive, indoor play area located within the Mermaid Lagoon area of Tokyo DisneySea. Designed to resemble the underwater world of Disney's *The Little Mermaid*, it offers a variety of soft play structures and interactive elements suitable for young children. The attraction is primarily a walk-through experience, providing a safe and engaging environment for children to explore.

## Narrative and Theming
The playground immerses guests in a recreation of Ariel's underwater grotto and other familiar locations from the film. The theming incorporates vibrant colors, whimsical sea creature sculptures, and ambient lighting to create an illusion of being submerged in the ocean. The environment is designed to be tactile and encourage exploration, with various interactive elements themed around characters and scenes from *The Little Mermaid*.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter the playground and are free to explore the various play structures at their own pace. These structures include soft climbing areas, tunnels, slides, and interactive touch-screen displays. The area also features a dedicated space for younger children, ensuring a safe environment for all ages. The experience is self-guided, with no set ride path or duration. Guests can spend as much time as they wish within the playground.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Ariel's Playground is primarily targeted towards young children, typically those aged 3 to 10 years old. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find certain areas challenging. The attraction is generally accessible, but parents should supervise their children at all times. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions beyond the standard park guidelines.
