# Peter Pan's Flight
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Peter Pan's Flight is a dark ride attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland. It is a classic "flying" ride system, manufactured by Arrow Development, that simulates the experience of soaring through the air above scenes from Disney's 1953 animated film, *Peter Pan*. The attraction is a Disneyland opening day attraction and remains one of the most popular rides in the park, often requiring extended wait times.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the world of Neverland. Guests board "flying pirate ships" that travel along a track suspended from the ceiling. The ride recreates key scenes from the film, including the Darling children's flight to Neverland, encounters with Captain Hook and his crew, and the final confrontation with Peter Pan. The environments are enhanced with detailed miniature sets, blacklight effects, and character animatronics.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue through a Victorian-themed nursery before boarding the ride vehicles. The "flying pirate ships" then ascend, giving the illusion of flight. The ride progresses through various scenes, including London at night, Skull Rock, and Captain Hook's ship. The ride utilizes a combination of practical sets, projected imagery, and special effects to create the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 2 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Peter Pan's Flight is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with a fear of heights or enclosed spaces may experience discomfort. The ride is a gentle experience and does not involve sudden drops or intense movements.
