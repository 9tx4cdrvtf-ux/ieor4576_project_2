# Radiator Springs Racers
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Radiator Springs Racers is a high-speed, dark ride and slot car-style attraction located in Cars Land at Disney California Adventure. Manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering, the ride is a significant investment and a centerpiece of Cars Land, offering a thrilling experience with immersive theming. It combines a dark ride experience with a competitive, side-by-side racing element.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed after the Pixar film *Cars*. Guests are transported to the town of Radiator Springs, where they become "new racers" preparing for the big race. The queue and pre-show areas immerse guests in the world of the film, featuring familiar characters and settings. The ride's narrative involves preparing the vehicles, visiting local establishments like Luigi's Casa Della Tires and Ramone's House of Body Art, and ultimately racing against another vehicle through the desert landscape.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board six-person vehicles designed to resemble cars from the *Cars* film. The ride begins as a dark ride, navigating through scenes featuring Audio-Animatronics of the film's characters. The vehicles then transition to an outdoor track, where they accelerate and race against another vehicle. The ride incorporates various sensory effects, including lighting, sound, and wind, and lasts approximately 4.5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Radiator Springs Racers is designed for a broad audience, but has a minimum height requirement of 40 inches (102 cm). Guests with certain health conditions, including heart problems, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant, are advised against riding. The ride involves high speeds and sudden movements.
