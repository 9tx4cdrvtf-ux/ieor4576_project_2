# Le Carrousel de Lancelot
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Le Carrousel de Lancelot is a classic carousel attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park Paris. It features a medieval Arthurian theme, offering guests a traditional carousel experience with intricately designed horses. The ride was manufactured by the Italian company Zamperla and opened with the park in 1992. It is a visually appealing and family-friendly attraction, providing a gentle ride suitable for all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The carousel is themed around the legend of King Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table. Guests are invited to ride on one of seventy-seven hand-painted horses, each adorned with detailed armor and heraldic symbols. The carousel's canopy is decorated with scenes depicting the Arthurian legend, further immersing riders in the medieval atmosphere. The surrounding area features architectural elements reminiscent of a medieval castle, enhancing the overall thematic experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a covered area before boarding the carousel. The ride cycle lasts approximately two minutes, during which the carousel rotates in a circular motion. The horses move up and down in a traditional carousel fashion. The ride is accompanied by a selection of classic Disney music, enhancing the festive atmosphere. There are no special effects or technological elements beyond the basic operation of the carousel.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Le Carrousel de Lancelot is designed for a broad audience, including children and adults. There are no height restrictions. Guests with any health concerns should consult with a medical professional before riding. The ride is generally considered safe, but guests should remain seated and hold onto the safety bars during the ride cycle.
