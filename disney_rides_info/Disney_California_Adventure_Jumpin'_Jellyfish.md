# Jumpin' Jellyfish
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Jumpin' Jellyfish is a family-friendly drop tower ride located in the Pixar Pier area of Disney California Adventure. The attraction simulates the experience of ascending and descending within the tentacles of a giant jellyfish. The ride was manufactured by S&S – Sansei Technologies, a prominent manufacturer of amusement park rides. It offers a gentler alternative to more intense drop tower attractions, catering to a wider range of guests.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's theming places guests within a whimsical underwater environment. The ride vehicles are designed to resemble jellyfish, complete with translucent "tentacles" that sway during the ascent and descent. The surrounding area features colorful marine-themed decorations, contributing to the immersive atmosphere. The overall aesthetic is intended to evoke a sense of playful exploration within a vibrant ocean setting.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of twelve jellyfish-shaped gondolas. The gondolas are lifted slowly to a height of approximately 40 feet (12 meters). After a brief pause, the gondolas are released, providing a series of short, controlled drops. The ride cycle includes multiple bounces, offering a sensation of weightlessness. The entire ride experience lasts approximately one minute.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Jumpin' Jellyfish is designed for families and children. The minimum height requirement is 40 inches (102 cm). Guests with back or neck problems, heart conditions, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding. Guests should also be aware of the potential for motion sickness.
