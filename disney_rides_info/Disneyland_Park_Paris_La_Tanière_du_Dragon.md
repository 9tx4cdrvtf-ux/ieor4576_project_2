# La Tanière du Dragon
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
La Tanière du Dragon (The Dragon's Lair) is a walk-through attraction located beneath Sleeping Beauty Castle in Disneyland Park Paris. It features an Audio-Animatronic dragon, representing the final form of Maleficent from the animated film *Sleeping Beauty*. The attraction, designed by Walt Disney Imagineering, provides a unique immersive experience within the park's central icon.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers on the dragon's imprisonment within a cavern beneath the castle. Guests descend into a dimly lit grotto, encountering stalactites, bubbling pools, and the sounds of the dragon's breathing and movement. The theming emphasizes a sense of foreboding and danger, reflecting the dragon's menacing presence and the implied threat it poses. The environment is designed to create a sense of claustrophobia and anticipation.

## Attraction Experience
Guests proceed through a winding pathway, eventually arriving in the main chamber where the dragon is housed. The Audio-Animatronic dragon is the focal point, exhibiting realistic movements, including head turns, eye movements, and the occasional puff of smoke. The dragon's roar and the heat emanating from its mouth are enhanced by lighting and sound effects. The entire experience lasts approximately two to three minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
La Tanière du Dragon is suitable for most guests. There are no height restrictions. However, the attraction's dark environment and potentially frightening imagery may not be suitable for very young children or those easily frightened. Guests with claustrophobia or sensitivity to loud noises should consider the experience before entering.
