# Enchanted Tale of Beauty and the Beast
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Enchanted Tale of Beauty and the Beast is a dark ride attraction located in the Fantasyland expansion of Tokyo Disneyland. It is based on Disney's 1991 animated film *Beauty and the Beast*. The ride, notable for its innovative ride system, transports guests through key scenes from the film. It is a significant addition to the park, offering a highly immersive and technologically advanced experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the story of Belle and the Beast. The queue area leads through the Beast's castle, with detailed theming that evokes the film's atmosphere. Guests are seated in oversized, animated teacups that move along a track, allowing them to witness scenes from the film, including Belle's encounter with the Beast, the ballroom dance, and the final transformation. The ride incorporates elaborate sets, animatronics, and projections to enhance the storytelling.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the ride vehicles, which are designed to resemble enchanted teacups. The ride system utilizes a trackless system, allowing the vehicles to move freely and fluidly through the scenes. The journey lasts approximately 8 minutes. The ride incorporates audio-animatronics, projection mapping, and special effects, including lighting and music, to create a multi-sensory experience. The climactic ballroom scene features a large-scale projection of Belle and the Beast dancing.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or sensitivity to loud noises should be aware of the ride's effects. The ride is accessible to guests using wheelchairs.
