# Goofy's Paint 'n' Play House
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Goofy's Paint 'n' Play House is a walkthrough attraction located in Toontown at Tokyo Disneyland. It is a unique interactive experience where guests assist Goofy in painting his house. The attraction is designed to be family-friendly and offers a playful environment for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers around Goofy's attempt to repaint his house. Guests enter Goofy's house, which is in a state of disrepair, and are provided with virtual paint guns. The environment is themed to resemble a cartoon, with exaggerated shapes, bright colors, and whimsical details. The setting is designed to immerse guests in a playful and interactive experience, reflecting the chaotic nature of Goofy's character.

## Attraction Experience
Guests are provided with interactive paint guns and are encouraged to "paint" various elements within Goofy's house. Using the paint guns, guests can aim at specific targets, triggering animations and visual effects. The attraction utilizes projection mapping and other special effects to create the illusion of paint splattering and changing the appearance of the house. The experience lasts approximately 5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Goofy's Paint 'n' Play House is designed for a general audience, with no height restrictions. The attraction is suitable for all ages, but young children may require assistance from adults. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions listed for this attraction.
