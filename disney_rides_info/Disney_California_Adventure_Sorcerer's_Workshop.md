# Sorcerer's Workshop
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
The Sorcerer's Workshop is an interactive attraction located within the Animation Building at Disney California Adventure. It is a walk-through experience that allows guests to explore various rooms themed after the film *Fantasia* and engage in interactive activities. The attraction is designed to entertain guests of all ages and provides a hands-on exploration of animation and character design.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as the workshop of Yen Sid, the sorcerer from *Fantasia*. Guests enter through a grand library filled with books and magical artifacts. The environment is designed to evoke a sense of wonder and discovery, with elements of classic animation and storytelling incorporated throughout. The rooms are decorated with props and artwork inspired by the film, creating an immersive atmosphere that transports guests into the world of *Fantasia*.

## Attraction Experience
The Sorcerer's Workshop consists of several interactive rooms. Guests can enter "Be Our Guest," a room where they can create their own animated character. In "Be Animated," guests can experiment with animation techniques, including creating their own flipbook. The final room, "The Magic of Disney Animation," allows guests to learn about the animation process and see how Disney characters are brought to life. The entire experience typically lasts approximately 15-20 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Sorcerer's Workshop is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with the attraction. The experience is primarily a walk-through and does not involve any intense physical activity.
