# Jungle Cruise
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
The Jungle Cruise is a boat ride attraction located in Adventureland at Disneyland. It is a slow-moving riverboat journey through a fictionalized jungle environment. The attraction, a Disneyland opening day staple, is known for its pun-filled narration and animatronic animal figures. It was designed by Walt Disney and Imagineers, and is a classic example of early immersive theme park design.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests embark on a riverboat expedition, ostensibly led by a skipper, through the rivers of the world. The narrative presents the journey as a perilous exploration of exotic locales, encountering various dangers and wildlife. The setting is a stylized representation of the Amazon, the Nile, and other tropical rivers, populated by animatronic animals and indigenous figures. The humor is derived from the skipper's scripted jokes and the exaggerated reactions of the animatronic figures to the boat's passage.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a boat, which travels along a man-made waterway. The journey takes approximately 8-10 minutes. The boat passes through various themed scenes, including a jungle teeming with animatronic animals such as hippos, elephants, and gorillas. The skipper provides a live narration, pointing out the sights and delivering pre-written jokes. The ride utilizes a combination of audio-animatronics, forced perspective, and lighting effects to create the illusion of a dangerous and exotic adventure.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Jungle Cruise is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find boarding and disembarking the boats challenging. The ride is not recommended for individuals sensitive to loud noises or those who may be easily frightened by the animatronic figures.
