# Cinderella Carousel
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Cinderella Carousel is a classic carousel attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Hong Kong. It is a traditional carousel featuring intricately designed horses and carriages, offering a gentle and visually appealing ride experience. The attraction is a staple of Disney theme parks worldwide, providing a timeless experience for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around the classic Disney animated film *Cinderella*. The carousel's design incorporates elements from the film, including the iconic pumpkin carriage and the overall color scheme of the movie. The horses are adorned with ornate details and painted in various colors, reflecting the elegance and charm of Cinderella's world. The surrounding area is decorated with fairytale imagery, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of the carousel's horses or carriages. The carousel then begins to rotate in a circular motion, accompanied by classic Disney music. The ride lasts approximately two minutes. The horses move up and down in a simulated galloping motion, providing a gentle and enjoyable experience. The attraction utilizes traditional carousel technology, with a central mechanism powering the rotation and movement of the horses.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Cinderella Carousel is designed for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with certain medical conditions, such as those prone to motion sickness, may wish to consider the ride's gentle motion before boarding. Wheelchairs are accommodated.
