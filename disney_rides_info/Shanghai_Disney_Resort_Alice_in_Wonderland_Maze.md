# Alice in Wonderland Maze
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
The Alice in Wonderland Maze is a walk-through attraction located in the Gardens of Imagination area of Shanghai Disney Resort. It is a themed maze designed to immerse guests in the whimsical world of Disney's *Alice in Wonderland*. The maze offers a series of interactive elements and visual gags, providing a unique experience for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed after the classic animated film *Alice in Wonderland*. Guests navigate through a series of themed environments, including the Mad Hatter's Tea Party, the Queen of Hearts' rose garden, and the Cheshire Cat's disappearing act. The environment incorporates oversized props, character cutouts, and thematic music to enhance the immersive experience. The overall narrative follows Alice's journey through Wonderland, with guests encountering various iconic characters and scenes from the film.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter the maze and choose from several pathways, each leading to different themed areas. The maze incorporates forced perspective, optical illusions, and interactive elements such as talking flowers and the Queen of Hearts' croquet game. The experience includes a climb to the top of the Queen of Hearts' castle, offering panoramic views of the maze and surrounding gardens. The duration of the experience is approximately 15-20 minutes, depending on the guest's pace and exploration.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Alice in Wonderland Maze is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find certain areas challenging due to the narrow pathways and stairs. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
