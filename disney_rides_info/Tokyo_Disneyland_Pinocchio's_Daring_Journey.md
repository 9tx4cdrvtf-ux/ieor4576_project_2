# Pinocchio's Daring Journey
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Pinocchio's Daring Journey is a dark ride attraction located in Fantasyland at Tokyo Disneyland. It is based on Disney's 1940 animated film *Pinocchio*. The ride utilizes a track-based system to transport guests through various scenes from the film, showcasing Audio-Animatronic figures and themed environments. The attraction is a classic dark ride experience, offering a whimsical journey through the story.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests experience the story of Pinocchio, a wooden puppet brought to life by the Blue Fairy. The ride follows Pinocchio's adventures as he attempts to become a real boy, encountering characters such as Geppetto, Jiminy Cricket, Honest John, and Stromboli. The environments are designed to evoke the film's settings, including Geppetto's workshop, Pleasure Island, and the inside of Monstro the whale. The overall atmosphere is intended to immerse guests in the narrative.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board ride vehicles designed to resemble wooden carts. The ride progresses through a series of scenes, each depicting a key moment from the film. Audio-Animatronic figures and special effects, including lighting and sound, bring the story to life. The ride lasts approximately 2 minutes and 30 seconds. The vehicles move along a predetermined track, guiding guests through the various scenes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with sensitivity to darkness or enclosed spaces should be aware of the ride's environment. The ride is not recommended for those with certain medical conditions; specific warnings are posted at the attraction entrance.
