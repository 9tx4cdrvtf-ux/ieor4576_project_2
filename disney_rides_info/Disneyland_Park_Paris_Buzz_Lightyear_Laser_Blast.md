# Buzz Lightyear Laser Blast
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Buzz Lightyear Laser Blast is a shooting dark ride located in Discoveryland at Disneyland Park Paris. The attraction, manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering, allows guests to participate in a laser-based shooting game within a themed environment based on the *Toy Story* franchise. It is a clone of the original Buzz Lightyear Astro Blasters attraction found at other Disney parks.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are recruited as Space Rangers by Star Command to help Buzz Lightyear defeat the Evil Emperor Zurg. The ride's narrative places guests in a battle against Zurg, who is attempting to steal batteries from toys to power a weapon. The queue area features various themed elements, including a briefing room and a launch bay, immersing guests in the *Toy Story* universe. The ride vehicles are styled as spaceships.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual ride vehicles equipped with laser blasters. The vehicles travel along a track through various scenes depicting Zurg's forces and targets. Riders aim their laser blasters at targets, scoring points based on the target's value. The ride incorporates audio-animatronics, special effects, and vibrant lighting to enhance the experience. The ride duration is approximately 4 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Buzz Lightyear Laser Blast is generally suitable for all ages. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or those prone to motion sickness should be aware of the ride's effects. The attraction is accessible to guests with disabilities, with specific accommodations available.
