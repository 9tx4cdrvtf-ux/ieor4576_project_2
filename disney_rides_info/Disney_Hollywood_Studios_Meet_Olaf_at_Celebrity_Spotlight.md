# Meet Olaf at Celebrity Spotlight
**Location:** Disney Hollywood Studios

## Overview
Meet Olaf at Celebrity Spotlight is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in the Echo Lake area of Disney's Hollywood Studios. It allows guests to interact with the beloved snowman Olaf from Disney's *Frozen* franchise. The attraction provides a photo opportunity and brief conversation with the character.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as a Hollywood studio spotlight, with a backdrop designed to resemble a movie set. Guests are invited to meet Olaf as he prepares for his next big role. The environment is decorated with props and set pieces that evoke the whimsical atmosphere of the *Frozen* films, creating a cheerful and inviting space for interactions.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area, often with a covered queue line to provide shade. Upon reaching the front of the line, guests are escorted into the meet-and-greet area. Here, they have the opportunity to pose for photos with Olaf and engage in brief conversations. Disney PhotoPass photographers are available to capture professional photos, which are linked to the guest's My Disney Experience account. The entire experience typically lasts for a few minutes per guest or group.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Meet Olaf at Celebrity Spotlight is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. Guests with mobility issues can access the meet-and-greet area, and the queue is generally accessible.
