# TRON Realm, Chevrolet Digital Challenge
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
TRON Realm, Chevrolet Digital Challenge is an interactive experience located in Tomorrowland at Shanghai Disney Resort. It is a sponsored attraction, developed in partnership with Chevrolet, offering guests a blend of interactive games and photo opportunities themed around the TRON franchise. The attraction provides a digital environment for guests to engage with the TRON universe.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the world of TRON, specifically the Grid. Guests are presented with a futuristic environment inspired by the film series, featuring neon lights, sleek architecture, and digital projections. The narrative positions guests as recruits entering the Grid to participate in various challenges. The Chevrolet sponsorship is integrated through the presence of digital vehicles and interactive elements.

## Attraction Experience
Guests navigate through a series of interactive stations. These stations typically involve digital games and challenges, such as virtual light cycle races and other skill-based activities. The experience incorporates digital displays, motion tracking, and projection mapping to create an immersive environment. Guests can also take photos with TRON-themed backdrops and vehicles. The duration of the experience varies depending on guest participation, typically lasting between 15-20 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for a wide audience, including families and guests of all ages. There are no specific height restrictions. Guests with photosensitive epilepsy or other conditions that may be triggered by flashing lights or intense visual effects should exercise caution.
