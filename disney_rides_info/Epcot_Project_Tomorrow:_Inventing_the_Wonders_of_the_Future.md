# Project Tomorrow: Inventing the Wonders of the Future
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Project Tomorrow is an interactive exhibit located within the former Wonders of Life pavilion in Epcot's World Discovery neighborhood. It is a series of interactive games and exhibits focused on the future of technology, health, and innovation. The attraction allows guests to engage with various concepts through hands-on experiences, emphasizing the potential of scientific advancements.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction does not have a specific overarching narrative or backstory. Instead, it presents a collection of interactive experiences designed to showcase different aspects of future technologies. The theming focuses on a clean, futuristic aesthetic, utilizing bright colors, digital displays, and interactive kiosks to create an engaging environment. The setting is designed to evoke a sense of optimism and excitement about the possibilities of the future.

## Attraction Experience
Guests explore a series of interactive stations. These stations typically involve touch-screen interfaces, physical challenges, and collaborative gameplay. Examples include games focused on medical advancements, energy conservation, and communication technologies. The duration of each individual game varies, but guests can spend a significant amount of time exploring the various exhibits. The experience is self-guided, allowing guests to choose which activities they wish to participate in.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Project Tomorrow is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with photosensitivity or other sensory sensitivities should be aware of the use of flashing lights and loud noises in some of the interactive exhibits. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
