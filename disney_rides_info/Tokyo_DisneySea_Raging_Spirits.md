# Raging Spirits
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Raging Spirits is a steel roller coaster located in the Lost River Delta area of Tokyo DisneySea. Manufactured by Intamin, the attraction is a modified version of their "Inverted Coaster" model, featuring a 360-degree vertical loop. It is themed around an archaeological dig site and offers a relatively short but intense thrill ride experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers on an archaeological excavation site where ancient spirits are believed to be disturbed. Guests are presented as explorers venturing into this site, encountering a sacrificial altar and other artifacts. The queue line and surrounding environment are designed to evoke a sense of mystery and danger, with weathered ruins, dense foliage, and ambient sound effects contributing to the atmosphere. The coaster's layout is integrated into the themed environment, simulating a chaotic and unpredictable journey through the dig site.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a train that ascends a lift hill. The ride then features a series of twists, turns, and a single vertical loop. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds. The coaster utilizes a lap bar restraint system. Sensory effects include wind, speed, and the visual impact of the surrounding themed environment.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Raging Spirits is designed for thrill-seekers. The height requirement is 117 cm (46 in). Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or other health concerns are advised against riding. Expectant mothers are also prohibited.
