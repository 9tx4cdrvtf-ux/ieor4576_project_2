# Chip 'n Dale's Treehouse
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Chip 'n Dale's Treehouse is a walk-through attraction located in Critter Country at Tokyo Disneyland. It offers guests a chance to explore a multi-level, themed treehouse environment based on the Disney characters Chip and Dale. The attraction is designed to provide an interactive and immersive experience for guests of all ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents a whimsical environment depicting the home of Chip and Dale. The treehouse is constructed with oversized acorns, branches, and other natural elements. Inside, guests encounter various rooms and areas decorated with furniture and belongings reflecting the characters' personalities and their love for nuts. The theming incorporates elements of the surrounding Critter Country, blending seamlessly with the area's overall aesthetic.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter the treehouse via a series of stairs and ramps. The attraction features multiple levels, including a kitchen, living room, and observation deck. Visitors can explore the various rooms, interact with themed props, and enjoy views of Critter Country from the treehouse's vantage points. The experience is self-guided, allowing guests to move at their own pace. The duration of a typical visit is approximately 5-10 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Chip 'n Dale's Treehouse is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction is accessible via stairs and ramps; however, guests with mobility impairments may find certain areas challenging to navigate. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
