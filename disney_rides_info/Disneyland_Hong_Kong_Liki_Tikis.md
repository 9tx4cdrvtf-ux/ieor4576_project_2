# Liki Tikis
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Liki Tikis is a water play area located in Adventureland at Disneyland Hong Kong. It provides a series of interactive water features and splash zones designed for children and families. The attraction is a permanent installation, offering a refreshing respite from the Hong Kong heat.

## Narrative and Theming
Themed to a Polynesian village, Liki Tikis immerses guests in a tropical environment. The area features stylized tiki statues, lush vegetation, and themed rockwork. The narrative centers around a playful village where the tiki gods have brought the gift of water to the inhabitants. The environment is designed to evoke a sense of adventure and discovery, encouraging exploration and interaction with the various water elements.

## Attraction Experience
Guests navigate a series of interconnected splash zones. These zones include geysers, water cannons, tipping buckets, and fountains. The water features are activated by guest interaction, creating a dynamic and unpredictable play experience. The area is primarily designed for children, with shallow water depths and soft surfaces. The duration of a typical visit varies depending on guest participation, but generally lasts between 15 and 30 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Liki Tikis is primarily targeted towards children and families. There are no specific height requirements. Guests are advised to wear appropriate swimwear or clothing that can get wet. Children must be supervised by a responsible adult. Guests with certain medical conditions or sensitivities to water may wish to avoid the attraction.
