# Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance is a multi-platform, immersive dark ride located within Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge at Disneyland. The attraction, notable for its innovative use of ride systems and elaborate theming, is a collaboration between Walt Disney Imagineering and various technology providers. It is considered a flagship attraction of Galaxy's Edge, offering a highly detailed and interactive experience.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the Star Wars universe, joining the Resistance in a fight against the First Order. The experience begins with guests being recruited as new Resistance members, traveling aboard a transport ship, and subsequently captured by a First Order Star Destroyer. The narrative unfolds through multiple ride systems and environments, including a briefing room, a transport shuttle, a Star Destroyer detention cell, and a climactic escape sequence. The theming incorporates detailed sets, animatronics, and projections to create a realistic and engaging atmosphere.

## Attraction Experience
The attraction utilizes a combination of ride systems, including a trackless ride vehicle, motion simulators, and drop sequences. The experience lasts approximately 18 minutes and involves multiple stages. Guests move through various scenes, encountering characters such as Rey, Kylo Ren, and stormtroopers. The ride incorporates special effects such as projections, physical effects, and audio-animatronics to enhance the immersive experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a broad audience, but guests must be at least 40 inches (102 cm) tall to ride. Due to the intense nature of the experience, including simulated motion, darkness, and loud noises, guests with certain health conditions, such as heart problems, back or neck issues, or those who are pregnant, are advised against riding.
