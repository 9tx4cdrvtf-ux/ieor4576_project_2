# Scuttle's Scooters
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Scuttle's Scooters is a spinning amusement ride located in the Mermaid Lagoon area of Tokyo DisneySea. The attraction is a Zamperla "Disk'O" ride, offering a family-friendly experience with gentle spinning and undulating motion. It is themed after the character Scuttle from Disney's *The Little Mermaid* and provides a whimsical, underwater-themed experience for guests.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in a whimsical underwater environment, designed to resemble Scuttle's collection of human artifacts. The ride vehicles are themed as various types of sea creatures and objects, such as seashells and seahorses. The surrounding area is decorated with vibrant colors, playful props, and animated figures, creating a lively and immersive atmosphere that reflects the lighthearted nature of the *Little Mermaid* setting.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual ride vehicles that are arranged along a large, circular platform. The platform moves along a curved track, providing a gentle wave-like motion while simultaneously rotating. The ride cycle lasts approximately 90 seconds. The experience is enhanced by the surrounding themed environment, including lighting and sound effects that contribute to the underwater illusion.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Scuttle's Scooters is designed for a broad audience, including families with young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with a sensitivity to motion sickness or other health concerns should consider the ride's spinning motion before boarding.
