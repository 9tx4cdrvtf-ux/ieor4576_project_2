# King Arthur Carrousel
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
King Arthur Carrousel is a classic carousel attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park. It is a traditional, circular ride featuring hand-carved horses, offering a gentle, family-friendly experience. The carousel is a significant part of Disneyland's original Fantasyland, contributing to the park's fairytale atmosphere.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed after the legend of King Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table. The carousel's horses are adorned with ornate saddles, armor, and banners, each uniquely decorated. The central hub of the carousel features scenes depicting Arthurian lore, enhancing the immersive experience. The surrounding architecture and landscaping further contribute to the medieval fantasy setting, creating a cohesive and thematic environment.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of the 68 hand-carved horses, each with a different pose and decoration. The carousel then begins to rotate in a circular motion, accompanied by traditional carousel music. The ride cycle lasts approximately two minutes. The horses move up and down in a gentle, rhythmic pattern, providing a visual and tactile experience.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
King Arthur Carrousel is designed for a broad audience, including children and adults. There are no height restrictions. Guests with certain health conditions, such as those prone to motion sickness, may wish to consider the ride's gentle nature before boarding.
