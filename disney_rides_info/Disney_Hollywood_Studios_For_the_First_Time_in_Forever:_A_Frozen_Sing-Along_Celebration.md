# For the First Time in Forever: A Frozen Sing-Along Celebration
**Location:** Disney's Hollywood Studios

## Overview
"For the First Time in Forever: A Frozen Sing-Along Celebration" is a live stage show attraction located in the Hyperion Theater at Disney's Hollywood Studios. It is a musical performance featuring characters and songs from Disney's animated film *Frozen*. The show is a replacement for the previous "American Idol Experience" and offers a family-friendly entertainment option within the park.

## Narrative and Theming
The show is presented as a comedic retelling of the events of *Frozen*, narrated by two Royal Historians of Arendelle. The Historians guide the audience through the story, punctuated by musical performances of key songs from the film. The setting is a stylized recreation of Arendelle, with projected backdrops and stage elements enhancing the immersive experience. The narrative focuses on the relationship between Anna and Elsa, incorporating humor and audience participation.

## Attraction Experience
The show runs approximately 30 minutes. Guests are seated in the Hyperion Theater and experience a live performance featuring costumed performers portraying Anna, Elsa, Kristoff, and Hans. The show incorporates large-screen projections of scenes from *Frozen* and sing-along lyrics. The Royal Historians interact with the audience, encouraging participation in the songs and comedic moments.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a general audience, particularly families with young children. There are no height restrictions. The show is suitable for guests of all ages. No specific health warnings or restrictions are listed.
