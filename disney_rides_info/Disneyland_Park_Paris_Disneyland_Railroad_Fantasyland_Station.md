# Disneyland Railroad Fantasyland Station
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
The Disneyland Railroad Fantasyland Station is a railway station located within the Fantasyland area of Disneyland Park Paris. It serves as one of the four stops on the Disneyland Railroad, a narrow-gauge steam train that circles the park. The station allows guests to embark and disembark the train, providing transportation and scenic views of Fantasyland. The Disneyland Railroad is a significant attraction, offering a relaxing journey and a unique perspective of the park's various lands.

## Narrative and Theming
The Fantasyland Station is themed to blend seamlessly with the surrounding fairytale environment. The architecture evokes a whimsical, storybook aesthetic, featuring pastel colors, ornate detailing, and elements reminiscent of classic children's literature. The station's design aims to immerse guests in the magical atmosphere of Fantasyland, preparing them for the attractions and experiences within the land.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter the station and queue for the next available train. The train, pulled by a steam locomotive, stops at the platform for boarding. The journey around the park takes approximately 20 minutes, with stops at Main Street, U.S.A., Frontierland, and Discoveryland. Passengers can enjoy views of the various themed lands, including miniature versions of iconic scenes and attractions. The ride offers a leisurely pace, allowing for observation and photography.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Disneyland Railroad is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues can access the train with assistance. The ride is generally considered gentle, but guests with certain medical conditions should consult with a medical professional before riding.
