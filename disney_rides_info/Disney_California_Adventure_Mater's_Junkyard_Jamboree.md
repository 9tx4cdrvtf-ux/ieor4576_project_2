# Mater's Junkyard Jamboree
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Mater's Junkyard Jamboree is a whip-style amusement ride located in Cars Land at Disney California Adventure. The attraction, manufactured by Zamperla, features a unique "square dance" concept where guests ride in trailers pulled by tractors. It is a family-friendly attraction designed to immerse guests in the world of the *Cars* film franchise.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as Mater's junkyard, a fictional setting within the town of Radiator Springs. Guests are invited to join Mater for a "square dance" where they are towed around a circular track by one of several tractor-themed vehicles. The surrounding environment is filled with various junkyard props, including tires, car parts, and other automotive debris, creating an immersive and whimsical atmosphere. Country music plays throughout the ride, enhancing the overall experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of sixteen trailers, each pulled by a tractor. The tractors move in a circular pattern, while the trailers whip back and forth in a randomized manner. The ride lasts approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds. The ride system utilizes a combination of mechanical and hydraulic systems to create the swaying motion. There are no significant special effects beyond the themed environment and the music.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Mater's Junkyard Jamboree is designed for a family audience. Guests must be at least 32 inches (81 cm) tall to ride. Guests with back problems, neck problems, heart conditions, or other health concerns should consider the ride's motion before participating. Expectant mothers are advised not to ride.
