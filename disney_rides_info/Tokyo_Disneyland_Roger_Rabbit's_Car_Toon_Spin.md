# Roger Rabbit's Car Toon Spin
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
Roger Rabbit's Car Toon Spin is a dark ride attraction located in Toontown at Tokyo Disneyland. It is a track-based, spinning dark ride manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering. The attraction is based on the 1988 film *Who Framed Roger Rabbit* and is a key component of the Toontown area, providing a whimsical and interactive experience for guests.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are immersed in the world of Toontown, specifically the setting of the film. The story places riders in a taxicab, tasked with helping Roger Rabbit escape the villainous Judge Doom and his weasels. The queue area features elements from the film, including posters and props, further establishing the narrative. The ride environment is designed to resemble the chaotic and cartoonish streets of Toontown.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a taxi cab that seats up to three riders. The vehicles travel along a track, navigating through various scenes from the film. Riders have the ability to spin their vehicles 360 degrees using a steering wheel, allowing them to control their view of the action. The ride incorporates various special effects, including animatronics, lighting, and sound, to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for a general audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or a predisposition to motion sickness should consider the ride's spinning nature before boarding. Pregnant women and individuals with certain health conditions are advised to consult with a medical professional before riding.
