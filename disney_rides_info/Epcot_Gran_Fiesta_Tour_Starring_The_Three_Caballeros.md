# Gran Fiesta Tour Starring The Three Caballeros
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Gran Fiesta Tour Starring The Three Caballeros is a slow-moving boat ride located within the Mexico Pavilion at Epcot. The attraction, a re-theming of the former El Rio del Tiempo, provides a gentle journey through scenes depicting Mexican culture and landmarks. It features Audio-Animatronic figures of The Three Caballeros: Panchito Pistoles, José Carioca, and Donald Duck. The ride is manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering and is a popular family-friendly attraction.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride's narrative follows Donald Duck as he attempts to reunite with his friends, Panchito and José, for a concert in Mexico City. The journey takes guests through various regions of Mexico, showcasing vibrant cultural celebrations, iconic landmarks, and traditional art forms. The storyline is interwoven with musical numbers from the 1944 Disney film, *The Three Caballeros*. The setting is designed to evoke the atmosphere of a festive Mexican celebration.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a boat that gently floats through the attraction. The ride begins with a tour of a bustling marketplace, transitioning to scenes featuring Audio-Animatronic figures, including a performance by The Three Caballeros. The ride incorporates projections onto water screens, creating dynamic visuals. The journey culminates in a grand finale featuring the Caballeros performing together. The ride duration is approximately 8 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Gran Fiesta Tour Starring The Three Caballeros is designed for a general audience, including families with young children. There are no height restrictions. The ride is considered a gentle attraction and is accessible to guests with mobility limitations. Guests with photosensitivity should be aware of the use of projections and flashing lights.
