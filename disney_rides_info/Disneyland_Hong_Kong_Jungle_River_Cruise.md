# Jungle River Cruise
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
The Jungle River Cruise is a boat ride attraction located in Adventureland at Disneyland Hong Kong. It is a classic Disney dark ride, offering guests a guided tour through a fictional jungle environment. The attraction is a variation of the original Jungle Cruise found at Disneyland and other Disney parks, utilizing similar ride technology and theming.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests embark on a riverboat journey led by a live skipper, navigating through various themed environments representing different regions of the world's jungles. The narrative centers around the skipper's humorous commentary and encounters with animatronic animals and other jungle inhabitants. The theming incorporates elements of adventure, exploration, and the exotic, with environments including the African Veldt, the Amazon River, and Southeast Asian ruins.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with guests boarding a boat that travels along a man-made river. The boats are guided by an underwater track system. During the approximately 8-minute journey, guests encounter animatronic animals such as elephants, hippos, and crocodiles, as well as special effects like simulated fire and water. The skipper provides a live narration, pointing out various sights and delivering pre-scripted jokes. The ride concludes with the boat returning to the loading dock.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Jungle River Cruise is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may need assistance boarding and disembarking the boats. The attraction is not recommended for individuals sensitive to loud noises or sudden movements.
