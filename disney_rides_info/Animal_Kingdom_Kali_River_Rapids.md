# Kali River Rapids
**Location:** Animal Kingdom

## Overview
Kali River Rapids is a river rapids ride located in the Asia section of Disney's Animal Kingdom. The attraction simulates a whitewater rafting experience on the Chakranadi River. Manufactured by Intamin, the ride is notable for its environmental theme and its focus on conservation.

## Narrative and Theming
The ride's narrative centers around a journey through the fictional forests of the fictional Anandapur region. Guests are cast as tourists on a river expedition operated by the "Himalayan Escapes" tour company. The ride's storyline highlights the impact of deforestation and illegal logging, culminating in a confrontation with the destructive forces impacting the environment. The queue area features detailed theming, including artifacts and signage that enhance the narrative.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a circular raft capable of holding up to twelve passengers. The raft is propelled by water currents through a series of rapids, waterfalls, and themed environments. The ride incorporates various special effects, including mist, fire, and sound effects to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately five minutes, with the potential for guests to become significantly wet.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Kali River Rapids is suitable for guests of all ages, though young children may find the experience intense. The height requirement is 38 inches (97 cm). Guests with back or neck problems, heart conditions, or other health concerns should consult with a medical professional before riding. Expectant mothers are advised not to ride.
