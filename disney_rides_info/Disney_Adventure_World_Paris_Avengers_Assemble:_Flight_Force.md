# Avengers Assemble: Flight Force
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
Avengers Assemble: Flight Force is a steel roller coaster located in the Avengers Campus of Disney Adventure World Paris. Manufactured by Vekoma, the ride is a high-speed, launched coaster featuring a unique multi-launch system and immersive theming based on the Marvel Cinematic Universe. It is a significant thrill ride within the park, offering a high capacity and a visually engaging experience.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are recruited as new recruits to assist the Avengers in a mission to stop a threat from space. The queue line immerses guests in the S.H.I.E.L.D. headquarters, featuring interactive elements and pre-show videos with Iron Man and Captain Marvel. The ride vehicles are designed to resemble Stark Industries' high-speed vehicles, enhancing the narrative of a mission to intercept a cosmic threat.

## Attraction Experience
The ride begins with a high-speed launch, followed by a series of inversions and twists. The coaster utilizes a multi-launch system, providing several bursts of acceleration throughout the ride. Sensory effects include onboard audio, lighting, and projections that enhance the experience. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Avengers Assemble: Flight Force is designed for thrill-seekers. The minimum height requirement is 1.20 meters (47 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, particularly those related to the heart, back, or neck, and those who are pregnant, are advised against riding.
