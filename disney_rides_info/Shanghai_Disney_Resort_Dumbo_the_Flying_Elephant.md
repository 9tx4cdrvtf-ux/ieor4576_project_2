# Dumbo the Flying Elephant
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Dumbo the Flying Elephant is a classic spinner ride located in Fantasyland at Shanghai Disney Resort. Based on the 1941 Disney animated film *Dumbo*, the attraction allows guests to experience the sensation of flight aboard a Dumbo-themed vehicle. The ride is a standard model manufactured by Zamperla, a prominent amusement ride manufacturer. It is a popular family-friendly attraction, offering a gentle and visually engaging experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's setting is inspired by the circus environment depicted in the film. Guests queue within a themed area featuring circus tents and whimsical decorations. The ride vehicles are designed to resemble Dumbo the elephant, complete with large ears and a trunk. The surrounding area incorporates elements from the movie, creating an immersive atmosphere that transports guests into the world of *Dumbo*.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of sixteen Dumbo vehicles, each accommodating a small number of riders. Once seated, the ride begins with the vehicles ascending and descending in a circular motion. Riders can control the height of their individual Dumbo by using a lever inside the vehicle. The ride cycle lasts approximately two minutes, providing views of Fantasyland and the surrounding areas.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Dumbo the Flying Elephant is designed for a broad audience, particularly families with young children. There is a height requirement of 97 cm (38 inches) to ride. Guests with certain medical conditions, such as heart problems or back issues, should consult with a medical professional before riding. Expectant mothers are advised against riding.
