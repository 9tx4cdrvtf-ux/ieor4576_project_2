# Iron Man Experience - Presented by AIA
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
The Iron Man Experience - Presented by AIA is a 3D motion simulator ride located in the Tomorrowland area of Disneyland Hong Kong. It is the first Marvel-themed ride-through attraction at a Disney park. The attraction allows guests to experience a simulated flight over Hong Kong alongside Iron Man, utilizing a combination of motion-based vehicles and immersive projection mapping.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are invited to attend the Stark Expo, a showcase of Stark Industries' latest innovations. The Expo is set within a futuristic Hong Kong, and guests are briefed on the Iron Wing, a new Iron Man-designed flight vehicle. The narrative involves a flight over Hong Kong, which is interrupted by an attack from Hydra forces. Iron Man, along with the guests, must defend the city.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a motion-based vehicle, the Iron Wing, which simulates flight. The ride utilizes a large, curved screen displaying 3D visuals of Hong Kong and the surrounding areas. The vehicle moves in sync with the on-screen action, creating the sensation of soaring through the city. Sensory effects, including wind and water, are incorporated to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 4 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a general audience. Guests must be at least 102 cm (40 inches) tall to ride. Guests with pre-existing health conditions, including heart problems, back or neck issues, or those who are pregnant, are advised to consider their health before riding.
