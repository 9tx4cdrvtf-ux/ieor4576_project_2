# Blowfish Balloon Race
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Blowfish Balloon Race is a gentle, aerial carousel-style attraction located in the Port Discovery area of Tokyo DisneySea. The ride, manufactured by Zamperla, offers guests a simulated hot air balloon experience, themed to resemble blowfish. It is designed to provide a visually appealing and family-friendly experience, contributing to the immersive atmosphere of Port Discovery.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed around a whimsical underwater world. Guests are invited to board individual blowfish-shaped balloons, which are tethered to a central rotating platform. The setting evokes a playful marine environment, with colorful coral, animated sea creatures, and other nautical elements contributing to the overall aesthetic. The ride's narrative suggests a playful race through the ocean currents, offering a lighthearted adventure for all ages.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual blowfish-shaped gondolas, each accommodating a small number of riders. The ride begins with the central platform rotating, while the blowfish balloons gently ascend and descend, simulating the sensation of flight. The ride cycle lasts approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds. The attraction utilizes simple mechanical systems and lighting effects to enhance the experience, providing a visually engaging and non-intimidating ride suitable for young children and adults alike.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Blowfish Balloon Race is primarily targeted towards families and young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with a fear of heights or those prone to motion sickness should consider their sensitivities before riding. The attraction is generally accessible to guests with disabilities, though specific accommodations may be required.
