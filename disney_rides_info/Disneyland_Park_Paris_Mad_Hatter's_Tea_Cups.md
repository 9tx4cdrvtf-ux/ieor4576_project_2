# Mad Hatter's Tea Cups
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Mad Hatter's Tea Cups is a spinning teacup ride located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Park Paris. The attraction is a classic "tea cup" ride, manufactured by Zamperla, and is based on the tea party scene from Disney's animated film *Alice in Wonderland*. It is a popular family-friendly attraction, offering a whimsical and visually engaging experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the whimsical world of the Mad Hatter's tea party. The ride area is themed to resemble a garden party setting, featuring oversized teacups and colorful, stylized decorations. The surrounding environment includes whimsical elements inspired by the film, such as oversized flowers and playful props, enhancing the immersive experience. The overall aesthetic is designed to evoke a sense of playful chaos and lighthearted fun, consistent with the film's narrative.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual teacups, each capable of seating up to four riders. The ride begins with the teacups rotating on a circular platform. Each teacup also features a central wheel, allowing riders to control the speed of their individual cup's spinning. The ride duration is approximately 90 seconds. The attraction utilizes simple mechanical technology to provide the spinning motion, enhanced by the visual theming and the overall atmosphere of the Fantasyland setting.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Mad Hatter's Tea Cups is designed for a general audience, particularly families with young children. There are no height restrictions. Guests with a predisposition to motion sickness or other health concerns should consider the spinning nature of the ride before boarding.
