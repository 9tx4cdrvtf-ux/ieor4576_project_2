# Mexico Folk Art Gallery
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
The Mexico Folk Art Gallery is a walk-through exhibit located within the Mexico Pavilion at Epcot's World Showcase. It showcases a rotating collection of traditional Mexican folk art, including sculptures, paintings, and crafts. The exhibit provides guests with an opportunity to appreciate the diverse artistic heritage of Mexico.

## Narrative and Theming
The gallery is housed within the iconic Mesoamerican pyramid structure that dominates the Mexico Pavilion. The interior is designed to evoke the atmosphere of a twilight marketplace, with soft lighting and ambient sounds creating an immersive environment. The exhibit's theming aims to transport guests to a vibrant Mexican town, highlighting the beauty and craftsmanship of the displayed artwork.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter the gallery and proceed at their own pace through a series of display cases and open areas. The exhibits feature a variety of folk art, including pottery, textiles, and wood carvings. Informational plaques provide details about the artists, techniques, and cultural significance of each piece. The experience is self-guided, allowing guests to spend as much time as they wish examining the artwork. The duration of a visit varies depending on individual interest.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Mexico Folk Art Gallery is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. Guests with mobility concerns can navigate the exhibit, as the pathways are generally accessible.
