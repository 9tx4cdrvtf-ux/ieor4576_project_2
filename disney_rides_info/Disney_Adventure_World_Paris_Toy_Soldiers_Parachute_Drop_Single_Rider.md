# Toy Soldiers Parachute Drop Single Rider
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
Toy Soldiers Parachute Drop Single Rider is a drop tower-style amusement ride located in the Toy Story Playland area of Disney Adventure World Paris. The attraction simulates a parachute jump, offering guests a brief but thrilling experience. Manufactured by S&S – Sansei Technologies, it is themed after the iconic green plastic toy soldiers from the *Toy Story* film franchise.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are "recruited" as new toy soldiers and are instructed to participate in a training exercise. The queue line winds through a military-themed area, complete with oversized toy boxes and camouflage netting. The ride vehicles, resembling parachutes, are positioned on a central tower. The setting immerses guests in Andy's backyard, scaled to the size of a toy.

## Attraction Experience
Riders are seated in open-air gondolas, each holding several guests. The gondolas are lifted to the top of the tower and then repeatedly dropped, simulating a parachute descent. The ride features a series of controlled drops, providing a sensation of weightlessness. The entire experience lasts approximately one minute, including the ascent and descent phases. The Single Rider queue allows guests to bypass the main queue by filling in available spaces on the ride vehicles.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is generally suitable for guests of all ages, provided they meet the minimum height requirement of 81 cm (32 inches). Guests with pre-existing health conditions, particularly those affecting the heart, back, or neck, are advised to consult with a medical professional before riding. Expectant mothers are also advised against riding.
