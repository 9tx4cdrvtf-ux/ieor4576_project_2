# Redwood Creek Challenge Trail
**Location:** Disney California Adventure

## Overview
Redwood Creek Challenge Trail is a self-guided, interactive nature trail located in the Grizzly Peak area of Disney California Adventure. It is a walk-through attraction designed to provide guests with a series of physical challenges and educational experiences themed around the natural environment of the California redwood forests. The attraction is not a ride but rather a series of activity stations.

## Narrative and Theming
The trail immerses guests in a simulated redwood forest environment. Theming elements include towering redwood trees, rock formations, and representations of wildlife native to the region. Guests are encouraged to explore and discover, with the narrative focusing on the importance of conservation and the beauty of the natural world. The area is designed to evoke a sense of adventure and exploration, with various interactive elements that encourage physical activity and engagement with the environment.

## Attraction Experience
Guests navigate the trail at their own pace, encountering various activity stations. These stations include rope bridges, climbing structures, a rock-climbing wall, and a slide. Interactive elements such as a "nature's call" game and a "wilderness explorer" badge station are also present. The duration of the experience varies depending on the guest's pace and participation in the activities, typically ranging from 15 to 45 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Redwood Creek Challenge Trail is suitable for guests of all ages and abilities, though some activities may be more challenging for younger children or those with mobility limitations. There are no height requirements. Guests should be in good physical condition to participate in the activities. Guests with certain health conditions, such as heart problems or back issues, should consider their limitations before participating.
