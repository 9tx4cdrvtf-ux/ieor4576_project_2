# Crush's Coaster Single Rider
**Location:** Disney Adventure World Paris

## Overview
Crush's Coaster Single Rider is a steel spinning roller coaster located in the Toon Studio area of Disney Adventure World Paris. The attraction is themed after the animated film *Finding Nemo* and allows guests to experience a high-speed journey through the East Australian Current. It is a popular attraction known for its long wait times, often mitigated by the availability of a single rider queue.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests enter the attraction through a recreation of the beach from *Finding Nemo*. The queue winds through a darkened area, simulating the ocean depths, and features various props and projections related to the film. The narrative centers around joining Crush and Squirt on a thrilling ride through the ocean currents. The ride environment incorporates elements from the film, including coral reefs, jellyfish, and other marine life.

## Attraction Experience
The ride vehicles are themed as turtle shells, each seating four riders back-to-back. The coaster begins with a slow ascent into a darkened show building. The vehicles then experience a series of twists, turns, and drops, enhanced by special effects such as lighting and projections. The spinning of the vehicles is randomized, providing a unique experience for each ride. The ride duration is approximately one minute.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Crush's Coaster is suitable for guests of most ages, but it is a thrilling ride and may not be appropriate for those with a fear of heights or enclosed spaces. The minimum height requirement is 1.07 meters (42 inches). Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding.
