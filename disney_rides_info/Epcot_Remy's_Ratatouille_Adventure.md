# Remy's Ratatouille Adventure
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Remy's Ratatouille Adventure is a trackless dark ride located in the France Pavilion at Epcot in Walt Disney World. Based on the Disney-Pixar film *Ratatouille*, the attraction shrinks guests to the size of a rat and immerses them in a culinary adventure through Gusteau's restaurant. The ride system utilizes trackless ride vehicles, allowing for a fluid and dynamic experience. The attraction was manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering and is a significant addition to the France Pavilion, expanding the area and enhancing the park's family-friendly offerings.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests enter a Parisian cityscape, finding themselves on the rooftops of Paris. The queue area is themed to resemble the streets of Paris, leading into a pre-show area where guests are introduced to the story. The narrative follows Remy as he attempts to create a delicious meal while avoiding Chef Skinner. The attraction's theming incorporates oversized props and environments, creating the illusion of being small.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board a four-person rat-shaped ride vehicle. The trackless vehicles move through various scenes, utilizing 3D projections, physical sets, and sensory effects such as heat, water, and smells. The ride features a combination of physical sets and digital projections to create the illusion of movement and interaction with the characters. The ride duration is approximately five minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Remy's Ratatouille Adventure is designed for a general audience, including families with children. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or those who are prone to motion sickness should consider the ride's effects before experiencing it.
