# Fairy Tale Forest - presented by PANDORA
**Location:** Disneyland Hong Kong

## Overview
Fairy Tale Forest - presented by PANDORA is a walk-through attraction located in Fantasyland at Disneyland Hong Kong. It allows guests to experience miniature, interactive scenes from classic Disney animated films. The attraction features detailed miniature sets and figures, enhanced by special effects, and is sponsored by the jewelry brand PANDORA.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction presents a series of meticulously crafted miniature scenes depicting iconic moments from various Disney fairy tales. Guests traverse a winding pathway through a forest setting, encountering scenes from films such as *Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs*, *Cinderella*, *Beauty and the Beast*, and *Tangled*. Each scene is accompanied by ambient sound effects and lighting designed to immerse guests in the respective story's atmosphere.

## Attraction Experience
Guests walk along a designated path, observing the miniature scenes. Each scene features animated figures and interactive elements. For example, in the *Snow White* scene, the Seven Dwarfs' cottage is depicted, and guests can observe the dwarfs at work. The attraction utilizes a combination of animatronics, lighting, and sound effects to bring the stories to life. The entire experience typically lasts approximately 10-15 minutes, depending on the guest's pace.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Fairy Tale Forest is designed to be accessible to guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction is a gentle experience and is generally suitable for all guests. However, guests with mobility issues may find navigating the pathway challenging. Guests with photosensitivity should be aware of the lighting effects.
