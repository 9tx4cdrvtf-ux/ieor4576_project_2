# Buzz Lightyear Planet Rescue
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Buzz Lightyear Planet Rescue is a shooting dark ride attraction located in Tomorrowland at Shanghai Disney Resort. It is a unique iteration of the Buzz Lightyear Astro Blasters ride system, developed by Walt Disney Imagineering. The attraction allows guests to participate in a laser-based shooting game, aiming at targets to defeat the Evil Emperor Zurg and his robot army.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are recruited as Space Rangers by Star Command to assist Buzz Lightyear in defending the planet from Zurg's invasion. The ride environment immerses guests in a vibrant, animated space setting, featuring various alien planets and robotic enemies. The queue area incorporates interactive elements and pre-show videos that establish the narrative and provide instructions for the mission.

## Attraction Experience
Riders board individual two-person vehicles equipped with laser blasters. The vehicles navigate through a series of themed scenes, where guests aim at various targets to score points. The ride incorporates animatronics, projected imagery, and sound effects to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 4 minutes, with a final score displayed at the end of the journey.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Buzz Lightyear Planet Rescue is designed for a broad audience, including families and children. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or those prone to motion sickness should consider the ride's effects before boarding.
