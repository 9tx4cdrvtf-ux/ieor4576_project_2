# Anna and Elsa's Frozen Journey
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Anna and Elsa's Frozen Journey is a boat ride attraction located in the Scandinavian-themed area of Tokyo DisneySea, Fantasy Springs. The attraction, manufactured by Walt Disney Imagineering, allows guests to experience key scenes from the Disney animated film *Frozen*. It is a dark ride that utilizes Audio-Animatronics, projections, and practical effects to immerse riders in the story. The ride is a significant addition to the park, expanding its storytelling capabilities and appeal to a wider audience.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative follows the story of *Frozen*, depicting Anna's journey to find her sister Elsa and save Arendelle from an eternal winter. The queue line and ride environment are themed to resemble Arendelle, including the village, Elsa's ice palace, and various locations from the film. The atmosphere is enhanced by detailed set design, lighting, and musical scores from the film.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board boats that travel through various scenes from the movie. The ride features Audio-Animatronic figures of Anna, Elsa, Kristoff, Olaf, and other characters. The boats navigate through a series of show scenes, incorporating projections, lighting effects, and water effects to create a dynamic and immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately six minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is suitable for a general audience. There is no height requirement. Guests with photosensitivity or those prone to motion sickness should consider the ride's effects before boarding. Specific health warnings are posted at the attraction entrance.
