# Advanced Training Lab
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
The Advanced Training Lab was a hands-on interactive experience located within Innoventions West at Epcot. It offered guests the opportunity to participate in various simulated training exercises, focusing on physical and mental challenges. The attraction was designed to be educational and entertaining, allowing visitors to experience aspects of teamwork, problem-solving, and physical coordination.

## Narrative and Theming
The Advanced Training Lab was presented as a futuristic facility where guests could test their skills in a variety of simulated scenarios. The environment was designed to resemble a high-tech laboratory, with interactive stations and digital displays. The fictional context involved guests becoming recruits undergoing training for future missions, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and individual performance.

## Attraction Experience
Guests could choose from several interactive stations, each offering a different challenge. These included activities such as virtual sports simulations, reaction time tests, and collaborative problem-solving games. The technology utilized included touchscreens, motion sensors, and projection mapping. The duration of each activity varied depending on the chosen station, with the overall experience lasting approximately 15-20 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Advanced Training Lab was generally suitable for all ages, though some activities may have been more engaging for older children and adults. There were no specific height requirements. Guests with certain medical conditions or physical limitations were advised to review the activity descriptions and use their discretion when choosing which stations to participate in.
