# Roaring Rapids
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Roaring Rapids is a river rapids ride located in the Adventure Isle area of Shanghai Disney Resort. The attraction, manufactured by Intamin, simulates a thrilling journey through a prehistoric jungle environment. It is a key attraction within Adventure Isle, offering a high-capacity, water-based experience that complements the area's overall theme of exploration and discovery.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests embark on an expedition into the heart of Adventure Isle, a land shrouded in mystery and ancient secrets. The narrative centers around a journey through the perilous Raging River, a waterway said to be home to prehistoric creatures and volatile geological activity. The theming incorporates lush vegetation, towering rock formations, and animatronic dinosaurs, creating an immersive environment that evokes a sense of adventure and danger.

## Attraction Experience
Riders board circular rafts that navigate a winding course through the themed environment. The ride utilizes a combination of conveyor belts and water currents to propel the rafts. The journey includes encounters with animatronic dinosaurs, sudden drops, and intense rapids. Sensory effects include water sprays, mist, and sound effects designed to enhance the immersive experience. The ride duration is approximately 5 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Roaring Rapids is designed for a broad audience, but guests must meet a minimum height requirement of 107 cm (42 inches) to ride. Guests with certain medical conditions, including back or neck problems, heart conditions, or those who are pregnant, are advised against riding. The attraction involves significant splashing and potential for getting wet.
