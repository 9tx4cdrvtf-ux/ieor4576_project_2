# Spaceship Earth
**Location:** Epcot

## Overview
Spaceship Earth is a slow-moving dark ride located inside the iconic geodesic sphere at Epcot in Walt Disney World, Florida. The attraction explores the history of communication, from prehistoric cave paintings to modern technology. It is a signature attraction of Epcot, serving as a visual landmark and a symbolic representation of the park's focus on innovation and the future. The ride was originally developed by WED Enterprises (now Walt Disney Imagineering).

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative follows the evolution of human communication. Guests journey through various historical periods, witnessing key moments and inventions that shaped communication methods. The ride's setting is a blend of physical sets, Audio-Animatronics figures, and projected scenes. The overall atmosphere aims to create a sense of historical progression and technological advancement. The ride culminates in a look at the future of communication, with a personalized interactive segment.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board Omnimover ride vehicles that travel through the interior of Spaceship Earth. The ride begins with scenes depicting the Stone Age and progresses through ancient civilizations, the Renaissance, the invention of the printing press, and the advent of radio and television. The ride utilizes various special effects, including lighting, sound, and projections, to enhance the storytelling. The ride concludes with a look at the future, with interactive screens allowing guests to create their own personalized future scenarios. The entire experience lasts approximately 16 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Spaceship Earth is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with claustrophobia or a sensitivity to dark environments should consider the ride's setting. The ride vehicles are enclosed, and the ride path includes slow inclines and declines.
