# Main Street Vehicles
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
Main Street Vehicles is a transportation attraction located on Main Street, U.S.A., in Disneyland Park Paris. It offers guests a leisurely ride along the street in authentic-looking vehicles, providing a unique perspective of the themed environment. The vehicles are designed to resemble early 20th-century automobiles and transportation methods.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the atmosphere of a turn-of-the-century American town. The vehicles are presented as part of the daily life of Main Street, U.S.A., allowing guests to experience the area as if they were residents or visitors of the era. The theming extends to the vehicles themselves, which are meticulously detailed to reflect the period.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board one of several vehicles, including a horseless carriage, a fire engine, a jitney, and a double-decker bus. The vehicles travel along a predetermined route down Main Street, U.S.A., from Town Square to Central Plaza. The ride provides views of the shops, buildings, and other attractions along the street. The duration of the ride is approximately 5-7 minutes, depending on traffic.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Main Street Vehicles is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests must be able to board and disembark the vehicles with minimal assistance. Guests with mobility issues may require assistance. There are no specific health warnings associated with this attraction.
