# Pirates of the Caribbean
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Pirates of the Caribbean is a dark ride attraction located in New Orleans Square at Disneyland. It is a boat ride that transports guests through a series of scenes depicting a pirate raid on a Caribbean town. Originally conceived by Walt Disney, the attraction is notable for its innovative use of Audio-Animatronics and its immersive storytelling. It is considered a landmark attraction in the history of theme park design.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction's narrative centers on the Golden Age of Piracy. Guests are immersed in a world of swashbuckling pirates, treasure hunts, and naval battles. The queue area sets the scene with a dimly lit grotto and the sounds of the sea. The ride itself progresses through various scenes, including a pirate ship battle, a town under siege, a burning city, and a treasure-filled grotto. The theming incorporates detailed sets, special effects, and a memorable musical score.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board boats that travel through a water-filled flume. The ride begins with a descent into a cavern, followed by scenes depicting a pirate ship battle. The boats then enter the town, where Audio-Animatronic figures portray pirates engaged in various activities. The ride concludes with a descent into a treasure room and a final scene featuring the pirates' escape. The ride duration is approximately 15 minutes.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Pirates of the Caribbean is generally suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with a sensitivity to loud noises, flashing lights, or enclosed spaces should consider their personal comfort levels before riding. The ride does include a small drop.
