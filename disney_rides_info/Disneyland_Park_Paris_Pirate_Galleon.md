# Pirate Galleon
**Location:** Disneyland Park Paris

## Overview
The Pirate Galleon is a swinging ship attraction located in Adventureland at Disneyland Park Paris. It is a classic amusement ride manufactured by Intamin, offering a simulated experience of sailing on a pirate ship. The attraction provides a moderate thrill level and is a popular family-friendly experience within the park.

## Narrative and Theming
The Pirate Galleon is themed as a weathered pirate ship, anchored in a lagoon within Adventureland. The ship's design incorporates elements of classic pirate lore, including tattered sails, wooden planks, and nautical details. The surrounding area is designed to evoke a tropical island setting, complete with themed rockwork and foliage, enhancing the immersive experience. The attraction's narrative places guests aboard a pirate vessel, ready to set sail on the high seas.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board the ship, which then begins to swing back and forth, gradually increasing in height and speed. The ship's motion simulates the rocking of a vessel at sea. The ride cycle lasts approximately two minutes. The attraction utilizes a hydraulic system to control the swinging motion. There are no special effects beyond the physical movement of the ship and the surrounding themed environment.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Pirate Galleon is generally suitable for guests of all ages, although it is recommended that guests be at least 1.02 meters (40 inches) tall to ride. Guests with heart conditions, back problems, or other health concerns should consider the ride's motion before boarding. Pregnant women are advised against riding.
