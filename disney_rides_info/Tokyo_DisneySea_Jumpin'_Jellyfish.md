# Jumpin' Jellyfish
**Location:** Tokyo DisneySea

## Overview
Jumpin' Jellyfish is a family-friendly drop tower ride located in the Port Discovery area of Tokyo DisneySea. Manufactured by S&S – Sansei Technologies, the attraction simulates the experience of gently floating up and down on a jellyfish. It offers a milder thrill than other drop tower rides, making it suitable for a wider range of guests.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed to resemble a collection of colorful jellyfish. Guests are seated in individual gondolas suspended from the "bell" of a large, animated jellyfish. The surrounding area is designed to evoke an underwater environment, with vibrant colors and whimsical marine-themed decorations. The queue line winds through a stylized coral reef, enhancing the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual gondolas, each accommodating a small number of riders. The gondolas are lifted slowly towards the top of the jellyfish "bell." Once at the apex, the gondolas are gently dropped, providing a brief sensation of weightlessness before being smoothly lowered. The ride cycle repeats several times, offering a series of small drops and ascents. The entire experience lasts approximately one minute.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Jumpin' Jellyfish is designed for families and children. The height requirement is 90 cm (35 inches). Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or other pre-existing medical conditions should consult with a physician before riding. Pregnant women are advised against riding.
