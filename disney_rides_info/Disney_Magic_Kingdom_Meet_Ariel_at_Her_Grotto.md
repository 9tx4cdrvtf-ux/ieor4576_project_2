# Meet Ariel at Her Grotto
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Meet Ariel at Her Grotto is a character meet-and-greet attraction located in Fantasyland at the Magic Kingdom theme park. It allows guests to interact with the Disney character Ariel from the animated film *The Little Mermaid*. The attraction is a permanent fixture, offering a photo opportunity and brief conversation with the character.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed as Ariel's underwater grotto, as depicted in the film. The queue area is designed to resemble the ocean floor, with various props and decorations evoking a sense of being submerged. These include coral formations, treasure chests, and other nautical elements. The grotto itself is a brightly lit space where Ariel is positioned to meet guests.

## Attraction Experience
Guests queue in a designated area, progressing through the themed queue. Upon reaching the meeting area, guests are greeted by Ariel. Guests can pose for photographs with Ariel, taken by a Disney PhotoPass photographer or using their own cameras. The interaction typically lasts for a few minutes, allowing for brief conversation and photo opportunities. The entire experience, including the queue, can vary in duration depending on crowd levels.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Meet Ariel at Her Grotto is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions or specific health warnings associated with this attraction. Guests with mobility issues can access the attraction, though they may need to utilize the accessible entrance.
