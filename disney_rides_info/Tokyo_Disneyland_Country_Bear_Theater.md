# Country Bear Theater
**Location:** Tokyo Disneyland

## Overview
The Country Bear Theater is a musical audio-animatronics show located in Tokyo Disneyland's Westernland. It is a clone of the original attraction at Walt Disney World's Magic Kingdom, featuring a cast of singing and dancing bear characters. The attraction is a theater-style show, providing a relaxed and family-friendly experience.

## Narrative and Theming
The Country Bear Theater presents a variety show performed by a troupe of animatronic bears. The setting is a rustic, wooden theater, designed to evoke a frontier-era atmosphere. The bears, each with unique personalities and musical styles, perform a series of country and western songs. The show's narrative is primarily episodic, with each song featuring a different bear or group of bears.

## Attraction Experience
Guests enter a large theater and are seated in rows facing a stage. The show lasts approximately 15 minutes and features a series of musical numbers. The audio-animatronic bears move in sync with the music, performing various actions such as singing, playing instruments, and dancing. The theater is dimly lit, with spotlights illuminating the performers. There are no significant special effects beyond the animatronics and lighting.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The Country Bear Theater is suitable for all ages. There are no height restrictions. The attraction is generally accessible to guests with disabilities. Guests with photosensitivity may want to consider the lighting effects. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions.
