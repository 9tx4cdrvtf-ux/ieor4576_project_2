# Discovery Island Trails
**Location:** Animal Kingdom

## Overview
Discovery Island Trails is a self-guided walking path located on Discovery Island within Disney's Animal Kingdom. It offers guests the opportunity to observe various animal species in naturalistic habitats and enjoy views of the Tree of Life. The attraction is a permanent fixture of the park, designed to immerse guests in the park's central themed area.

## Narrative and Theming
The trails are themed to represent a natural environment, blending seamlessly with the surrounding landscaping and the Tree of Life. The area is designed to evoke a sense of exploration and discovery, with pathways winding through lush vegetation and around animal habitats. Theming elements include rockwork, waterfalls, and carefully chosen plant life to create immersive environments for both guests and animals.

## Attraction Experience
Guests traverse the trails at their own pace, encountering a variety of animal species. These include lemurs, flamingos, Galapagos tortoises, and other birds and mammals. Informational signage is provided throughout the trails, offering details about the animals and their habitats. The experience duration varies depending on the guest's pace, typically ranging from 20 to 45 minutes. The trails are accessible via multiple entry points from Discovery Island.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Discovery Island Trails is suitable for guests of all ages. There are no height restrictions. Guests with mobility issues may find some areas challenging due to uneven terrain. Strollers and wheelchairs are permitted. There are no specific health warnings or restrictions, but guests are advised to remain on designated pathways and to respect the animals' space.
