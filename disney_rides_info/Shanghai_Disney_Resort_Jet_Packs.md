# Jet Packs
**Location:** Shanghai Disney Resort

## Overview
Jet Packs is a spinner-style amusement ride located in Tomorrowland at Shanghai Disney Resort. The attraction allows guests to experience the sensation of flight in individual jet pack vehicles. The ride is a unique offering within the park, providing a visually striking and interactive experience for guests of various ages.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction is themed to a futuristic vision of flight, immersing guests in a world of advanced technology. The queue area features design elements suggestive of a research facility or flight training center. Guests are presented with the narrative of becoming test pilots, preparing to take flight in experimental jet packs. The surrounding environment incorporates futuristic architecture, lighting effects, and soundscapes to enhance the immersive experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual jet pack vehicles, each accommodating one rider. The ride cycle begins with the vehicles ascending a central support structure. Once elevated, the jet packs rotate in a circular motion, allowing riders to control the vertical angle of their vehicle via a joystick. The ride offers panoramic views of Tomorrowland and surrounding areas. The ride duration is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Jet Packs is designed to be enjoyed by a wide audience. Guests must meet a minimum height requirement of 110 cm (43 inches) to ride. Guests with pre-existing health conditions, particularly those affecting the neck, back, or inner ear, are advised to consult with a medical professional before riding. Pregnant women and guests susceptible to motion sickness are also advised to consider the ride's effects.
