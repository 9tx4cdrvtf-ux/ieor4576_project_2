# Avatar Flight of Passage
**Location:** Animal Kingdom

## Overview
Avatar Flight of Passage is a 3D dark ride located within Pandora – The World of Avatar at Disney's Animal Kingdom. The attraction utilizes a flight simulator system, developed by Walt Disney Imagineering, to simulate the experience of riding a banshee across the landscape of Pandora. It is a significant draw for the park, known for its immersive theming and advanced ride technology.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are recruited as avatars to connect with a mountain banshee and experience the thrill of flight. The queue area immerses visitors in the bioluminescent flora and fauna of Pandora, including the research facility of the Alpha Centauri Expeditions (ACE). The narrative centers on the Na'vi culture and the unique ecosystem of Pandora, providing a fictional context for the flight experience.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual ride vehicles designed to resemble the back of a banshee. The ride utilizes a motion-based simulator combined with 3D projections and sensory effects, including wind, mist, and scents, to create a sensation of flight. The experience lasts approximately five minutes, during which riders soar through Pandora's landscapes, encountering various creatures and environments.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is generally suitable for a wide audience. The height requirement is 44 inches (112 cm). Guests with certain health conditions, including back, neck, or heart problems, as well as those who are pregnant, are advised to consider the ride's intensity before boarding.
