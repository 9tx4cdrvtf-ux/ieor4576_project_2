# Indiana Jones™ Adventure
**Location:** Disneyland

## Overview
Indiana Jones™ Adventure is a motion-simulator dark ride located in Adventureland at Disneyland. The attraction, which opened in 1995, was designed and built by Walt Disney Imagineering. It utilizes a unique ride system featuring enhanced motion-base vehicles that navigate a complex track layout, immersing guests in a perilous archaeological expedition. The ride is a significant investment and a landmark attraction within the park.

## Narrative and Theming
Guests are cast as adventurers exploring the Temple of the Forbidden Eye, seeking the legendary Fountain of Youth. The queue area, themed as an archaeological dig site and briefing area, establishes the narrative. Guests are warned of the temple's guardian, Mara, and the dangers within. The ride's environment is richly detailed, featuring ancient ruins, booby traps, and supernatural elements, all contributing to the immersive experience. The story is further advanced through audio-animatronics and special effects.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board troop transport vehicles, each holding twelve passengers. The vehicles traverse a track, encountering various scenes including collapsing bridges, giant snakes, and a rolling boulder. The ride incorporates motion simulation, synchronized with the visuals and audio, to enhance the sense of peril and adventure. The ride duration is approximately 3 minutes and 30 seconds.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
The attraction is designed for a broad audience, but has a height requirement of 46 inches (117 cm). Guests with heart conditions, back or neck problems, or those who are pregnant are advised against riding. The ride features sudden movements, loud noises, and darkness.
