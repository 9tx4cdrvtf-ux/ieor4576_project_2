# Mad Tea Party
**Location:** Disney Magic Kingdom

## Overview
Mad Tea Party is a spinning tea cup ride located in Fantasyland at the Magic Kingdom. The attraction is a classic amusement ride, manufactured by Arrow Development, and is a staple of Disney theme parks worldwide. It is based on the "Mad Tea Party" scene from Walt Disney's 1951 animated film *Alice in Wonderland*.

## Narrative and Theming
The attraction immerses guests in the whimsical world of the Mad Hatter's tea party. The ride area is designed to resemble a garden setting, with oversized teacups placed on a large, rotating platform. The surrounding environment features colorful decorations, stylized trees, and ambient music from the film, creating a playful and surreal atmosphere.

## Attraction Experience
Guests board individual teacups, each capable of holding several riders. The platform begins to rotate, and riders can further control the spinning of their individual teacups via a wheel located in the center. The ride cycle lasts approximately 90 seconds. The attraction utilizes a simple mechanical design, relying on a central rotating mechanism and individual cup axles.

## Target Audience and Restrictions
Mad Tea Party is generally suitable for all ages, though the spinning motion may cause motion sickness in susceptible individuals. There are no height restrictions. Guests with a history of motion sickness, vertigo, or other health concerns should consider the potential effects before riding.
